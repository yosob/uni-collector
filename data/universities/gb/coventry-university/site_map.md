## Site Structure Notes
- **Course URL pattern**: `https://www.coventry.ac.uk/course-structure/{level}/{faculty}/{course-slug}/`
- **Levels**: `ug` (undergraduate), `pg` (postgraduate)
- **Faculty codes**:
  - `fah` — Faculty of Arts and Humanities (School of Art, Design & Media)
  - `eec` — Faculty of Engineering, Environment and Computing
  - `cas` — College of Arts and Society (postgraduate)
  - `cbl` — Coventry Business School / Law
  - `fbl` — Faculty of Business and Law
- **London campus**: `https://www.coventry.ac.uk/london/course-structure/{level}/{course-slug}/`
- **Cookie wall**: Site uses aggressive CookieYes consent wall that blocks automated content extraction (Jina/HTTP fetchers only receive cookie consent text; actual course content requires browser interaction)
- **URL volatility**: Many historical course pages return 404; the site underwent major restructuring in 2024–2025

## Extraction Notes
1. **Cookie wall issue**: All page fetches via Jina/HTTP return only cookie consent overlay content. The `<h1>` tag in the response confirms the page title (e.g., "BA (Hons) Fine Art" or "Error 404"), which is how verified vs. 404 was distinguished.
2. **Base URL**: `https://www.coventry.ac.uk/course-structure/`
3. **Recommended extraction approach**: Use browser automation (Playwright/Puppeteer) to accept cookies before extracting content, or use the smart-extractor with a headless browser configuration.
