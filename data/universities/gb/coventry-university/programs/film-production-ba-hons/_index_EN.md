## Curriculum Summary

**Common First Year** (shared with related Film and Media courses):

**Year 1:**
- ScreenCraft – 30 credits (Compulsory)
- Key Concepts in Media and Film Production – 30 credits (Compulsory)
- Storytelling: From Page to Production – 30 credits (Compulsory)
- Working in Creative Practice – 30 credits (Compulsory)
- Film and Media: Industry and Aesthetics – 30 credits (Compulsory)

**Year 2:**
- Production Studio – 30 credits (Compulsory)
- Documentary Craft – 30 credits (Compulsory)
- Advanced Cinematography and Sound Design – 30 credits (Compulsory)
- Working in Creative Practice 2 – 30 credits (Compulsory)

**Placement Year (Optional):**
- UK Work Placement – 0 credits (Optional)
- International Study/Work Placement – 0 credits (Optional)

**Final Year:**
- Final Major Project – 60 credits (Compulsory)
- Collaborative Project – 30 credits (Compulsory)
- Film Industry Professional Practice – 30 credits (Compulsory)

**Assessment methods:** Coursework, practical projects, portfolios, showreels, essays, presentations, group projects, reflective journals, written examinations.

## Scholarship Info

Vice-Chancellor Scholarship: Fee waiver up to £5,000 for self-funded international students. Undergraduate (3 years): £4,000 total (£2,000 in Year 1, £1,000 in each subsequent year). Not applicable for UK/EU citizens. Coventry University also offers the Global Leaders Scholarship and country-specific scholarships.
