## Curriculum Summary

**Year 1 (Common First Year):**
Shared with Advertising and Digital Marketing BA (Hons), Business Administration BBA (Hons), Business and Human Resources Management BA (Hons), Business and Marketing BA (Hons), Business Management BA (Hons), Enterprise and Entrepreneurship BA (Hons), International Business Management BSc (Hons), Marketing BA (Hons), Sport Management BA (Hons).

- Marketing, Digital and Social Media – 30 credits (Compulsory)
- People and Organisation – 30 credits (Compulsory)
- Analytics for Decision Making – 30 credits (Compulsory)
- Additional common modules to total 120 credits (Compulsory)

**Year 2:**
- Corporate Events – 20 credits (Compulsory)
- Developing a Professional Identity – 10 credits (Compulsory)
- Research Methods – 20 credits (Compulsory)
- Live Event Management – 20 credits (Compulsory)
- Add+vantage module – 10 credits (Compulsory)
- Optional Modules – 40 credits (Optional)

**Placement Year (Optional):**
- UK Work Placement – 0 credits (Optional)
- International Study/Work Placement – 0 credits (Optional)

**Final Year:**
- Strategic Management in Sport and Events – 20 credits (Compulsory)
- Managing Your Career – 10 credits (Compulsory)
- International Event Management – 20 credits (Compulsory)
- Event Design – 20 credits (Compulsory)
- Project (Dissertation) – 20 credits (Compulsory)
- Add+vantage module – 10 credits (Compulsory)
- Optional Modules – 20 credits (Optional)

**Assessment methods:** Formal examinations, phase tests, essays, group work, presentations, reports, projects, coursework, individual assignments. Variety of methods depending on module.

## Scholarship Info

Vice-Chancellor Scholarship: Fee waiver up to £5,000 for self-funded international students. Undergraduate (3 years): £4,000 total (£2,000 in Year 1, £1,000 in each subsequent year). Not applicable for UK/EU citizens. Coventry University also offers the Global Leaders Scholarship and country-specific scholarships.

## Accreditation

Accredited by the Chartered Management Institute (CMI). Students have the option to register and gain a Level 5 Certificate in Management and Leadership. Member of the Association of Event Management Education (AEME), European Foundation for Management Development (EFMD), and Chartered Association of Business Schools (CABS).
