## Curriculum Summary

**Year 1 – Common First Year (shared across Business and Marketing cluster):**
- People and Organisations – 30 credits: Management and organisation theories; workplace behaviour, leadership, motivation, engagement and team dynamics
- Marketing, Digital and Social Media – 30 credits: Fundamental marketing concepts; buyer behaviour, segmentation, targeting, positioning, branding, digital and social media marketing
- Analytics for Decision-Making – 30 credits: Business finance and data analytics fundamentals; measuring and analysing performance; financial management and data analytic tools
- Responsible Management, Sustainability and Entrepreneurship – 30 credits: Sustainability and entrepreneurship in management; UN SDGs; ethical business practices and innovation

**Year 2:**
- Research and Insight – 30 credits: Research process including literature review, methodology, data collection and analysis; designing research to meet specific objectives
- Consumer Behaviour – 30 credits: Consumer decision-making processes; psychological underpinnings; business-to-business marketing and organisational buyer behaviour
- Integrated Marketing Communications – 30 credits: IMC strategies and plans; practical application of marketing communications to real-life cases; ethics in IMC
- Creative Marketing – 30 credits: Innovative advertising and digital marketing campaigns; advertising tools, techniques and media; data and analytics in campaign strategy and evaluation

**Placement Year (Optional):**
- UK Work Placement – 0 credits: Minimum 26 weeks of approved UK placement experience
- International Study/Work Placement – 0 credits: Minimum 26 weeks of approved international study/work placement

**Final Year:**
- Sustainable Marketing Management – 30 credits: Strategic marketing vision for people, planet and profit; product and supply chain management; change management; sustainable marketing plan development
- Applied Project – 30 credits: Real-world business issue; research project, consultancy investigation or management simulation; evidence-based insights and recommendations
- Digital Consumer Experience – 30 credits: Digital impact on consumer experience and behaviour; social media, mobile apps and digital channels; omnichannel strategy and customer journey design
- Social Media and Influencer Marketing – 30 credits: Social media marketing strategy; role of influencers and content creators; tools for evaluating influencer marketing impact

**Assessment methods:** Phase tests, essays, group work, presentations, reports, projects, coursework, exams and individual assignments.

**Teaching approach:** Theory and practice-based lectures and seminars; group projects; advertising campaign development; simulation games; industry-standard software and hardware in the specialist DigiComm Lab. First year: 12–15 teaching hours per week.

**Accreditation:** Accredited by the Chartered Institute of Marketing (CIM) for 2025/26 academic year (subject to re-accreditation for 2026/27). Graduates with 2:2 or above eligible for CIM modular exemptions.

## Scholarship Info

Vice-Chancellor Scholarship: Fee waiver up to £5,000 for self-funded international students. Undergraduate (3 years): £4,000 total (£2,000 in Year 1, £1,000 in each subsequent year). Not applicable for UK/EU citizens. Coventry also offers the Academic Excellence Scholarship and sports scholarships. Contextual/Fair Access Offer available: 16 UCAS points lower than standard entry requirements for eligible applicants.
