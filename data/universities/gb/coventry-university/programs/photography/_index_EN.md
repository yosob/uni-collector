## Admission Requirements

- A-level: BBC (Art and Design or Photography preferred)
- GCSE: 5 GCSEs at grade 4/C or above including English and Mathematics
- BTEC: DMM in an Art or Design related subject
- IB Diploma: 29 points minimum
- Portfolio submission required
- Interview may be required
- UCAS code: W640

## Curriculum Summary

**Year 1:**
- Histories and Practices (30 credits)
- Documents and Fictions (20 credits)
- Expanded Practices (20 credits)
- Representation and the Image (20 credits)
- Professional Development (10 credits)

**Year 2:**
- Concepts and Approaches (40 credits)
- Community, Culture and Identity (20 credits)
- Photography and Narrative (20 credits)
- Professional Experience (10 credits)

**Year 3 (Final Year):**
- Independent Research (30 credits)
- Independent Practice: Exploration (30 credits)
- Independent Practice: Realisation (40 credits)
- Professional Futures (10 credits)

Optional placement year available between Year 2 and Year 3 (additional fee applies).

**Assessment methods:** Coursework, portfolios, practical projects, presentations, written assignments, group work, exhibitions, degree show.

## Scholarship Info

Vice-Chancellor Scholarship: Fee waiver up to £5,000 for self-funded international students. Undergraduate (3 years): £4,000 total (£2,000 in Year 1, £1,000 in each subsequent year). Not applicable for UK/EU citizens.
