## Admission Requirements

- A-level: BBC (any subject accepted; Art and Design preferred)
- GCSE: 5 GCSEs at grade 4/C or above including English and Mathematics
- BTEC: DMM in an Art or Design related subject
- IB Diploma: 29 points minimum
- Portfolio submission required
- Interview may be required
- UCAS code: W100

## Curriculum Summary

**Year 1 (Common First Year):**
- Creative Thinking Processes and Materials
- Professional Contexts
- Creative Practice

**Year 2:**
- Explore: Currency of Connections
- Contextual and Professional Explorations
- Fine Art studio-based projects

**Year 3 (Final Year):**
- Art Consolidations
- Art Consolidation and Professional Practice
- Contextual Studies Consolidations
- Professional Consolidations

Optional placement year available between Year 2 and Year 3 (additional fee applies).

**Assessment methods:** Artefacts, practical coursework, digital coursework, individual work, group work, presentations, projects, individual assignments.

## Scholarship Info

Vice-Chancellor Scholarship: Fee waiver up to £5,000 for self-funded international students. Undergraduate (3 years): £4,000 total (£2,000 in Year 1, £1,000 in each subsequent year). Not applicable for UK/EU citizens.
