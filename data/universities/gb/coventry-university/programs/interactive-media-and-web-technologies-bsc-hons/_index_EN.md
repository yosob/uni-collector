## Curriculum Summary

**Year 1:**
- Introduction to software techniques and technologies
- Foundations of web development (front-end and back-end)
- Principles of technical art and digital media
- Introduction to animation, VFX, modelling and motion graphics
- Programming fundamentals
- Interactive media concepts

**Year 2:**
- Advanced web development and full-stack techniques
- Immersive app development
- User interface design and user experience
- Advanced motion graphics and technical art
- Collaborative and interdisciplinary projects
- Professional contexts and industry practice

**Year 3 (Final Year):**
- Major project in interactive media or web technologies
- Advanced interactive media development
- Professional consolidation and career development
- Portfolio development and showcase

Optional placement year available between Year 2 and Year 3 (sandwich mode, 4 years total). Additional fee applies for placement year.
