# International Fashion Management and Marketing BA (Hons)

## Overview

The **International Fashion Management and Marketing BA (Hons)** is a business-oriented fashion degree offered at **Coventry University London**, located in the heart of the City of London. The programme combines fashion industry knowledge with management and marketing expertise, preparing students for careers in the global fashion business.

The course covers key areas including fashion management, international marketing, sustainable supply chain management, digital consumer analytics, and creative storytelling. Students develop both creative and business skills relevant to the fast-paced fashion industry.

- **A-levels**: BBC

- **BTEC**: DMM
- **GCSE**: 5 GCSEs at grade 9-4 / A*-C including English and Maths
- **IELTS**: 6.0 (no component below 5.5)

## Curriculum

### Year 1 (Level 4) — Fashion Business Foundations

| Module | Credits |
|--------|---------|
| Business Economics | 30 |
| International Business Environment | 30 |
| Marketing Essentials | 30 |
| Evolution of Fashion Landscapes | 30 |

### Year 2 (Level 5) — Industry Practice and Digital Innovation

| Module | Credits |
|--------|---------|
| Research Methods and Applications | 30 |
| Digital Consumer Insights and Big Data | 30 |
| Sustainable Fashion Supply Chain Management | 30 |
| Creative Storytelling and Fashion Campaigns | 30 |

### Year 3 (Level 6) — Strategic Fashion Management and Final Project

Year 3 modules focus on strategic-level fashion management and include a Final Major Project. Specific module names are to be confirmed from the university's official course specification.

### Optional Sandwich Year

Students may take a sandwich year between Year 2 and Year 3 for a professional placement (UK or overseas) or international study at a partner institution.

## Careers

Graduates can pursue roles such as Fashion Buyer, Brand Manager, Digital Marketing Executive, Fashion Entrepreneur, Supply Chain Manager, Social Media Manager, E-commerce Manager, and Fashion PR. The London location provides direct access to one of the world's leading fashion capitals.
