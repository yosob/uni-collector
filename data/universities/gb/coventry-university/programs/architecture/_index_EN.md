## Curriculum Summary

**Year 1:**
- Construction and Environmental Fundamentals
- Experimental Design and Communication
- Spatial Design
- Material Cultures and Histories
- Digital Skills 1

**Year 2:**
- Architectural Design (focus on tectonics and urban context)
- Technology and Environment (materials, structure, environmental design)
- Urban Design and Emerging Contemporary Planning
- History and Theory of Architecture
- Digital Skills 2 (3D modelling and digital drawing)

**Year 3 (Final Year):**
- Comprehensive Design Project
- Design Technology and Environment Integration
- Dissertation
- Professional Practice and Ethics

Optional sandwich placement year available between Year 2 and Year 3 (4-year sandwich route). The course is ARB (Architects Registration Board) and RIBA (Royal Institute of British Architects) accredited, providing Part 1 exemption.

**Assessment methods:** Practical/project work, coursework, essays, individual assignments, presentations, group work.

## Scholarship Info

Vice-Chancellor Scholarship: Fee waiver up to £5,000 for self-funded international students. Undergraduate (3 years): £4,000 total (£2,000 in Year 1, £1,000 in each subsequent year). Not applicable for UK/EU citizens. Coventry also offers the Academic Excellence Scholarship and Sports Scholarship.
