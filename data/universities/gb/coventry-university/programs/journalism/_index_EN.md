## Curriculum Summary

**Common First Year** (shared with Media and Communications BA (Hons)):

**Year 1:**
- Multiplatform Skills in the Digital Age – 30 credits (Compulsory)
- The Power to Influence: Media and Responsibility – 30 credits (Compulsory)
- Creating Stories: Narratives and Communities – 30 credits (Compulsory)
- Digital Tribes: Understanding Audiences – 30 credits (Compulsory)

**Year 2:**
- Chasing the Deadline: Newsrooms in Action – 30 credits (Compulsory)
- Being A Credible Journalist: Public Affairs, Law and Ethics – 30 credits (Compulsory)
- Networking, Professionalism and Self-branding in the Media – 30 credits (Compulsory)
- Entertainment and Lifestyle Journalism – 30 credits (Compulsory)

**Placement Year (Optional):**
- UK Work Placement – 0 credits (Optional)
- International Study/Work Placement – 0 credits (Optional)

**Final Year:**
- Running a Professional Newsroom – 30 credits (Compulsory)
- Political and Investigative Journalism – 30 credits (Compulsory)
- Diving Deep: Long-form Journalism and Innovation – 30 credits (Compulsory)
- Optional Module (choose one):
  - Sports Journalism – 30 credits
  - Persuasion: Public Relations and Marketing – 30 credits

**Assessment methods:** Portfolio, audio/radio stories, video/TV stories, online/web articles, individual website, social media posts, court report, audio essay or commentary, video essay or commentary, written reflective essay, research report, diary or log, open book test (media law and ethics), employment material (CV, cover letter, skills audit), networking material, strategy pitch, long-form journalism including documentary.
