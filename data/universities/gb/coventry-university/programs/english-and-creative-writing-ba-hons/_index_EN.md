## Curriculum Summary

**Year 1 (Level 4):**
- Discourse and Digital Media (30 credits) — mandatory
- Poetry for Digital Times (20 credits) — mandatory
- Skills for Writers (10 credits) — mandatory
- Approaches to Literature for English (30 credits) — mandatory
- Magazine Publishing (20 credits) — mandatory
- Add+Vantage module (10 credits) — mandatory

**Year 2 (Level 5):**
- Language and Society (20 credits) — mandatory
- Interactive and Game Narratives (20 credits) — mandatory
- The Short Story Workshop (20 credits) — mandatory
- Modernism (20 credits) — mandatory
- Cine-aesthetics: The Language of Film (10 credits) — mandatory
- Add+Vantage module (10 credits) — mandatory
- Choose one option: Stylistics (20 credits) / Graphic Storytelling (20 credits) / The Victorians and Empire (20 credits)

**Year 3 (Level 6):**
- Screenwriting (20 credits) — mandatory
- Post War Literature (20 credits) — mandatory
- Writing for the Theatre and Radio (20 credits) — mandatory
- Employability for Writers (10 credits) — mandatory
- Add+Vantage module (10 credits) — mandatory
- Choose 40 credits from options: English Dissertation (40 credits) / Creative Dissertation (40 credits) / African American Writing: Place, Race and Identity (20 credits) / Speculative Fiction (20 credits) / Language and Globalisation (20 credits) / Utopias and Dystopias (20 credits)

Optional placement year (Professional Enhancement) or study abroad year (International Enhancement) available between Year 2 and Year 3, extending the course to 4 years.

**Assessment methods:** Coursework, creative writing portfolios, essays, reflective commentaries, presentations, group projects, dissertation.

## Scholarship Info

Vice-Chancellor Scholarship: Fee waiver up to £5,000 for self-funded international students. Undergraduate (3 years): £4,000 total (£2,000 in Year 1, £1,000 in each subsequent year). Not applicable for UK/EU citizens.
