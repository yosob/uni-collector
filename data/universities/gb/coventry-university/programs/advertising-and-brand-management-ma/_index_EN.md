## Course Summary

**Semester 1:**
- Marketing for Sustainable Growth (20 credits) — Core
- Brand Management and Storytelling (20 credits) — Core
- Creative Disruptive Advertising (20 credits) — Core

**Semester 2:**
- Brand and Digital Advertising Strategies (20 credits) — Core
- Disruptive Marketing and Advertising Strategies (20 credits) — Core
- Professional Development (20 credits) — Core

**Semester 3 (Summer):**
- Advertising and Brand Management Final Project (60 credits) — Core

This course is designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills required to succeed in the fast-evolving advertising and brand management industry. It combines creative advertising practices with strategic brand management, enabling students to develop integrated marketing communications campaigns that leverage both traditional and digital channels. The curriculum explores disruptive marketing techniques incorporating emerging technologies such as AI, AR, and VR, alongside brand storytelling and sustainable growth strategies. Students will gain hands-on experience using industry-standard software and bespoke teaching facilities, developing transferable business and advertising management skills. The course benefits from strong industry links and practical project-based learning, preparing graduates for leadership roles in advertising agencies, brand consultancies, and corporate marketing departments.

**Assessment Methods:** Coursework, presentations, portfolio submissions, written assignments, project reports, professional research reports, final major project.
