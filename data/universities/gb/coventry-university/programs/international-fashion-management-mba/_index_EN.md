# International Fashion Management MBA

## Overview

Equip yourself with the leadership and management skills needed to succeed in the dynamic international fashion industry.

The International Fashion Management MBA at **Coventry University London** is a challenging, fast-paced programme designed to offer the industry-specific business and management skills you need to build a successful career in international fashion management. This course provides a comprehensive understanding of the business of fashion, rather than teaching design or production — preparing you for management and leadership roles.

The programme focuses on:
- The global and technological shifts that have characterised the fashion industry in recent years
- The management of fashion design and product development
- International fashion manufacturing and retailing strategies
- Ethics and sustainability in fashion
- Digital technologies and their impact on fashion business
- Logistics and supply chain management

Every aspect of your learning is set within an international and intercultural context.

## Why Choose This Course?

- **Industry-Focused MBA** — gain business and management skills tailored specifically to the international fashion industry
- **London Location** — study in the heart of one of the world's fashion capitals at Coventry University's London campus
- **CMI Accredited** — graduate with a CMI Level 7 Certificate in Strategic Management and Leadership Practice
- **Real-World Projects** — engage with actual industry clients through the Strategic International Brand Immersion module
- **Multiple Intakes** — start your studies in September, March, May, or July
- **Diverse Cohort** — study alongside students from around the world, enriching your global perspective

## Entry Requirements

- An honours degree 2:2 or above (or international equivalent) in a relevant discipline
- Applicants with a degree in another discipline may be admitted on a case-by-case basis
- English language: IELTS 6.5 (minimum 5.5 in each component)
