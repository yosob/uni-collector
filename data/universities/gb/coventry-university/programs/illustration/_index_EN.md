## Admission Requirements

- A-level: BBC (any subject; Art and Design preferred)
- GCSE: 5 GCSEs at grade 4/C or above including English and Mathematics
- BTEC: DMM in an Art or Design related subject
- IB Diploma: 29 points minimum
- Portfolio submission required
- Interview required
- UCAS code: W225

## Curriculum Summary

**Year 1 (Common First Year — shared with Fine Art BA):**
- Creative Thinking Processes and Materials
- Professional Contexts
- Creative Practice

**Year 2:**
- Illustration Practice (core spine module)
- Negotiated Self-Directed Illustration Projects
- Contextual and Professional Explorations
- Printmaking, Digital and 3D Illustration workshops

**Year 3 (Final Year):**
- Extended Study in Illustration (major project)
- Contextual Studies and Critical Research
- Professional Consolidation and Career Development

Optional placement year available between Year 2 and Year 3 (additional fee applies).

> **Note:** This course was restructured from the former Illustration and Animation BA (Hons). Module names for Year 2 and Year 3 are inferred from third-party sources (Education Index, Uni Compare) as the official course specification PDF has been removed from the Coventry website. Year 1 shares a common first year with Fine Art BA (Hons).

**Assessment methods:** Artefacts, practical coursework, digital coursework, individual work, group work, live/video presentations, reports, projects, individual assignments.

## Scholarship Info

Vice-Chancellor Scholarship: Fee waiver up to £5,000 for self-funded international students. Undergraduate (3 years): £4,000 total (£2,000 in Year 1, £1,000 in each subsequent year). Not applicable for UK/EU citizens.
