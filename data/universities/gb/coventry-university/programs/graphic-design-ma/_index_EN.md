- Interview may be required (virtual interview available)
- International: 60% in a 3-year undergraduate degree or 55% in a 4-year undergraduate degree

## Curriculum Summary

**Semester 1:**
- Collaboration in Practice (20 credits)
- Graphic Design Specialism 1 (40 credits)
- Creative Futures and Social Change (20 credits)

**Semester 2:**
- Professional Context (20 credits)
- Graphic Design Specialism 2 (40 credits)
- Graphic Design Specialist Investigation (20 credits)

The course provides the opportunity to explore graphic design practice through practical design work shaped by design principles and processes that can include a wide range of disciplines. Students develop their specialist practice and interests, and learn to work in a professional context. The programme encourages research, ethics awareness, and creative activities while working closely with peers.

**Assessment methods:** Design projects, coursework, presentations, portfolio submissions, written assignments, specialist investigation report.
