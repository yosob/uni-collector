## Admission Requirements

- A-level: BBC (Art or Design subject preferred)
- GCSE: 5 GCSEs at grade 4/C or above including English and Mathematics
- BTEC: DMM in an Art or Design related subject
- IB Diploma: 29 points minimum
- Portfolio submission required
- Interview may be required
- UCAS code: W24D

## Curriculum Summary

**Year 1 (Common First Year — shared with other FAH design programmes):**
- Creative Thinking Processes and Materials
- Professional Contexts
- Creative Practice

**Year 2:**
- Design Exploration and Innovation
- Materials, Processes and Prototyping
- User-Centred Design Projects
- Contextual and Professional Studies

**Year 3 (Final Year):**
- Product Design Major Project
- Design Research and Development
- Professional Practice and Portfolio
- Contextual Studies Consolidation

Optional sandwich placement year available between Year 2 and Year 3 (additional fee applies).

**Assessment methods:** Artefacts, practical coursework, digital coursework, individual work, group work, live/poster presentations, reports, projects, individual assignments.

Vice-Chancellor Scholarship: Fee waiver up to £5,000 for self-funded international students. Undergraduate (3 years): £4,000 total (£2,000 in Year 1, £1,000 in each subsequent year). Not applicable for UK/EU citizens.
