## Course Summary

This course reflects global trends in fashion marketing and business systems, standards and practices from around the world, drawing upon international case studies and fostering intercultural fluency among students.

Students will gain comprehensive knowledge across all major areas of international fashion marketing, branding and retail — from global market analysis and product development through to consumer behaviour and fashion brand management. The course develops knowledge, understanding, critical thinking and problem-solving abilities in areas required by contemporary international fashion business organisations.

**Key Learning Themes:**
- Sustainability and Diversity
- Problem Solving
- Consumer Behaviour
- Innovation

**Course Structure:**
- **Semester 1:** Builds foundational core academic and creative skills and a critical understanding of key fashion business and marketing concepts
- **Semester 2:** Applies concepts, theories and research to fashion industry scenarios
- **Final Semester (Summer):** Major project/dissertation — choose from several options including a traditional dissertation, a business project, or a consultancy project

**Course Variations:**
- MSc International Fashion Marketing (1 year full-time)
- MSc International Fashion Marketing with Extended Professional Practice (18 months, includes additional professional placement)

**Accredited by:** Chartered Management Institute (CMI) Level 7 Certificate in Strategic Management and Leadership Practice

**Assessment methods:** Coursework, presentations, practical project reports, portfolio submissions, written assignments, group work, dissertation.
