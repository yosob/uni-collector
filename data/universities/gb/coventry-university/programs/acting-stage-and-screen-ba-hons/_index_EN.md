## Curriculum Summary

**Year 1:**
- The Actor's Toolkit: Character and Rehearsal – 30 credits (Compulsory)
- The Performer and The Space – 30 credits (Compulsory)
- The Actor's Toolkit: Performance Styles and Forms – 30 credits (Compulsory)
- Devising Performance Texts – 30 credits (Compulsory)

**Year 2:**
- The Actor's Toolkit: Acting for Camera – 30 credits (Compulsory)
- The Actor's Toolkit: Performing Shakespeare – 30 credits (Compulsory)
- Exploring New Forms of Creative Practice – 30 credits (Compulsory)
- Directed Performance – 30 credits (Compulsory)

**Placement Year (Optional):**
- UK Work Placement – 0 credits (Optional)
- International Study/Work Placement – 0 credits (Optional)

**Final Year:**
- The Actor's Toolkit: Industry Readiness – 30 credits (Compulsory)
- Final Major Performance Project – 60 credits (Compulsory)
- Additional 30-credit module (TBC – likely research/reflective practice module; course spec confirms 120 credits per level)

**Assessment methods:** Practical performances, portfolios, reflective journals, presentations, coursework, vivas, group projects, self-directed performance work. No formal written examinations.

## Scholarship Info

Vice-Chancellor Scholarship: Fee waiver up to £5,000 for self-funded international students. Undergraduate (3 years): £4,000 total (£2,000 in Year 1, £1,000 in each subsequent year). Not applicable for UK/EU citizens. Coventry University also offers the Global Leaders Scholarship and country-specific scholarships.
