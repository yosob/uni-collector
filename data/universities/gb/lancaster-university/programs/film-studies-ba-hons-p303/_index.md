## Overview

Film Studies at Lancaster offers a cutting-edge programme that combines intellectual rigour with practical filmmaking experience. Cinema shapes our emotions, our societies, and our futures — this programme develops your unique voice by exploring rich cinema history and theories and using them to inspire practical film projects.

The programme provides access to top-of-the-line production equipment including Black Magic 4K cameras, cinema-ready digital cameras, and 24/7 access to editing suites. Lancaster's surroundings — from cityscape and rural backdrops to coastal charm — provide an ideal film setting.

Students benefit from the university's partnership with the British Film Institute (BFI), with free access to BFI Player resources, as well as community screenings and events at The Dukes Cinema.

## Course Structure

### Year 1

**Core Modules:**
- Introduction to Film Studies
- Understanding Media and Culture
- British Cinema

**Optional Modules:**
- Transformations in Digital Media
- Raw Materials of Performance
- Screens and Selves
- Re-Making Tradition
- World Literatures
- Art Histories and Contexts

### Year 2

**Core Modules:**
- Global Cinemas
- Classic Hollywood Genres

**Optional Modules:**
- Film, Gender, Race and Ideology
- Screenwriting
- Film Theory and Aesthetics
- Short Film Production
- Visual Cultures
- Digital Cultures

### Year 3

**Core Modules:**
- Independent Project (Film, English, Creative Writing and Media)

**Optional Modules:**
- Global Film Genres
- Documentary Production
- Audiences, Fans and Participatory Cultures
- Contemporary Literature
- Film Industries, Practices and Movements
- Film Circulation, Exhibition and Audiences
- Experimental Film Production
- The Making of Media and AI Futures

## Careers

Graduates pursue careers as:
- Film and Media Producer
- Advertising Executive
- Marketing Executive
- Broadcast and Print Journalist
- Film Programmer or Curator
- Script Supervisor
- Content Producer (TV, Film, Video, and Social Media)
- Art Director
- Teacher
- Film Director

Previous graduates have continued their studies at institutions such as the London Film School, Met Film School, National Film and Television School, and New York Film Academy.

## Facilities

- Black Magic 4K cameras and cinema-ready digital cameras
- 24/7 access to acoustic editing suites with Adobe Creative Cloud
- Specialist production facilities and purpose-built workshop
- Professional lighting, grip equipment, and DSLR cameras
