## English {#english}

### Programme Overview

Film, Media and Cultural Studies at Lancaster University combines hands-on filmmaking practice with the critical study of media and culture. Explore the complex connections between film, media and culture, examining how they shape our understanding of the world from local and global perspectives.

**Key Facts:**
- **UCAS Code:** PL36
- **Duration:** 3 years full-time
- **Department:** School of Arts, Faculty of Humanities, Arts and Social Sciences
- **Tuition (2026/27):** £9,790 (Home) | £25,490 (International)
- **Entry Requirements:** ABB at A Level | IB 32 points | IELTS 6.5
- **Portfolio Required:** No

### Why Choose This Programme?

- Interdisciplinary approach blending film production with media and cultural analysis
- Emphasis on global perspectives — examining media and culture across national and transnational contexts
- Cinema-ready digital cameras, DSLRs, professional lighting and grip equipment
- 24/7 access to editing suites with Adobe Creative Cloud
- University partnership with the British Film Institute (BFI) — free BFI Player access
- Strong industry connections with BFI, The Bay International Film Festival and La1 Shorts Festival
- Student media societies including television station, newspaper, radio and student-run cinema (Take2)

### Course Structure

**Year 1** — Core modules in Introduction to Film Studies, Understanding Media and Culture, and British Cinema. Optional modules include Transformations in Digital Media, The Reflective Design Process, Media, Power and Identity, and World Literatures.

**Year 2** — Core modules in Global Cinemas and Key Perspectives in Global Media and Culture. Optional modules include Screenwriting, Short Film Production, Classic Hollywood Genres, Film Theory and Aesthetics, and Gender and Sexuality in Popular Culture.

**Year 3** — Core modules in Independent Project and Media Industries. Optional modules include Documentary Production, Experimental Film Production, Global Film Genres, Audiences, Fans and Participatory Cultures, and The Making of Media and AI Futures.

### Careers

Film and Media Producer · Content Creator · Journalist (Broadcast, Print and Digital) · Film Programmer/Curator · Media Researcher · Advertising and Marketing Executive · Public Relations · Arts Administrator · Digital Media Strategist · Teacher

- Film, Media and Cultural Studies (Study Abroad) BA Hons — PL38

## 中文 {#中文}

### 专业概述

兰卡斯特大学的电影、媒体与文化研究专业将实践性电影制作与媒体文化的批判性研究相结合。深入探索电影、媒体与文化之间的复杂联系，从本土和全球视角审视它们如何塑造我们对世界的认知。

**基本信息：**
- **UCAS 代码：** PL36
- **学制：** 3年全日制
- **院系：** 人文、艺术与社会科学学院，艺术学院
- **学费（2026/27）：** £9,790（本国）| £25,490（国际）
- **入学要求：** A-Level ABB | IB 32分 | 雅思 6.5
- **是否需要作品集：** 否

### 为什么选择这个专业？

- 跨学科方法，融合电影制作与媒体文化分析
- 强调全球视角——从国家和跨文化维度审视媒体与文化
- 配备专业级数字摄影机、单反相机、灯光和稳定设备
- 24/7 使用配备 Adobe Creative Cloud 的剪辑室
- 与英国电影协会（BFI）合作，免费获取 BFI Player 资源
- 与 BFI、The Bay 国际电影节和 La1 短片电影节等行业机构紧密合作
- 丰富的学生媒体社团，包括电视台、报社、广播电台和学生运营影院（Take2）

### 课程结构

**第一年** — 核心课程包括电影研究导论、媒体与文化理解、英国电影。选修课程包括数字媒体转型、反思性设计流程、媒体、权力与身份、世界文学等。

**第二年** — 核心课程包括全球电影和全球媒体与文化关键视角。选修课程包括编剧、短片制作、经典好莱坞类型片、电影理论与美学、流行文化中的性别与性等。

**第三年** — 核心课程包括独立项目和媒体产业。选修课程包括纪录片制作、实验电影制作、全球电影类型、受众、粉丝与参与式文化、媒体与 AI 未来的构建等。

### 就业方向

影视制作人 · 内容创作者 · 记者（广播、纸媒与数字媒体）· 电影策展人 · 媒体研究员 · 广告与市场营销 · 公共关系 · 艺术管理 · 数字媒体策略师 · 教师

### 专业变体
