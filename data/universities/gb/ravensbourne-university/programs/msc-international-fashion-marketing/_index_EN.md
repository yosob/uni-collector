## Course Overview

The MSc International Fashion Marketing at Ravensbourne University London is a one-year full-time master's programme designed for those looking to enhance their academic and practical marketing skillsets specialising in the fast-paced, dynamic world of fashion. The programme delivers on the core subject of International Fashion Marketing alongside a broad range of practice and emergent processes in Digital Fashion Marketing, Innovation and Strategy through research and development.

**Note: This course is closed for 2026 entry.**

## Why Study This Degree?

1. Gain master's level skills in the use of data measurement in the context of international fashion marketing
2. Learn to identify how brands are supported through strategic marketing
3. Improve your future employment prospects through simulated tasks emulating real world practice
4. Enhance your professional network by meeting people with similar interests and different skill-sets
5. Be part of a tech-literate, creative community

During this course, you will develop your skills in innovation and strategy through a combination of research and development techniques. Through business lectures and classes led by both industry experts and academics, you will gain the broad skillset needed to succeed in international fashion marketing.

An essential part of success in this field is understanding data. On this course, you will learn how to measure data and metrics, using these insights to guide future developments, assess the effectiveness of emerging technologies, and identify new platforms to support future marketing strategies.

## Course Structure

### Semester 1

- **Introduction to International Fashion Marketing** — Fundamentals of international fashion marketing and the dynamic fashion business environment
- **Research Methods and Practice** — Methodologies and practice of research and how this informs fashion marketing practice
- **Strategic Marketing and Brand Management** — How fashion brands are supported through strategic marketing

### Semester 2

- **Fashion Marketing Analytics** — Measuring data and metrics to inform future developments
- **Innovation Strategy and Research** — Emerging technologies and new platforms supporting marketing efforts
- **Creative Digital Fashion Marketing** — Developing data-driven marketing campaigns through digital processes

### Semester 3

- **International Fashion Marketing MSc Professional Practice** — Commissioned industry report or international marketing report culminating your studies

Teaching sessions take place Monday to Friday between 9am and 9pm. The course may include some evening teaching.

## Entry Requirements

**Home applicants**: A lower second-class honours degree (2:2) or equivalent non-UK qualification in a relevant subject, or an equivalent professional qualification in a related field. CV/Resume required. Applicants applying directly from an undergraduate degree without professional practice experience must demonstrate a good knowledge of the chosen subject area.

**International applicants**: Must meet country-specific entry requirements and submit academic qualifications along with CV/Resume.

**English language**: IELTS 6.0 overall (minimum 5.5 in each sub-test) or CEFR Level B2 in each component.

**Selection criteria**: Applications welcome from a wide range of backgrounds and experience, including students who may not possess formal academic qualifications. Applicants should demonstrate an aptitude for learning and continued professional development through a research-led approach.
