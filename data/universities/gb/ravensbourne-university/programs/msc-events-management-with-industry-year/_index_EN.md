## Course Overview

MSc Events Management with Industry Year is a two-year full-time master's programme at Ravensbourne University London, designed for aspiring professionals aiming to lead and innovate in the global events industry. The course builds expertise in event frameworks, operational planning and audience insight, preparing students to manage live experiences, evaluate performance and support effective decision-making across a range of event sectors.

The integrated industry year allows students to apply for year-in-industry placements, offering hands-on experience and professional readiness alongside academic study.

*Course subject to validation.

## Why Study This Degree?

1. Shape yourself into a confident event leader
2. Explore innovative event design and management approaches
3. Strengthen your understanding of contemporary event technologies and tools
4. Apply for year in industry placements alongside your studies

On this Events Management with Industry Year degree in London, you will develop the practical and strategic skills to lead events with impact. You will learn to plan, deliver and evaluate experiences, interpret audience insights, and communicate ideas clearly and confidently. Using operational, strategic and evidence-based approaches, you will address real-world challenges and turn insights into memorable events.

You will explore emerging industry trends, apply creative problem-solving, and build strong commercial awareness, ensuring every event you manage delivers meaningful results. Live projects and industry placements give you hands-on experience, letting you work like a professional from day one.

- Strategic communication for event planning and stakeholder alignment

## Entry Requirements

**Home applicants**: A higher second-class honours degree (2:1) or equivalent non-UK qualification in a relevant subject, or an equivalent professional qualification in a related field. CV/Resume required. Applicants without professional experience must demonstrate a strong understanding of the chosen subject area.

**International applicants**: Must meet country-specific entry requirements and submit academic qualifications along with CV/Resume.

**English language**: IELTS 6.0 overall (minimum 5.5 in each sub-test) or CEFR Level B2 in each component.

**Selection criteria**: Applicants from a wide range of backgrounds are welcome. Demonstrated enthusiasm for the subject area, aptitude for learning and continued professional development are valued.
