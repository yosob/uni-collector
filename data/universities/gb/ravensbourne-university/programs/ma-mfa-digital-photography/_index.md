## MA/MFA Digital Photography — Ravensbourne University London

A postgraduate programme exploring contemporary and commercial photographic practice through the lens of emerging technologies — computational photography, AI, machine learning, and augmented reality. Available as MA (1 year FT) and MFA (1.5 years FT) pathways. Accredited by the Association of Photographers (AOP). London campus with evening delivery option.
