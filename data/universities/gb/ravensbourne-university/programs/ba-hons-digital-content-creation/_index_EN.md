  with photography, video, podcasting, web design, and copywriting. Explore graphic
  design principles, branding, and SEO techniques to help audiences find content.

  Video Content Production, Storytelling with Photography, Brand Design, Content Marketing,
  Professional Life Practice — Developing your Practice, Professional Life Practice
  — Exploring your Practice.

  research to design an app prototype. Build professional connections, stay updated
  on industry trends, and gain hands-on experience through a work-based learning opportunity.
  Modules: Social Media, Digital Storytelling, UX and App Prototyping, Professional
  Life Practice (Applying your Practice), Work-Based Learning.


  Year 3 (Level 6): Refine skills and prepare for life after Ravensbourne by responding
  to client and competition briefs and collaborating with peers. Build a portfolio
  that highlights your best work, boosting employability and showing you''re industry-ready.

  Modules: Live and Competition Briefs (e.g. D&AD New Blood competition), Professional
  Life Practice (Situating your Practice), Planning, Strategy and Creation, The Future
