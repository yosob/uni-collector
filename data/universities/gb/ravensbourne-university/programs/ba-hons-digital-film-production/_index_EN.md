curriculum_summary: 'Year 1 (Level 4): Focuses on the development of craft skills,
  industry awareness, and deepening contextual understanding of moving image. Production
  projects across both semesters are collaborative, combining practical craft workshops
  with analytical sessions to strengthen contextual understanding. Career development
  supported through employability workshops including CV and online professional profile
  building. Modules: Production Skills & Technologies, Critical Thinking Through Film,
  Storytelling On Screen, Professional Life Practice: "Developing your Practice" and
  "Exploring your Practice".


  Year 2 (Level 5): Specialise in up to three moving image disciplines aligned with
  career goals, shifting from conventions to experimentation in content creation.
  Collaboration with students from other courses develops soft skills and emotional
  intelligence. Opportunity for work-based learning experience. Modules: Collaboration,
  Professional Life Practice: "Applying your Practice", Work-Based Learning, Optional
  modules.


  Year 3 (Level 6): More self-directed collaboration. Apply everything learned through
  a final major project that serves as a calling card for potential employers. Contextual
  understanding and critical thinking deepen through a Professional Practice Research
  Project aligned with individual career aspirations. Modules: FMP1 Planning and Development,
  FMP2 Production & Reflection, Professional Practice Research Project, Professional
  Life Practice: "Situating your Practice".
