## Overview

The MSc Information Technology Management programme at Ravensbourne University London blends technical, managerial and ethical perspectives to address challenges in data privacy, intellectual property, and cybersecurity. Students explore frameworks for protecting digital assets, ensuring compliance with evolving regulations, and fostering responsible innovation. By combining practical expertise and digital rights, graduates are equipped to lead digital integrity safeguarding in a variety of industries.

## Why Study This Degree?

1. Design and implement advanced digital infrastructure and cloud management solutions that meet the evolving needs of industry
2. Develop leadership capabilities for managing information technology infrastructure, resources and projects across diverse organisational contexts
3. Apply advanced cyber security and risk management practices to protect digital assets and ensure data integrity and confidentiality
4. Utilise research, data analytics and visualisation techniques to generate insights that support innovation and creative decision-making
5. Critically evaluate information technology governance, project management frameworks and data management tools to inform ethical, strategic and operational decisions

## Course Description

This course combines business and IT management principles, focusing on how technology can optimise operations. Graduates will be equipped to lead digital infrastructure and transformation strategies across industries such as finance, manufacturing, retail, media, and creative agencies. The programme emphasises the management of technological resources, cybersecurity, and governance, enabling professionals to lead organisations in a digital world where innovative technology is key to success.

Uniquely, the course integrates technical, managerial, and ethical perspectives, addressing challenges like data privacy, intellectual property, and cybersecurity. Students will explore the protection of digital assets, compliance with evolving regulations, and best practices for responsible innovation.

This postgraduate course prepares students for leadership roles in IT management, equipping them with the skills to strategise, secure, manage, and innovate IT solutions that enhance business performance. Graduates will be able to align IT initiatives with business strategies, lead projects effectively, and apply advanced problem-solving skills to meet IT challenges across various sectors.

**Teaching sessions are Monday to Friday, between 9:00am and 9:00pm, with some evening sessions included.**

## Course Structure

### Key Study Topics

- **Digital infrastructure and transformation** — Design and implementation of advanced digital infrastructure and cloud management solutions
- **Business strategy & digital transformation** — Technology-driven business strategy and organisational transformation
- **Cyber security and risk management** — Security practices and risk assessment for protecting digital assets
- **IT governance and project management** — Governance frameworks and project management methodologies for IT resources
- **Data management and business intelligence** — Data analytics, visualisation and generation of business insights
- **Data privacy and intellectual property** — Privacy protection and IP management in the digital age
- **Final project** — Independent research or practical IT management project

## Entry Requirements

### UK Students

- A lower second-class honours degree (2:2) or higher in a relevant subject, OR
- An equivalent professional qualification in a related subject
- Submission of CV/Resume required
- Applicants without professional practice experience must demonstrate good knowledge of the chosen subject area

### International Students

- Equivalent international qualifications accepted — check the university's country-specific entry requirements
- Submission of CV/Resume required
- English language requirement: IELTS 6.0 overall (minimum 5.5 in each sub-test) or CEFR Level B2 in each component
- Contact postgrad@rave.ac.uk for more information

### Selection Criteria

The university welcomes applicants from a wide range of backgrounds and experience. Selection considers enthusiasm and interest in the subject area, how you manage the creative process, previous study and your professional skill-set. It is important that applicants can demonstrate an aptitude for learning and continued professional development through a research-led approach.
