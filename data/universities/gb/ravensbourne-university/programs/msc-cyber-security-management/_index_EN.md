## Overview

The MSc Cyber Security Management programme at Ravensbourne University London takes a practical approach to provide students with an understanding of the core aspects of cyber security management. In a rapidly evolving technological landscape, students learn how the latest insights are being adopted within industry.

## Why Study This Degree?

1. Tailor the course to align with individual career goals and professional interests
2. Emphasise the practical application of cyber security within real organisational contexts
3. Gain insight into the implementation, management and governance of cyber security across organisations of varying sizes
4. Apply theoretical concepts to real-world case studies while developing strong professional, intellectual and transferable life skills
5. Develop the knowledge and ethical awareness required to uphold professional standards in data management and cyber security

## Course Description

Cyber security is a rapidly growing field, with increasing demand for professionals as cyber-attacks rise. This MSc Cyber Security Management course in London focuses on implementing cyber security strategies across organisations of all sizes and industries.

Students develop strategic thinking skills to manage resources, implement solutions, and communicate effectively with senior decision-makers. The course also provides a clear understanding of the cyber threat landscape and how incidents evolve, supporting business growth.

In addition to core modules, students can choose a technical elective to tailor the course to their career goals.

**Classes are held Monday to Friday, 9:00am – 9:00pm, with some evening sessions included.**

## Course Structure

### Key Study Topics

- **International business management** — Management theory and practice in multinational business environments
- **Strategic leadership and management** — Organisational strategy formulation and leadership development
- **Cyber security management** — Implementation, management and governance of cyber security strategies
- **Finance for cyber security** — Financial planning and resource management in the cyber security domain
- **Technical elective** — Choose a technical module aligned with your career goals
- **Dissertation / Consultancy project** — Independent research or practical consultancy project

## Entry Requirements

### UK Students

- A lower second-class honours degree (2:2) or higher in a relevant subject, OR
- An equivalent professional qualification in a related subject
- Submission of CV/Resume required
- Applicants without professional practice experience must demonstrate good knowledge of the chosen subject area

### International Students

- Equivalent international qualifications accepted — check the university's country-specific entry requirements
- Submission of CV/Resume required
- English language requirement: IELTS 6.0 overall (minimum 5.5 in each sub-test) or CEFR Level B2 in each component
- Contact postgrad@rave.ac.uk for more information

### Selection Criteria

The university welcomes applicants from a wide range of backgrounds and experience. Selection considers enthusiasm and interest in the subject area, how you manage the creative process, previous study and your professional skill-set. It is important that applicants can demonstrate an aptitude for learning and continued professional development through a research-led approach.
