overview: "BEng (Hons) Architectural Design Engineering blends creativity, engineering expertise, and industry experience to design innovative structures addressing the challenges of today's urban environments. The course combines architectural design, structural engineering, and building performance within a collaborative, practice-based curriculum. Students work on live briefs from industry and community, using physical and digital tools including model-making, Revit (BIM), and IES (thermal modelling). Shared interdisciplinary modules allow collaboration with Architecture, Interior Design, and Urban Landscape students. Studios prioritise infrastructure briefs while teaching engineering principles, providing dual career pathways in architecture and engineering."
