## Course Overview

The MSc Strategic Marketing and Analytics with Industry Year at Ravensbourne University London is a two-year full-time postgraduate programme that prepares students to design, analyse and deliver impactful marketing strategies with clarity, confidence and evidence-driven precision.

Exploring a range of analytical tools, strategic frameworks and contemporary marketing approaches, students develop the skills to interpret data, understand consumer behaviour and drive effective decision-making across diverse sectors. The integrated industry year strengthens professional readiness, providing hands-on experience and real insight into modern marketing and analytics practice.

*Course subject to validation

## Why Study This Degree?

1. Explore cutting-edge marketing and analytics methodologies
2. Strengthen your understanding of contemporary digital tools and technologies
3. Accelerate your professional growth and industry-ready skills
4. Tap into exceptional industry connections
5. Apply for year in industry placements alongside your studies

On this programme, you will develop the skills to lead in marketing and analytics across industries. You will learn to analyse consumer behaviour, evaluate campaign performance, and turn data into actionable strategies with clarity and confidence. Using strategic, analytical, and evidence-based approaches, you will tackle real-world challenges and apply insights across diverse sectors.

Through hands-on projects and the year in industry placement, you will gain professional experience, applying digital tools and techniques from modern marketing analytics.

## Key Study Topics

- **Strategic Communication** — Clear marketing insight and stakeholder alignment
- **Contemporary Strategic Marketing** — Collaborative, interdisciplinary approaches
- **Marketing Leadership** — Teamwork, dialogue, and organisational learning
- **Ethical, Evidence-Based Marketing** — Analytics with professional principles
- **Sustainable Marketing Strategy** — Responsible practice applied across sectors
- **Professional Skills** — Modern marketing methods, analytics, and digital tools
- **Emerging Technologies** — Engagement and data-driven marketing insights
- **Information Sharing** — Structured, interactive, immersive platforms

## Entry Requirements

### UK Students

- A higher second-class honours degree (2:1) or equivalent non-UK qualification in a relevant subject, OR
- An equivalent professional qualification in a related field
- Submission of CV/Résumé required
- Applicants without professional experience must demonstrate a strong understanding of the chosen subject area

### International Students

- Equivalent international qualifications accepted — check the university's country-specific entry requirements
- Submission of CV/Résumé required
- English language requirement: IELTS 6.0 overall (minimum 5.5 in each sub-test) or CEFR Level B2 in each component
- Contact postgrad@rave.ac.uk for more information

### Selection Criteria

The university welcomes suitably-qualified applicants who can demonstrate a good level of skills in areas associated with this subject. Selection considers enthusiasm and interest in the subject area, how you manage the creative process, previous study and your professional skill-set. Applications are welcome from a wide range of backgrounds and experience.
