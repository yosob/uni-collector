## Course Overview

MSc International Finance is a one-year full-time master's programme at Ravensbourne University London, designed for individuals aspiring to excel in the fast-paced and competitive world of global finance. The course provides students with foundational principles in financial theory, quantitative analysis, and strategic decision-making, with each discipline taught within a global context.

Students will learn to navigate complex global markets, foreign exchange, and cross-border investments, gaining the analytical tools to assess financial risks, evaluate investment opportunities, and create effective strategies for multinational corporations and financial institutions. The course integrates fintech, reflecting its growing importance in the financial landscape.

Graduates will gain both a Ravensbourne master's degree and a CMI Level 7 qualification (Chartered Management Institute), significantly enhancing employability, leadership capability, and professional credibility.

## Why Study This Degree?

1. Explore international finance and regulatory frameworks to effectively navigate the opportunities within global financial markets
2. Gain advanced expertise in global financial markets and cross-border transactions to enhance career progression
3. Develop professional skills in risk management, investment strategy and financial modelling
4. Understand the complexities of foreign exchange markets and associated financial strategies
5. Apply financial theory through real-world case studies, simulations and projects, supported by fintech, quantitative methods and data-driven analysis

## Entry Requirements

**Home applicants**: A lower second-class honours degree (2:2) or equivalent non-UK qualification in a relevant subject (plus basic maths), or an equivalent professional qualification in a related field. CV/Resume required. Applicants without professional experience must demonstrate a good knowledge of the chosen subject area.

**International applicants**: Must meet country-specific entry requirements and submit academic qualifications along with CV/Resume.

**English language**: IELTS 6.0 overall (minimum 5.5 in each sub-test) or CEFR Level B2 in each component.

**Selection criteria**: Applicants from a wide range of backgrounds are welcome. Demonstrated enthusiasm for the subject area, ability to manage the creative process, previous study and professional skill-set are valued.
