## Overview

BSc (Hons) Information Technology Management at Ravensbourne University London is a three-year programme that prepares future leaders by aligning IT systems with business goals. The course combines technical expertise in cybersecurity, cloud computing, and emerging technologies with essential management skills including operations, project and data management, business intelligence, and innovation.

Delivered in Ravensbourne's creative environment, the course fosters problem-solving and strategic thinking, preparing graduates for roles like IT manager, systems analyst, or digital transformation leader in industries embracing rapid technological change.

Students will develop creative, real-world solutions in software design and IT management, gain strategic business insight for effective digital technology leadership, and implement advanced information security, risk management, and ethical IT practices.

## Course Structure

### Year 1 (Level 4)

Year 1 develops the fundamentals of designing and operating computer systems for businesses and organisations.

**Modules:**
- Software Design and Development
- The Business Landscape
- Operations and Project Management
- Information Security and Risk Management
- Professional Life and Practice 1
- Professional Life and Practice 2

### Year 2 (Level 5)

Modules in Year 2 build upon the computational principles established in the first year, focusing on different ways of learning and working.

**Modules:**
- Digital Ethics and Sustainable Technology
- Emerging Technologies and Innovation
- Data Management and Business Intelligence
- Work Placement or Live Project
- Professional Life and Practice 3

### Year 3 (Level 6)

The final year consolidates the programme's learning through a longer project and sophisticated modules with real-world applications.

**Modules:**
- Technology Entrepreneurship
- Information Technology Management and Consultancy
- Final Project
- Professional Life and Practice 4

## Entry Requirements

### BSc (Hons) Information Technology Management (3-year)

- GCSE Grade 4 or above in English and Maths
- Accepted qualifications:
  - Three A Levels at grade BCC or above
  - BTEC Extended Diploma at grade DMM
  - UAL Level 3 Extended Diploma
  - T Level (pass or above)
  - Access to Higher Education Diploma
  - International Baccalaureate at grade 24 or above
  - Other/Mixed equivalent qualifications

### BSc (Hons) Information Technology Management with Integrated Foundation Year (4-year)

- GCSE Grade 4 or above in English and Maths
- Lower tariff entry requirements apply
- UCAS code: N109

**No interview or portfolio requirements** for this course.
