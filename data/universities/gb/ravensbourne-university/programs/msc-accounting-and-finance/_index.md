## Overview

The MSc Accounting and Finance at Ravensbourne University London is a professionally focused postgraduate degree designed to equip students with advanced knowledge and practical skills in accounting, financial management, and strategic decision-making. Ideal for graduates and early-career professionals, the programme provides a clear pathway to achieving professional body recognition, offering potential ACCA exemptions.

This course blends rigorous academic learning with real-world application, preparing students to navigate complex financial environments across public, private, and third-sector organisations. Students engage with core areas such as financial reporting, management accounting, taxation, audit and assurance, corporate governance, and financial strategy. Emphasis is placed on developing critical thinking, ethical judgement, and professional integrity, in line with the ACCA's global competency framework.

A key strength of the course is its strong connection to industry. Located near London's financial district — City of London and Canary Wharf — students benefit from access to guest speakers, site visits, and networking opportunities with accounting firms, finance professionals, and industry bodies. Technology and data-driven decision-making are central to the programme, with students working with cloud-based accounting software and professional strategy platforms like DecideAct.

**Classes are held Monday to Friday, between 9:00am and 9:00pm, including some evening sessions.**

## Key Study Topics

- **Essential accounting principles** — Core foundations of financial and management accounting
- **Financial management** — Strategic financial planning, investment decisions, and performance evaluation
- **Taxation** — UK and international tax principles and compliance
- **Auditing** — Audit and assurance practices and professional standards
- **Corporate governance** — Regulatory frameworks and ethical business conduct
- **Financial analysis** — Advanced financial data interpretation and reporting

## Why Study This Degree?

1. Develop comprehensive knowledge of accounting and financial principles in line with professional body foundation level papers
2. Explore, discover and analyse the breadth and depth of domestic and global accounting and finance
3. Graduate as an industry-ready accounting and finance professional, equipped with the proficiencies expected by practitioners and specialists
4. Engage in opportunities to respond to live, simulated and consultancy accounting and finance projects, developing your real-world expertise
5. Gain an understanding of diverse regulatory frameworks and accounting environments, essential in an interconnected economy
6. Align with rigorous professional body standards (ACCA) to enhance your academic credentials

## Entry Requirements

### UK Students

- A lower second-class honours degree (2:2) or higher in a relevant subject (plus basic maths), OR
- An equivalent professional qualification in a related subject
- Submission of CV/Resume required
- Applicants without professional practice experience must demonstrate good knowledge of the chosen subject area

### International Students

- Equivalent international qualifications accepted — check the university's country-specific entry requirements
- Submission of CV/Resume required
- English language requirement: IELTS 6.0 overall (minimum 5.5 in each sub-test) or CEFR Level B2 in each component
- Contact postgrad@rave.ac.uk for more information

### Selection Criteria

The university welcomes applicants from a wide range of backgrounds and experience. Selection considers enthusiasm and interest in the subject area, creative process management, previous study and professional skill-set. Applications are welcome from students who may not necessarily possess formal academic qualifications, provided they can demonstrate an aptitude for learning and continued professional development through a research-led approach.
