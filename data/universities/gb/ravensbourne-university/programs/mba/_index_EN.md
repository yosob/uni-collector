## Overview

The MBA at Ravensbourne University London is uniquely designed for those who want to explore business on an international and global level, studying among the UK's finest creatives in a design-led environment. The programme provides a holistic and integrated approach to business practice, combining academic rigour with real-world application.

In today's uncertain world, cultural agility and adaptability are essential for entrepreneurs and senior business professionals. This MBA equips students with global perspectives and expertise in addressing current business management challenges. The course offers in-depth study in business administration, with lectures from industry and academic experts, helping you develop a broad skillset for advancing your career.

Students enhance their leadership abilities through simulated tasks and collaborative projects. This versatile MBA prepares graduates for roles across sectors such as consulting, finance, technology, operations, and HRM.

**Professional Accreditation: CMI Level 7** — Students graduate not only with a Ravensbourne master's degree, but also with a CMI Level 7 qualification, a powerful combination that enhances employability, leadership capability, and professional credibility.

**Classes are held Monday to Friday, between 9:00am and 9:00pm, including some evening sessions.**

## Key Study Topics

- **Contemporary business practices** — Innovation, digital transformation and enterprise in a global context
- **Financial planning** — Adapting to changes in the economy and international trade
- **Project management** — Long-term business planning and project delivery skills
- **Leadership and management** — Simulated tasks and collaborative projects to build leadership capability
- **Business research** — Final dissertation addressing a long-standing business research problem
- **Cultural and creative industries** — Business strategy within the rapidly growing creative economy

## Why Study This Degree?

1. Gain global perspectives and expertise in addressing current business management challenges
2. Study in a design-led environment among the UK's finest creatives
3. Graduate with both an MBA and a CMI Level 7 qualification, enhancing employability and professional credibility
4. Develop broad business skills applicable across consulting, finance, technology, operations, and HRM
5. Build leadership abilities through simulated tasks and collaborative projects with industry and academic experts

## Course Structure

### Terms 1 and 2

Explore contemporary business practices in learning innovation, digital transformation and enterprise. The course prepares you for the dynamic, ever-changing international business landscape. You will learn how to adapt to changes in the economy and in trade through teaching around financial planning, and develop skills in long-term business planning supported by the development of project management skills.

### Term 3

Through your final dissertation, you will identify a long-standing business research problem and create a proposal which supports both existing research findings and your own.

## Entry Requirements

### UK Students

- A lower second-class honours degree (2:2) or higher in a relevant subject, OR
- An equivalent professional qualification in a related subject
- Submission of CV/Resume required
- Applicants without professional practice experience must demonstrate good knowledge of the chosen subject area

### International Students

- Equivalent international qualifications accepted — check the university's country-specific entry requirements
- Submission of CV/Resume required
- English language requirement: IELTS 6.0 overall (minimum 5.5 in each sub-test) or CEFR Level B2 in each component
- Contact postgrad@rave.ac.uk for more information

### Selection Criteria

The university welcomes suitably-qualified applicants who can demonstrate a good level of skills in areas associated with this subject. Selection considers enthusiasm and interest in the subject area, how you manage the creative process, previous study and your professional skill-set. Applications are welcome from a wide range of backgrounds and experience, including from students who may not necessarily possess formal academic qualifications, provided they can demonstrate an aptitude for learning and continued professional development through a research-led approach.
