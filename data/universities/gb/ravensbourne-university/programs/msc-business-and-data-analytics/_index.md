## Overview

The MSc Business and Data Analytics programme at Ravensbourne University London is designed to equip graduates with the skills to leverage data in making informed business decisions. The programme integrates advanced data analytics with business acumen, preparing graduates to address real-world challenges across creative arts, media, technology, finance and other sectors.

The curriculum combines technical expertise in data science, AI, and data mining with strong business strategy foundations. Students learn to manage complex data systems, use big data and cloud technologies, and apply analytical methods to solve business problems. The hands-on, project-based approach ensures students gain experience with industry-standard tools and are prepared to drive innovation and provide strategic insights across various sectors.

Delivered at Ravensbourne University London, the course incorporates design thinking and collaborative problem-solving, setting graduates apart as forward-thinking analytics professionals.

**Classes are held Monday to Friday, between 9:00am and 9:00pm, including some evening sessions.**

## Key Study Topics

- **Data analysis and understanding big data** — Core analytical techniques and large-scale data processing
- **Consumer behaviour** — Data-driven insights into consumer patterns and preferences
- **Creative and research-based thinking** — Innovative approaches to data problem-solving
- **Artificial intelligence, data mining and machine learning** — AI/ML techniques for predictive analytics
- **Data governance** — Ethical and regulatory frameworks for responsible data management
- **Cloud technologies** — Cloud infrastructure for scalable data solutions

## Why Study This Degree?

1. Develop a deep and systematic understanding of data analytics to support informed and strategic decision-making
2. Analyse and critically evaluate business strategies and digital transformations that integrate creative and emerging technologies
3. Apply and analyse big data and cloud technologies to drive creative and strategic decisions aligned with dynamic industry needs
4. Foster a culture of continuous innovation by remaining current with emerging technologies and applying them to real-world business challenges
5. Employ advanced research, data analytics, visualisation, and computing techniques to generate actionable insights in complex and unpredictable business environments

## Entry Requirements

### UK Students

- A lower second-class honours degree (2:2) or higher in a relevant subject, OR
- An equivalent professional qualification in a related subject
- Submission of CV/Resume required
- Applicants without professional practice experience must demonstrate good knowledge of the chosen subject area

### International Students

- Equivalent international qualifications accepted — check the university's country-specific entry requirements
- Submission of CV/Resume required
- English language requirement: IELTS 6.0 overall (minimum 5.5 in each sub-test) or CEFR Level B2 in each component
- Contact postgrad@rave.ac.uk for more information

### Selection Criteria

The university welcomes applicants from a wide range of backgrounds and experience. Selection considers enthusiasm and interest in the subject area, creative process management, previous study and professional skill-set. Applications are welcome from students who may not necessarily possess formal academic qualifications, provided they can demonstrate an aptitude for learning and continued professional development through a research-led approach.
