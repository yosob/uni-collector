## Course Overview

MA Luxury Brand Management is a one-year full-time master's programme at Ravensbourne University London, designed for creative professionals who want to blend design thinking and innovation models to redefine the luxury sector. The course equips students to analyse customer market data and engineer new ways to influence the relationship between luxury brands, high-value services, consumer and market behaviours.

Students will explore the impact of new technology on luxury industries and learn to redefine luxury in the era of Industry 4.0, preparing to lead in a competitive global market spanning fashion, travel, wellbeing and beyond.

## Why Study This Degree?

1. Help redefine the contemporary fashion marketplace
2. Become a decisive leader
3. See your creative visions come to life
4. Tap into exceptional industry connections
5. Explore factors behind disruptive movements in smart luxury
6. Augment your skill set to stand tall in a highly competitive sector

## Key Study Topics

- Develop knowledge and research on the luxury sector, especially emergent markets in the BRIC countries, as well as current lead sectors in Europe and North America
- Develop a framework for designing a unique brand concept and identity supported by a branding strategy for generating commercial, social and environmental value
- Explore the context, scale, scope and shift in strategies within the luxury landscape — its territories, economics, design style and experiential influences
- Define 'future luxury' in the 21st century in terms of products, services and experience

## Entry Requirements

**Home applicants**: A lower second-class honours degree (2:2) or equivalent non-UK qualification in a relevant subject, or an equivalent professional qualification in a related field. CV/Resume required. Applicants without professional experience must demonstrate a good knowledge of the chosen subject area.

**International applicants**: Must meet country-specific entry requirements and submit academic qualifications along with CV/Resume.

**English language**: IELTS 6.0 overall (minimum 5.5 in each sub-test) or CEFR Level B2 in each component.

**Selection criteria**: Applicants from a wide range of backgrounds and experience are welcome, including those who may not possess formal academic qualifications. Demonstrated enthusiasm for the subject area, aptitude for learning and continued professional development through a research-led approach are valued.
