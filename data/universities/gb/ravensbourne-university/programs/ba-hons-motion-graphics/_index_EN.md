curriculum_summary: 'Year 1 (Level 4, 120 credits): Lays the foundations of Motion
  Design practice through three Core applied skills modules — Design for Motion, Animation
  Principles, and Production — which cover storytelling, sound recording, typography,
  and compositing. Students then apply these skills to Main Title Design. Professional
  Life Practice modules focus on collaboration, subject awareness, and situating the
  practice and history of Motion Design.

  Modules: Design for Motion (20cr), Core: Production (20cr), Core: Animation Principles
  (20cr), Main Title Design (20cr), Professional Life Practice ×2 (40cr total).


  Year 2 (Level 5, 120 credits): Continues building essential skills with a Core Motion
  Branding module that tests conceptual and strategic skills, collaborative working,
  and analysis of Motion Design''s impact. Students then undertake projects using
  Motion Design in specific critical contexts through A Project with Purpose. Emerging
  & Immersive Media explores new technologies and platforms. Work-based learning and
  professional practice modules develop industry readiness.

  Modules: Motion Branding (20cr), Emerging & Immersive Media (40cr), Work-Based Learning
  (20cr), A Project with Purpose (20cr), Professional Life Practice (20cr).


  Year 3 (Level 6, 120 credits): Students work on industry-facing projects with External
  Partners, develop a professional Portfolio, and complete a Final Major Project that
  synthesises all learning. Professional Life Practice focuses on career preparation
  and positioning within the industry.

  Modules: External Partners (40cr), Final Major Project (40cr), Core: Portfolio (20cr),
  Professional Life Practice (20cr).
