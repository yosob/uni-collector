## Overview

The MSc Strategic Marketing and Analytics at Ravensbourne University London is a taught Master's programme that develops professional practices and emergent processes around strategic marketing concepts, with an emphasis on multi-channel marketing strategy outcomes.

The course reflects and contributes to the expanding nature of marketing management with a global perspective. It has been created to encompass omnichannel marketing concepts, strategic marketing with emphasis on global marketing strategies, analytics to inform decision making, and marketing via digital channels — all supported by innovation and creative practice.

Students engage with live briefs and collaborative projects, working alongside industry and research-led staff in a state-of-the-art building in the heart of London's creative district.

**Professional Accreditation: CIM** — Students have the opportunity to gain professional qualifications through an Accredited Degree partnership with The Chartered Institute of Marketing (CIM). CIM qualifications are highly sought after by employers.

**Professional Accreditation: CMI Level 7** — Students graduate not only with a Ravensbourne master's degree, but also with a CMI Level 7 qualification — a powerful combination that enhances employability, leadership capability, and professional credibility.

**Classes are held Monday to Friday, between 9:00am and 9:00pm, including some evening sessions.**

## Key Study Topics

- **Strategic Marketing** — Contemporary marketing theory, global marketing strategies, and omnichannel communication
- **Marketing Analytics** — Data-driven decision making, quantitative and qualitative analysis for marketing performance
- **Brand Management and Communication** — Building, managing, and communicating brand value across channels
- **Digital Marketing** — Social media strategy, digital channels, and emerging web-based technologies
- **Consumer Behaviour** — Consumer journey mapping, insights analysis, and understanding the social-digital consumer
- **Evidence-Based Management** — Data-informed management decisions and research-led strategic planning
- **Global and Sustainable Marketing** — Marketing strategies with sustainability and ethical implications

## Why Study This Degree?

1. Develop master's-level expertise in strategic marketing, informed by contemporary practice and emerging global trends
2. Build highly desirable skills in data analytics, strategic marketing and social media strategy to enhance employability
3. Explore cutting-edge digital and web-based technologies shaping the future of marketing practice
4. Develop innovative research capabilities through a learning-by-doing approach to strategic marketing education
5. Gain business and entrepreneurial skills while engaging with a tech-literate, creative community and expanding your professional network

## Entry Requirements

### UK Students

- A lower second-class honours degree (2:2) or higher in a relevant subject, OR
- An equivalent professional qualification in a related subject
- Submission of CV/Resume required
- Applicants without professional practice experience must demonstrate good knowledge of the chosen subject area

### International Students

- Equivalent international qualifications accepted — check the university's country-specific entry requirements
- Submission of CV/Resume required
- English language requirement: IELTS 6.0 overall (minimum 5.5 in each sub-test) or CEFR Level B2 in each component
- Contact postgrad@rave.ac.uk for more information

### Selection Criteria

The university welcomes suitably-qualified applicants who can demonstrate a good level of skills in areas associated with this subject. Selection considers enthusiasm and interest in the subject area, how you manage the creative process, previous study and your professional skill-set. Applications are welcome from a wide range of backgrounds and experience.
