## Overview

The MSc International Finance with Industry Year at Ravensbourne University London is a professionally focused postgraduate degree that develops students' analytical, technical and strategic skills for success in the UK and global financial sector. Combining academic study with practical application and an industry year, students graduate ready to interpret financial data, manage risk and support sound decision making across international markets.

The integrated industry year provides hands-on experience, giving students professional insight and readiness for a career in the international financial sector. Students explore the role of financial leadership in a fast-changing global economy, developing the confidence to interpret financial information, evaluate performance and communicate insights clearly and professionally.

Live projects, live briefs and industry placements give students hands-on experience, allowing them to tackle real organisational challenges, analyse financial data and respond to emerging economic trends. By the end of the degree, students will be ready to design financial strategies that matter and turn insight into action.

## Key Study Topics

- **International finance models** — Key models and interdisciplinary practice
- **Financial leadership** — Teamwork, organisational learning and strategic thinking
- **Risk management** — Global finance strategies focused on responsibility and risk
- **Digital tools and technologies** — Modern finance tools and stakeholder engagement
- **Ethical, evidence-led finance** — Professional standards and evidence-based approaches
- **Industry immersion** — Hands-on professional placement in the financial sector
- **Consultancy skills** — Practical problem-solving and commercial awareness

## Why Study This Degree?

1. Develop strategic and analytical finance skills for global markets
2. Learn to use modern digital tools shaping today's finance industry
3. Work on live projects that reflect real business challenges
4. Build global connections with employers and industry partners
5. Apply for year in industry placements alongside your studies

## Entry Requirements

### UK Students

- A higher second-class honours degree (2:1) or equivalent non-UK qualification in a relevant subject, OR
- An equivalent professional qualification in a related field
- Submission of CV/Resume required
- Applicants without professional experience must demonstrate a strong understanding of the chosen subject area

### International Students

- Equivalent international qualifications accepted — check the university's country-specific entry requirements
- Submission of CV/Resume required
- English language requirement: IELTS 6.0 overall (minimum 5.5 in each sub-test) or CEFR Level B2 in each component
- Contact postgrad@rave.ac.uk for more information

### Selection Criteria

The university welcomes applicants from a wide range of backgrounds and experience. Selection considers enthusiasm and interest in the subject area, creative process management, previous study and professional skill-set. Applications are welcome from students who may not necessarily possess formal academic qualifications, provided they can demonstrate an aptitude for learning and continued professional development through a research-led approach.
