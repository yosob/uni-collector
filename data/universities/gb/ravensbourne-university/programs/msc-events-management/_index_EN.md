## Course Overview

MSc Events Management is a one-year full-time master's programme at Ravensbourne University London, designed for aspiring professionals aiming to lead and innovate in the global events industry. The course combines strong academic foundations with hands-on experience, preparing students to manage exceptional events across corporate, entertainment, sports, festivals, and cultural sectors.

Students will graduate with both a Ravensbourne master's degree and a CMI Level 7 qualification (Chartered Management Institute), significantly enhancing employability, leadership capability, and professional credibility.

## Why Study This Degree?

1. Develop comprehensive, contemporary knowledge of events production, including future industry directions, ethical responsibilities, social impact and sustainability regulations
2. Understand the interconnected domestic and global events industries and develop the ability to operate effectively across both contexts
3. Build an industry-ready events portfolio through engagement with live, simulated and consultancy-based projects
4. Confidently manage client relationships, budgeting processes and risk within complex event environments
5. Explore real-world event production through collaboration with industry partners and access to cutting-edge facilities and the latest event technologies

## Entry Requirements

**Home applicants**: A lower second-class honours degree (2:2) or equivalent non-UK qualification in a relevant subject, or an equivalent professional qualification in a related field. CV/Resume required.

**International applicants**: Must meet country-specific entry requirements and submit academic qualifications along with CV/Resume.

**English language**: IELTS 6.0 overall (minimum 5.5 in each sub-test) or CEFR Level B2 in each component.

**Selection criteria**: Applicants from a wide range of backgrounds are welcome. Demonstrated enthusiasm for the subject area and aptitude for learning and continued professional development are valued.
