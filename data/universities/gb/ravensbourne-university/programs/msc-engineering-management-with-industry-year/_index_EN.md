## Course Overview

The MSc Engineering Management with Industry Year at Ravensbourne University London is a two-year full-time postgraduate programme designed to equip students with the analytical, technical and strategic skills needed to lead in today's complex engineering environments. The course combines advanced academic study with practical application, enabling students to interpret data, optimise systems and support evidence-based decision-making across a range of engineering sectors.

This programme includes an industry year placement, providing students with the opportunity to apply their knowledge in a real engineering environment and gain valuable professional experience.

## Why Study This Degree?

1. Build the capability to lead engineering projects, people and systems
2. Apply advanced engineering management methodologies to complex technical environments
3. Strengthen your understanding of digital engineering tools, data and emerging technologies
4. Apply for year in industry placements alongside your studies
5. Access strong industry networks across engineering and technology sectors

## Key Study Topics

- Strategic communication for clear engineering insight and stakeholder alignment
- Contemporary engineering management models and collaborative approaches
- Engineering leadership, teamwork and organisational learning
- Ethical, evidence-based engineering management practice
- Engineering applications across sectors with a focus on sustainability and innovation
- Professional skills development using modern engineering management tools
- Emerging technologies for stakeholder engagement and technical communication
- Sharing engineering information through interactive and immersive platforms

## Entry Requirements

**Home Applicants**: A higher second-class honours degree (2:1), or equivalent non-UK qualification, in a relevant subject, or an equivalent professional qualification in a related field. A CV/Resume must be submitted as part of the application.

**International Applicants**: International qualifications are assessed per country. A CV/Resume is also required.

**English Language**: IELTS 6.0 overall (minimum 5.5 in each sub-test) or CEFR Level B2 in each component.

**Selection Criteria**: Applicants from a wide range of backgrounds and experience are welcome. Candidates should demonstrate aptitude for learning and continued professional development through a research-led approach.

## Fees

- UK students: £9,750 Year 1 + £4,000 Year 2 (Total: £13,750)
- International students: £18,500 Year 1 + £4,000 Year 2 (Total: £22,500)

*Fees apply to 2026-27 entry only.
