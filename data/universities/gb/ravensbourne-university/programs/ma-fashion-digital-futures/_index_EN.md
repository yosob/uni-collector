## Course Overview

MA Fashion: Digital Futures is a one-year full-time postgraduate programme at Ravensbourne University London. The course sits at the intersection of fashion design and digital technology, preparing students for the rapidly growing field of digital fashion. Experts suggest that digital fashion will soon become a vital revenue stream for every major fashion retailer, and this programme equips graduates to develop innovative digital platforms, create avatars and clothing, and build the virtual environments in which these interactions take place.

> ⚠️ **Note**: This course is **not available for 2026 entry**. The university advises prospective students to browse related postgraduate courses.

## Why Study This Degree?

1. Enter the rapidly growing digital fashion industry, developing skills in 3D modelling, digital design, and virtual environments
2. Explore cutting-edge technologies including AR/VR, mixed reality, sensory (haptic) design, and gaming applications for fashion
3. Build industry-ready skills in digital creative direction, digital marketing, and marketplace creation
4. Engage with an industry project to develop a proof of concept for a real-world digital fashion application
5. Position yourself at the forefront of fashion's digital transformation across ecommerce, gaming, and virtual experiences

## Key Study Topics

- Digital fashion design and 3D modelling for clothing and avatars
- Mixed reality (MR) and virtual environment creation
- Digital creative direction and brand innovation
- AR/VR design for fashion retail and experiences
- Sensory design (haptic technology) in fashion
- Digital marketing strategies and ecommerce platform development
- Gaming applications in the fashion industry
- New and emerging technologies in digital fashion experimentation

## Entry Requirements

**Home**: A lower second-class honours degree (2:2) or equivalent non-UK qualification in a relevant subject, or an equivalent professional qualification in a related subject area. CV/Resume required.

**International**: Must meet country-specific entry requirements. Academic qualifications and CV/Resume required.

**English language**: IELTS 6.0 overall (minimum 5.5 in each sub-test) or CEFR Level B2 in each component.

**Selection criteria**: Applicants should demonstrate skills associated with digital fashion and design technology. The university considers enthusiasm, creative process management, previous study, and professional skill-set holistically.

## Learning Outcomes

Upon completing the course, students will have:

- Advanced 3D modelling and digital fashion design skills
- Ability to create immersive mixed reality and virtual fashion experiences
- Understanding of digital marketing and ecommerce strategies for fashion
- Practical experience developing a proof of concept for industry applications
- Knowledge of emerging technologies and their application in the fashion industry
