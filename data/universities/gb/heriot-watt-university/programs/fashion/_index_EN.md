# BA (Hons) Fashion

## Programme Overview

Our BA (Hons) Fashion degree focuses on developing your creative and intellectual capabilities for a career in fashion design across a broad industry — from luxury fashion houses and high street brands to independent designers. You'll create a variety of garments from mass-produced ready-to-wear to custom bespoke pieces.

You'll develop a theoretical and practical understanding of **pattern cutting** and **garment construction**, transforming design ideas from drawings into finished garments. The programme explores market sectors from high-street fashion to ready-to-wear for both UK and global markets, with a strong emphasis on **sustainability**, ethics, and diversity in the fashion industry.

The programme is taught at our **Scottish Borders Campus**, close to the heart of Scotland's luxury textile industry, combining traditional craftsmanship with contemporary fashion innovation.

## Why Choose This Programme?

- **3rd in Scotland** for Fashion and Textiles (Guardian University Guide 2026)
- Purpose-built workshops with industrial-standard production equipment
- **Go Global** mobility programme — study at partner universities worldwide
- Strong industry links with luxury textile manufacturers
- Final year **Graduate Showcase** with industry exposure
- Optional Year 3 industry placement and live projects

- **English Language:** IELTS 6.0 overall with no score lower than 5.5

### Year 1 — Foundation (Common Level 1)

A broad common year for all Fashion, Communication, Marketing and Retailing students, developing individual strengths in analysis, evaluation and contextualisation in the contemporary fashion marketplace.

| September | Design Communication |

- **Creative Director / Fashion Illustrator**
- **Textile Designer / Buyer**
- **Fashion Brand Consultant**
