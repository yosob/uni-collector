# MSc/MS Global Textile Design

## Programme Overview

This **world-leading joint degree** is offered by two of the world's oldest and most esteemed textile institutions: **Heriot-Watt University** (Scotland) and **Thomas Jefferson University** (USA).

In the textile, fashion, interiors and related fields, there is established interest and importance placed on **design sustainability, artisanal heritage and entrepreneurship**. The think global/live local movement emphasizes a return to artisanal roots through ethical sourcing and reconnecting with handwork interfaced with technology.

## Why Choose This Programme?

- **Dual-institution experience** — study at both Heriot-Watt (Scotland) and Thomas Jefferson (Philadelphia, USA)
- Earn a **joint degree** (MSc + MS) from both universities — a competitive advantage for employability
- Travel to major **global trade fairs**: London Fashion Week, Premier Vision (Paris), Texworld (New York)
- **Summer internship** with an established textile brand included
- Focus on **sustainability, artisanal heritage, and entrepreneurship**
- Small cohort size — initial intake of **12 students** for a personalised experience
- Access to **world-class facilities** at two textile institutions
- Co-directed by **Marnie Collins** and **Marcia Weiss**, both experienced textile design professionals

## Programme Structure (18 Months)

The programme is split across two campuses with travel to international trade events:

1. **Semesters at Thomas Jefferson University (Philadelphia, USA)** — textile design studio, design technology, entrepreneurship
2. **Semesters at Heriot-Watt University (Galashiels, Scotland)** — textile heritage, artisanal making, sustainable design, research methods
3. **Summer Internship** — industry placement with an established textile brand
4. **Global Trade Fairs** — London Fashion Week, Premier Vision Paris, Texworld NYC
5. **Major Design Project** — self-directed project drawing on the international experience

## Entry Requirements

- **Academic:** Minimum 2:2 honours degree (or non-UK equivalent) in textile design, fashion design, or a related design discipline
- **Portfolio:** Required — demonstrating textile/fashion design skills and creative practice
- **Selection:** Rigorous selection process with limited places (initial cohort of 12)
- **English Language:** IELTS applicable to Heriot-Watt postgraduate programmes

## Fees

Contact Heriot-Watt University or Thomas Jefferson University for fee information. This joint programme has a bespoke fee structure.

*Heriot-Watt alumni may be eligible for the 20% fee discount on the Heriot-Watt portion.*

## Career Prospects

Graduates earn a **joint degree from both institutions**, providing a significant competitive advantage:

- **Textile Designer / Textile Creative / Textile Artist**
- **Fashion Textile Designer / Interior Textile Designer**
- **Entrepreneur** — launch your own textile or design venture
- **Sustainable Textile Consultant**

The programme provides extensive professional networks across the USA, UK, and Europe.
