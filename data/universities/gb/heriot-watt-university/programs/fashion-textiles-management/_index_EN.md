# MSc Fashion and Textiles Management

## Programme Overview

MSc Fashion and Textiles Management provides you with an **in-depth understanding of business processes and functions** within the industry's creative foundations. You'll challenge traditional and contemporary uses of fashion and textiles and look at ways to create opportunities to promote new approaches and processes in fashion and textiles.

The programme is aimed at those from **both business and textile backgrounds** and offers the chance to carry out structured research investigations. It allows you to concentrate on an area of fashion and textile management that is particularly suited to you, while you cover everything from **design and manufacture to technology and creativity**.

## Why Choose This Programme?

- Gain in-depth understanding of core concepts and contemporary topics in design, fashion and textiles
- Cover **design, manufacture, technology and creativity** in one programme
- Suitable for students from **both business and textile backgrounds**
- Focus on **fashion and textile sustainability, circularity and innovation**
- Study at the **Scottish Borders Campus** — a purpose-built design environment in a historic mill
- Faculty expertise in **Smart Nanotextiles, Textile-to-Textile recycling, Medical Compression**, and more
- September and January intake options
- **20% alumni discount** available for Heriot-Watt graduates

## Entry Requirements

- **Academic:** Minimum 2:2 honours degree (or non-UK equivalent) with evidence of understanding of creative processes and/or business systems (Fashion, Design, Technology, Manufacture, Retail, or Management)
- **CV:** Required as part of application — creative-related work experience encouraged
- **English Language:** IELTS 6.0 overall with no score lower than 5.5 (writing must be 6.0 minimum)
- Pre-sessional English courses available for applicants below IELTS 6.0

## Career Prospects

Graduates pursue diverse roles across the fashion and textile industries:

- **Fashion Buyer / Brand Manager**
- **Textile Technologist / Product Development Manager**
- **Supply Chain Manager / Design Manager**
- **Fashion Business Consultant / Innovation Manager**

The programme equips graduates with an in-depth understanding of the creative process and its management in both international and local contexts, preparing them for leadership roles across the global fashion and textiles industries.
