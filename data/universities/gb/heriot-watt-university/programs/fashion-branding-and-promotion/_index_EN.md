# BA (Hons) Fashion Branding and Promotion

## Programme Overview

Our BA (Hons) Fashion Branding and Promotion programme offers a unique combination of **fashion communication**, **marketing**, **branding** and **management** specifically tailored for the demands of the global fashion industry.

You'll gain a blend of practical and creative design skills alongside a comprehensive understanding of the commercial aspects of fashion. The programme offers specialised modules in communication design, photography, moving image, visual communication, fashion marketing, branding and consumer behaviour.

The focus is on **sustainable and inclusive commercial practices**. You'll develop skills in management and promotion of live branding events, evaluate current market trends and anticipate future trends.

The programme is taught at our **Scottish Borders Campus**, with opportunities to study abroad at our Dubai campus or international exchange partners in Europe, India and USA.

## Why Choose This Programme?

- Unique blend of fashion communication, marketing, branding and management
- Access to Mac and PC-based **CAD labs** and **Adobe Creative Cloud**
- Fully equipped **photographic studio** for fashion, portraiture and product photography
- **Go Global** mobility programme — study in Dubai, Europe, India or USA
- Live industry projects with partners including **Louis Vuitton, W.L Gore**
- Optional Year 3 industry placement

## Entry Requirements

- **Academic (Year 1 Standard):** Highers BBBB / A-Levels BCC / IB 29 points / BTEC Extended Diploma DMM
- **Minimum (Fair Access):** Highers BBBC / A-Levels BCC / IB 29 points
- **Year 2 Direct Entry:** Advanced Highers BB / A-Levels BBC / IB 30 points
- **Year 3 Direct Entry:** HND BB in relevant subject (Retail Management, Fashion Design & Production with Retail, etc.)
- **English Language:** IELTS 6.0 overall with no score lower than 5.5

- **Events Manager / Brand Strategist**
