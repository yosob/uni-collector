# MA Interior Architecture and Design

## Programme Overview

Our MA Interior Architecture and Design is designed for those with **art, design, and architectural backgrounds**. The programme provides a creative blend of practical skills, research, and technology to develop designs that offer tangible solutions to current **socio-economic issues and sustainability**.

You'll study through a range of **studio projects, specialist subjects and cross-disciplinary theoretical courses**, developing your own projects that test and challenge perceived norms. The emphasis is on **innovation, technology, and commercial relevance**.

## Why Choose This Programme?

- Creative blend of practical skills, research, and technology
- Focus on **sustainability, adaptive reuse, and circular thinking**
- **Human-centred and inclusive design** as a core principle
- Study at the **Scottish Borders Campus** — a purpose-built design environment
- Specialised courses in tectonics, design thinking, community engagement, and professional practice
- September and January intake options
- **20% alumni discount** available for Heriot-Watt graduates

## Entry Requirements

- **Academic:** Minimum 2:2 honours degree (or non-UK equivalent) in interior design or architecture; engineering degrees also considered
- **Portfolio:** Required — demonstrating Auto CAD skills and creativity in interiors, floor plans and elevations; CV required
- **English Language:** IELTS 6.0 overall with no score lower than 5.5 (writing must be 6.0 minimum)
- Pre-sessional English courses available for applicants below IELTS 6.0

## Career Prospects

Graduates pursue diverse roles across interior architecture and spatial design:

- **Interior Architect / Interior Designer**
- **Spatial Designer / Design Consultant**
- **Sustainable Design Specialist**
- **Architectural Consultant**
- **Community Design Practitioner**

The interdisciplinary approach prepares graduates for roles requiring creative, technical and strategic thinking in commercial and social contexts.
