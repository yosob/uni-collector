## 课程详情页 URL 模式

```
ECA 专业概述:  https://www.eca.ed.ac.uk/programme/{slug}
UoE PG 详情:  https://postgraduate.degrees.ed.ac.uk/index.php?r=site/view&edition=2025&id={id}
UoE UG 详情:  https://www.ed.ac.uk/studying/undergraduate/degrees/
DRPS 课程表:  http://www.drps.ed.ac.uk/
```

课程 slug 命名规则:
- 格式: `{programme-name}-{degree-suffix}`
- 本科: `animation-ba-hons`, `graphic-design-ba-hons`, `architecture-bama`
- 研究生: `design-and-digital-media-msc`, `graphic-design-ma`, `interior-architectural-and-spatial-design-ma`
- 研究型: `design-mphilphd`, `architecture-phdmphilmsc-research`
- 连字符使用: 全部小写，单词间用连字符连接，`&` 转为 `and`

## 网站特点与注意事项

- **ECA 站 (eca.ed.ac.uk)**: 提供专业概述、简介、就业信息、校园设施等展示内容

- ECA 页面明确标注 "View full programme details on the main UoE website" 并链接到 Degree Finder
