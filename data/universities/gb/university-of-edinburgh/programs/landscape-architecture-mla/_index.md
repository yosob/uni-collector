## Quick Reference

| Item | Detail |

| **Duration** | 2 years full-time |

| **School** | ESALA (Edinburgh School of Architecture and Landscape Architecture) |
| **Credits** | 240 SCQF (120 per year) |

## Fees (2026/27)

| Fee Category | Amount (per year) |
|-------------|-------------------|
| Scotland | £13,700 |
| Rest of UK | £13,700 |

> No deposit required. No application fee. 10% alumni discount available.

## Entry Requirements

- **Academic**: UK 2:1 honours degree or international equivalent in a relevant subject
- **Professional**: Relevant professional experience also considered (contact programme to check before applying)
- **Portfolio**: Required — digital portfolio (one PDF, max 20 pages, max 20MB)
- **Personal statement**: Required (~300 words) — must address: why landscape architecture at Edinburgh, understanding of the profession, relevant skills/experience
- **CV**: Required
- **English**: IELTS 6.5 (6.0 each), TOEFL 92 (20 each), or equivalent

## Curriculum (240 credits over 2 years)

### Year 1 — Compulsory (120 credits)
| Course | Code | Credits | Timing |
|--------|------|---------|--------|
| Situating Landscape Architectural Theory | ARCH11272 | 20 | Semester 1 |
| Landscape Architecture Design: Context and Grounding | ARCH11273 | 20 | Semester 1 |
| Landscape Architecture Techniques: Material Knowledge and Detailed Design | ARCH11274 | 20 | Semester 1 |
| Landscape Architecture Techniques: Ground-Works and Construction Design | ARCH11276 | 20 | Semester 2 |
| Landscape Architecture Design: Terrain and Ecologies | ARCH11280 | 40 | Semester 2 |

> All Year 1 courses must be passed at 50% or above.

### Year 2 — Compulsory (100 credits)
| Course | Code | Credits | Timing |
|--------|------|---------|--------|
| Landscape Architecture Design Exploration: Part 1 | ARCH11271 | 40 | Semester 1 |
| Landscape Architecture Design Exploration: Part 2 | ARCH11270 | 40 | Semester 2 |
| Master in Landscape Architecture: Academic Portfolio | ARCH11269 | 20 | Semester 2 |

> All Year 2 courses must be passed at 50% or above.

### Year 2 — Elective (20 credits)
Select exactly 20 credits from Level 10 and 11 courses in Schedules A to R, T, W and Y (Semester 1).

## Programme Highlights

- **Professionally accredited**: Fully accredited by the Landscape Institute — the only accredited postgraduate landscape architecture programme in Scotland
- **Conversion programme**: Open to students from a range of academic and professional backgrounds
- **Balanced curriculum**: 4 design-focused courses, 4 theory/technique courses, and 1 elective
- **Art school setting**: Access to Edinburgh College of Art's photography studios, metalworking and woodworking workshops
- **Scale range**: Design methods and skills from regional scale to detailed design
- **Science-integrated**: Integration of geology, geomorphology, soils, ecology and climate
- **Fieldwork**: Site-based learning across Scotland's rich natural and built landscapes
- **Licentiate membership**: Provides licentiate membership of the Landscape Institute

## Careers

This degree prepares graduates for work in:
- Landscape architecture (private practice or public sector)
- Urban design
- Environmental consultancy
- Landscape planning
- Green infrastructure
- Ecological design
- Heritage landscape management
- Academic research
