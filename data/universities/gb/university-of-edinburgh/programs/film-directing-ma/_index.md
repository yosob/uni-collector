## Entry Requirements

- **Academic**: UK 2:1 honours degree or international equivalent, normally in a relevant subject
- **Professional**: Significant relevant professional experience may be considered without a degree
- **Portfolio**: Required — link to **one finished film** (subtitled in English), demonstrating technical skills in filmmaking and experience in directing. Not a showreel.
- **Personal statement + CV**: Required
- **English**: IELTS 7.0 (6.0 each), TOEFL 100 (20 each), or equivalent
