## Entry Requirements

- **Academic**: UK 2:1 honours degree or international equivalent, in music, sound design, media arts or related
- **Professional**: Relevant professional experience also considered
- **Portfolio**: Required — 3 pieces of work (acoustic, electronic, score-based, media music, recordings, installations)
- **English**: IELTS 6.5 (6.0 each), TOEFL 92 (20 each before Jan 2026), C1/C2 176 (169 each), or equivalent

## Curriculum Overview (180 credits)

- **6 taught courses** (120 credits) — composition techniques, technologies, interdisciplinary practice
- **Final Composition Project** (60 credits) — portfolio of compositions

Areas of creation:
- Acoustic and instrumental music
- Screen/film/animation music
- Interactive game audio
- Sound installations and sonic art

## Programme Highlights

- **Ensemble in Residence** — supports creation, rehearsal, recording and performance
- **State-of-the-art facilities** — 5 production studios, 360° audio mixing for VR/AR/Dolby Atmos
- **All Steinway School** — 28 pianos available
- **Interdisciplinary** — collaboration with Schools of Art, Architecture, Design, History of Art
- **Venues** — Reid Concert Hall, West Court, Wee Red Bar, St Cecilia's Hall

## Career Paths

- Theatre, radio, TV, video games
- Sound recording, live performance
- Composers in residence (orchestras, ensembles)
- Music-related businesses
- Academic careers (PhD)
