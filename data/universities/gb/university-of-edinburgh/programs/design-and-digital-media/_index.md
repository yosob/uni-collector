description_en: 'This innovative programme offers the best of studio-based design
  learning and provides opportunities to develop skills and insights in the creative
  use of design practices and technologies for digital media across a range of uses
  and platforms.

  Digital media technology is at the heart of modern life. At work, in education,
  in leisure and entertainment, even in sport, digital tools and devices are everywhere.
  How we communicate, our social life, the core of our society, is now based on digital
  media.

  Design also continually shapes contemporary experience. The products and services
  we use, the environment we inhabit, the information we use, or are presented with,
  are all designed with specific intent. Things that are not themselves digital are
  commonly designed digitally.

  Digital design requires a multi-perspective approach to developing media and interactive
  experiences, and this programme deals with the complex relationships between design
  and digital media.

  The programme is practice-led and students create innovative design for, and with,
  digital media and technologies across a range of creative contexts including visual
  design, user interface design, user experience design, interactive media, mobile
  web applications, animation, game design, augmented reality, and virtual environments.

## Entry Requirements

- **Academic**: UK 2:1 honours degree or international equivalent in design, technology or computing
- **Professional**: Relevant professional experience also considered
- **Portfolio**: Required — single PDF with examples of creative design/technology work
- **Personal statement + CV**: Required
- **English**: IELTS 7.0 (6.0 each), TOEFL 100 (20 each), or equivalent
