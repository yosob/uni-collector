## Programme Highlights

- **Theory-based**: No technical filmmaking or screenwriting — focuses on critical analysis, film theory and film-philosophy
- **European & American cinema emphasis**: Central concepts in film-philosophy with emphasis on European and American auteur cinema
- **Edinburgh environment**: Home to art-house cinemas, cinephile culture, and the Edinburgh International Film Festival
- **Student media**: Contribute to Edinburgh Film Podcast and The Film Dispatch magazine
- **Ranking**: 3rd in UK for Arts and Humanities (QS World Rankings by Subject, 2026)

## Part-time Study

- **Duration**: 2 years
- **Year 1**: Two core courses in Semester 1 + one core course in Semester 2
- **Year 2**: One core course in Semester 2 + one elective in each semester + dissertation
- **Note**: Part-time option not available to international students

## Assessment

- Most courses assessed by academic essays
- Shorter works of close film analysis
- Dissertation: 15,000-word independent research project (normally submitted in August)
