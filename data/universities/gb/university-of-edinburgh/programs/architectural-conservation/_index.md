## Entry Requirements

- **Academic**: UK 2:1 honours degree or international equivalent in a relevant subject
- **Professional**: Relevant professional experience also considered
- **Portfolio**: **Not required** — no prior architecture or design background needed
- **Personal statement**: Required — must address specific conservation interests, career ambitions, and motivation for applying
- **English**: IELTS 7.0 (6.0 each), TOEFL 100 (20 each) [before 21 Jan 2026], or equivalent

| Summer | MSc Dissertation (60) | — | 60 |

## Accreditation

**Institute of Historic Building Conservation (IHBC)** — Britain's official organisation for architectural heritage professionals recognises this programme.

## Key Features

- 🏛️ **50+ years old** — longest-established graduate historic preservation programme in Scotland and neighbouring nations
- 🌍 **World Heritage Site** — based in the heart of the Edinburgh UNESCO World Heritage Site
- 📝 **No exams** — assessed through written papers, group projects, presentations, and reports
- 🎓 **First-principles approach** — no prior architecture/design background required
- 🚌 **Field trips** — across Scotland, England, and a study tour to Germany
- 🤝 **Work placements** — partnerships with Edinburgh's national and civic heritage organisations
