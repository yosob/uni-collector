## Key Features

- 🏙️ **Urban design specialist** — for architecture graduates seeking an urban design qualification
- ✈️ **International fieldwork** — annual study trip to an internationally significant city (past: Shanghai, Florence, Mumbai, Kolkata, Ahmedabad, Derry)
- 🎨 **Studio-centred** — daily studio work with dedicated tuition and workshops
- 📐 **Multi-scale design** — operates between body, building, city and metropolitan scales
- 🌿 **Environmentally & socially sensitive** — all design work engages sustainability
- 🎬 **Multimedia exhibitions** — curate, produce and present sophisticated presentations
- 🌍 **Multi-disciplinary environment** — work alongside students from related programmes and disciplines

Graduates add a significant urban design qualification to their existing architecture degree:
- Architectural and urban design practice at a higher level
- Specialist urban design consultancy
- Academic research / PhD preparation
- Leadership roles in international design teams
- Branching into urban design specialism
