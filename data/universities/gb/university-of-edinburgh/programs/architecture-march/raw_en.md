### Programme Overview
Architecture MArch (ARB/RIBA Part 2) is a two-year full-time (four-year part-time) professionally accredited programme offered by ESALA (Edinburgh School of Architecture and Landscape Architecture) at Edinburgh College of Art. It leads to a Master of Architecture (MArch) award and satisfies the educational requirements for ARB/RIBA Part 2.

The programme is unique in being a postgraduate programme that is funded as undergraduate — Scottish students are eligible for SAAS tuition fee support, and RUK students can access undergraduate student finance.

### Key Features
- Dual ARB/RIBA Part 2 accreditation
- Choice of Integrated or Modular studio pathways
- Studio-based learning in purpose-designed ECA facilities
- Strong emphasis on design research, critical thinking, and architectural technology
- UCAS Code: K130

### Entry Requirements
- UK 2:1 honours degree or international equivalent in Architecture or closely related subject
- RIBA Part 1 (or equivalent) qualification required
- Portfolio of architectural design work (PDF max 15MB or online link)
- Personal statement and CV
- Two references (at least one academic)
- English language: IELTS 6.5 (6.0 each component) or equivalent

### Application
- Deadline: 25 March 2026
- Apply via EUCLID (UoE online system)
- Shortlisted applicants may be invited for interview

### Degree Finder Description
Architecture at ESALA is a research-driven programme that combines studio-based design work with taught courses in architectural technology, theory, and professional practice. Students choose between Integrated and Modular studio pathways, allowing either a continuous immersive design experience or flexible exploration of different design approaches.

The programme culminates in a Design Thesis project and report, supported by courses in Architectural Management, Practice and Law, ensuring graduates are prepared for professional practice.

### Year 1 (120 credits)

Compulsory:
- ARCH11075 — Architectural Technology Research — 20 credits (Semester 1) — Must pass
- ARCH11070 — Studies in Contemporary Architectural Theory — 20 credits (Semester 2)

Integrated Pathway (80 credits):
- ARCH11091 — Architectural Design Studio C (Integrated) — 40 credits (Semester 1)
- AND ARCH11092 — Architectural Design Studio D (Integrated) — 40 credits (Semester 2)

Modular Pathway (80 credits) — Group A:
Semester 1 (select exactly 40 credits):
- ARCH11089 — Architectural Design Studio C (Modular) — 40 credits
- ARCH11296 — Architectural Design Studio E (Modular) — 40 credits
- ARJA11003 — Architectural Design Studio G — 40 credits

Semester 2 (select exactly 40 credits):
- ARCH11090 — Architectural Design Studio D (Modular) — 40 credits
- ARCH11096 — Architectural Design Studio B (Modular) — 40 credits
- ARCH11295 — Architectural Design Studio A (Modular) — 40 credits

### Year 2 (120 credits)

Compulsory:
- ARJA11002 — Architectural Management, Practice and Law — 20 credits (Semester 1) — Must pass
- ARCH11069 — Design Report — 10 credits (Semester 2)
- ARJA11001 — Academic Portfolio 2 — 10 credits (Semester 2) — Must pass
- ARCH11297 — Architectural Design Thesis Report — 20 credits (Semester 2) — Must pass

Integrated Pathway (80 credits):
- ARCH11093 — Architectural Design Studio A (Integrated) — 40 credits (Semester 1)
- AND ARCH11174 — Architectural Design Studio H — 40 credits (Semester 2)

Modular Pathway (80 credits) — Group B:
Same options as Year 1 Modular

Assessment rule: Any failed learning outcome in Architectural Design Thesis Report, Architectural Technology Research (ARCH11075) and Architectural Management, Practice and Law (ARJA11002) will trigger a forced fail.
