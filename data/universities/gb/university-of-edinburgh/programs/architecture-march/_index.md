## Studio Pathways

The programme offers two studio pathways:

1. **Integrated Pathway**: Students take paired studios sequentially across both semesters, providing a continuous, immersive design experience
2. **Modular Pathway**: Students select from a menu of studio options each semester, offering flexibility to explore different design approaches and scales

Both pathways lead to the same MArch award and ARB/RIBA Part 2 accreditation.

## Professional Accreditation

This programme is prescribed by the **Architects Registration Board (ARB)** and validated by the **Royal Institute of British Architects (RIBA)** at Part 2 level. Upon successful completion, graduates satisfy the educational requirements for Part 2 and can progress to Part 3 (Professional Practice) to become registered architects.

- Exhibition spaces for student work
