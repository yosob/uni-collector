## Entry Requirements

- **Academic**: UK 2:1 honours degree or international equivalent in a relevant subject
- **Professional**: Relevant professional experience also considered
- **Portfolio**: **Required** — one single PDF, max 10 pages. Must demonstrate engagement with sustainable issues and design
- **English**: IELTS 6.5 (6.0 each), TOEFL 92 (20 each) [before 21 Jan 2026], or equivalent

## Key Features

- 🌿 **Design-oriented** — fuses creative solutions with sustainable strategies
- 🏗️ **Building simulation** — gain skills in environmental modelling and prediction
- 🌍 **Global outlook** — projects set in Edinburgh, Dubai, Singapore and beyond
- 🎯 **Holistic approach** — social, environmental and economic aspects, not just technical fixes
- 🧑‍🏫 **Practitioner staff** — all experienced in practice with sustainable design track records
- 🏛️ **Academy affiliation** — part of the University's Global Environment and Society Academy
