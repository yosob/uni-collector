# MComp Games Computing — University of Lincoln

## Overview

The **MComp Games Computing** is a four-year Integrated Master's degree that develops advanced skills in game design, programming, and development. Years 1–3 follow the BSc programme, with Year 4 providing advanced study in specialist topics.

Students gain comprehensive knowledge of game engine development, AI, computer graphics, and human-computer interaction at Master's level.
