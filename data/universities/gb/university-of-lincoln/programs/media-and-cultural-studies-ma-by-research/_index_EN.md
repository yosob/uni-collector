# MA Media and Cultural Studies by Research — University of Lincoln

## Overview

The **MA Media and Cultural Studies by Research** at the University of Lincoln allows students to pursue an independent research project in media and cultural studies. Students work closely with a supervisory team on a sustained piece of research.

## Key Information

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Campus** | Brayford Pool, Lincoln |
| **Duration** | 1 year (full-time) / 2 years (part-time) |
| **Start Date** | September 2026 |
| **Credits** | 180 CATS |
| **Assessment** | Written thesis |

## Entry Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Degree** | 1st or 2:1 honours degree in a relevant subject |
| **IELTS** | 6.5 overall (min 6.0 in each element) |

## Fees (2026/27)

| Fee Type | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| **UK Students** | £5,006 per year |
| **International Students** | £9,800 per year |

## Career Prospects

Academic researchers, media analysts, cultural policy advisors, PhD progression.
