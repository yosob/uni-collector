# MA Design by Research — University of Lincoln

## Overview

The **MA Design by Research** at the University of Lincoln is a research-based postgraduate programme allowing students to pursue an independent design research project. Students work with a supervisory team on practice-led or text-based research.

The programme supports research across design disciplines including graphic design, product design, interior design, and design theory. Assessment is through a written thesis (approximately 25,000 words) or a viva voce examination.
