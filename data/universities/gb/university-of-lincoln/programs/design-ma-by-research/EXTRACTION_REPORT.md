## Programme Type

This is a **research-based** MA programme (not a taught programme). Students conduct independent research under academic supervision, culminating in a 25,000-word written thesis. The focus is on contextually situating and critically evaluating the value of proposed design interventions/solutions — not portfolio production.

Two supervisors are assigned; students meet monthly (full-time) or every alternate month (part-time).

## Research Areas

The School of Design and Architecture offers expertise in:
- Graphic Design, Typography, Book Arts
- Product Design, Illustration, Design Thinking
- Creative Advertising, Social Design
- Material Culture, Diaspora and Placemaking
- Fashion, Heritage, UX Design
- Photography, Interior Design
- Architecture and the Built Environment
