# BA (Hons) Music — University of Lincoln

## Overview

The **BA (Hons) Music** at the University of Lincoln offers a broad musical education encompassing performance, composition, music technology, and musicology. Students develop their practical musicianship alongside critical and analytical skills.

The programme is taught in purpose-built facilities and encourages collaboration across the creative arts. Students benefit from workshops with visiting musicians and opportunities for public performance.

## Curriculum

### Year 1 (Level 4)
**Core (6) + Options (7):** Including Music Performance, Music Theory, Composition, and creative arts shared modules.

### Year 2 (Level 5)
**Core (5) + Options (15):** Advanced performance, composition, music technology, and interdisciplinary options.

### Year 3 (Level 6)
**Core (4) + Options (6):** Specialist performance/recital, dissertation, and advanced creative projects.
