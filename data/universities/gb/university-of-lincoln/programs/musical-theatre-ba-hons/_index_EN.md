# BA (Hons) Musical Theatre — University of Lincoln

## Overview

The **BA (Hons) Musical Theatre** at the University of Lincoln is designed to develop the triple-threat skills of singing, acting, and dancing for a career in musical theatre and the performing arts.

Students receive intensive practical training in all three disciplines, alongside critical study of musical theatre history and contemporary practice. The programme includes opportunities for public performance.
