# BA (Hons) Film and Media — University of Lincoln

## Overview

The **BA (Hons) Film and Media** programme at the University of Lincoln offers students the opportunity to study film, television, and digital media through a combination of critical analysis and creative production.

Students develop skills in film analysis, media theory, screenwriting, and production, gaining a comprehensive understanding of how media shapes our culture and society. The programme combines academic study with practical creative work.

## Curriculum

### Year 1 (Level 4)

**Core:** Analysing Film & TV, Cultivating Creativity, Digital Media Cultures, Production Fundamentals

**Options:** Creative Criticism, Emerging Technologies, Expanded Film, Experimental Animation Practices, Sound for Visual Media

### Year 2 (Level 5)

**Core:** Contemporary Visual Culture, Creative Collaboration, Global Film & TV, Screen Histories, Social Media & Society, Specialist Elective

**Options:** Animation & VFX Compositing, Commercial Motion Design, Game Sound, Horror on Screen, Industry Film Practices, Industry Placement, Industry Screenplay Practices, LSCA Industry Placement Year, LSCA Study Abroad, Production Design, Screening Youth, Sound Design, Storytelling for Animation & VFX

### Year 3 (Level 6)

**Core:** Creative Futures, Dissertation Project, Film & Media Showcase, Media Arts
