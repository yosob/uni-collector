# MArch Architecture — University of Lincoln

## Overview

The **Master of Architecture (MArch)** at the University of Lincoln is a professionally validated programme that forms the second stage of architectural education (RIBA Part 2). The programme is designed to develop creative, critical, and professional architects.

The course is validated by the **RIBA** and prescribed by the **ARB**, and is also accredited by the Commonwealth Association of Architects. Students explore design at an advanced level, engaging with complex urban, environmental, and social challenges.

## Curriculum

### Year 1 (5 Core Modules)

- Introductory Design Project
- Cities Plus
- Building Design Project
- Architectural Research Process
- Research: Methods and Project

### Year 2 (5 Core Modules)

- Comprehensive Design: Brief and Context
- Comprehensive Design: Concept
- Comprehensive Design: Project
- Technical Appraisal
- Professional Practice
