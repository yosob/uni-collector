# BSc (Hons) Games Computing with VR — University of Lincoln

## Overview

The **BSc (Hons) Games Computing with VR** combines games computing with specialist virtual reality development skills. Students study game design and programming alongside VR/AR technologies, immersive experience design, and 3D interaction.
