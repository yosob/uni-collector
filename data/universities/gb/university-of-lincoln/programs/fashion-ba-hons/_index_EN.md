# BA (Hons) Fashion — University of Lincoln

> ⚠️ **This programme has been discontinued.** The official course page redirects to a generic "Study with Us" page. Data below was reconstructed from archived pages and third-party sources.

## Overview

The **BA (Hons) Fashion** programme at the University of Lincoln was a creative, practice-based degree that encouraged students to develop a personal design identity through experimentation with fashion design, construction, illustration, and contextual studies. Housed within the **Lincoln School of Fine & Performing Arts**, it was ranked **3rd in the UK** in the National Student Survey (NSS) 2015.

The course combined traditional craft skills with contemporary design thinking, providing access to specialist workshops and studios equipped with industrial machinery, millinery equipment, digital fabric printing, laser cutting, and 3D printing.
