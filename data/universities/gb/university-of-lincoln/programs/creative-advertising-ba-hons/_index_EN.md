# BA (Hons) Creative Advertising — University of Lincoln

## Overview

The **BA (Hons) Creative Advertising** programme at the University of Lincoln is designed to develop students into creative thinkers and practitioners ready for the advertising and creative industries.

The course focuses on developing skills in ideation, visual communication, strategic thinking, and craft. Students learn to create compelling advertising campaigns across multiple platforms, working on live industry briefs and competition entries.

## Why Choose Lincoln

- 🎯 Work on **live industry briefs** and competition entries
- 🏢 Industry trips to London agencies (Year 2 & 3) and New York agencies (Year 2, approx. £850)
- 💼 Graduate employers include **Spotify, adam&eveDDB, Saatchi & Saatchi, Wieden+Kennedy**
- 🎨 Cross-disciplinary collaboration with Graphic Design, Illustration, and Product Design students
- 🌍 Optional Year in Industry and Study Abroad opportunities
