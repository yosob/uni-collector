# MPhil/PhD Architecture — University of Lincoln

## Overview

The **MPhil/PhD Architecture** at the University of Lincoln offers the opportunity to pursue advanced research in architecture and related fields. Students work with a supervisory team to develop a sustained piece of original research.

Research areas include sustainable design, urbanism, heritage conservation, architectural history and theory, and digital architecture.

## Key Information

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Campus** | Brayford Pool, Lincoln |
| **Duration** | MPhil: 2 years / PhD: 3–4 years (full-time) |
| **Start Date** | Flexible |
| **School** | Lincoln School of Design and Architecture |
| **Assessment** | Thesis + Viva Voce |

## Entry Requirements

| Requirement | Details |

Academic researchers, architecture practitioners, urban planners, heritage consultants, and policy advisors.
