# BA (Hons) Technical Theatre and Stage Management — University of Lincoln

## Overview

The **BA (Hons) Technical Theatre and Stage Management** at the University of Lincoln provides practical training in the technical and management aspects of theatre production. Students develop skills in stage management, lighting, sound, set construction, and production management.

The programme benefits from the purpose-built Lincoln Arts Centre, providing real-world production experience in a professional theatre venue.
