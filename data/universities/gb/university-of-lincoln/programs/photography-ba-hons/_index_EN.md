# BA (Hons) Photography — University of Lincoln

## Overview

The **BA (Hons) Photography** at the University of Lincoln is designed to develop students' creative and technical abilities in photography, encouraging them to explore the medium's potential as a form of artistic expression and visual communication.

Students have access to professional-standard facilities including studios, darkrooms, digital suites, and a range of photographic equipment. The programme covers both analogue and digital photography, with opportunities to specialise in areas such as documentary, fine art, editorial, and commercial photography.

## Curriculum

### Year 1 (Level 4) — 6 Core Modules
Foundation modules covering photographic practice, theory, and technical skills.

### Year 2 (Level 5) — 5 Core + 2 Optional
Advanced practice with opportunities for specialisation and professional development.

### Year 3 (Level 6) — 4 Core + 3 Optional
Self-directed projects, professional practice, and final portfolio/exhibition.
