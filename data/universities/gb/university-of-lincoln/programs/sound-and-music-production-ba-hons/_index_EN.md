# BA (Hons) Sound and Music Production — University of Lincoln

## Overview

The **BA (Hons) Sound and Music Production** at the University of Lincoln is designed to develop skills in audio engineering, music production, sound design, and creative audio technologies.

Students work in professional-standard recording studios and production suites, developing expertise in music recording, mixing, mastering, sound for film and games, and electronic music production.
