# MCons Conservation of Cultural Heritage — University of Lincoln

## Overview

The **MCons Conservation of Cultural Heritage** is a standalone Master's-level conservation programme for students who have completed a relevant undergraduate degree. It focuses on advanced conservation practice, research methods, and professional development.

Students work with genuine historic objects in high-specification laboratories and develop specialist conservation skills to a professional standard.
