# MPhil/PhD Drama — University of Lincoln

## Overview

The **MPhil/PhD Drama** at the University of Lincoln offers advanced research opportunities in drama, theatre, and performance studies. Students pursue original research in areas such as performance practice, theatre history, dramatic theory, and applied theatre.

## Key Information

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Campus** | Brayford Pool, Lincoln |
| **Duration** | MPhil: 2 years / PhD: 3–4 years (full-time) |
| **Start Date** | Flexible |
| **School** | Lincoln School of Creative Arts |
| **Assessment** | Thesis + Viva Voce |

## Entry Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Degree** | Master's degree in a relevant subject |
| **IELTS** | 6.5 overall (min 6.0 in each element) |
| **Research Proposal** | Required |

## Career Prospects

Academic researchers, theatre practitioners, drama educators, arts administrators, and cultural sector leaders.
