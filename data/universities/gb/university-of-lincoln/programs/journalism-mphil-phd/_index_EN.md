# MPhil/PhD Journalism — University of Lincoln

## Overview

The **MPhil/PhD Journalism** at the University of Lincoln offers advanced research opportunities in journalism studies, media ethics, digital journalism, and news production. Students pursue original research under expert supervision.

## Key Information

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Campus** | Brayford Pool, Lincoln |
| **Duration** | MPhil: 2 years / PhD: 3–4 years (full-time) |
| **Start Date** | Flexible |
| **School** | Lincoln School of Film, Media and Journalism |
| **Assessment** | Thesis + Viva Voce |

## Entry Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Degree** | Master's degree in a relevant subject |
| **IELTS** | 6.5 overall (min 6.0 in each element) |
| **Research Proposal** | Required |

## Fees (2026/27)

| Fee Type | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| **UK Students** | £5,006 per year |
| **International Students** | £9,800 per year |

## Career Prospects

Academic researchers, media policy advisors, journalism educators, media analysts, and communications consultants.
