# MPhil/PhD Performing Arts — University of Lincoln

## Overview

The **MPhil/PhD Performing Arts** at the University of Lincoln offers advanced research opportunities across the performing arts disciplines, including dance, drama, music, and interdisciplinary performance practice.

Students can pursue practice-led or text-based research, working with a specialist supervisory team.

## Key Information

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Campus** | Brayford Pool, Lincoln |
| **Duration** | MPhil: 2 years / PhD: 3–4 years (full-time) |
| **Start Date** | Flexible |
| **School** | Lincoln School of Creative Arts |
| **Assessment** | Thesis + Viva Voce |

## Entry Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Degree** | Master's degree in a relevant subject |
| **IELTS** | 6.5 overall (min 6.0 in each element) |
| **Research Proposal** | Required |

## Career Prospects

Academic researchers, performing arts practitioners, arts administrators, educators, and cultural sector leaders.
