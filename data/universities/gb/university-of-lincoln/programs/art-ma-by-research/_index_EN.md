# MA Art by Research — University of Lincoln

## Overview

The **MA Art by Research** at the University of Lincoln is a research-based postgraduate programme that allows students to pursue an independent art research project. Students work closely with a supervisory team to develop their practice-led or text-based research.

The programme is suitable for those wanting to develop a sustained piece of research in fine art, with the flexibility to focus on practice-led or theoretical approaches.

## Key Information

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Campus** | Brayford Pool, Lincoln |

| **Start Date** | September 2026 |
| **School** | Lincoln School of Creative Arts |
| **Credits** | 180 CATS |
| **Assessment** | Thesis (practice-led with written element, or text-based) |

| Requirement | Details |

## Career Prospects

Practising artists, academic researchers, arts educators, curators, and creative practitioners. Many graduates progress to PhD study.
