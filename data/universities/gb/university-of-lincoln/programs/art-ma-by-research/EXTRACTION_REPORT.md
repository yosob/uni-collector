## Programme Type

This is a **research-based** MA programme (not a taught programme). Students conduct independent research under academic supervision, culminating in either:
- A written thesis of 25,000 words, or
- A practice-based submission: substantial body of art + 15,000 word thesis

There are no traditional taught modules; the programme is structured around independent study and research supervision.

## Research Areas

- Posthumanism
- Aesthetics
- Cognitive Science
- Art-science methodologies
- Contemporary fine art practice
- Live art
- Socially engaged practice
