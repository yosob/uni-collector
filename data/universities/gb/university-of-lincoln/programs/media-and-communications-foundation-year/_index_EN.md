# Media and Communications Foundation Year — University of Lincoln

## Overview

The **Media and Communications Foundation Year** at the University of Lincoln provides an alternative route of entry onto a range of media-related degree programmes. It is designed for students who may not meet the direct entry requirements for their chosen degree or who wish to develop their skills and knowledge before starting a full degree.

## Key Information

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Campus** | Brayford Pool, Lincoln |
| **Duration** | 1 year (foundation) + 3 years (degree) = 4 years total |
| **Start Date** | September 2026 |
| **School** | Lincoln School of Film, Media and Journalism |
| **Assessment** | Coursework-based |

## Pathway Degrees

- Film Production BA (Hons)
- Film and Media BA (Hons)
- Journalism BA (Hons)
- Media Production BA (Hons)
- Sports Journalism BA (Hons)

## Entry Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **A Level** | Minimum 80 UCAS Tariff points |
| **IELTS** | 6.0 overall (no element below 5.5) |

## Fees (2026/27)

| Fee Type | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| **UK Students** | £9,790 per year |
| **International Students** | £18,300 per year |

## Career Prospects

The foundation year leads directly into one of the media degree programmes, opening pathways to careers in film, television, journalism, media production, and digital media.
