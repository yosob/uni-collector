# MA Conservation of Cultural Heritage — University of Lincoln

## Overview

The **MA Conservation of Cultural Heritage** at the University of Lincoln is a postgraduate programme for students with a background in conservation or related fields, offering advanced study in conservation theory and practice.

Students develop specialist conservation skills and conduct original research, working with genuine historic objects in high-specification laboratories.
