## Modules Summary

### Year 1 (Level 4) — 3 Core + 7 Options
- **Core:** Bodily Encounters, Exploring 1: The Creative Arts, Frameworks
- **Options:** Introduction to Prose, Introduction to Stage Design, Project Space Plus, Screen Performance, Staging a Musical 2, Technical Theatre Technology, The Physical Performer

### Year 2 (Level 5) — 3 Core + 13 Options
- **Core:** Exploring 2: Place and Space, Material Entanglements 1, Specialist Elective
- **Options:** Acting the Song, Arts and Health, Contemporary Performance Technologies, Contemporary Production Practices, Creative Audio Technologies, Digital Performance, Industry Placement, LSCA Industry Placement Year, LSCA Study Abroad, Stage Combat, Teaching Practices, The Craft of Creative Non-Fiction, The Craft of Fiction

### Year 3 (Level 6) — 4 Core

- The course is ranked **top 20 in the UK** for the subject area (Complete University Guide 2025, out of 84 institutions).
- Staff include practising artists and writers with international reputations.
- **Contextual offers** available: one grade or 8 UCAS point reduction for eligible applicants.
