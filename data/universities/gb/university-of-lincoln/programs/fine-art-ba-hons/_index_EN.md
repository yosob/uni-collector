# BA (Hons) Fine Art — University of Lincoln

## Overview

The **BA (Hons) Fine Art** at the University of Lincoln is designed to enable students to develop their creative potential through a combination of studio practice and critical study. Students can work across painting, sculpture, printmaking, installation, and digital media.

The programme encourages students to develop an individual artistic voice while engaging with contemporary art practices and critical debates. Staff include practising artists and writers with international reputations.

## Curriculum

### Year 1 (Level 4)

**Core:** Bodily Encounters, Exploring 1: The Creative Arts, Frameworks

**Options:** Introduction to Prose, Introduction to Stage Design, Project Space Plus, Screen Performance, Staging a Musical 2, Technical Theatre Technology, The Physical Performer

### Year 2 (Level 5)

**Core:** Exploring 2: Place and Space, Material Entanglements 1, Specialist Elective

**Options:** Acting the Song, Arts and Health, Contemporary Performance Technologies, Contemporary Production Practices, Creative Audio Technologies, Digital Performance, Industry Placement, LSCA Industry Placement Year, LSCA Study Abroad, Stage Combat, Teaching Practices, The Craft of Creative Non-Fiction, The Craft of Fiction

### Year 3 (Level 6)

**Core:** Arts and Cultural Industries, Exploring 3: Investigating Creative Practice, Launching Practice: Degree Show, Locating Practice
