# MA Media and Communications — University of Lincoln

## Overview

The **MA Media and Communications** at the University of Lincoln is designed to develop critical understanding of the role of media in contemporary society. Students explore digital media, global communication, and research methods.

The programme is suitable for those seeking careers in media, communications, PR, digital content, or further academic research.

**Core Modules (4):**

- Global Media and Communication
- Independent Project or Dissertation
