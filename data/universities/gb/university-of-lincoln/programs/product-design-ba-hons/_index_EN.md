# BA (Hons) Product Design — University of Lincoln

## Overview

The **BA (Hons) Product Design** at the University of Lincoln combines creative design thinking with practical making skills. Students learn to design products that are functional, beautiful, and sustainable.

The programme covers design methodology, materials, digital design (2D/3D), UX design, and professional practice. Students benefit from interdisciplinary collaboration with Graphic Design, Illustration, and Creative Advertising students.

## Curriculum

### Year 1 (Level 4)
- **Contextual Studies 1** — Design theory and critical thinking
- **Design Fundamentals** — Design methodology, materials, visual language
- **Digital Design** — 2D/3D digital applications, 3D modelling
- **Drawing** — Drawing techniques, rendering, digital image generation

### Year 2 (Level 5)
- **Cultures** — Sustainable design, social responsibility
- **Emotional Design** — Emotion-driven design, material exploration
- **Interdisciplinary Collaboration** — Cross-discipline project work
- **UX Design** — User experience design
- Year in Industry / Study Abroad — Optional

### Year 3 (Level 6)
- **Contextual Studies 3** — Independent research/dissertation
- **Futures** — Design strategy, brand impact, innovation proposals
- **Negotiated Project 1** — Self-directed design proposal
- **Negotiated Project 2** — Design development and realisation
