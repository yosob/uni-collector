# BA (Hons) Journalism Studies — University of Lincoln

## Overview

The **BA (Hons) Journalism Studies** at the University of Lincoln offers a critical and analytical approach to journalism, focusing on the role of media in society, journalism ethics, and the theoretical foundations of news production.

Students develop practical journalism skills alongside critical understanding of media institutions, news values, and the social impact of journalism.
