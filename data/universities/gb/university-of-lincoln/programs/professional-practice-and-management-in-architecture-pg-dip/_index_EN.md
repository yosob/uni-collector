# PG Dip Professional Practice and Management in Architecture — University of Lincoln

## Overview

The **PG Dip Professional Practice and Management in Architecture** is a postgraduate diploma designed for architecture graduates seeking to develop professional practice and management skills required for architectural practice.

The programme supports preparation for professional registration and covers practice management, contract law, building regulations, and professional ethics.
