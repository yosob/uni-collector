# MA Journalism by Research — University of Lincoln

## Overview

The **MA Journalism by Research** at the University of Lincoln allows students to pursue an independent research project in journalism studies. Students work with a supervisory team on a sustained piece of original research in areas such as media ethics, digital journalism, news production, and media policy.

## Key Information

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Campus** | Brayford Pool, Lincoln |
| **Duration** | 1 year (full-time) / 2 years (part-time) |
| **Start Date** | September 2026 |
| **School** | Lincoln School of Film, Media and Journalism |
| **Credits** | 180 CATS |
| **Assessment** | Written thesis |

## Entry Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Degree** | 1st or 2:1 honours degree in a relevant subject |
| **IELTS** | 6.5 overall (min 6.0 in each element) |
| **Research Proposal** | Required |

Academic researchers, media analysts, journalism educators, policy advisors, and PhD progression.
