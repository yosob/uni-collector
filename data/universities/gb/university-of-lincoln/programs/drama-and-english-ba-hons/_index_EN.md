# BA (Hons) Drama and English — University of Lincoln

## Overview

The **BA (Hons) Drama and English** at the University of Lincoln combines the study of drama, theatre, and performance with English literature. Students explore the connections between theatrical practice and literary study, developing skills in performance, critical analysis, and creative writing.

The programme is taught in the purpose-built Lincoln Arts Centre and benefits from workshops with visiting practitioners and opportunities for public performance.
