# MA Interior Architecture and Design — University of Lincoln

## Overview

The **MA Interior Architecture and Design** at the University of Lincoln is a design-led postgraduate programme that focuses on adaptive reuse — the transformation of existing buildings for new purposes. Students develop advanced design skills through studio-based practice and research.

Two pathways are offered: **Treatise by Practice** (design-led) and **Treatise by Research** (academic/research-led).

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|

| **Credits** | 180 CATS |
| **Assessment** | 100% coursework (portfolio, presentations, written documentation) |

## Curriculum

**Core Modules (7):**
- Advanced Interior Design Studio
- Design Research Methods
- Interior Architecture Theory
- Adaptive Reuse Strategies
- Professional Practice
- Treatise (Practice or Research pathway)
- Exhibition and Presentation
