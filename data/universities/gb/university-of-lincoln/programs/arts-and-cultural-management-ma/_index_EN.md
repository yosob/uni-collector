# MA Arts and Cultural Management — University of Lincoln

## Overview

The **MA Arts and Cultural Management** at the University of Lincoln is designed for those seeking careers in the management and leadership of arts and cultural organisations. The programme combines theoretical understanding with practical skills in cultural management, policy, and enterprise.

## Key Information

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Campus** | Brayford Pool, Lincoln |
| **Duration** | 1 year (full-time) / 2 years (part-time) |
| **Start Date** | September 2026 |
| **Credits** | 180 CATS |
| **Assessment** | Coursework-based |

| Requirement | Details |

## Career Prospects

Arts administrators, cultural managers, gallery directors, festival organisers, cultural policy advisors, and creative industry consultants.
