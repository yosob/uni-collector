# BA (Hons) Media Production — University of Lincoln

## Overview

The **BA (Hons) Media Production** at the University of Lincoln is designed to equip students with the practical and theoretical skills needed for a career in the media industries. Students can specialise in areas such as television, radio, and digital media production.

## Key Information

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Campus** | Brayford Pool, Lincoln |
| **Duration** | 3 years (full-time) |
| **Start Date** | September 2026 |
| **School** | Lincoln School of Film, Media and Journalism |
| **Assessment** | Coursework-based |

## Entry Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **A Level** | 104–112 UCAS Tariff points |
| **IELTS** | 6.0 overall (no element below 5.5) |

## Fees (2026/27)

| Fee Type | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| **UK Students** | £9,790 per year |
| **International Students** | £18,300 per year |

## Career Prospects

Graduates work in television production, radio broadcasting, digital media, content creation, video editing, and media management.
