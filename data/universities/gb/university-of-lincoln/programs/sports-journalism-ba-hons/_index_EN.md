# BA (Hons) Sports Journalism — University of Lincoln

## Overview

The **BA (Hons) Sports Journalism** at the University of Lincoln is accredited by the **NCTJ** and is designed for students who want to combine their passion for sport with a career in journalism.

Students develop core journalism skills alongside specialist sports reporting, learning to cover live events, write match reports, produce multimedia content, and understand the business and social aspects of sport.

## Curriculum

### Year 1 (Level 4)

**Core (7):** Essential Journalism 1, Essential Law & Crime Reporting, Journalism Production One, Politics for Journalists, Shorthand, Sport and the Media, Sport Writing and Reporting

### Year 2 (Level 5)

**Core:** Essential Journalism 2, Journalism Production Two, Law, Ethics and Regulation, Specialist Reporting and Production, Sport and Society, The Business of Sport

**Options:** Industry Placement, Journalism International Exchange, Shorthand

### Year 3 (Level 6)

**Core (4):** Dissertation, Journalism Production Three: News and Sport Production Days, Journalism Project, Sport, PR and Communication
