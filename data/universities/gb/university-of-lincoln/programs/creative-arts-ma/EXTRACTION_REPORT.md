## Programme Type

This is a **taught** MA programme focused on interdisciplinary creative arts. Students engage with multiple creative disciplines, develop their unique creative voice, and build a professional portfolio through three core modules spanning arts marketing, creative process development, and artistic identity exploration.

## Modules

### Core Modules (3 total, no optional modules listed)

| Module | Description |
|---|---|
| How Do We Tell Them? Communications and Marketing for Arts and Culture | Marketing, communications, and audience development for creative practitioners |
| Process and Product | In-depth creative process from ideation to delivery, with portfolio development |
| Vision and Identity | Creative vision exploration with contextual essay and guest artist seminars |
