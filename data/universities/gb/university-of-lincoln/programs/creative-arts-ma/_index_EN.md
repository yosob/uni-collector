# MA Creative Arts — University of Lincoln

## Overview

The **MA Creative Arts** at the University of Lincoln is a multidisciplinary postgraduate programme that allows students to pursue creative work across a range of artistic disciplines. Students develop their creative practice through studio-based work, critical reflection, and research.

## Key Information

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Campus** | Brayford Pool, Lincoln |
| **Duration** | 1 year (full-time) / 2 years (part-time) |
| **Start Date** | September 2026 |
| **School** | Lincoln School of Creative Arts |
| **Credits** | 180 CATS |
| **Assessment** | Coursework (portfolio, written work) |

## Entry Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Degree** | 1st or 2:1 honours degree in a creative arts subject |
| **IELTS** | 6.5 overall (min 6.0 in each element) |
| **Portfolio** | Required |

## Fees (2026/27)

| Fee Type | Amount |
|----------|--------|

| **International Students** | £18,800 per year |

## Career Prospects

Practising artists, creative practitioners, arts educators, curators, and creative industry professionals.
