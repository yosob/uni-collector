# BA (Hons) Film Production — University of Lincoln

## Overview

The **BA (Hons) Film Production** at the University of Lincoln is designed to develop the next generation of filmmakers. The programme offers a comprehensive education in film production, from scriptwriting and directing to cinematography and editing.

Students learn the craft of filmmaking through hands-on practice using industry-standard equipment and facilities. The course covers fiction and non-fiction filmmaking, with opportunities to specialise in areas such as directing, cinematography, editing, and sound design.

## Curriculum

### Year 1 (Level 4) — Discovering Film

**Core:** Cultivating Creativity, Fiction Film, Non-Fiction Film, Screenwriting

**Options:** Creative Criticism, Emerging Technologies, Expanded Film, Experimental Animation Practices, Sound for Visual Media

### Year 2 (Level 5) — Developing Practice

**Core:** Interdisciplinary Collaboration, Short Film, Specialist Elective, Visualising Stories

**Options:** Animation & VFX Compositing, Commercial Motion Design, Documentary Film Practices, Game Sound, Horror on Screen, Industry Film Practices, Industry Placement, LSCA Industry Placement Year, LSCA Study Abroad, Production Design, Sound Design, Storytelling for Animation & VFX, plus additional options

### Year 3 (Level 6) — Professional Practice

**Core:** Creative Futures, Dissertation Project, Film Showcase
