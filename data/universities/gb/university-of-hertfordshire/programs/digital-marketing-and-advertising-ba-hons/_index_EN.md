# Digital Marketing and Advertising BA (Hons)

## Overview

This dynamic degree is perfect for aspiring digital marketers and creatives who want to shape the future of marketing. Blending creativity with technical expertise, the course equips students to craft compelling brand stories, manage digital campaigns, and analyse data to drive impact.

Whether you're looking to work in a fast-paced agency, in-house marketing team, or as a freelancer, this course sets you up for success in roles such as digital marketing executive, content strategist, social media manager, or campaign planner.

You'll gain practical experience using tools like Adobe, Google Ads & Analytics, and Meta Ads Manager, while learning to design and execute integrated marketing communications across platforms.

Through live client briefs, marketing hackathons and creative workshops, you'll build a portfolio that showcases your abilities in search engine optimisation (SEO), Pay Per Click, UX design, content marketing, and campaign strategy. You'll also explore emerging technologies, such as AI and data-driven marketing, in various modules.

Designed around real-world expectations, the course embeds professional development and career readiness from the start. You'll develop key skills in communication, storytelling, leadership, and digital literacy, giving you the edge in a competitive job market.

- **Creativity meets data:** Learn everything from copywriting and UX design to SEO, Google Ads, and campaign performance analysis. Modules like Click to Convert and Marketing Disrupted keep you ahead of digital trends and technologies.
- **Modern campus facilities:** Based on the de Havilland campus with access to specialist labs, 24/7 study zones, and Adobe Express. Just 25 minutes from central London, putting you in easy reach of marketing hubs and major industry events.
- **Flexible degree experience:** Choose to study abroad, take a placement year, or gain employment experience. The final-year capstone project lets you design and deliver a digital campaign from concept to execution working with a live client.
- **Expert teaching:** Learn from academic staff with backgrounds in creative agencies, digital marketing, branding, and strategy who bring deep sector insight and industry connections into the classroom.
- **Strong graduate outcomes:** Over 90% of undergraduates are in work or study (Graduate Outcomes, 2025).

## Professional Accreditations

This course is accredited by the Digital Marketing Institute (DMI), a leading global authority in digital marketing education. DMI accreditation shows that the course meets high industry standards and stays up to date with the latest trends and best practices. This recognition gives your qualification international credibility and helps you stand out to employers in the fast-growing digital marketing field.

### Chartered Institute of Marketing (CIM)

This course is aligned with the Chartered Institute of Marketing (CIM), the leading professional body for marketers. As a strategic partner, CIM helps ensure the course reflects current industry practice and supports the development of relevant, in-demand marketing skills.

## What You'll Study

The BA (Hons) Digital Marketing and Advertising course is delivered full-time over three years, with flexible options including a sandwich year for placement or study abroad. The structure is designed to balance creative practice, strategic thinking, and digital expertise through a blend of workshops, live briefs, hackathons, and interactive seminars.

### Year 1 (Level 4)

Your first year is shared with BA (Hons) Advertising and Public Relations, giving you a broad understanding of the industry before specialising in digital marketing. You'll build strong foundations in marketing principles, consumer behaviour, and brand storytelling, exploring key themes such as creative content creation and digital platforms while developing essential academic and professional skills.

**Modules:**
- Mastering Marketing Foundations: Building Connections with Consumers (30 Credits, Compulsory)
- Supporting Personal and Marketing Professional Practice (SPP) (0 Credits, Compulsory)
- The Creative Toolkit: Turning Ideas into Stories and Engaging Content (30 Credits, Compulsory)

### Year 2 (Level 5)

Your second year focuses on applying skills in real-world contexts. You'll design digital strategies, analyse user journeys, and work on integrated campaigns through modules like Click to Convert and From Insights to Impact. Career development and industry engagement are key, with opportunities to build a portfolio and take on live client work.

**Modules:**
- Click to Convert (Compulsory)
- From Insights to Impact (Compulsory)

### Year 3 (Level 6)

In your final year, you'll be prepared for the professional world. In the Showstopper Project, you'll design and deliver a full digital marketing campaign. You'll also explore the future of marketing in areas such as AI, sustainability, and innovation.

**Modules:**
- Showstopper Project (Compulsory)
- Marketing Disrupted (Compulsory)

Throughout all three years, you'll complete the Personal and Professional Development modules, focused on building the core soft skills every business student needs — communication, digital literacy, time management, resilience, and leadership. You'll also have the chance to earn additional tailored skills, from project management to advanced presentation techniques. All of this feeds into your Graduate Success Portfolio, a powerful showcase of your capabilities, experience, and readiness for the workplace.
