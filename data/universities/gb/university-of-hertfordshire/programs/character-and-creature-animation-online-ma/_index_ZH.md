# Character and Creature Animation (Online) MA

## Entry Requirements

A Bachelor's degree in a relevant discipline at a level equivalent to a UK second-class Honours degree. Applicants must submit a portfolio of creative work showing appropriate skills in the intended area of study. Non-standard applicants with at least 3 years of relevant work experience will also be considered (via CV and/or LinkedIn profile). All applicants must also provide a personal statement (approx. 300–500 words) and/or video presentation about why they want to study this programme.

## English Language Requirements

- english: IELTS 6.0 (with no less than 5.5 in any band) or equivalent

## Portfolio Requirements

A portfolio of creative work showing appropriate skills in the intended area of study — character animation, creature animation, or related 3D animation work.
