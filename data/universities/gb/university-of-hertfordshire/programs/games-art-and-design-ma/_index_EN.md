# Games Art and Design MA

## Overview

The MA Games Art and Design course enables you to study game art creation at an advanced level, developing your creative and technical skills to produce art for AAA game development. You will work with industry-standard tools and pipelines, creating characters, environments, props, and visual effects for interactive entertainment.

The course is designed for those passionate about game art who wish to push their practice to a professional standard. You will explore both the artistic and technical dimensions of game art production, from concept through to final in-engine implementation. The programme connects creative practice with critical media theory, enabling you to develop a distinctive voice within the games industry.

Students benefit from the University of Hertfordshire's strong reputation in games art education, with access to specialist facilities and close industry connections. The course encourages experimentation with emerging technologies and AI-driven workflows that are reshaping game art production.

Students also have the opportunity for unique collaboration with students on other pathways, working with animators, concept artists, 3D modellers and VFX artists.

- **Study Mode:** Full Time / Part Time
- **Duration:** 1 year (full time) / 2 years (part time)
- **Campus:** College Lane

## Entry Requirements

A degree, ideally in a relevant subject (2.2 minimum), plus a portfolio demonstrating recent engagement and creative activities in your intended study area. Accreditation of professional experience may be possible for practitioners without a first degree.

## English Language Requirements

- IELTS 6.5 (with no less than 5.5 in any band) or equivalent

## Portfolio Requirements

A portfolio demonstrating recent engagement and creative activities in the intended study area of games art and design. The portfolio should evidence creative practice, technical proficiency in game art tools (such as Unreal Engine, Maya, ZBrush, Substance Painter), and engagement with character design, environment art, or asset creation.
