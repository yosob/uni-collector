# Architecture BA (Hons)

## Entry Requirements

120–128 UCAS points from at least 2 A-levels (BBB–ABB, creative subjects preferred such as Art, Media or Design). BTEC Extended Diploma at DDM. International Baccalaureate: 120–128 points from a minimum of two HL subjects at H4 or above to include Visual Arts at SL or HL. Access Course: Diploma with 45 Level 3 credits at Merit. Accepted T Levels include Design Survey and Planning for Construction, Digital Production, Design and Development, Craft and Design, among others. Interview required. GCSE Mathematics and English Language at grade 4/C or above (or equivalent).

## English Language Requirements

- english: IELTS 6.0 (with no less than 5.5 in any band) or equivalent
