# Product and Industrial Design BA (Hons)

## Entry Requirements

96 UCAS points from at least 2 A-levels (or equivalent qualifications such as BTEC Extended Diploma, International Baccalaureate, or Access to HE Diploma). GCSE Mathematics and English Language at grade 4/C or above (or equivalent). A portfolio of creative/design work is required. Applicants with relevant professional experience or a strong portfolio may also be considered.

## English Language Requirements

- english: IELTS 6.0 (with no less than 5.5 in any band) or equivalent

## Portfolio Requirements

Portfolio of creative/design work required. Should demonstrate design thinking, sketching and drawing skills, 3D making and prototyping ability, creative problem-solving, and awareness of product/industrial design. Examples of sketchbooks, development work, models, and finished pieces are welcome. Portfolio advice video available from the University of Hertfordshire International YouTube channel.
