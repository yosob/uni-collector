# Illustration BA (Hons)

## Entry Requirements

104–112 UCAS points from at least 2 A-levels (or equivalent qualifications such as BTEC Extended Diploma, International Baccalaureate, or Access to HE Diploma). IB requirement: 104–112 UCAS points from a minimum of two HL subjects at H4 or above to include Visual Arts at SL or HL. Access course: Diploma with 45 level 3 credits at merit. GCSE Mathematics and English Language at grade 4/C or above (or equivalent). A portfolio of creative artwork is required and all suitable applicants are invited to interview.

## English Language Requirements

- english: IELTS 6.0 (with no less than 5.5 in any band) or equivalent (TOEFL 72)

## Portfolio Requirements

Presentation of an acceptable portfolio of artwork is required. Should demonstrate visual thinking, drawing ability, creative ideas, and experimentation across a range of media. Examples of sketchbooks, development work, and finished pieces are welcome. Portfolio advice available at: https://www.herts.ac.uk/courses/undergraduate-courses/art-design-and-fashion/undergraduate-art-and-design-applicant/undergraduate-art-and-design-portfolio-advice
