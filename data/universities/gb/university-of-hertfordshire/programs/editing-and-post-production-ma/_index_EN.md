# Editing and Post Production MA

## Overview

The MA Editing and Post Production course equips you with the creative and technical skills required to work in a range of roles in post-production for film, television, and digital media. You will develop advanced editing techniques, colour grading, sound design, and visual effects compositing to a professional standard.

The course is designed for those who wish to specialise in the art and craft of editing and post-production. You will explore both the technical and creative dimensions of post-production, developing a deep understanding of narrative structure, pacing, rhythm, and visual storytelling. The programme connects creative practice with critical media theory, enabling you to develop a distinctive editorial voice.

You will work with industry-standard software and hardware, gaining hands-on experience with professional editing suites, colour grading tools, and audio post-production facilities. The course reflects the rapidly evolving landscape of post-production, incorporating emerging technologies such as AI-assisted editing, HDR workflows, and immersive media formats.

Students also have the opportunity for unique collaboration with students on other pathways, working with filmmakers, animators, and visual effects artists.

- **Study Mode:** Full Time / Part Time
- **Duration:** 1 year (full time) / 2 years (part time)
- **Campus:** College Lane

## Entry Requirements

A degree, ideally in a relevant subject (2.2 minimum), plus a portfolio demonstrating recent engagement and creative activities in your intended study area. Accreditation of professional experience may be possible for practitioners without a first degree.

## English Language Requirements

- IELTS 6.5 (with no less than 5.5 in any band) or equivalent

## Portfolio Requirements

A portfolio demonstrating recent engagement and creative activities in the intended study area of editing and post-production. The portfolio should evidence editing practice, storytelling ability, and technical proficiency with post-production tools (such as Avid Media Composer, DaVinci Resolve, Adobe Premiere Pro, or equivalent).

- Post-Production Supervisor
