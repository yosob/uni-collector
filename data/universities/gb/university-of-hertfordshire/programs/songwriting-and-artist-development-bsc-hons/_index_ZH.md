# Songwriting and Artist Development BSc (Hons)

## Entry Requirements

112–120 UCAS points from at least 2 A-levels (or equivalent qualifications such as BTEC Extended Diploma, International Baccalaureate, or Access to HE Diploma). GCSE Mathematics and English Language at grade 4/C or above (or equivalent). Applicants with relevant professional experience may also be considered. The course is designed for talented songwriters, performers, producers or other music-creators.

## English Language Requirements

- english: IELTS 6.0 (with no less than 5.5 in any band) or equivalent
