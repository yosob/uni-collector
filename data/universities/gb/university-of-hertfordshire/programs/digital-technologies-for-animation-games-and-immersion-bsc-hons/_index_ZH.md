# Digital Technologies for Animation, Games & Immersion BSc (Hons)

## Entry Requirements

112–120 UCAS points. A Level: BBC–BBB (or equivalent). BTEC: DMM (Applied Science, Creative Media, or related subject). GCSE: Grade 4/C in English Language and Mathematics. Foundation Year entry available through Hertfordshire International College (HIC).

## English Language Requirements

- english: IELTS 6.0 overall with no less than 5.5 in each band (or equivalent: TOEFL 72, PTE 59, Duolingo 105)

## Portfolio Requirements

No portfolio required for the BSc (Hons) pathway. Entry is based on academic qualifications and UCAS points. The BSc route focuses on technical skills rather than art-based portfolio work.
