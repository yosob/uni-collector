# Interior Architecture and Design BA (Hons)

## Entry Requirements

112–120 UCAS points from at least 2 A-levels (BBC–BBB, creative subjects preferred such as Art, Media or Design). BTEC Extended Diploma at DMM–DDM. International Baccalaureate: 112–120 points. Access Course: Diploma with 45 Level 3 credits at Merit. GCSE Mathematics and English Language at grade 4/C or above (or equivalent). Portfolio required. Interview may be required.

## English Language Requirements

- english: IELTS 6.0 (with no less than 5.5 in any band) or equivalent

## Portfolio Requirements

A portfolio of creative work demonstrating design and art skills is required. The portfolio should showcase your ability in spatial thinking, drawing, making and visual communication. See the Art and Design portfolio advice page for detailed guidance: https://www.herts.ac.uk/courses/undergraduate-courses/art-design-and-fashion/undergraduate-art-and-design-applicant/undergraduate-art-and-design-portfolio-advice
