# Fine Art BA (Hons)

## Entry Requirements

Good grades in Art or Design subjects at A-level or equivalent. Portfolio submission required. At least 1 academic reference and a motivation letter. UCAS code: W100, Institution code: H36.

## English Language Requirements

- english: IELTS 6.0 (minimum 5.5 in each component) or CAE 169 (minimum 162 in each component)
- german: None

## Portfolio Requirements

Portfolio of artwork demonstrating creative ability and engagement with fine art practice. Contact the School of Creative Arts for specific portfolio guidelines.
