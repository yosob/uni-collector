# Global Film and Television (Online) MA

## Entry Requirements

A Bachelor's degree at a level equivalent to a UK second class Honours degree (2:2). Non-standard applicants who can demonstrate 3 years of relevant work experience (via a Curriculum Vitae and/or LinkedIn profile) will also be considered. A reference letter from a current or previous manager or HR department, personal identification documents, and a substantial personal statement (approx. 300–500 words) or video presentation are required.

## English Language Requirements

- english: IELTS 6.0 (with no less than 5.5 in any band) or equivalent
