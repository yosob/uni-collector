# Photography BA (Hons)

## Entry Requirements

96–104 UCAS points from at least 2 A-levels (or equivalent qualifications such as BTEC Extended Diploma, International Baccalaureate, or Access to HE Diploma). GCSE Mathematics and English Language at grade 4/C or above (or equivalent). A portfolio of photographic work is required and all suitable applicants are invited to interview. Applicants with relevant professional experience or a strong portfolio may also be considered.

## English Language Requirements

- english: IELTS 6.0 (with no less than 5.5 in any band) or equivalent

## Portfolio Requirements

Portfolio of photographic work required for interview. Should demonstrate visual thinking, creative approach to image-making, technical competence in analogue and/or digital photography, and an understanding of composition, lighting, and visual storytelling. Examples of sketchbooks, development work, contact sheets, and finished prints across a range of subjects and styles are welcome.
