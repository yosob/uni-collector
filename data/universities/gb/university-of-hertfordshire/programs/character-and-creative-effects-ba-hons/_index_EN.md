# Character and Creative Effects BA (Hons)

## Overview

Made for the talented and creative figurative sculptor and designer, this innovative course opens the door to a vibrant career in the model design industry. All three of the Model Design degrees share the same First Year, giving you an excellent introduction to model making, prop making, sculpture and special effects. Building on this foundation, you'll learn to use a wide range of specialist materials and processes to create original sculptural characters, props and set dressings — including the option of developing stop-motion puppets or collaborating with special effects students.

Explore the fundamentals of the design and creation of puppets and theatre props for performance, public sculptures and display pieces. Shape your career and determine where your skills take you — whether you want to work on films, television, museums and exhibits or theatre productions and spectaculars, you will be equipped with the tools to confidently move into the career path of your choice. Benefit from outstanding links to industry, providing you with regular contact with industry professionals, as well as excellent placement opportunities and collaborative projects.

## Entry Requirements

96 UCAS points from at least 2 A-levels or equivalent qualifications. BTEC Extended Diploma accepted. GCSE English Language and Mathematics at grade 4/C or above (or equivalent). A strong portfolio of creative work demonstrating sculptural and design ability is essential.

## English Language Requirements

- english: IELTS 6.0 overall with no less than 5.5 in each band, or equivalent

## Portfolio Requirements

Portfolio of creative work required, demonstrating figurative sculpture, design and making abilities. Evidence of drawing skills and three-dimensional work is expected.

## Career Prospects

Graduates work in film, television, advertising, theatre, theme parks, museums and exhibitions, fine art installations, and interactive displays. The programme has consistently high graduate employment rates. Industry partners include Warner Bros, Harry Potter Experience, Merlin Entertainments (Madame Tussauds), and leading UK film studios. Students have worked on major productions including Captain America, Guardians of the Galaxy, Thor: The Dark World, Maleficent, World War Z, and Edge of Tomorrow.
