# Animation MA

## Entry Requirements

A degree in a relevant subject (minimum 2.2 or equivalent). Applicants should also submit a collection of work demonstrating recent engagement and creative activities in their intended study area. Relevant professional experience may also be considered. All suitable applicants may be invited to interview.

## English Language Requirements

- english: IELTS 6.5 (with no less than 5.5 in any band) or equivalent

## Portfolio Requirements

A collection of work demonstrating recent engagement and creative activities in the intended area of animation study. The portfolio should evidence creative practice, visual storytelling ability, and engagement with animation techniques.
