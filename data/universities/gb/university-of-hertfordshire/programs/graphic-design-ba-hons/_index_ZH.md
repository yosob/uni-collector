# Graphic Design BA (Hons)

## Entry Requirements

112–120 UCAS points from at least 2 A-levels (or equivalent qualifications such as BTEC Extended Diploma, International Baccalaureate, or Access to HE Diploma). GCSE Mathematics and English Language at grade 4/C or above (or equivalent). A portfolio of creative work is required and all suitable applicants are invited to interview. Applicants with relevant professional experience or a strong portfolio may also be considered.

## English Language Requirements

- english: IELTS 6.0 (with no less than 5.5 in any band) or equivalent

## Portfolio Requirements

Portfolio of creative work required for interview. Should demonstrate visual thinking, design process, typography skills, drawing ability, and creative flair. Examples of sketchbooks, development work, and finished pieces across a range of media are welcome. Portfolio advice available at: https://www.herts.ac.uk/courses/undergraduate-courses/art-design-and-fashion/undergraduate-art-and-design-applicant/undergraduate-art-and-design-portfolio-advice
