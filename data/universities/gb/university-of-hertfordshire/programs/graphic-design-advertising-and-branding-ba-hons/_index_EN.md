# Graphic Design: Advertising and Branding BA (Hons)

## Entry Requirements

112–120 UCAS points from at least 2 A-levels (BBC–BBB, creative subjects preferred) or equivalent qualifications including BTEC Extended Diploma (DMM–DDM), International Baccalaureate (112–120 points from a minimum of 2 HL subjects at H4 or above to include Visual Arts at SL or HL), Access Course (Diploma with 45 Level 3 credits at Merit), or accepted T Levels. GCSE Grade 4/C in English Language and Mathematics required. A portfolio of creative artwork is required.

## English Language Requirements

- english: IELTS 6.0 overall with no less than 5.5 in each band or equivalent

## Portfolio Requirements

Presentation of an acceptable portfolio of artwork. Should demonstrate visual thinking, design process, typography skills, creative ability, and engagement with advertising and branding concepts. Examples of sketchbooks, development work, and finished pieces across a range of media are welcome. Portfolio advice available at: https://www.herts.ac.uk/courses/undergraduate-courses/art-design-and-fashion/undergraduate-art-and-design-applicant/undergraduate-art-and-design-portfolio-advice
