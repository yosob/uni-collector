# Digital Arts for Animation, Games & Immersion BA (Hons)

## Entry Requirements

96–112 UCAS points. A Level: BBC (or equivalent). BTEC: MMM–DMM (Art & Design or related subject). GCSE: Grade 4/C in English Language and Mathematics. Foundation Year entry available through Hertfordshire International College (HIC).

## English Language Requirements

- english: IELTS 6.0 overall with no less than 5.5 in each band (or equivalent: TOEFL 72, PTE 59, Duolingo 105)

## Portfolio Requirements

Portfolio of creative work required. Should demonstrate visual creativity, digital media skills, and artistic potential. May include examples of digital art, photography, video, animation, interactive media, or other creative projects.
