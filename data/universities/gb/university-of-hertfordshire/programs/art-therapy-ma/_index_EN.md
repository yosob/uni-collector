# Art Therapy MA

## Entry Requirements

An honours degree (minimum 2:2) in a closely relevant subject, such as Art and Design (Fine Art, Textiles, Ceramics, Film, etc.). A minimum of 1,000 hours of relevant work experience (paid or voluntary) in health, education, or community settings, accrued by the date of application. Evidence of an identity as an artist. Interview and portfolio are required. Non-graduates may be considered through APEL (Accredited Prior Experiential Learning).

## English Language Requirements

- english: IELTS 7.0 (with no less than 6.5 in any band) or equivalent

## Portfolio Requirements

A portfolio demonstrating a strong identity as an artist, showing engagement with creative practice and art making. The portfolio should evidence the applicant's artistic process and commitment to visual arts.
