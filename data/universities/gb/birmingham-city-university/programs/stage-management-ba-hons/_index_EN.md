## Overview

Prepare yourself for a successful career in Stage Management by studying at Royal Birmingham Conservatoire. Our BA (Hons) Stage Management degree provides in-depth, practical training; we aim to produce confident graduates who are ready for a successful career in the industry as soon as they leave university.

You will be involved in a range of practical production projects throughout the course, developing your skills in stage management, production management, technical theatre and design. From your very first term you will be taking on roles of increasing responsibility within our busy production schedule.

Our strong industry links ensure that you will have opportunities for professional placements and networking throughout your studies.

## Modules

### Year One
- Practical Stage Management 1 (ASM) — 40 credits
- Production Realisation — 40 credits
- Technical Theatre 1 — 20 credits
- Production Design 1 — 20 credits

### Year Two
- Practical Stage Management 2 (DSM) — 40 credits
- Production Administration — 20 credits
- Technical Theatre 2 — 20 credits
- Production Design 2 — 20 credits
- Collaborative Practice — 20 credits

### Year Three
- Practical Stage Management 3 (SM) — 40 credits
- Professional Development — 40 credits
- Final Major Project — 40 credits


## Entry Requirements

96 UCAS Tariff points. A-level, BTEC or equivalent qualifications. IELTS 6.0 with no less than 5.5 in each band for international students. Interview required.

## Career Prospects

Stage manager, deputy stage manager, assistant stage manager, production manager, technical manager, company manager, tour manager, event manager.
