## Overview

This course allows you to learn the secrets of a successful audition, alongside voice, movement, dance and acting classes.

The Foundation Course in Acting will give you a broad foundation in performance techniques and methods, and will be invaluable to anyone wanting to go to Drama School or take up a career in the performing arts.

You will develop a strong understanding of acting technique, voice production, movement and dance, as well as learning how to present yourself at auditions and interviews.

## Modules

### Course Modules
- Acting Technique
- Voice Production
- Movement and Dance
- Audition Preparation


## Entry Requirements

No formal UCAS points required. Selection is by audition and interview. IELTS 6.0 with no less than 5.5 in each band for international students.

## Career Prospects

Drama school preparation, acting, performing arts, further study in acting or performance-related disciplines.
