## Overview

Our BA Fashion Design Course at Birmingham City University will provide you with a comprehensive fashion design education. The course encourages innovation in both design skills and technical craftsmanship with a focus on sustainability, system thinking and socioeconomic practice.

This four-year programme includes a Foundation Year that will introduce you to fashion and textiles fundamentals, before you progress to the full BA (Hons) Fashion Design programme where you'll develop skills in design, pattern cutting, garment construction and fashion illustration.

## Modules

### Foundation Year
- Developing a Narrative — 20 credits
- Understanding Fundamental Processes — 20 credits
- Introducing Trends — 20 credits
- Brand Development — 20 credits
- Signature Project — 40 credits

### Year One
- Creative Industries: Connections — 20 credits
- Fashion Design Illustration — 20 credits
- Pattern Technology — 40 credits
- Fashion Design Process — 40 credits

### Year Two
- Creative Industries: Industry Futures — 20 credits
- Fashion Design Tailoring — 40 credits
- Fashion Design Technology — 40 credits
- Work Placement — 20 credits

### Year Three
- Industry Project — 20 credits
- Independent Creative Entrepreneur — 20 credits
- Creative Industries: Research Development and Enquiry — 20 credits
- Fashion Design Pre-Collection (FMP) — 40 credits
- Fashion Design Collection Construction — 20 credits
- Fashion Design Industry Portfolio — 40 credits


## Entry Requirements

80 UCAS Tariff points. A-level, BTEC or equivalent qualifications. IELTS 6.0 with no less than 5.5 in each band for international students. Portfolio may be required.

## Career Prospects

Fashion designer, pattern cutter, garment technologist, fashion illustrator, design director, creative director, fashion buyer, trend forecaster.
