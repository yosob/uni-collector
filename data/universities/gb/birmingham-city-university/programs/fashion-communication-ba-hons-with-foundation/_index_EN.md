## Overview

The BA (Hons) Fashion Communication with Foundation Year is a four-year programme that develops your skills in visual communication, styling, photography, journalism and creative direction within the fashion industry.

The Foundation Year introduces you to fundamental creative processes before you progress to study art direction, fashion media, branding, and visual storytelling. You'll develop a portfolio of work that reflects current industry practice and emerging trends in fashion communication.

## Modules

### Foundation Year
- Developing a Narrative — 20 credits
- Understanding Fundamental Processes — 20 credits
- Introducing Trends — 20 credits
- Brand Development — 20 credits
- Signature Project — 40 credits

### Year One
- Creative Industries: Connections — 20 credits
- Art Direction for Print and Journalism — 20 credits
- Future Thinking and Idea Generation — 20 credits
- Visual Storytelling — 40 credits

### Year Two
- Fashion Metaverse — 20 credits
- Creative Industries: Industry Futures — 20 credits
- Art Direction for Branding and Promotion — 40 credits
- Fashion Film with Styling — 40 credits

### Year Three
- Work Placement — 20 credits
- Industry Project — 20 credits
- Independent Creative Entrepreneur — 20 credits
- Creative Industries: Development and Enquiry — 20 credits
- Final Major Project — 40 credits
- Craft & Make — 20 credits
- Commercialisation & Portfolio Prep — 40 credits


## Entry Requirements

80 UCAS Tariff points. A-level, BTEC or equivalent qualifications. IELTS 6.0 with no less than 5.5 in each band for international students. Portfolio may be required.

## Career Prospects

Fashion stylist, art director, fashion journalist, creativedirector, fashion photographer, brand strategist, fashion editor, visual merchandiser, social media content creator.
