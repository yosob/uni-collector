## Overview

How media is made, who makes it, how it is consumed, and whose interests it serves are key questions for our time. This four-year programme includes a Foundation Year to help you build the skills and knowledge needed for degree-level study in media and communication.

The Foundation Year provides an introduction to key media concepts and practical skills, preparing you for the full BA (Hons) programme. You will then progress to study media analysis, content creation, and communication strategy while gaining a deep understanding of media industries and audiences.

## Entry Requirements

80 UCAS Tariff points. A-level, BTEC or equivalent qualifications. IELTS 6.0 with no less than 5.5 in each band for international students.

## Career Prospects

Media analyst, communications officer, content strategist, PR executive, social media manager, media researcher, digital marketing executive, brand consultant.
