## Overview

If you want to be a designer within the contemporary jewellery and silversmithing industry, this Level 6 top-up course is designed for you. The BA (Hons) Jewellery and Silversmithing — Design for Industry is a one-year programme that builds on your existing HND or foundation degree.

You will develop advanced skills in CAD/CAM, digital design technologies, and production-focused design thinking. The course is based at Birmingham's historic Jewellery Quarter at the School of Jewellery.

## Modules

### Year One (Level 6)
- Advanced Digital Technologies — 20 credits
- Design for Production — 40 credits
- Graduate Entrepreneurship — 20 credits
- Major Project — 40 credits


## Entry Requirements

HND or Foundation Degree in Jewellery/Silversmithing or related subject (240 credits at Level 4 and 5). IELTS 6.0 with no less than 5.5 in each band for international students.

## Career Prospects

Jewellery designer, production designer, CAD/CAM specialist, design consultant, bespoke jeweller, product developer, design technologist.
