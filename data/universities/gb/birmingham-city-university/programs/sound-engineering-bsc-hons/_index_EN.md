# Sound Engineering BSc (Hons)

## Overview

BSc (Hons) Sound Engineering at Birmingham City University immerses you in the cutting-edge world of audio technology and production, equipping you with the skills to pursue careers in live sound, acoustics, audio hardware and software development, and studio production. Delivered in collaboration with the Royal Birmingham Conservatoire (RBC), the course blends technical expertise, creative flair, and hands-on experience.

You will explore the science of acoustics and its application to live sound, broadcast audio, studio recording, and immersive technologies. The curriculum covers audio software development using languages such as Python, C++, and MATLAB, embedded audio systems, machine learning in audio, and multi-channel sound design.

## Course Structure

### Year One (Level 4)

Students build foundational knowledge in acoustics, audio fundamentals, and the principles of sound engineering. Core concepts include the physics of sound, signal flow, and basic studio techniques. Students are introduced to audio software tools and begin developing technical skills in recording and sound analysis.

**Core Modules (120 credits)** — Module names require verification from the BCU course specification as they are loaded dynamically.

### Year Two (Level 5)

Students progress to advanced topics including live sound engineering, broadcast audio, and immersive audio technologies. Software development for audio applications is introduced, including programming in Python and C++. Students work on collaborative projects simulating real-world audio production environments.

**Core Modules (120 credits)**

### Professional Placement Year (Optional)

Spend a full year with an employer after Year 2. Fees are 20% of the full-time course fee for that year. Previous placement hosts include leading audio companies and broadcasters.

### Final Year (Level 6)

Students specialise in advanced areas such as embedded audio systems, machine learning in audio, and multi-channel sound design. A major individual project consolidates learning and demonstrates professional competence. Focus on career preparation and portfolio development.

**Core Modules (120 credits)**
