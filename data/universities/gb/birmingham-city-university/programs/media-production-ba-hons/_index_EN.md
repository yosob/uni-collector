## Overview

Launch your media career with our BA (Hons) Media Production degree. Our industry-focussed programme is designed to develop critically thinking, innovative content creators for television, radio, podcasting, online, social media as well as new and emerging platforms.

You'll learn practical production skills alongside critical analysis of media industries and audiences. The course offers specialist pathways in television, radio and podcasting, with opportunities for professional placements and live industry projects.

## Modules

### Year One
- Understanding Media Texts, Industries and Audiences — 20 credits
- Television Skills — 20 credits
- Radio & Podcast Production — 20 credits
- Investigating Media Texts, Industries and Audiences — 20 credits
- Creative Production 1 — 20 credits
- Community and Industry Collaboration — 20 credits

### Year Two
- Researching the Broadcast Media — 20 credits
- Documentary & Features — 20 credits
- Creative Production 2 — 20 credits
- Work Placement — 20 credits
- Live Project — 20 credits
- Collaborative Practice — 20 credits

### Year Three
- Creative Production 3 — 20 credits
- Major Project Preparation — 20 credits
- Major Project - Dissertation — 40 credits
- Major Project - Hybrid — 40 credits
- Major Project - Production — 40 credits


## Entry Requirements

112 UCAS Tariff points. A-level, BTEC or equivalent qualifications. IELTS 6.0 with no less than 5.5 in each band for international students.

## Career Prospects

Television producer, radio producer, podcast producer, content creator, media researcher, broadcast journalist, video editor, social media manager.
