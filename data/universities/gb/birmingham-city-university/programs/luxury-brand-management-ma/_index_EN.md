# Luxury Brand Management MA

## Overview

MA Luxury Brand Management at Birmingham City University is a specialist postgraduate programme designed for students who aspire to build careers in the global luxury industry. The course provides a comprehensive understanding of luxury brand strategy, marketing, public relations, and cultural contexts — equipping graduates with the knowledge and skills to manage and grow luxury brands in an increasingly competitive international marketplace.

Delivered at BCU's City Centre Campus, the programme combines theoretical frameworks with practical industry engagement. Students explore ethical and sustainable development, marketing and PR specific to luxury sectors, luxury culture and lifestyles, and advanced research methodologies. The course also offers an optional Professional Placement route, extending study to 18 months with a 20-week credit-bearing work placement.

## Course Structure

### Core Modules (180 credits)

| Module | Credits | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **Responsible, Ethical and Sustainable Development** | 20 | Investigates the cultural, social, ethical, and environmental impacts of activities across the luxury industry, from sourcing to marketing. Addresses current and emerging sustainability issues globally. |
| **Analysis and Planning for Management** | 20 | Develops business planning skills and the ability to analyse business environments. Covers trends, competitors, market influences, product/service development, sustainable innovation, finance, and marketing. |
| **Advanced Research Skills** | 20 | Equips students with advanced research methodologies through blended learning. Covers qualitative, quantitative, and mixed methods approaches. |
| **Marketing and PR in the Luxury Sectors** | 20 | Comprehensive exploration of marketing principles with emphasis on fashion and luxury industries. Examines historical and contemporary fashion marketing techniques, PR strategies, and digital communication methods through case studies. |
| **Luxury Brand Management** | 20 | Introduces core concepts of luxury brands and brand management. Covers systematic and strategic branding processes through lectures, seminars, workshops, tutorials, and case studies. |
| **Luxury Culture and Lifestyles** | 20 | Explores the cultural and lifestyle dimensions that define and sustain the luxury industry. |
| **Research and Professional Skills** | 20 | Develops research skills and methods to craft a research proposal supporting the major project. Students choose their own area of interest within their specialism. |
| **Major Project: Luxury Brand Management** | 40 | A sustained, in-depth, theoretically informed research project on a topic of personal interest relevant to the programme. Outcome may take the form of a written dissertation or a practical outcome with accompanying reflective, critical, and contextual material. |

### Professional Placement Route (Optional)

Students opting for the Professional Placement complete an additional module:

- **Professional Placement** (60 credits, Level 6) — A 20-week credit-bearing work placement designed to enhance employability skills and provide evidence of professional capabilities at the point of entry to the postgraduate job market.

Upon successful completion, students receive the award of **MA Luxury Brand Management with Professional Placement**.
