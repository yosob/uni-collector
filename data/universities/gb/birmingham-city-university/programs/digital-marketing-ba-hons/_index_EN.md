## Overview

This BA (Hons) Digital Marketing degree is a two-year fast-track course that immerses you in creative, real-world, professional advertising and marketing strategy.

It's a powerful mix where you'll be working with creative professionals to learn key skills in the fast-paced design industries. You'll develop a strong understanding of digital marketing principles, consumer behaviour, brand strategy, and data analytics while building practical campaign management skills.

## Modules

### Year One
- Digital Marketing Primer — 20 credits
- Audiences and Brand Development — 40 credits
- Research Methodologies — 20 credits
- Planning and Strategy — 40 credits

### Year Two
- Collaborative Practice – Developing and Pitching — 20 credits
- Collaborative Practice – Production — 40 credits
- Contemporary Market Research Methods — 20 credits
- Methodologies for Digital Marketing Campaigns — 40 credits
- Content Marketing — 20 credits
- Professional Practice — 40 credits
- Major Project — 60 credits


## Entry Requirements

120 UCAS Tariff points. A-level, BTEC or equivalent qualifications. IELTS 6.0 with no less than 5.5 in each band for international students.

## Career Prospects

Digital marketing manager, social media strategist, content marketing manager, SEO specialist, brand manager, marketing analyst, advertising executive, digital campaign manager.
