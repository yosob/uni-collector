overview: Our Masters in Global Communications will give you insights, experience

why_this_programme:
- Gain the critical and practical skills needed to navigate the complex and ever-changing
  world of global communications.
- Build cross-cultural understanding and the ability to collaborate and form partnerships
  through the study of global (and globalised) media and communication practices and
  movements.
- Strengthen your ability to respond to shifts in a media-rich society, with increased
  literacy around communications bias, 'fake news' and misinformation.
- Approach social media and new digital communications technologies (including artificial
  intelligence, LLMs, and immersive technology) with confidence and critical thinking.
- Learn to analyse and develop communications strategies for business, public relations,
  politics, and news media.
- Develop the knowledge and skills needed for ethical and equitable communications.
- Glasgow is a culturally vibrant city, a UNESCO City of Music, and has played a pivotal
  role in global communications both past and present.
