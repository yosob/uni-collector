overview: The Glasgow MMus offers a rich, stimulating introduction to Musicology in
  all its current diversity. Solidly grounded in traditional historical and analytical
  methods, the programme opens out to embrace the most vibrant recent developments
  in the field — including cultural musicology, as applied to all imaginable genres
  and styles; practice-based research, rooted in active music-making by staff and
  students; cutting-edge digital research methods; and interdisciplinary criticism
  of all kinds, including the study of contemporary audiovisual media. The programme
  is delivered within the School of Culture and Creative Arts, which brings together
  Music, Film and TV Studies, History of Art, Theatre Studies, and the Centre for
  Cultural Policy Research.
