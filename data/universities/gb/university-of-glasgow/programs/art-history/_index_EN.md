### Entry Requirements

- **Academic**: 2:1 Honours degree or non-UK equivalent in Art History, Fine Art, or related humanities discipline
- **Portfolio**: Not required
- **English Language**: IELTS 6.5 (no sub-test less than 6.0)

### Programme Structure

The flexible structure offers a mix of taught and research components:
- **1 core course** + **5 optional courses** + **15,000-word dissertation**
- Semester 1: Research Methods and Skills (core) + 2 optional courses
- Semester 2: 3 optional courses
- Summer: Dissertation research and writing (April–August)

### Career Prospects

The programme prepares graduates for careers in museums, cultural heritage, auction houses, galleries, arts administration, and academic research. It also provides excellent grounding for PhD study.
