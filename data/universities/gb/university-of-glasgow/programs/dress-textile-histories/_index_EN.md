overview: This Masters provides you with the skills to research and interpret everyday
  clothing, fashion and textiles in historical contexts. The programme introduces
  you to theoretical and object-based approaches through a combination of taught and
  research-based components, showing you how historic dress and textiles can be studied
  both in their own right and as a means to understand the past. Close links with
  local and national archives, libraries and museums, including Glasgow Museums and
  National Museums Scotland, means the programme offers you a unique insight into
  the study, curation, interpretation and preservation of historic dress and textile
  collections. Glasgow is the ideal city in which to study dress & textile history
  because of its vibrant local textile heritage and close links to national institutions.
