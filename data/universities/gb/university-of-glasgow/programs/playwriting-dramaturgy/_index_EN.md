overview: This Masters gives you a practical and theoretical engagement with the many
  forms of writing and production for theatre. Blending practice, theory and history,
  the programme is designed for those wishing to develop playwriting skills and knowledge
  of script development and support, opening the way to many theatre roles, including
  dramaturgy. You will learn about the changing roles of the playwright and dramaturge
  in contemporary theatre in the UK and elsewhere around the world, and be supported
  to define your own creative practice while developing valuable transferable skills
  in research, project management and critical reflection. This is the only programme
  in Scotland that combines playwriting with dramaturgy.
