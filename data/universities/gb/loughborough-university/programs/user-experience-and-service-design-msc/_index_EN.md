# User Experience and Service Design MSc

## Overview

The **MSc in User Experience and Service Design** at **Loughborough University London** unites the disciplines of service design, experience design and innovation strategy to prepare you to create meaningful, human-centred solutions for a rapidly changing world.

Based in **London's thriving innovation and creative hub**, this programme immerses you in a dynamic learning environment where design thinking meets real-world application. You'll explore how design can drive **organisational change**, shape new **service systems**, and deliver user experiences that are engaging, ethical and sustainable.

As digital transformation reshapes industries, the boundaries between services, products and experiences are becoming increasingly fluid. This course brings together the **strategic and systemic thinking of service design** with the **creative, human-focused approach of user experience design**.

Your studies will be **project-based, collaborative and deeply practical**. Working with industry partners on live and conceptual briefs, you'll tackle authentic design challenges that call for empathy, strategic insight and experimentation. You'll also explore the growing influence of **technology — such as AI, data and digital production** — on how experiences and services are conceived and delivered.

## Entry Requirements

- **Academic:** A 2:2 honours degree (or equivalent international qualification) in **design, innovation, business, media, technology or a related subject**. Non-design students must have achieved a minimum of 55% in their final year.
- **English Language:** IELTS 6.5 overall with 6.0 in each component.

## Teaching & Assessment

The programme features a variety of teaching and learning methods including **lectures, seminars, tutorials, independent study, group work, workshops and practical sessions**. You'll also have the chance to take part in **guest lectures and projects** on a range of topics.

Assessment is through a combination of **essays, reports, presentations, projects** and **examinations**.

## Career Prospects

Graduates are prepared for exciting roles at the intersection of user experience, service design and innovation:

- **Service Designer**
- **Design Researcher**
- **Experience Designer**
- **Design Strategist**
- **User-Centred Consultant / UX Designer**
- **Innovation Consultant**
- **Product-Service-System Designer**
- **Systems Thinker/Designer**

You'll graduate ready for roles in **business, government, the charity sector**, or as an **independent consultant**, with the expertise to innovate and improve services while advancing the field of service design.

## Facilities

The programme is based at **Loughborough University's London campus**, located at **Here East** on the Queen Elizabeth Olympic Park — at the heart of the UK's digital innovation hub. Students have access to state-of-the-art **design studios, collaborative workspaces** and direct connections to London's thriving creative and technology industries.
