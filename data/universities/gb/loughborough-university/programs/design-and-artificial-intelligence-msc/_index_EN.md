# Design and Artificial Intelligence MSc

## Programme Overview

The **Design and Artificial Intelligence MSc** is an innovative interdisciplinary programme that brings together the creative power of design with the transformative potential of artificial intelligence. Based at Loughborough University's vibrant **London campus**, this programme equips students to harness AI to build human-centred design solutions for the complex challenges facing society.

Over the next decade, AI is forecast to become a **€1 trillion industry**, creating up to **50 million new jobs** globally. This programme trains the next generation of industry leaders who can bridge the gap between design thinking and AI technology.

Students will gain a comprehensive understanding of how AI and design can drive innovation, develop applied skills in **coding, algorithms and digital application development**, and explore the **ethical, policy and regulatory dimensions** of AI.

The programme is linked to Loughborough London's **AI and Humanity Research Hub**, providing access to cutting-edge research and industry networks. No prior computing background is required.

## Why Choose This Programme?

- **4th in the UK** for Art and Design (The Times and Sunday Times Good University Guide 2026)
- **Top 50 in the World** for Art and Design (QS World University Rankings by Subject 2026)
- **7th in the UK** for Art and Design (Complete University Guide 2026)
- Study at the heart of the UK's design community and tech scene at the **London campus**
- Linked to the **AI and Humanity Research Hub**
- No computing background required — open to **all discipline backgrounds**
- Full immersion in each topic area through **block teaching**
- Work in teams to develop **AI-driven solutions** to grand challenges
- Explore the **ethical, policy and regulatory dimensions** of AI

## Entry Requirements

- **Academic:** A 2:2 honours degree (or equivalent international qualification) with a minimum of 55% overall, in **any subject area**. No computing background is required.
- **English Language:** IELTS 6.5 overall with 6.0 in each component.

## Programme Structure

The programme consists of **180 credits** delivered through block teaching across two semesters and a summer dissertation period.

### Semester 1 (60 credits — Compulsory)

| Module | Credits |
|--------|---------|
| Design Innovation | 15 |
| Programming Fundamentals | 15 |
| Principles of Artificial Intelligence and Data Analytics | 15 |
| Grand Challenges | 15 |

### Semester 2 (60 credits — Compulsory + Optional)

| Module | Credits |
|--------|---------|
| AI-Driven Design | 15 |
| Design Research Methods | 15 |
| Entrepreneurship | 15 |
| Optional Module (choose one) | 15 |

**Optional Module Options:**

| Module | Credits |
|--------|---------|
| Collaborative Project | 15 |
| Immersive Experience Design | 15 |

### Summer (60 credits)

| Module | Credits |
|--------|---------|
| Dissertation | 60 |

## Teaching & Assessment

The programme features a variety of teaching and learning methods including **lectures, seminars, tutorials, independent study, group work, supervision, practical sessions** and access to **online material**. The programme uses a **block teaching** approach, allowing full immersion in each topic area.

Assessment is through a combination of **essays, reports, presentations, projects** and **examinations**.

## Career Prospects

AI is forecast to become a **€1 trillion industry** over the next decade, creating up to **50 million new jobs** globally. Graduates are prepared for exciting roles at the intersection of design and AI:

- **AI Design Specialist** / AI Design Consultant
- **UX/UI Designer** with AI expertise
- **Digital Innovation Manager**
- **Design Technologist** / Creative Technologist
- **Product Designer** (AI-driven products)
- **AI Strategy Consultant**
- **Service Designer** (AI-enhanced services)
- **Entrepreneur** / Startup Founder

Graduates will possess the **digital and cognitive skills** that are highly sought after by employers across creative, technology and business sectors, including design and branding consultancies, and in-house design and marketing departments.

## Facilities

The programme is based at Loughborough University's **London campus**, located at **Here East** on the Queen Elizabeth Olympic Park — at the heart of the UK's thriving design community and tech scene. Students have access to state-of-the-art **design studios, computing labs with AI development tools, collaborative workspaces**, and the resources of the **AI and Humanity Research Hub**.

## Research

The programme is linked to Loughborough London's **AI and Humanity Research Hub**, which explores the intersection of artificial intelligence and human experience. Students can engage with cutting-edge research in areas such as AI ethics, human-centred AI, and the societal impact of emerging technologies.
