# Design Innovation MA/MSc

## Overview

The Design Innovation MA/MSc programme at Loughborough University London offers a framework for developing design skills and approaches to innovation across a range of disciplines. Students learn about the key approaches and methods for design innovation, develop a range of designing skills and gain experience of design innovation in practice.

Working with leading innovation researchers and designers, students explore new insights, develop mastery of innovation design methods and cultivate an entrepreneurial mindset. The programme combines theoretical understanding with practical application, enabling students to work both individually and in multi-disciplinary teams.

Students develop collaborative behaviour through active learning and teamwork, analysing and evaluating problems and responding to challenges in real time. The programme culminates in a substantial dissertation project that draws on design innovation and evaluation.

### Key Features

- Based at the London campus in the Queen Elizabeth Olympic Park
- You can choose to graduate with either an MA or MSc depending on module choices and dissertation topic
- Develop an entrepreneurial mindset
- A flexible programme with a wide range of optional modules
- Specialist facilities and studio spaces in London

## Entry Requirements

- **Degree:** A 2:2 honours degree (or equivalent international qualification) in a relevant subject.
- **English Language:** IELTS 6.5 overall, with 6.0 in each individual component (Listening, Reading, Writing, Speaking).
- **International qualifications:** The University accepts a wide range of international qualifications.

## Teaching & Assessment

### How You'll Study

- Lectures
- Seminars
- Tutorials
- Independent study
- Group work
- Collaborative projects with external partners

As well as regular timetabled teaching, students have the chance to take part in guest lectures and projects on a range of topics.

### How You'll Be Assessed

You can expect to complete essays and reports of varying lengths, as well as presentations, projects and exams.

## Your Future Career

This programme prepares you for careers across the design, innovation and enterprise sectors. Graduates go on to work for major global organisations and SMEs in roles such as design managers, innovation consultants, brand managers, design researchers and creative directors. Some graduates set up their own businesses or work as freelance design consultants.

The programme equips you with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in roles that require creative thinking, strategic design and innovation management.
