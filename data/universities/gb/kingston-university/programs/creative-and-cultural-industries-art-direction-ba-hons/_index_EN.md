# Creative and Cultural Industries: Art Direction BA (Hons)

> Combine creative thinking with strategic art direction skills. This course prepares you for a career in the creative and cultural industries, focusing on art direction, visual communication and cultural entrepreneurship.

## Overview

BA (Hons) Creative and Cultural Industries: Art Direction at Kingston School of Art is designed for students who want to work at the intersection of creativity and business within the cultural industries. The course develops your ability to think strategically about visual communication while building practical art direction skills.

You will explore how creative ideas are shaped, directed and delivered across a range of media and platforms. The programme covers art direction for advertising, editorial, branding, digital media and cultural events. You will learn to manage creative projects, understand audiences, and develop campaigns that connect with people in meaningful ways.

The course is part of Kingston's Creative and Cultural Industries (CCI) family, which shares a common first year before you specialise in Art Direction. This structure gives you a broad understanding of the creative industries before focusing on your chosen pathway.

### Key Features

- Part of the Creative and Cultural Industries programme family at Kingston School of Art
- Common first year shared across CCI pathways before specialisation
- Focus on art direction for advertising, editorial, branding and digital media
- Develop skills in creative project management and cultural entrepreneurship
- Opportunities for professional placement year (sandwich route)
- Located at Knights Park campus — Kingston School of Art's dedicated creative hub

## Course Structure

### Year 1 — CCI Common Year

Year 1 is shared across all Creative and Cultural Industries pathways. Students build foundational knowledge of the creative industries, develop core creative skills and explore how ideas become cultural products.

| Module | Credits | Type |
|--------|---------|------|
| Creative Journeys | 30 | Core |
| Navigating Industry | 30 | Core |
| The Tools of Writing | 30 | Core |
| Thinking about Ideas | 60 | Core |

### Year 2 — Art Direction Specialism

Year 2 focuses on developing art direction skills alongside creative project management and audience understanding. Students begin to apply their knowledge through live projects and case studies.

| Module | Credits | Type |
|--------|---------|------|
| Art Direction | 30 | Core |
| Creative Project Management | 30 | Core |
| Customer Mindfulness | 30 | Core |
| Live Case Study | 30 | Core |

### Year 3 — Advanced Practice and Major Project

Year 3 deepens art direction practice and culminates in a major self-directed project. Students develop entrepreneurial skills and prepare for professional practice in the creative industries.

| Module | Credits | Type |
|--------|---------|------|
| Art Direction 2 | 30 | Core |
| Culturepreneurship | 30 | Core |
| The Major Project | 60 | Core |
