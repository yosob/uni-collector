## Course Description

Experience Design BDes (Hons) at the University of Dundee's Duncan of Jordanstone College of Art & Design teaches you to design great experiences on and off screen, connect with people, and experiment with new technologies. The programme combines UX/UI design, graphic design, physical computing, connected objects, and speculative design to prepare you for careers as a UX/UI designer, service designer, user researcher, or creative technologist.

You'll have opportunities to draw, design, program, make circuits, 3D print, woodwork, screenprint, throw ceramics, laser cut, cast metals, make films, and prototype apps — finding your medium and identity as a designer.
