# Produktdesign B.A.

## Program Overview

The Bachelor's program in Product Design at FH Potsdam focuses on the design of physical products, materials, and user experiences. Students learn to develop innovative products that combine functionality, aesthetics, and sustainability.

## Study Structure

| Semester | Focus |
|----------|-------|
| 1-2 | Foundational design studies (shared with Communication Design and Interface Design) |
| 3-5 | Specialization in Product Design |
| 6 | Internship (6 months) |
| 7 | Bachelor thesis |

## Key Features

- **Interdisciplinary foundation**: First year shared with all design students
- **Project-based learning**: Strong emphasis on practical projects
- **6-month internship**: Required practical experience
- **Workshop facilities**: Access to prototyping and material workshops

## Focus Areas

- Product design
- Industrial design
- Material design
- Sustainable design
- User-centered design
- Prototyping and model making

## Career Paths

Graduates work in:
- Product design studios
- Industrial design consultancies
- Manufacturing companies
- Design agencies
- Freelance design practice

## WERKSCHAU

Students present their work at the annual WERKSCHAU exhibition, part of the Design Universum events.
