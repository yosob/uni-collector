# Interfacedesign B.A.

## Program Overview

The Bachelor's program in Interface Design at FH Potsdam is characterized by a very high proportion of practical work. The Interface Workshop and UX – User Experience Lab are located on campus.

## Study Structure

| Semester | Focus |
|----------|-------|
| 1-2 | Foundational design studies (shared with Communication Design and Product Design) |
| 3-5 | Specialization in Interface Design |
| 6 | Internship (6 months, in Germany or abroad) |
| 7 | Bachelor thesis |

## Key Features

- **High practical content**: Extensive hands-on learning
- **Dedicated facilities**: Interface Workshop and UX Lab on campus
- **6-month internship**: Required practical experience (domestic or international)
- **Interdisciplinary foundation**: First year shared with all design students
- **Create information spaces**: Learn to design digital experiences

## Focus Areas

- User Experience (UX) Design
- User Interface (UI) Design
- Interaction Design
- Human-Computer Interaction
- Digital product design
- Web and app design
- Information architecture

## Facilities

- **Interface-Werkstatt**: Workshop for prototyping and testing
- **UX – User Experience Lab**: Research and usability testing facility

## Research

The Interaction Design Lab (IDL) is the research facility of the Bachelor's degree programme in Interface Design, offering opportunities for research-based learning.

## Career Paths

Graduates work as:
- UX designers
- UI designers
- Interaction designers
- Product designers (digital)
- UX researchers
- Freelance designers

## WERKSCHAU

Students present their work at the annual WERKSCHAU exhibition.
