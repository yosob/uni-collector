# Arts Management and Cultural Work B.A.

## Program Overview

The Bachelor's program in Arts Management and Cultural Work (Kulturarbeit) at FH Potsdam is a generalist program that connects cultural, media, and social science topics with business management and management theory. It prepares students for diverse professional fields in cultural work and cultural management.

## Study Structure

| Semester | Focus |
|----------|-------|
| 1-2 | Foundations: cultural studies, media studies, social sciences |
| 3-4 | Management basics: business administration, marketing, project management |
| 5-6 | Specialization and practical projects |
| 7-8 | Advanced modules and Bachelor thesis |

## Key Features

- **Generalist orientation**: Broad education across culture, media, and management
- **Interdisciplinary**: Combines humanities, social sciences, and business
- **High practical content**: Project-based learning with real cultural projects
- **Wide career field**: Cultural institutions, festivals, media, education
- **Winter semester only**: Starts only in winter semester

## Focus Areas

- Cultural and humanities foundations
- Media studies and media practice
- Social science methods
- Business administration
- Cultural management and marketing
- Project management
- Cultural education and outreach
- Practical project work

## Admission

- General higher education entrance qualification (Abitur)
- Evidence of cultural/practical experience advantageous
- Application through FH Potsdam portal

## Career Paths

Graduates work in:
- Cultural management and administration
- Festival and event organization
- Cultural education and outreach
- Cultural policy and consulting
- Media and public relations
- Cultural institutions (theaters, museums, galleries)
- Community cultural centers
