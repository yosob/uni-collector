# European Media Studies M.A.

## Program Overview

The Master's program in European Media Studies (Europäische Medienwissenschaft) is a research-oriented consecutive program jointly offered by FH Potsdam and the University of Potsdam. It deepens the study of media theory, digital cultures, and European media landscapes.

## Study Structure

| Semester | Focus |
|----------|-------|
| 1 | Advanced media theory and research methods |
| 2 | Specialization modules and research projects |
| 3 | Master project and research colloquium |
| 4 | Master thesis |

## Key Features

- **Joint degree**: Offered by FH Potsdam and Universität Potsdam
- **Research-oriented**: Strong emphasis on academic research and theory
- **Interdisciplinary**: Bridges media studies, cultural studies, and design
- **European focus**: Emphasis on European media landscapes
- **Bilingual elements**: Some courses offered in English

## Focus Areas

- Advanced media theory
- Media philosophy and aesthetics
- Digital media cultures
- Visual culture and analysis
- European media history
- Media and society
- Cultural analysis

## Admission

- Bachelor's degree in Media Studies, Cultural Studies, or related field
- Joint application through FH Potsdam and Universität Potsdam
- No portfolio required

## Career Paths

Graduates work in:
- Academic research and teaching
- Media and cultural journalism
- Cultural institutions and museums
- Media consulting and strategy
- Publishing and editorial work
- Digital media and cultural policy
