# Design M.A.

## Program Overview

The Master's program in Design at FH Potsdam is a research-oriented, consecutive program that builds on the three Bachelor programs in Communication Design, Interface Design, and Product Design. It offers students the opportunity to deepen their design practice and develop individual design projects with a strong research focus.

## Study Structure

| Semester | Focus |
|----------|-------|
| 1 | Advanced design methods and research approaches |
| 2 | Project development and specialization |
| 3 | Master project (individual focus) |
| 4 | Master thesis |

## Key Features

- **Research-oriented**: Strong emphasis on design research methodologies
- **Interdisciplinary**: Open to graduates from all three design Bachelor programs
- **Individual focus**: Students develop their own project direction
- **Project-based learning**: Hands-on approach with real-world design challenges
- **Strategic thinking**: Combines design practice with strategic and conceptual skills

## Focus Areas

- Communication Design
- Interface Design / UX Design
- Product Design
- Design Research
- Strategic Design
- Speculative Design
- Social Design
- Design Thinking

## Admission

- Bachelor's degree in Design or related field (210 ECTS, or 180 ECTS with additional professional experience)
- Portfolio demonstrating advanced design competencies
- Aptitude assessment process

## Career Paths

Graduates work in:
- Design research and strategy
- Senior design positions
- Design consultancies
- Creative direction
- Academic research and teaching
- Freelance design practice
- Design entrepreneurship
