# European Media Studies B.A.

## Program Overview

The Bachelor's program in European Media Studies (Europäische Medienwissenschaft) is a joint degree program offered by FH Potsdam and the University of Potsdam (Universität Potsdam). It combines theoretical reflection with practical engagement with media, covering the breadth of European media cultures.

## Study Structure

| Semester | Focus |
|----------|-------|
| 1-2 | Foundations in media theory, media history, and cultural studies |
| 3-4 | Specialization in chosen media areas (film, digital media, visual culture) |
| 5 | Practical project or internship |
| 6 | Bachelor thesis |

## Key Features

- **Joint degree**: Offered by FH Potsdam and Universität Potsdam
- **Interdisciplinary**: Bridges humanities, social sciences, and design
- **European focus**: Emphasis on European media landscapes and cultures
- **Theory and practice**: Combines analytical thinking with hands-on media work
- **Bilingual elements**: Some courses offered in English

## Focus Areas

- Media theory and philosophy
- Media history
- Film studies
- Digital media culture
- Visual culture and aesthetics
- European cultural studies
- Media and society

## Admission

- General higher education entrance qualification (Abitur)
- Joint application through FH Potsdam and Universität Potsdam
- No portfolio required

## Career Paths

Graduates work in:
- Media and cultural journalism
- Film and television production
- Cultural institutions and museums
- Media education and research
- Public relations and communication
- Publishing houses
- Digital media companies
