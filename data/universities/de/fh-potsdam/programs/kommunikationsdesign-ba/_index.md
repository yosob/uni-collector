# Kommunikationsdesign B.A.

## Program Overview

The Bachelor's program in Communication Design at FH Potsdam teaches you to creatively, clearly, and effectively design information and messages – from individual characters to complex campaigns.

## Study Structure

| Semester | Focus |
|----------|-------|
| 1-2 | Foundational design studies (shared with Interface Design and Product Design) |
| 3-5 | Specialization in Communication Design |
| 6 | Internship (6 months) |
| 7 | Bachelor thesis |

## Key Features

- **Interdisciplinary foundation**: First year shared with all design students
- **Project-based learning**: Strong emphasis on practical projects
- **6-month internship**: Required practical experience
- **Close industry ties**: Located in Babelsberg media district

## Focus Areas

- Graphic design and typography
- Visual communication
- Brand design and corporate identity
- Editorial design
- Digital design and web
- Advertising and campaign design

## Career Paths

Graduates work in:
- Advertising agencies
- Design studios
- Publishing houses
- Corporate communications
- Media companies
- Freelance design practice

## WERKSCHAU

Students present their work at the annual WERKSCHAU exhibition, part of the Design Universum events.

## Related Programs

- Interface Design (B.A.)
- Product Design (B.A.)
- Design (M.A.) - consecutive master's program
