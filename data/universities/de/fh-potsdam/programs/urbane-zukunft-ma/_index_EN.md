# Urban Futures M.A.

## Program Overview

The Master's program in Urban Futures (Urbane Zukunft) at FH Potsdam offers students from diverse academic backgrounds the opportunity for an inter- and transdisciplinary study of urban transformation processes. Students acquire the professional and interdisciplinary competencies needed for independent, scientific, creative, and interdisciplinary work.

## Study Structure

| Semester | Focus |
|----------|-------|
| 1 | Urban transformation fundamentals and research methods |
| 2 | Specialization and interdisciplinary projects |
| 3 | Master project and research colloquium |
| 4 | Master thesis |

## Key Features

- **Inter- and transdisciplinary**: Open to students from all academic fields
- **Creative + scientific**: Combines creative and research-oriented working methods
- **Urban transformation focus**: Addresses real urban challenges
- **Sustainability and resilience**: Focus on sustainable urban futures
- **Practice-oriented**: Project work with real urban contexts

## Focus Areas

- Urban transformation processes
- Urban development and planning
- Sustainability and resilience
- Participation and governance
- Interdisciplinary research methods
- Creative and design approaches
- Project work and case studies
- Critical reflection on urban futures

## Admission

- First professional university degree (Bachelor or Diplom) in any field
- Proof of aptitude for the program
- Application through FH Potsdam portal

## Career Paths

Graduates work in:
- Urban development and city planning
- Urban design and strategic consulting
- Sustainability management
- Research and teaching
- Public administration and policy consulting
- NGOs and community organizations
- Urban innovation labs
