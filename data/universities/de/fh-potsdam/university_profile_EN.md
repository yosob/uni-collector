# Potsdam University of Applied Sciences — University Profile

## University Overview

**Potsdam University of Applied Sciences (FH Potsdam)** is a public university of applied sciences in Brandenburg, founded in 1991 with approximately 3,700 students. The Department of Design is among the most prestigious in Germany, offering Bachelor's programs in Communication Design, Interface Design, and Product Design, as well as a consecutive Master's program in Design. The university is characterized by a close integration of theory and practice.

- **Founded:** 1991
- **Location:** Potsdam, Brandenburg, Germany
- **Type:** University of Applied Sciences (public)
- **Campus:** Campus Griebnitzsee / Potsdam-Babelsberg
- **Website:** https://www.fh-potsdam.de

## Program Details

### Communication Design (BA)

7-semester Bachelor program with foundational design studies in the first two semesters (shared with Interface Design and Product Design). Specialization from semester 3 in graphic design, typography, visual communication, brand design, and editorial design. 6-month internship required. Students present their work at the annual WERKSCHAU exhibition.

### Interface Design (BA)

7-semester Bachelor program with a very high proportion of practical work. Dedicated Interface Workshop and UX – User Experience Lab on campus. Specialization in UX/UI design, interaction design, human-computer interaction, and digital product design. 6-month internship (in Germany or abroad). Research opportunities at the Interaction Design Lab (IDL).

### Product Design (BA)

7-semester Bachelor program focusing on physical products, materials, and user experiences. Combines functionality, aesthetics, and sustainability. Foundational design education shared with Communication Design and Interface Design. 6-month internship required.

### Design (MA)

4-semester research-oriented consecutive Master program building on the three Bachelor design programs. Students choose their own focus and develop individual design projects with a strong research emphasis. Focus areas: design research, strategic design, speculative design, social design.

### European Media Studies (BA/MA)

Joint degree program offered by FH Potsdam and the University of Potsdam. Combines media theory, media history, and practical media skills. Interdisciplinary approach bridging humanities and design. Some courses offered in English.

### Conservation and Restoration (BA/MA)

Bachelor: 8-semester program with three fields of study (Wood, Stone, Wall Painting). Combines scientific analysis, traditional craft techniques, and art historical knowledge. Master: 4-semester research-oriented program for specialization.

### Arts Management and Cultural Work (BA)

8-semester generalist program combining cultural studies, media studies, social sciences, and business management. Prepares students for diverse roles in cultural work and cultural management.

### Urban Futures (MA)

4-semester inter- and transdisciplinary Master program open to students from all academic backgrounds. Addresses urban transformation processes with focus on sustainability, resilience, and creative urban strategies.

## Application Process

1. **Online application:** via FH Potsdam portal
2. **Deadlines:** July 15 for winter semester (application period starts in May)
3. **Documents:** Higher education entrance qualification, portfolio (for design programs), CV
4. **Aptitude test:** Design programs require portfolio review + aptitude assessment
5. **Language requirement:** German DSH-2 / TestDaF TDN 4 (CEFR C1) for international applicants

## Tuition Fees

- **Semester fee:** approx. €300 per semester (incl. semester ticket)
- **No tuition fees** for EU/EEA students (Brandenburg does not charge tuition)

## Key Facilities

- **Interface Workshop**: Prototyping and testing workshop
- **UX – User Experience Lab**: Research and usability testing facility
- **Interaction Design Lab (IDL)**: Research facility
- **Design Workshops**: Printing workshop, photo lab, media lab
- **Conservation Workshops**: Wood, stone, wall painting
- **Stadtlabor (City Lab)**: For the Urban Futures program
