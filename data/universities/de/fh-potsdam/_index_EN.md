# Potsdam University of Applied Sciences

## Overview

Potsdam University of Applied Sciences (FH Potsdam) was founded in 1991 and has approximately 3,700 students. It is one of the most important universities in Brandenburg and is characterized by a close integration of theory and practice.

FH Potsdam comprises five departments:
1. **Department of Design** — Communication, Interface, Product, Media
2. **Department of CITY | BUILDING | CULTURE** — Architecture, Conservation, Cultural Work, Urban Studies
3. **Department of Information Sciences** — Library, Information, Data Science
4. **Department of Social and Educational Sciences** — Education, Social Work
5. **Department of Civil Engineering** — Engineering Sciences

## Design and Art-Related Programs

### Department of Design
- **Communication Design (B.A.)** — Visual communication, typography, editorial design
- **Interface Design (B.A.)** — UX/UI design, interaction design, digital products
- **Product Design (B.A.)** — Industrial design, materials, form, sustainability
- **Design (M.A.)** — Master Studio for Design as Strategy (Fast-Track)
- **European Media Studies (B.A.)** — Cooperation with University of Potsdam
- **European Media Studies (M.A.)** — Cooperation with University of Potsdam

### Department of CITY | BUILDING | CULTURE
- **Conservation and Restoration (B.A.)** — Wood, stone, mural painting
- **Conservation and Restoration (M.A.)** — Specialization
- **Arts Management and Cultural Work (B.A.)** — Cultural management and cultural education
- **Urban Futures (M.A.)** — Urban transformation processes
