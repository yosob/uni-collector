# University of Applied Sciences Potsdam

The University of Applied Sciences Potsdam (FHP) was founded in 1991 and comprises five departments offering more than 30 study programs in fields such as design, architecture, city planning, social sciences, and IT. Located in the Babelsberg media district of Potsdam, the university maintains close ties to the region's creative and media industries. FHP is known for its interdisciplinary approach and strong emphasis on project-based learning.
