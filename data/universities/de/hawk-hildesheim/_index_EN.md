# HAWK University of Applied Sciences and Arts

## Overview

The HAWK University of Applied Sciences and Arts is a university of applied sciences with campuses in Hildesheim, Holzminden, and Göttingen. The Faculty of Design at the Hildesheim campus is one of the most renowned design faculties in Germany, offering an exceptionally broad range of programs.

## Location

The Faculty of Design is located at the Hildesheim campus, specifically in the historic Hubsmühlen building and other modern facilities. Hildesheim is in Lower Saxony, approximately 30 km southeast of Hanover.

## Faculty of Design

The Faculty of Design offers programs in the following areas:
- **Visual Communication**: Advertising, Branding, Graphic Design, Communication Design
- **Product and Industrial Design**: Product Design, Digital Environments
- **Spatial Design**: Interior Architecture, Lighting Design, Color Design
- **Craft and Art**: Metal Design
- **Open Design**: BA and MA Gestaltung

## Special Features

- **Broad Range of Disciplines**: From Visual Communication to Product Design, Interior Architecture, and Metal Design
- **Practice-Oriented**: Close integration of theory and practice
- **Craft Tradition**: Unique programs such as Metal Design and Color Design
- **Historical Roots**: Design education in Hildesheim has a long tradition
