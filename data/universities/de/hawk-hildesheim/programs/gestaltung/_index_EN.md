# Design (Gestaltung)

The Design (Gestaltung) program at HAWK Hildesheim offers a unique interdisciplinary design education with **nine competence fields**. This is the umbrella entry covering both degree levels (BA and MA).

> **Detailed entries:** [BA Design](ba-gestaltung/_index_EN.md) | [MA Design](ma-gestaltung/_index_EN.md)

## Course Content

### Bachelor of Arts (B.A.) — 6 Semesters, 180 ECTS

After one semester of foundation studies, students can choose one of nine competence fields and acquire interdisciplinary knowledge from other areas:

- **Advertising Design** — Advertising, campaign development
- **Branding Design** — Brand communication, corporate identity
- **Digital Environments** — Digital media, interactive applications, UX/UI
- **Colour Design** — Colour design, surface design, colour systems
- **Graphic Design** — Visual communication, editorial design, typography
- **Interior Architecture** — Spatial design, exhibition design
- **Metal Design & Jewellery** — Craft metalworking, jewellery
- **Lighting Design** — Lighting planning, light art, architectural lighting
- **Product Design** — Industrial design, product development, prototyping

The focus is on conception, creation, theory and technologies, as well as teamwork and project work.

### Master of Arts (M.A.) — 4 Semesters, 120 ECTS

The Master's program enables an in-depth and reflective engagement with the spectrum of design. The focus areas are:

- **Integrative Design**
- **Team-oriented Leadership**
- **Strategic Thinking**
- **Design-oriented Research**

**Courses:** Design management, design science and futures research, media theory, communication science and strategies, cultural history, social sciences, design marketing, design philosophy, team and strategy, design thinking.

## Application

### Requirements
- **Bachelor:** Higher education entrance qualification + artistic aptitude test
- **Master:** First university degree (at least 180 ECTS) + selection interview

### Deadlines
- Winter semester: July 15
- Summer semester: January 15
- **Portfolio submission (BA):** February 1 – April 30 (WiSe) / August 1 – October 30 (SoSe)
