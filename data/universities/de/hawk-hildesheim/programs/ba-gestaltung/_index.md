# BA Gestaltung (Design) — Bachelor of Arts

## Overview

The **Bachelor of Arts in Gestaltung (Design)** at HAWK's Fakultät Gestaltung in Hildesheim is a comprehensive design program offering 9 unique competence fields. It is one of the broadest design programs in Germany, combining traditional crafts with digital innovation.

## Key Facts

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| Degree | Bachelor of Arts (B.A.) |
| Duration | 6 Semesters (180 ECTS) |
| Start | Winter & Summer semester |

| Language | German |
| Admission | Restricted (NC + aptitude test) |
| Portfolio | Required (künstlerische Befähigungsprüfung) |

## Competence Fields (Kompetenzfelder)

Students choose from **9 specialization areas** starting from semester 3:

1. **Advertising Design (Werbegestaltung)** — Campaign development, copywriting, media planning
2. **Branding Design** — Brand strategy, corporate identity, brand communication
3. **Digital Environments** — UX/UI design, interactive media, web development
4. **Colour Design (Farbdesign)** — Colour theory, colour in architecture and design, colour systems
5. **Graphic Design (Grafikdesign)** — Typography, editorial design, poster design, illustration
6. **Interior Architecture (Innenarchitektur)** — Spatial design, material concepts, interior planning
7. **Lighting Design (Lichtdesign)** — Architectural lighting, light art, lighting concepts
8. **Metal Design & Jewellery (Metallgestaltung & Schmuck)** — Jewellery design, metalworking, objets d'art
9. **Product Design (Produktdesign)** — Industrial design, product development, prototyping

## Curriculum Structure

- **Semesters 1–2:** Foundation studies — design fundamentals, drawing, colour theory, materials, digital tools
- **Semesters 3–5:** Specialization in chosen competence field(s), interdisciplinary projects
- **Semester 6:** Bachelor thesis project

## Application & Admission

### Application Deadlines
- **Winter semester:** July 15
- **Summer semester:** January 15

### Artistic Aptitude Test (künstlerische Befähigungsprüfung)
- **Portfolio submission period (WiSe):** February 1 – April 30
- **Portfolio submission period (SoSe):** August 1 – October 30
- The test evaluates artistic talent and design aptitude

### Requirements
- Higher education entrance qualification (Abitur or equivalent)
- Artistic aptitude test (portfolio + practical examination)
- Pre-study internship (8 weeks, recommended)
