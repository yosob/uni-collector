# Colour Design

> **Kompetenzfeld** within [BA Gestaltung](../ba-gestaltung/_index_EN.md) / [MA Gestaltung](../ma-gestaltung/_index_EN.md)

The **Colour Design** kompetenzfeld is a specialization within the interdisciplinary Gestaltung program at HAWK Hildesheim.

## Philosophy and Approach

> "Colour is more than just a visual feature — it influences mood, perception, and behaviour."

Colour is omnipresent and one of the most powerful design tools. In the Colour Design kompetenzfeld, colour is understood as a creative, functional, and psychological instrument.

This kompetenzfeld offers a unique study programme in Germany that combines artistic colour design with scientific colour theory and practical application.

## Course Content

The kompetenzfeld teaches skills in:

- **Colour Design** — Artistic and design application of colour
- **Surface Design** — Designing surfaces and materiality
- **Colour Systems and Theory** — Systematic colour theory (NCS, RAL, Pantone etc.)
- **Colour Psychology** — The effect of colours on people
- **Material Colour** — Colouring of materials and pigments
- **Light and Colour** — Interaction of light and coloured surfaces
- **Spatial Colour** — Colour concepts for interior and exterior spaces
- **Architectural Colour Concepts** — Architecture-related colour planning

## Professors

- **Prof. Axel Buther** — Colour Design
- **Prof. Dr. Stefanie Wettberg** — Colour Design / Surface Design

## Workshops and Labs

- **Colour Design Workshops** — Comprehensive colour inventory with pigments, paints, and materials
- **Experimental Technologies Lab (ETLab)** — Digital methods in colour development
- **Surface/Lacquer Workshop** — Professional lacquering techniques

## Position within the Program

Colour Design is offered as one of nine kompetenzfeld within the Gestaltung program. After one semester of foundation courses, students choose their main kompetenzfeld and can additionally take courses from other areas.

Possible combinations with related kompetenzfeld:
- Interior Architecture (Innenarchitektur)
- Product Design (Produktdesign)
- Lighting Design (Lichtdesign)

## Application

Application is through the **Gestaltung** program. See [BA Gestaltung](../ba-gestaltung/_index_EN.md) for details.
