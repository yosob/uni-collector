# Advertising Design

> **Kompetenzfeld** within [BA Gestaltung](../ba-gestaltung/_index_EN.md) / [MA Gestaltung](../ma-gestaltung/_index_EN.md)

The **Advertising Design** kompetenzfeld is a specialization within the interdisciplinary Gestaltung program at HAWK Hildesheim.

## Course Content

> "Inspiring people through ideas and convincing them of brands, products, and services — that is your task in Advertising Design." — Prof. Mathias Rebmann

The kompetenzfeld teaches skills in:

- **Advertising Campaigns** — Development of holistic campaign concepts
- **Poster Design** — Print and outdoor advertising
- **Advertisements and Flyers** — Classic print advertising
- **Film** — TV, cinema, and internet commercials
- **Online Platforms** — Digital advertising formats
- **Social Media Campaigns** — Facebook, Instagram, TikTok and more
- **Events and Activations** — Live marketing and experiential design

Good advertising is based on a good idea and is not limited to one medium — it happens everywhere: on smartphones, in newspapers, on the streets, on TV, in cinemas, on social networks, on clothing.

## Career Prospects

- Advertising agencies
- Start-ups
- Self-employment
- Freelance work

## Awards and Achievements

The Advertising Design kompetenzfeld is one of the most successful design programs in Germany:

- **1st Place ADC Creative Ranking** — Most creative German university (Art Directors Club)
- **DDC Gold Award** — Coalition agreement as HAWK magazine project
- **ADCE Student Awards** — 3 prizes at the European Student Awards
- **GWA Junior Agency Award** — Finalist in the practical test
- Regularly numerous ADC Nails and awards

## Professors

- **Prof. Mathias Rebmann** — Advertising Design
- **Prof. Sabine Cole** — Advertising Design (since SS 2022)

## Position within the Program

Advertising Design is offered as one of nine kompetenzfeld within the Gestaltung program. After one semester of foundation courses, students choose their main kompetenzfeld and can additionally take courses from other areas.

Possible combinations with related kompetenzfeld:
- Branding Design
- Digital Environments
- Graphic Design (Grafikdesign)

## Application

Application is through the **Gestaltung** program. See [BA Gestaltung](../ba-gestaltung/_index_EN.md) for details.
