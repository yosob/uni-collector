# Digital Environments

> **Kompetenzfeld** within [BA Gestaltung](../ba-gestaltung/_index_EN.md) / [MA Gestaltung](../ma-gestaltung/_index_EN.md)

The **Digital Environments** kompetenzfeld is a specialization within the interdisciplinary Gestaltung program at HAWK Hildesheim.

## Course Content

> "Digital Environments shapes the interaction between humans and the digital world — from user interfaces to immersive experiences."

The kompetenzfeld teaches skills in:

- **UX/UI Design** — User Experience and User Interface Design
- **Interaction Design** — Design of interactive systems
- **Web Design** — Responsive websites and web applications
- **App Design** — Mobile applications for iOS and Android
- **Digital Interfaces** — Touchscreens, wearables, IoT
- **Augmented & Virtual Reality** — Immersive technologies
- **Service Design** — Digital services and processes
- **Prototyping** — Rapid prototyping and user testing

## Workshops and Labs

- **Interaction Design Lab** — Where science and practice meet
- **Experimental Technologies Lab (ETLab)** — Digital methods in design and manufacturing
- **DTP Workshop** — Design, illustration, photography, text, layout

## Position within the Program

Digital Environments is offered as one of nine kompetenzfeld within the Gestaltung program. After one semester of foundation courses, students choose their main kompetenzfeld and can additionally take courses from other areas.

Possible combinations with related kompetenzfeld:
- Advertising Design
- Branding Design
- Graphic Design (Grafikdesign)

## Application

Application is through the **Gestaltung** program. See [BA Gestaltung](../ba-gestaltung/_index_EN.md) for details.
