# Graphic Design

> **Kompetenzfeld** within [BA Gestaltung](../ba-gestaltung/_index_EN.md) / [MA Gestaltung](../ma-gestaltung/_index_EN.md)

The **Graphic Design** kompetenzfeld is a specialization within the interdisciplinary Gestaltung program at HAWK Hildesheim.

## Philosophy and Approach

> "Good design begins with looking and listening carefully — it communicates visually what language alone cannot express."

Graphic design encompasses the full spectrum of visual communication: from the idea through conception to the finished publication. The kompetenzfeld combines traditional craftsmanship (lead typesetting, letterpress, screen printing) with contemporary digital methods.

## Course Content

The kompetenzfeld offers a broad range:

- **Visual Communication** — Designing messages and their visual implementation
- **Typography** — Type design and typesetting, micro- and macro-typography
- **Editorial Design** — Book and magazine design
- **Corporate Design** — Development of visual identities
- **Digital Media** — Screen design, interactive applications
- **Illustration** — Drawing and painting implementation
- **Poster Design** — Poster design, large format
- **Information Design** — Information visualization and infographics

## Professors

- **Prof. André Heers** — Graphic Design / Typography
- **Prof. Markus Rath** — Graphic Design / Illustration

## Workshops and Labs

- **Artistic Print Workshops** — Relief printing, intaglio, screen printing
- **DTP Workshop** — Design, illustration, photography, text, layout
- **Experimental Technologies Lab (ETLab)** — Digital methods

## Awards

- Regular awards at **100 Best Posters**
- **Joseph Binder Award**
- Participation in international poster biennials

## Position within the Program

Graphic Design is offered as one of nine kompetenzfeld within the Gestaltung program. After one semester of foundation courses, students choose their main kompetenzfeld and can additionally take courses from other areas.

Possible combinations with related kompetenzfeld:
- Advertising Design
- Branding Design
- Digital Environments

## Application

Application is through the **Gestaltung** program. See [BA Gestaltung](../ba-gestaltung/_index_EN.md) for details.
