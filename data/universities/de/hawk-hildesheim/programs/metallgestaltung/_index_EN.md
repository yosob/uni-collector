# Metal Design & Jewellery

> **Kompetenzfeld** within [BA Gestaltung](../ba-gestaltung/_index_EN.md) / [MA Gestaltung](../ma-gestaltung/_index_EN.md)

The **Metal Design & Jewellery** kompetenzfeld is a specialization within the interdisciplinary Gestaltung program at HAWK Hildesheim.

## Course Content

- **Jewellery Design** — Rings, pendants, brooches, necklaces, headpieces
- **Metal Design** — Sculptures, spatial objects, chandeliers, functional objects
- **Object Design** — Vessels, cutlery, home accessories
- **Body-related Objects** — Jewellery as artistic expression
- **Material Iconology Design** — Content engagement with materials
- **Experimental Jewellery** — Border areas between jewellery, sculpture, and installation

## Materials and Techniques

- **Precious Metals** — Silver, gold, platinum
- **Non-ferrous Metals** — Copper, brass, bronze, aluminium
- **Steel and Stainless Steel** — Forging, welding, polishing
- **Patination and Enamel** — Surface design
- **Mixed Media** — Combination with wood, glass, textiles, paper, natural materials
- **Casting Techniques** — Sand casting, lost-wax casting

## Workshops and Labs

- **Metal Workshops** — Comprehensive equipment for all metalworking techniques
- **Foundry** — Sand casting and precision casting
- **Experimental Technologies Lab (ETLab)** — Digital methods

## Position within the Program

Metal Design & Jewellery is offered as one of nine kompetenzfeld within the Gestaltung program.

Possible combinations with related kompetenzfeld:
- Product Design (Produktdesign)
- Colour Design (Farbdesign)
- Interior Architecture (Innenarchitektur)

## Application

Application is through the **Gestaltung** program. See [BA Gestaltung](../ba-gestaltung/_index_EN.md) for details.
