# Lighting Design

> **Kompetenzfeld** within [BA Gestaltung](../ba-gestaltung/_index_EN.md) / [MA Gestaltung](../ma-gestaltung/_index_EN.md)

The **Lighting Design** kompetenzfeld is a specialization within the interdisciplinary Gestaltung program at HAWK Hildesheim.

## Philosophy

> "With daylight and artificial lighting, we shape the atmosphere, quality of stay, and sustainability of spaces and cities." — Prof. Dr. Cornelia Moosmann

Light is a fundamental component of every construction project in architecture, urban spaces, and events.

## Course Content

The Lighting Design program is interdisciplinary. In addition to skills in lighting technology and design theory, important competencies for later professional life are gathered in joint design courses with interior architects, colour designers, and product designers.

Focus areas:

- **Lighting Planning** — Planning of lighting systems
- **Light Design** — Artistic and creative light application
- **Daylight Design** — Utilization of natural light
- **Artificial Lighting** — Technologies and design
- **Light and Space** — Interplay of light, space, and people
- **Light Art** — Installative and artistic works
- **Sustainable Lighting Concepts** — Environmentally friendly lighting
- **Architectural Lighting** — Lighting concepts for buildings and urban spaces

## Certification and Professional Recognition

HAWK Lighting Design enables registration as a **European Lighting Expert (ELE)**.

HAWK is a **partner university of PLDC** (Professional Lighting Design Convention).

## Professors

- **Prof. Dr. Cornelia Moosmann** — Lighting Design (since SS 2020)
- **Prof. Dr.-Ing. Paul Walter Schmits-Reinecke** — Lighting Design

## Workshops and Labs

- **Lighting Lab** — Professional light measurement and simulation
- **Space Lab** — Experimental spatial design with light
- **Artificial Sky** — Daylight simulation

## Position within the Program

Lighting Design is offered as one of nine kompetenzfeld within the Gestaltung program.

Possible combinations with related kompetenzfeld:
- Interior Architecture (Innenarchitektur)
- Colour Design (Farbdesign)
- Product Design (Produktdesign)

## Application

Application is through the **Gestaltung** program. See [BA Gestaltung](../ba-gestaltung/_index_EN.md) for details.
