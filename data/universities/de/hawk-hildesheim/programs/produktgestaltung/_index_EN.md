# Product Design

> **Kompetenzfeld** within [BA Gestaltung](../ba-gestaltung/_index_EN.md) / [MA Gestaltung](../ma-gestaltung/_index_EN.md)

The **Product Design** kompetenzfeld is a specialization within the interdisciplinary Gestaltung program at HAWK Hildesheim.

## Philosophy and Approach

Those who choose to study product design are open to new topics, innovative developments, and the needs of users. Product design is present in our everyday lives like hardly any other professional field and therefore bears special responsibility towards the environment, engagement with materials, and their sustainability or recyclability.

## Course Content

- **Industrial Design** — Development of serial products
- **Product Development** — From concept to finished product
- **Material Design** — Material research and application
- **Sustainable Design** — Ecological and social responsibility
- **Prototyping** — Craft and digital fabrication
- **User-Centered Design** — User-oriented design
- **Design Thinking** — Innovation methodology
- **Digital Fabrication** — 3D printing, CNC, laser cutting

## Professors

- **Prof. Hanna-Lisette Wiesener** — Product Design (Design, Technology and Social Responsibility)
- **Prof. Julian Witte** — Product Design (Design meets Strategy)

## Workshops and Labs

- **Design Model Making** — From idea to model
- **Rapid Prototyping Lab** — 3D printing and digital fabrication
- **Experimental Technologies Lab (ETLab)** — Digital methods
- **Spatial and Model Development Workshop** — Professional woodworking
- **Surface/Lacquer Workshop** — Professional lacquering techniques

## Awards

- Regular participation in **German Design Graduates (GDG)**
- **SaloneSatellite** at Milan Furniture Fair
- **New European Bauhaus Awards** — 1st Place
- **Dutch Design Week** — Regular participation

## Position within the Program

Product Design is offered as one of nine kompetenzfeld within the Gestaltung program.

Possible combinations:
- Metal Design & Jewellery (Metallgestaltung & Schmuck)
- Colour Design (Farbdesign)
- Interior Architecture (Innenarchitektur)
- Lighting Design (Lichtdesign)

## Application

Application is through the **Gestaltung** program. See [BA Gestaltung](../ba-gestaltung/_index_EN.md) for details.
