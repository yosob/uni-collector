# Branding Design

> **Kompetenzfeld** within [BA Gestaltung](../ba-gestaltung/_index_EN.md) / [MA Gestaltung](../ma-gestaltung/_index_EN.md)

The **Branding Design** kompetenzfeld is a specialization within the interdisciplinary Gestaltung program at HAWK Hildesheim.

## Course Content

> "A brand is a promise. And design is the way this promise becomes visible, audible, and tangible."

The kompetenzfeld teaches skills in:

- **Brand Identity** — Development of holistic brand images
- **Corporate Identity** — Building and maintaining corporate identities
- **Brand Strategy** — Strategic positioning of brands
- **Logo Design** — Creation of brand marks
- **Visual Systems** — Building design systems and style guides
- **Packaging Design** — Package design as brand ambassador
- **Touchpoint Design** — Designing all brand touchpoints
- **Brand Experience** — Immersive brand experiences

## Position within the Program

Branding Design is offered as one of nine kompetenzfeld within the Gestaltung program. After one semester of foundation courses, students choose their main kompetenzfeld and can additionally take courses from other areas.

Possible combinations with related kompetenzfeld:
- Advertising Design
- Graphic Design (Grafikdesign)
- Digital Environments
- Product Design (Produktdesign)

## Application

Application is through the **Gestaltung** program. See [BA Gestaltung](../ba-gestaltung/_index_EN.md) for details.
