# MA Gestaltung (Design) — Master of Arts

## Overview

The **Master of Arts in Gestaltung (Design)** at HAWK's Fakultät Gestaltung is an advanced, research-oriented design program focused on **Integrative Design**. It builds on the faculty's 9 competence fields and emphasizes strategic thinking, design leadership, and interdisciplinary collaboration.

## Key Facts

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| Degree | Master of Arts (M.A.) |
| Duration | 4 Semesters (120 ECTS) |
| Start | Winter & Summer semester |
| Location | Hildesheim, Fakultät Gestaltung |
| Language | German |
| Admission | Restricted (NC + aptitude assessment) |
| Portfolio | Required (work samples + aptitude interview) |

## Focus Areas

The MA Gestaltung program centers on **Integrative/Integrated Design**, allowing students to:
- Combine multiple competence fields in individual study paths
- Develop design strategy and management skills
- Engage in design-oriented research
- Lead interdisciplinary design projects

### Available Competence Fields
All 9 BA competence fields are also available at MA level:
Advertising Design, Branding Design, Digital Environments, Colour Design, Graphic Design, Interior Architecture, Lighting Design, Metal Design & Jewellery, Product Design

## Curriculum Structure

- **Semesters 1–2:** Advanced design projects, research methods, integrative design concepts
- **Semester 3:** Master project preparation, specialization
- **Semester 4:** Master thesis

## Application & Admission

### Application Deadlines
- **Winter semester:** July 15
- **Summer semester:** January 15

### Selection Procedure (Auswahlverfahren)
The admission is based on a points system (max 160 points):
| Criterion | Max Points |
|-----------|------------|
| Final Bachelor grade | 90 |
| Aptitude assessment interview | 60 |
| Professional design experience | 10 |
| **Total** | **160** |

### Requirements
- Completed Bachelor's degree (B.A., at least 180 ECTS in design or related field)
- Aptitude assessment interview (Auswahlgespräch)
- Work samples / portfolio
