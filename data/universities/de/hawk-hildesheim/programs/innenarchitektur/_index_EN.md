# Interior Architecture

> **Kompetenzfeld** within [BA Gestaltung](../ba-gestaltung/_index_EN.md) / [MA Gestaltung](../ma-gestaltung/_index_EN.md)

The **Interior Architecture** kompetenzfeld is a specialization within the interdisciplinary Gestaltung program at HAWK Hildesheim.

## Philosophy

> "Creative, technical, and craft competence for the human-centred realization of one's own spatial concepts."

Interior architecture combines creative vision with technical implementation. The kompetenzfeld enables students to think conceptually about spaces and realize them through craftsmanship — from furniture development to exhibition design to sustainable spatial concepts.

## Course Content

- **Spatial Design** — Conceptual and practical spatial planning
- **Exhibition Design** — Museum and commercial exhibitions
- **Trade Fair Design** — Temporary spatial installations
- **Furniture Design** — Design and construction of furniture
- **Spatial Concepts** — Development of innovative spatial solutions
- **Material and Surface Design** — Material selection and surface treatment
- **Lighting Planning** — Integration of light into spatial concepts
- **Sustainable Spatial Concepts** — Ecological and sustainable approaches

## Workshops and Labs

- **Spatial and Model Development Workshop** — Professional woodworking and furniture making
- **Design Model Making** — From idea to model
- **Rapid Prototyping Lab** — 3D printing and digital fabrication
- **Surface/Lacquer Workshop** — Professional lacquering techniques
- **Lighting Lab** — Light simulation and planning

## Professors

- **Prof. Silvia Böhm** — Interior Architecture
- **Prof. Klaus-Peter Goebel** — Interior Architecture

## Position within the Program

Interior Architecture is offered as one of nine kompetenzfeld within the Gestaltung program.

Possible combinations with related kompetenzfeld:
- Lighting Design (Lichtdesign)
- Colour Design (Farbdesign)
- Product Design (Produktdesign)

## Application

Application is through the **Gestaltung** program. See [BA Gestaltung](../ba-gestaltung/_index_EN.md) for details.
