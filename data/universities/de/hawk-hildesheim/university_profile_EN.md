# HAWK University of Applied Sciences and Arts — University Profile

## University Overview

**HAWK University of Applied Sciences and Arts** is a public university of applied sciences with campuses in Hildesheim, Holzminden, and Göttingen. Its Faculty of Design, based at the Hildesheim campus, is one of the most comprehensive design faculties in Germany, offering an exceptionally broad range of programs spanning visual communication, product design, spatial design, and craft disciplines.

- **Founded:** 1971
- **Location:** Hildesheim, Lower Saxony, Germany
- **Type:** Fachhochschule (University of Applied Sciences)
- **Student Count:** ~5,400 (university-wide)
- **Website:** https://www.hawk.de
- **Faculty of Design:** https://www.hawk.de/de/fakultaeten/fakultaet-gestaltung/

## Design & Art Programs

### Program Details

#### Visual Communication Area
- **Advertising Design**: Focus on creative advertising, campaign development, and strategic communication
- **Branding Design**: Corporate identity, brand strategy, and visual systems
- **Graphic Design**: Typography, editorial design, illustration, poster design, information design
- **Communication Design**: Broad visual communication encompassing digital and analog media

#### Product & Digital Design Area
- **Product Design**: Industrial and product design with emphasis on user-centered approaches
- **Digital Environments**: Interaction design, digital product design, and experience design

#### Spatial Design Area
- **Interior Architecture**: Spatial design, interior concepts, and architectural integration
- **Lighting Design**: Architectural lighting, light planning, and luminaire design
- **Color Design**: Color theory, color planning for architecture and product design

#### Craft & Art Area
- **Metal Design**: Jewellery, hollowware, and metal craft — a unique program in Germany
