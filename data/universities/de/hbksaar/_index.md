# Saar University of Fine Arts

The Hochschule der Bildenden Kunste Saar (HBKsaar) is a fine arts university in Saarbrucken, Saarland, offering programs in communication design, product design, media art and design, fine art, art education (Kunsterziehung), and media informatics. The school maintains an "Abendschule" continuing education program and has Erasmus+ partnerships with 27 schools across 11 European countries, as well as international cooperations with institutions in Brazil, China, Hong Kong, and South Korea.
