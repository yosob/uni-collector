# Saar University of Fine Arts

Hochschule der Bildenden Künste Saar (HBKsaar) is the only public art university in Germany's Saarland, located in the border city of Saarbrücken at the intersection of Germany, France, and Luxembourg. Founded in 1989, the school offers a comprehensive art and design education system from Bachelor's to doctoral level.

The school has 5 foundational programs (Fine Art, Communication Design, Product Design, Media Art & Design, Art Education) and multiple Master's and specialized Master's programs. HBKsaar is known for its interdisciplinary teaching approach, with all programs sharing the Bildnerische Grundlagen (Fundamentals of Visual Design) course. The school also operates Campus Völklingen, located at the UNESCO World Heritage Site Völklinger Hütte.

HBKsaar maintains partnerships with 27 institutions in 11 European countries through Erasmus+, and has international collaborations with institutions in Brazil, China, Hong Kong, South Korea, and beyond.
