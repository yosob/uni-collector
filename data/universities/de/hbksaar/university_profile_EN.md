# Saar University of Fine Arts (Hochschule der Bildenden Künste Saar)

> Last updated: 2026-05-03

## University Overview

Hochschule der Bildenden Künste Saar (HBKsaar) is the only public art university in Germany's Saarland, located in the border city of Saarbrücken at the intersection of Germany, France, and Luxembourg. Founded in 1989, it offers a comprehensive art and design education system from Bachelor's to doctoral level. HBKsaar is known for its interdisciplinary approach — all programs share the Bildnerische Grundlagen (Fundamentals of Visual Design) course, and the school operates Campus Völklingen at the UNESCO World Heritage Site Völklinger Hütte.

- **Type**: Public Art University (Kunsthochschule)
- **Location**: Saarbrücken, Saarland, Germany
- **Founded**: 1989
- **Tuition-free**: Only semester fees (including Deutschland-Semesterticket)

## Design/Art Programs

### Bachelor/Diplom/Staatsexamen

#### Fine Art (Freie Kunst) — B.A. / Diplom
- **Degree**: B.A. (8 semesters/240 ECTS) or Diplom (10 semesters/300 ECTS) | **Language**: German/English | **Start**: Winter semester
- **Focus**: Painting, sculpture, video/photography, sound art, conceptual art, public art
- **Admission**: Abitur + aptitude test (portfolio + practical exam)
- **Deadline**: May 31 (winter semester, applications open April 15)
- **Portfolio**: Approx. 20 works, max DIN A1
- **Professors**: Prof. Katharina Hinsberg (conceptual painting), Prof. Gabriele Langendorf (painting/drawing), Prof. Eric Lanz (video/photography), Prof. Andreas Oldörp (sound art), Prof. Georg Winter (sculpture/public art), Prof. Dr. Matthias Winzen (art history/theory)
- **Link**: https://www.hbksaar.de/studiengaenge/freie-kunst

#### Communication Design (Kommunikationsdesign) — B.A. / Diplom

- **Focus**: Typography, editorial design, corporate design, digital media, animation, interface design, wayfinding

- **Deadline**: May 31 (winter semester)

## How to Apply

### Application Portal
- **SIM-Bewerbungsportal**: https://www.hbksaar.de/sim-bewerbungsportal
- All programs apply through the SIM portal

### Required Materials
1. Online application form (via SIM portal)
2. Portfolio (approx. 20 works; 10 for teacher training)
   - Max DIN A1 format
   - 3D works as photographs (min. 24×30 cm)
   - Digital works on USB
   - Name and year labeled on the back
3. Proof of university entrance qualification (Abitur, etc.)
4. Internship certificate (12 weeks for design programs, completed before enrollment)
5. Language certificate (German B2 for international students)

### Timeline
| Program Type | Start Semester | Application Period | Deadline |
|-------------|----------------|-------------------|----------|
| Bachelor/Diplom/Staatsexamen | Winter semester | From April 15 | May 31 |
| Consecutive Master | Summer semester | — | November 30 |
| Specialized Master | Varies | — | Varies |

### Application Process
1. Register at SIM-Bewerbungsportal
2. Complete online application form
3. Mail portfolio to: Prüfungsamt, Keplerstraße 3–5, 66117 Saarbrücken
4. Attend Eignungsprüfung (aptitude test / interview)
5. Receive admission notification and complete enrollment

- Druckwerkstatt (traditional printing/risograph)
- dpz Digital Production Center (3D printing/rapid prototyping/physical computing)

- Fotoatelier (photography studio/darkroom)
- Videoatelier (video studio)
- Tonstudio (recording studio)
- Holzwerkstatt (woodworking)
- Formwerkstatt (plaster/ceramics/porcelain)
- Metallwerkstatt (metalworking/welding)
- Lichtlabor (lighting lab)
