# Master Career Changer Teaching Art (M.Ed.)

4-semester specialized master's program providing art teacher qualification for career changers with art or design degrees but no teacher training background.

## Program Overview

This program is designed for career changers who wish to teach art at vocational schools or grammar schools (Gymnasien). Core content includes:

- Art Didactics (Fachdidaktik Bildende Kunst)
- Pedagogy and Educational Theory
- School Practice Internships
- Subject Pedagogy
- Media and Digital Technologies in Art Education

## Features

- Provides teacher qualification for art/design graduates without a teaching background
- Close cooperation with HBKsaar's Art Education department
- Includes practical teaching components

## Application

- **Start Semester**: Winter semester
- **Prerequisites**: Bachelor's degree or Diplom in art/design (without teacher training background)
- **Process**: Apply via SIM-Bewerbungsportal
