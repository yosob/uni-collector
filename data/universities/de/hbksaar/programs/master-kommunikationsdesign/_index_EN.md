# Master Communication Design (M.A.)

Consecutive master's program in Communication Design, 1 year (2 semesters / 60 ECTS). Deepen conceptual and practical communication design skills through interdisciplinary research methods.

## Program Focus

This master's program features interdisciplinary research methods, expanding art-design conceptual and practical skills while reflecting on relevant theories of design, art, and media. Key areas include:

- New communication strategies in public, corporate, and cultural domains
- Structuring and visualization of complex content (information design, text design)
- Analog, digital, and interactive communication
- Editorial design in print and digital publishing
- Image design, 3D communication
- Corporate identity design
- Interdisciplinary and experimental design approaches

The core work is a self-selected in-depth design research project, supported by theoretical and practical courses.

## Application

- **Start Semester**: Summer semester
- **Deadline**: November 30
- **Prerequisites**: Bachelor's degree or Diplom in Communication Design or a related design field (recommended: at least 240 ECTS)
- **Process**: Apply via SIM-Bewerbungsportal, submit portfolio and project proposal
