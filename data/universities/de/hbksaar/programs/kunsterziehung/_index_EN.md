# Art Education — Kunsterziehung (Lehramt)

HBKsaar's Art Education program is a teacher training program (Lehramt) jointly offered by HBKsaar and Saarland University (Universität des Saarlandes). Students can choose a Fine Art or Design specialization at HBKsaar while studying a second subject at Saarland University.

## Degree Options

| Degree | Duration | Description |
|--------|----------|-------------|
| Lehramt Sekundarstufe I (LS1) | 8 semesters | Secondary school teacher (lower level) |
| Lehramt Sekundarstufe I+II (LS1+2) | 10 semesters | Secondary school teacher (lower + upper level) |
| Lehramt Arbeitsgemeinschaften bildende Kunst (LABG) | 10 semesters | Art club teacher |

## Teaching Features

- **Dual Institution Training**: HBKsaar (Art Education) + Uni Saarland (second subject)
- **Interdisciplinary Foundation**: Semesters 1–2 Bildnerische Grundlagen
- **Specialization Choice**: Choose between Fine Art or Design track
- **Reduced Portfolio**: Teacher training requires only 10 works (vs. 20 for other programs)

## Professors

- **Eva Biard** — Art Didactics in Primary Education
- **StD Andreas Möller** — Art Didactics

## Application

- **Application Portal**: SIM-Bewerbungsportal (HBKsaar) + Uni Saarland application system
- **Portfolio**: 10 works
- **Aptitude Test**: Eignungsprüfung
- **Deadline**: May 31 (winter semester)
