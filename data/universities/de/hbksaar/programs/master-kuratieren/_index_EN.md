# Master Curating and Exhibition Studies (M.A.)

4-semester specialized master's program in curatorial practice and exhibition studies.

> ⚠️ **Suspension Notice**: The HBKsaar board of directors resolved to suspend this program (ruhend gestellt) on April 15, 2026. No new students will be admitted for the 2026/27 winter semester.

## Program Overview

An interdisciplinary master's program focused on curatorial practice, exhibition design, art management, and art education. Students will study:

- Curatorial methodology and practice
- Exhibition planning and production
- Art institution management
- Cultural programming
- Art communication and education

## Application

- **Status**: Currently suspended for new admissions
- **Start Semester**: Winter semester
- **Prerequisites**: Bachelor's degree or Diplom in a related field (recommended: at least 240 ECTS)
- **Process**: Apply via SIM-Bewerbungsportal
