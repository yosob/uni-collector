# Communication Design (Kommunikationsdesign)

HBKsaar's Communication Design program offers both B.A. (8 semesters / 240 ECTS) and Diplom (10 semesters / 300 ECTS) degree options. The program covers typography, editorial design, corporate design, digital media, animation, interface design, and wayfinding systems.

## Teaching Features

- **Interdisciplinary Foundation**: Semesters 1–2 share Bildnerische Grundlagen (Fundamentals of Visual Design) with all design/art programs
- **Studio Teaching**: Small classes, learning directly in professors' studios
- **Practice-Oriented**: Access to Siebdruckwerkstatt (screen printing), Druckwerkstatt (printing), Digitale Druckwerkstatt (digital printing), and other facilities
- **12-Week Internship**: Relevant professional internship must be completed before enrollment
- **International**: Erasmus+ exchange opportunities, close cooperation with French institutions

## Application

- **Application Portal**: SIM-Bewerbungsportal
- **Portfolio**: Approx. 20 works demonstrating visual communication skills
- **Aptitude Test**: Eignungsprüfung (including interview)
- **Deadline**: May 31 (winter semester)
