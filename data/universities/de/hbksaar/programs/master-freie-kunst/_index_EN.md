# Master Fine Art (M.F.A.)

Consecutive master's program in Fine Art, 1 year (2 semesters / 60 ECTS). Deepen personal artistic practice in a professor's studio (Meisterklasse) and complete a master's final project.

## Program Focus

This master's program aims to deepen and expand artistic, methodological, strategic, and communication skills, training students to become independent artists. Core competencies include:

- Autonomy in independent creative processes
- Professional collaboration with cultural institutions (galleries, museums, art fairs, etc.)
- Ability to articulate and discuss one's artistic position across various media and audiences
- Preparation for further qualifications in teaching, design, and communication

## Application

- **Start Semester**: Summer semester
- **Deadline**: November 30
- **Prerequisites**: Bachelor's degree or Diplom in a related art field (recommended: at least 240 ECTS)
- **Process**: Apply via SIM-Bewerbungsportal, submit portfolio and project proposal
