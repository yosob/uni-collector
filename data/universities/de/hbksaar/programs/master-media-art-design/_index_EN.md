# Master Media Art & Design (M.A.)

Consecutive master's program in Media Art & Design, 1 year (2 semesters / 60 ECTS). Deepen digital media creation skills leveraging the xm:lab Experimental Media Lab.

## Program Focus

This master's program features interdisciplinary research methods, expanding art-design conceptual and practical skills while reflecting on relevant theories. Leveraging xm:lab and the Digital Production Center (dpz), key areas include:

- Digital media and interactive art
- Film/video and sound art
- Creative coding and physical computing
- Net art and game art
- Cross-media storytelling
- Experimental media production

The core work is a self-selected in-depth design research project, supported by theoretical and practical courses.

## Application

- **Start Semester**: Summer semester
- **Deadline**: November 30
- **Prerequisites**: Bachelor's degree or Diplom in Media Art & Design or a related field (recommended: at least 240 ECTS)
- **Process**: Apply via SIM-Bewerbungsportal, submit portfolio and project proposal
