# Product Design (Produktdesign)

HBKsaar's Product Design program offers both B.A. (8 semesters / 240 ECTS) and Diplom (10 semesters / 300 ECTS) degree options. The program covers industrial design, product system design, interface design, and social design.

## Teaching Features

- **Interdisciplinary Foundation**: Semesters 1–2 share Bildnerische Grundlagen with all design/art programs
- **Studio Teaching**: Small classes, learning directly in professors' studios
- **Practice-Oriented**: Access to advanced workshops (Werkstätten) including Holzwerkstatt (woodworking), Metallwerkstatt (metalworking), Formwerkstatt (plaster/ceramics), dpz Digital Production Center (3D printing / rapid prototyping)
- **12-Week Internship**: Relevant professional internship must be completed before enrollment
- **International**: Erasmus+ exchange opportunities, close cooperation with French institutions

## Professors

- **Prof. Mark Braun** — Product Design / Industrial Design
- **Prof. Maike Fraas** — Industrial Design / Product Design

## Application

- **Application Portal**: SIM-Bewerbungsportal (https://www.hbksaar.de/sim-bewerbungsportal)
- **Portfolio**: Approx. 20 works demonstrating design thinking and creativity
- **Aptitude Test**: Eignungsprüfung (including interview)
- **Deadline**: May 31 (winter semester)
