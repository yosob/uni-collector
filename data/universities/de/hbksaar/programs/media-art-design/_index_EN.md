# Media Art & Design

HBKsaar's Media Art & Design program offers both B.A. (8 semesters / 240 ECTS) and Diplom (10 semesters / 300 ECTS) degree options. Based at xm:lab (Experimental Media Lab), the program covers digital media art, interaction design, film art, sound art, physical computing, game design, and creative coding.

## Teaching Features

- **Interdisciplinary Foundation**: Semesters 1–2 share Bildnerische Grundlagen with all design/art programs
- **xm:lab Laboratory**: The school's experimental media lab providing cutting-edge technology support in physical computing, creative coding, etc.
- **dpz Digital Production Center**: Rapid prototyping, rapid manufacturing, and physical computing facilities
- **Practice-Oriented**: Professional facilities including Tonstudio (recording studio), Videoatelier (video studio), Lichtlabor (lighting lab)
- **12-Week Internship**: Relevant professional internship must be completed before enrollment
- **Medieninformatik Collaboration**: Cooperation with Saarland University's media informatics program

## Professors

- **Prof. Burkhard Detzler** — Computer-Generated Design: Animation, Interaction, Media Space
- **Prof. Sung-Hyung Cho** — Artistic Film / Moving Image
- **Prof. Eric Lanz** — Video and Art Photography
- **Prof. Detlef Hartung** — Light and Intermedia (acting)

## Application

- **Application Portal**: SIM-Bewerbungsportal
- **Portfolio**: Approx. 20 works demonstrating digital media creation skills
- **Aptitude Test**: Eignungsprüfung (including interview)
- **Deadline**: May 31 (winter semester)
