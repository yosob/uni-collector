# Master Experimental Media (M.A.)

A specialized master's program based at the xm:lab Experimental Media Lab and Digital Production Center (dpz). Project-oriented interdisciplinary learning at the intersection of art, design, and information technology.

## Program Features

- **Generative Design + Spatial Computing**: Creating with algorithms and AI, combined with AR/VR/MR technologies for 3D spatial art experiences
- **Small-Team Teaching**: Mastering creative strategies, technical skills, and critical thinking in small teams
- **Interdisciplinary Integration**: Deep collaboration with Saarland University (UdS) media informatics and data science programs, and the German Research Center for Artificial Intelligence (DFKI)
- **Practice-Oriented**: Cooperation with startups, incubators, and regional companies (e.g., Plastikfabrik, Centigrade, Captury)

## Facilities & Tools

- Digital Production Center (dpz): 3D printing, CNC milling, laser cutting, cutting plotter
- Interactive media facade (Medienfassade)
- Motion Capture Studio
- Media Theater (Medientheater): AR/VR/XR experiences
- Software: Blender, Unreal Engine, Unity, Processing, Arduino, etc.

## Research Topics (2024)

- Technologies of Play
- Immersive Media
- Generative Arts & Design
- Hybrid Prototyping

## Application

- **Start Semester**: Winter semester
- **Prerequisites**: Bachelor's degree in art/design or a technical field, recommended 240 ECTS
- **Process**: Apply via SIM-Bewerbungsportal, submit portfolio and project proposal

## Career Paths

Technology-oriented design agencies, game studios, VR production companies, UI/UX agencies, film and animation studios, creative departments in tech companies, and further academic research.
