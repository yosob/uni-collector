# Master Product Design (M.A.)

Consecutive master's program in Product Design, 1 year (2 semesters / 60 ECTS). Deepen conceptual and practical product design skills through interdisciplinary research methods.

## Program Focus

This master's program features interdisciplinary research methods, expanding art-design conceptual and practical skills while reflecting on relevant theories of design, art, and media. Key areas include:

- Sustainable design and social innovation
- Material research and experimental design
- Digital fabrication and prototyping
- Design strategy and brand development
- Industrial/product design methodology
- Interdisciplinary and experimental design approaches

The core work is a self-selected in-depth design research project, supported by theoretical and practical courses.

## Application

- **Start Semester**: Summer semester
- **Deadline**: November 30
- **Prerequisites**: Bachelor's degree or Diplom in Product Design or a related design field (recommended: at least 240 ECTS)
- **Process**: Apply via SIM-Bewerbungsportal, submit portfolio and project proposal
