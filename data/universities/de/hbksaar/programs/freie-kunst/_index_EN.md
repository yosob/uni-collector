# Fine Art (Freie Kunst)

HBKsaar's Fine Art program offers both B.A. (8 semesters / 240 ECTS) and Diplom (10 semesters / 300 ECTS) degree options. The program covers painting, sculpture, video and photography, sound art, conceptual art, and public art.

## Teaching Features

- **Interdisciplinary Foundation**: Semesters 1–2 share Bildnerische Grundlagen (Fundamentals of Visual Design) with all design/art programs
- **Meisterklasse Studio System**: From semester 3, students join a professor's studio for individualized guidance
- **Cross-Media Freedom**: Encouragement of cross-media creation and experimentation
- **Art Theory and History**: Art history and art theory courses led by Prof. Dr. Matthias Winzen
- **Exhibition Practice**: Regular exhibitions and Rundgang (campus open day)
- **Campus Völklingen**: Project workshops at the UNESCO World Heritage Site Völklinger Hütte

## Professors

- **Prof. Katharina Hinsberg** — Conceptual Painting
- **Prof. Gabriele Langendorf** — Painting and Drawing
- **Prof. Eric Lanz** — Video and Art Photography
- **Prof. Andreas Oldörp** — Sound Art (until 2026)
- **Prof. Georg Winter** — Sculpture / Public Art
- **Prof. Dr. Matthias Winzen** — Art History and Art Theory
- **Prof. Detlef Hartung** — Light and Intermedia (acting)

## Application

- **Application Portal**: SIM-Bewerbungsportal
- **Portfolio**: Approx. 20 works demonstrating personal artistic expression
- **Aptitude Test**: Eignungsprüfung (including practical exam / interview)
- **Deadline**: May 31 (winter semester)
