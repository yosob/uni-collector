# Master Museum Education (M.A.)

4-semester specialized master's program in museum education.

> ⚠️ **Suspension Notice**: The HBKsaar board of directors resolved to suspend this program (ruhend gestellt) on April 15, 2026. No new students will be admitted for the 2026/27 winter semester.

## Program Overview

An interdisciplinary master's program focused on museum education, cultural communication, exhibition didactics, and visitor studies. Students will study:

- Museum didactics theory and practice
- Art mediation and education strategies
- Exhibition didactic design
- Visitor studies and engagement
- Cultural education project management

## Application

- **Status**: Currently suspended for new admissions
- **Start Semester**: Winter semester
- **Prerequisites**: Bachelor's degree or Diplom in a related field (recommended: at least 240 ECTS)
- **Process**: Apply via SIM-Bewerbungsportal
