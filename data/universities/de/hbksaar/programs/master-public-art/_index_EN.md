# Master Public Art/Public Design (M.A.)

4-semester specialized master's program in public art and public space design (adjusted from 3 semesters starting SS2026).

## Program Focus

This master's program features interdisciplinary research methods, exploring creative practice in public spaces at the intersection of art and design. Key areas include:

- Public art and urban intervention
- Site-specific art and art in architecture
- Participatory design and social sculpture
- Digital media in public spaces
- Temporary art projects and festival curation
- International collaborative projects

The program works closely with the university's International Project Office and multiple partner institutions, emphasizing international perspectives and cross-cultural practice.

## Duration Change

Starting from the summer semester 2026, the program has been adjusted from 3 semesters (90 ECTS) to 4 semesters (120 ECTS).

## Application

- **Start Semester**: Summer semester
- **Prerequisites**: Bachelor's degree or Diplom in a related art or design field (recommended: at least 240 ECTS)
- **Process**: Apply via SIM-Bewerbungsportal, submit portfolio and project proposal
