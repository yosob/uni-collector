## Spatial Strategies (M.A.)

### Program Content
The Master's program in Spatial Strategies deals with artistic and design strategies for working with space. Students develop concepts for temporary and permanent spatial interventions in public spaces, exhibitions, and cultural contexts. The focus is on space-related art, scenography, and spatial design.

### Key Focus Areas
- Space-based art and intervention
- Scenography and exhibition design
- Public space and urbanism
- Temporary architecture
- Art in public space
- Spatial design

### Application Deadlines
- **Winter semester:** May 1 – 15
- **Summer semester:** November 1 – 15
