## Communication Design (B.A.)

### Program Content
The bachelor's program in Communication Design imparts fundamental design competencies in typography, photography, illustration, interactive media, and visual communication. Students learn to conceptualize and realize visual messages – from print media to digital applications.

### Key Focus Areas
- Typography and type design
- Photography
- Illustration
- Interactive media
- Corporate design and branding
- Editorial design

### Application Deadlines
- **Winter semester:** May 1 – 15

### Language Requirements (International Applicants)
German B2 (Goethe B2, DSH1, TestDaF 4×3, telc B2)

### Admission Requirements
- General or subject-specific university entrance qualification
- Passed aptitude test (artistic portfolio + practical exam)

### Tuition Fees
None (public art university)

### Special Features
- Project-oriented studies
- Workshops open around the clock
- PhD possible
