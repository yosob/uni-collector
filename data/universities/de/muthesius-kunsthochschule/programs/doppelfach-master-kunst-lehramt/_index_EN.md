## Double Subject Master Art Teacher Education for Grammar Schools (M.Ed.)

### Program Content
This program offers career changers a professionally qualifying academic degree for the career goal of "grammar school teacher." The artistic or design identity acquired in previous studies is complemented with a focus on Fine Arts or Design. Additionally, professionally qualifying competencies in subject theory, didactics, and pedagogy are acquired. Educational sciences are completed in cooperation with the Lübeck University of Music.

### Key Focus Areas
- Fine Arts or Design (complementary focus)
- Subject theory, didactics, and pedagogy
- Educational theory training

### Application Deadlines
- **Winter semester:** May 1 – 15

### Language Requirements (International Applicants)
German C1 (Goethe C1, Telc C1 Hochschule, TestDaF 4×4, DSH-2)

### Admission Requirements
- Completed degree (B.A., M.A., or Diplom) in an artistic program (e.g., Fine Arts, Communication Design, Industrial Design, Architecture)
- At an art university that is a member of the KHK

### Tuition Fees
None (public art university)

### Special Features
- **NEW from winter semester 2026/2027**
- In cooperation with the Lübeck University of Music (educational sciences)
- For career changers
- PhD possible
