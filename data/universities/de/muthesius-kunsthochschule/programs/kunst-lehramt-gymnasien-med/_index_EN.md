## Art Teacher Education for Grammar Schools (M.Ed.)

### Program Content
The Master's program in Art Teacher Education for Grammar Schools builds on the bachelor's degree and deepens artistic, subject-theoretical, and didactic qualifications. Students further develop their artistic practice and acquire advanced competencies in subject didactics, educational sciences, and school practice.

### Key Focus Areas
- Advanced Fine Arts or Design
- Advanced art didactics
- Educational sciences
- School practicum
- Master's thesis with artistic or didactic focus

### Application Deadlines
- **Winter semester:** May 1 – 15

### Language Requirements (International Applicants)
German C1 (Goethe C1, Telc C1 Hochschule, TestDaF 4×4, DSH-2)

### Admission Requirements
- Completed bachelor's degree in Art Teacher Education (B.Ed.) or equivalent
- Passed aptitude test

### Tuition Fees
None (public art university)

### Special Features
- Cooperation with the Christian-Albrechts-Universität zu Kiel
- Integrated school practicums
- PhD possible
