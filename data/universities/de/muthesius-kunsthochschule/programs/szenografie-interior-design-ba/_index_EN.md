## Scenography & Interior Design (B.A.)

### Program Content
The bachelor's program in Scenography & Interior Design imparts competencies in the creative engagement with space. Students learn to design spaces as experiential and communicative media – from interior design to exhibition design and scenographic spatial staging. The focus is on the connection between space, narration, and experience.

### Key Focus Areas
- Scenographic spatial design
- Interior design
- Exhibition design
- Spatial installation and intervention
- Materiality and light
- Digital spatial tools (3D modeling, VR)

### Application Deadlines
