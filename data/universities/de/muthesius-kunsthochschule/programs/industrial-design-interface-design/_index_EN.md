## Industrial Design – Interface Design (M.A.)

### Program Content
The Master's program in Interface Design deepens the engagement with digital interfaces and interactive systems. Students develop design concepts for human-machine interfaces, digital products, and services. The focus is on user experience, interaction concepts, and prototypical implementation.

### Key Focus Areas
- User Experience Design
- Interaction concepts
- Prototyping and digital tools
- Human-machine interfaces
- Service Design
- Physical Computing

### Application Deadlines
- **Winter semester:** May 1 – 15

### Language Requirements (International Applicants)
German B2 (Goethe B2, DSH1, TestDaF 4×3, telc B2)

### Admission Requirements
- Completed bachelor's degree in design or a related field
- Portfolio with interface/interaction design projects
- Passed aptitude test

### Tuition Fees
None (public art university)

### Special Features
- Dedicated prototyping labs
- Cooperation with industry and research
- Interdisciplinary projects
- PhD possible
