## Fine Arts (B.F.A.)

### Program Content
The Fine Arts program aims to develop an independent artistic identity. Students learn to realize their own artistic ideas, assess the quality of their work, place it in a theoretically relevant context, and present it appropriately. The basic studies begin with fundamental artistic training in the base class; as students progress, they choose an artistic class as their specialization.

### Key Focus Areas
- Fundamental artistic training (base class)
- Painting
- Drawing and printmaking
- Sculpture (sculpture, installation, spatial concepts)
- Media art / Time-based media
- Fine arts and ceramics

### Application Deadlines
- **Winter semester:** May 1 – 15

### Language Requirements (International Applicants)

### Admission Requirements
- General or subject-specific university entrance qualification
- Passed aptitude test (artistic portfolio + practical exam)

- Workshops open around the clock
- PhD possible
