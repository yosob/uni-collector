## Industrial Design (B.A.)

### Program Content
The bachelor's program in Industrial Design imparts fundamental design, technical, and methodological competencies. Students learn to design products and systems that are functional, aesthetic, and user-friendly. The curriculum covers design, construction, materials science, ergonomics, and digital tools.

### Key Focus Areas
- Product design
- Construction and technology
- Materials science and manufacturing
- Ergonomics and usability
- Digital tools (CAD, 3D printing)
- Design methodology and process

### Application Deadlines
- **Winter semester:** May 1 – 15

### Language Requirements (International Applicants)
German B2 (Goethe B2, DSH1, TestDaF 4×3, telc B2)

### Admission Requirements
- General or subject-specific university entrance qualification
- Passed aptitude test (artistic portfolio + practical exam)

### Tuition Fees
None (public art university)

### Special Features
- Project-oriented studies
- Workshops open around the clock
- PhD possible
