## Fine Arts (M.F.A.)

### Program Content
Building on a completed bachelor's degree, students deepen their artistic practice and develop an independent artistic position. The goal is the further development of a reflected artistic identity. Students work in artistic classes and are supported through individual supervision and group discussions.

### Key Focus Areas
- Painting
- Sculpture
- Media art / Time-based media
- Drawing and printmaking
- Ceramics
- Artistic research

### Application Deadlines
- **Winter semester:** May 1 – 15
- **Summer semester:** November 1 – 15

### Language Requirements (International Applicants)
German B2 (Goethe B2, DSH1, TestDaF 4×3, telc B2)

### Admission Requirements
- Completed bachelor's degree in an artistic program
- Portfolio
- Passed aptitude test

### Tuition Fees
None (public art university)

### Special Features
- Individual artistic supervision
- Workshops open around the clock
- PhD possible
