## Communication Design (M.A.)

### Program Content
The Master's program in Communication Design deepens design practice and enables individual specialization. Students develop independent design projects and positions in the areas of typography, photography, interactive media, illustration, or type design.

### Key Focus Areas
- Typography and type design
- Photography
- Illustration
- Interactive media and digital design
- Corporate identity
- Design research

### Application Deadlines
- **Winter semester:** May 1 – 15
- **Summer semester:** November 1 – 15

### Language Requirements (International Applicants)
German B2 (Goethe B2, DSH1, TestDaF 4×3, telc B2)

### Admission Requirements
- Completed bachelor's degree in communication design or a related field
- Portfolio
- Passed aptitude test

### Tuition Fees
None (public art university)

### Special Features
- Individual design supervision
- Workshops open around the clock
- PhD possible
