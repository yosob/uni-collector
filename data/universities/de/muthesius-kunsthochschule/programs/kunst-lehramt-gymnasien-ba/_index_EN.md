## Art Teacher Education for Grammar Schools (B.Ed.)

### Program Content
The bachelor's program in Art Teacher Education for Grammar Schools combines artistic and design practice with pedagogical and subject-theoretical training. Students acquire competencies in Fine Arts or Design as well as in subject didactics, subject theory, and educational sciences. The program is conducted in cooperation with the Christian-Albrechts-Universität zu Kiel.

### Key Focus Areas
- Fine Arts or Design
- Art didactics
- Subject theory and aesthetics
- Educational sciences (in cooperation with CAU Kiel)
- School practicum

### Application Deadlines
- **Winter semester:** May 1 – 15

### Language Requirements (International Applicants)
German B2 (Goethe B2, DSH1, TestDaF 4×3, telc B2)

### Admission Requirements
- General university entrance qualification
- Passed aptitude test (artistic portfolio + practical exam)
- Admission to CAU Kiel for the second subject

### Tuition Fees
None (public art university)

### Special Features
- Cooperation with the Christian-Albrechts-Universität zu Kiel
- Integrated school practicums
- PhD possible
