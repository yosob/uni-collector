## Industrial Design – Medical Design (M.A.)

### Program Content
The Master's program in Medical Design combines industrial design with medical applications. Students develop products and systems for the healthcare sector – from medical devices and rehabilitation aids to patient-centered services. The focus is on user-centered design in a medical context.

### Key Focus Areas
- Medical product design
- User-centered design in healthcare
- Rehabilitation design
- Interdisciplinary collaboration with medicine
- Prototyping and usability testing
- Standardization and certification of medical products

### Application Deadlines
- **Winter semester:** May 1 – 15

### Language Requirements (International Applicants)
German B2 (Goethe B2, DSH1, TestDaF 4×3, telc B2)

### Admission Requirements
- Completed bachelor's degree in design or a related field
- Portfolio
- Passed aptitude test

### Tuition Fees
None (public art university)

### Special Features
- Unique program in Germany
- Cooperation with University Medical Center Schleswig-Holstein (UKSH)
- Interdisciplinary projects with medicine
- PhD possible
