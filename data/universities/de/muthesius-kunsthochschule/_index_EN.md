## Muthesius University of Applied Sciences and Arts

### Overview
The Muthesius Kunsthochschule in Kiel is the only art university in the state of Schleswig-Holstein. With approximately 600 students, it offers a broad spectrum of artistic and design degree programs in the areas of Fine Arts, Communication Design, Industrial Design, Spatial Strategies, and Art Teacher Education. Founded in 1907, the university looks back on over 100 years of tradition in design education.

### Departments
- **Fine Arts:** Painting, Sculpture, Media Art, Ceramics, Drawing/Printmaking
- **Communication Design:** Typography, Photography, Interactive Media, Type Design, Illustration
- **Industrial Design:** Product Design, Interface Design, Medical Design
- **Spatial Strategies:** Scenography, Interior Design, Spatial Strategies
- **Art Teacher Education:** Teaching at Grammar Schools (in cooperation with CAU Kiel)

### Application Process
All degree programs require a passed aptitude test (Eignungsprüfung), consisting of the submission of an artistic portfolio and a practical exam. Application deadlines apply for the winter semester (May 1–15) and, if applicable, for the summer semester (November 1–15).

### Special Features
- The only art university in Schleswig-Holstein
- Small study groups with intensive supervision
- Workshops open around the clock
- PhD possible (artistic-scientific qualification)
- Membership in the Conference of Universities of Fine Arts (KHK)
- International networking and excursions
