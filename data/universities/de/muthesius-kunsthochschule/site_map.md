## 8. Programs Summary (12 programs documented)

### Bachelor (5)
1. **Freie Kunst (B.F.A.)** — 8 Semesters — Wintersemester
2. **Kommunikationsdesign (B.A.)** — 8 Semesters — Winter- und Sommersemester
3. **Industriedesign (B.A.)** — 8 Semesters — Winter- und Sommersemester
4. **Szenografie / Interior Design (B.A.)** — 8 Semesters — Wintersemester
5. **Kunst Lehramt an Gymnasien (B.A.)** — 6 Semesters — Wintersemester (Zwei-Fächer mit CAU Kiel)

### Master (7)
6. **Freie Kunst (M.F.A.)** — 4 Semesters — Winter- und Sommersemester
7. **Kommunikationsdesign (M.A.)** — 4 Semesters — Winter- und Sommersemester
8. **Industrial Design / Interface Design (M.A.)** — 4 Semesters — Sommersemester
9. **Industrial Design / Medical Design (M.A.)** — 4 Semesters — Sommersemester
10. **Raumstrategien / Spatial Strategies (M.A.)** — 4 Semesters — Wintersemester
11. **Kunst Lehramt an Gymnasien (M.Ed.)** — 4 Semesters — Wintersemester (ab WS 26/27)
12. **Doppelfach-Master Kunst Lehramt Gymnasien (M.Ed.)** — 4 Semesters — Wintersemester (ab WS 26/27)

### Application Deadlines
- **Sommersemester**: 1.–15. November (previous year)
- **Wintersemester**: 1.–15. Mai
