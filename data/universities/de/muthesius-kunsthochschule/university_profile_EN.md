The Muthesius Kunsthochschule is the only art university in the state of Schleswig-Holstein. It offers a broad spectrum of artistic and design degree programs across five departments: Fine Arts, Communication Design, Industrial Design, Spatial Strategies, and Art Teacher Education.

## How to Apply

1. **Check requirements:** All programs require an aptitude test (Eignungsprüfung)
2. **Prepare portfolio:** Compile an artistic/design portfolio (specific requirements vary by program)
3. **Submit application:** Apply online via the [Muthesius application portal](https://muthesius-kunsthochschule.de/der-weg-ins-studium/)
4. **Attend practical exam:** After portfolio review, invited applicants take a practical exam
5. **Deadlines:**
   - Winter semester: **May 1–15**
   - Summer semester (select programs): **November 1–15**

### International Applicants
- Language requirement: German B2 (most programs) or C1 (teacher education master's)
- Accepted certificates: Goethe, DSH, TestDaF, telc
