## Key Facts

- **Only Kunsthochschule** in Schleswig-Holstein
- **Nördlichste Kunsthochschule Deutschlands** (northernmost art university in Germany)
- Approximately **550 students** (estimated based on ~70 graduates per year)
- Member of **Cumulus Association** and **ELIA** (European League of Institutes of the Arts)
- Strong focus on individual artistic development with small class sizes
- **24/7 workshop access** for students
- Own exhibition space **spce | Muthesius** in Kiel city center
- Annual year-end exhibition **Einblick/Ausblick**
- Part of the **Kiel University** (CAU) network for teaching degree programs
- Houses the **Zentrum für Medien** (Center for Media) cross-departmental unit
- Active in **Artistic Research** with EU projects, excellence clusters, and PhDs

## Application Information

### Deadlines
- **Sommersemester**: 1.–15. November (of the previous year)
- **Wintersemester**: 1.–15. Mai

### Application Process
1. Online registration via digital application portal
2. Submission of portfolio (digital, "Die Mappe")
3. Eignungsprüfung (aptitude assessment) — includes review of portfolio and interview
4. Portfolio advisory sessions offered regularly ("Mappenberatung")

### Admission Requirements
- **Bachelor**: Hochschulzugangsberechtigung + künstlerische Eignung (artistic aptitude)
- **Master**: Bachelor degree in relevant field + künstlerische Eignung
- **Portfolio**: Required for all programs
- **Language**: German (DSH-2 or equivalent for international students)

### Study Info Days
- Annual "Alles offen" open days (typically February)
- Digital and on-campus formats
- Portfolio advisory sessions throughout the year
