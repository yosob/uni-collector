# Offenbach University of Art and Design (HfG)

The Hochschule für Gestaltung Offenbach am Main (HfG) is a state art university in Hesse with a tradition dating back to 1832 and university status since 1970. It offers a unique interdisciplinary education at the intersection of art and design, with two departments: Art and Design.

Since the winter semester 2022/23, the previous diploma system has been converted to bachelor's and master's programmes.

## Departments

- **Department of Art**: Fine and Applied Arts (BFA, MFA)
- **Department of Design**: Product and Process Design (BA, MA)

## Programmes

- BFA Fine and Applied Arts (8 semesters)
- MFA Fine and Applied Arts (4 semesters)
- BA Product and Process Design (8 semesters)
- MA Product and Process Design (4 semesters)
- Diploma (being phased out, last exam registration January 2028)
- Postgraduate Study (2 semesters)
- Doctoral Programme (Dr. phil.)
