# Doctoral Programme (Dr. phil.)

HfG Offenbach offers the opportunity to pursue a doctorate (Dr. philosophiae, Dr. phil.) with a unique combination of academic theory and artistic-design practice.

## Doctoral Format

### Dissertation
The dissertation consists of two components:
- **2/3 academic-theoretical component** — comprehensive theory-led research
- **1/3 artistic-design component** — practical-creative research component

This structure enables the integration of artistic research and academic science.

## Admission Requirements

### MFA Art (Department of Art)
- Completed MFA Art master's degree
- Direct doctoral admission at HfG Offenbach possible
- **Recommendation**: Choose MFA Art with greater theory component for optimal preparation
- Supervision commitment ("Annahmeerklärung") from a professor required

### MA Design (Department of Design)
- Completed MA Design master's degree
- Doctorate typically **in cooperation with another university** that holds doctoral degree awarding rights
- Cooperation partners must be arranged individually

## Doctoral Process

1. **Find supervision**: Contact a professor and obtain supervision commitment
2. **Prepare exposé**: Develop research proposal with theoretical and artistic-design components
3. **Apply for admission**: Submit application to the doctoral committee
4. **Acceptance as doctoral candidate**: Official admission to the doctoral procedure
5. **Research and dissertation**: Independent research with regular supervision
6. **Defence**: Oral examination (disputation)

## Special Features

- **No fixed application deadlines**: Doctoral studies can be started at any time
- **Interdisciplinary orientation**: Theory and practice are given equal weight
- **Offenbach Model**: Free access to all subject areas and professors in both departments
