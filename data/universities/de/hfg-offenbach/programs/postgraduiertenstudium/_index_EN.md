# Postgraduate Study (Aufbaustudium)

The postgraduate study at HfG Offenbach is a two-semester programme for graduates with a completed university degree. It can be pursued in both departments — Art and Design — and enables individual artistic or design specialisation.

## Key Facts

- **Duration:** 2 semesters
- **Degree:** Postgraduate study (no additional academic degree)
- **Requirement:** Completed university degree (Diploma or Master's)
- **Departments:** Art and Design

## Admission Requirements

- Completed university degree (Diploma or Master's)
- Proof of artistic-design aptitude
- Application via the regular application portal

## Application

- **Portal**: [bewerbung.hfg-offenbach.de](https://bewerbung.hfg-offenbach.de)
- **Period**: 01.04.–15.04. (winter semester)
- **Language proof**: B2 by 15 April, C1 by 15 September
- **Portfolio**: Artistic-design works analogous to the master's programmes
