# Diploma (being phased out)

The diploma programme (10 semesters) was replaced by the new bachelor's and master's programmes in the winter semester 2022/23. Currently enrolled students may still complete their diploma studies.

**Important deadlines:**
- Last exam registration: January 2028
- No exams possible after this date

**Transitional arrangement:**
Students who already hold a bachelor's degree and are at the beginning of the diploma programme may switch to the master's programme to maintain BAföG eligibility.
