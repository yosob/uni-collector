# Product and Process Design — Master (MA)

The Master of Arts in Product and Process Design at HfG Offenbach deepens and expands design, theoretical, technological and technical foundations over 4 semesters (120 CP). Students develop an individual profile across a broad range of topics, connecting design practice with academic and research contexts.

## Programme Structure

### MA Elective Projects — 50 CP
Design projects and experimental design work in the design subject areas. Students choose individually from the course offerings and develop disciplinary projects, including in collaboration with national and international partners (academia, research, industry).

### MA Electives — 40 CP
Individual selection from lectures, seminars, workshop and software courses. Covers design, theoretical and technological courses.

### Master's Thesis — 30 CP
- **Master's Thesis Design**: 20 CP — design thesis
- **Master's Thesis Theory**: 10 CP — theoretical thesis

**Total: 120 CP**

## Application

### Requirements
- Bachelor/Diploma (usually at least 210 CP)
- With 180 CP: application possible, studies may be extended
- Degree from another field possible
- **Exception**: approx. 3 years of professional experience related to the programme (instead of university degree)
- Proof of artistic-design aptitude

### Application Process
- **Portal**: [bewerbung.hfg-offenbach.de](https://bewerbung.hfg-offenbach.de) (from 1 April)
- **Submission**: 01.04.–15.04. (winter semester)
- **Portfolio**: Landscape format, at least 20 pages, max 20 MB (PDF)
- **Exposé**: Motivation letter + MA topic description (250–500 words, max 2 MB)
- **Language proof**: B2 by 15 April, C1 by 15 September
- **Aptitude test**: Late June/early July (in-person)
