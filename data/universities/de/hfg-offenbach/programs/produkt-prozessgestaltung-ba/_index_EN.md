# Product and Process Design — Bachelor (BA)

The Bachelor of Arts in Product and Process Design at HfG Offenbach combines design, theoretical, and technological foundations in an interdisciplinary programme over 8 semesters (240 CP).

## Programme Structure

### Foundation (1st–2nd semester)
Compulsory modules form the methodological and design basis:
- **Design**: 42 CP
- **Theory**: 12 CP
- **Methods**: 6 CP

### Practice and Design Modules (Elective compulsory)
6 modules à 6 CP = 36 CP:
- Modelling · Construction · Programming · Print/Production · Visualisation · Typography

### Technology
9 CP in 3 modules:
- Electronics · Materials Engineering · Print/Textiles

### Main Study Phase (3rd–8th semester)
Individual profiling through:
- **Elective projects**: 30 CP
- **Internship** (design office/research institute): 15 CP
- **Bachelor thesis**: Design (13 CP) + Theory (7 CP)
- **Electives**: 31 CP
