# Offenbach University of Art and Design (HfG)

**Hochschule für Gestaltung Offenbach am Main** — a state art university in Hesse, Germany, with a tradition dating back to 1832 and university status since 1970. Approximately 700 students study in two departments: Art and Design.

- **Address**: Schlossstraße 31, 63065 Offenbach am Main
- **Phone**: +49 69 80059-0 | **Email**: info@hfg-offenbach.de
- **Application Portal**: [bewerbung.hfg-offenbach.de](https://bewerbung.hfg-offenbach.de)

---

## Bachelor's Programmes

### BFA Fine and Applied Arts (8 semesters, 240 CP)
- **Department**: Department of Art
- **Focus**: Painting, Sculpture, Stage Design, Electronic Media, Film, Photography, Graphic Design, Typography, Fashion, Performance
- **Features**: "Offenbach Model" — no classes, no master classes, free access to all subject areas
- **Admission**: Aptitude test (portfolio + entrance exam + interview), 6 weeks manual internship
- **Application**: 01.04.–15.04. (winter semester only)
- **Language**: DSH-2/TestDaF-4/C1, at least B2 at application

### BA Product and Process Design (8 semesters, 240 CP)
- **Department**: Department of Design
- **Focus**: Industrial Design, Material Design, Information Design, Digital Design, Urban Design, Integrative Design
- **Features**: Design + theory + technology foundations; 5 research institutes
- **Admission**: Aptitude test, portfolio (landscape, ≥20 pages), motivation letter, 6 weeks manual internship
- **Application**: 01.04.–15.04. (winter semester only)
- **Language**: DSH-2/TestDaF-4/C1, at least B2 at application

---

## Master's Programmes

### MFA Fine and Applied Arts (4 semesters, 120 CP)
- **Department**: Department of Art
- **Focus**: Individual artistic development across all subject areas
- **Two variants**: (1) MFA Art (practice focus), (2) MFA Art with greater theory component (recommended for doctoral aspirations)
- **Admission**: Bachelor/Diploma (≥210 CP), portfolio, motivation letter
- **Application**: 01.04.–15.04. (winter semester only)

### MA Product and Process Design (4 semesters, 120 CP)
- **Department**: Department of Design
- **Focus**: Individual design profiling in 6 design subject areas
- **Features**: Collaboration with national/international partners (academia, research, industry)
- **Admission**: Bachelor/Diploma (≥210 CP), portfolio + exposé (MA topic description 250–500 words)
- **Application**: 01.04.–15.04. (winter semester only)

---

## Doctoral Programme

### Dr. phil. (Doctoral Programme)
- **Degree**: Doctor philosophiae (Dr. phil.)
- **Format**: 2/3 academic-theoretical + 1/3 artistic-design component
- **MFA Art graduates**: Direct admission possible at HfG
- **MA Design graduates**: Typically in cooperation with another university
- **No fixed deadlines**: Can start at any time with supervision commitment

---

## Postgraduate Study

### Postgraduate Study (2 semesters)
- **For graduates** with completed Diploma or Master's degree
- Individual artistic or design specialisation
- Available in both departments (Art and Design)
- **Application**: 01.04.–15.04. (winter semester)

---

## Diploma (being phased out)

- The diploma programme (10 semesters) was replaced by BA/MA programmes in WS 2022/23
- Currently enrolled students may complete their studies
- **Last exam registration**: January 2028
