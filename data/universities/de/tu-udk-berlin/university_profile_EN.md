## Design Programs

### Master Programs

#### Design & Computation (M.A.)
| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Degree** | Master of Arts (M.A.) — Joint degree from TU Berlin + UdK Berlin |
| **Duration** | 4 semesters (120 ECTS) |
| **Language** | German / English |
| **Start** | Winter semester |
| **Focus Areas** | Design as Medial Practice, Computational Design, Modelling and Simulation, Structures and Systems, Critical Design Thinking, Interdisciplinary Design Research |
| **Portfolio Required** | Yes — any media format (PDF, video, audio, website, etc.) |
| **Admission Requirements** | Bachelor's degree in humanities, sciences, engineering, art/design, economics, law, or medicine; motivation letter; portfolio; interview |
| **Application Deadline** | April 1 – May 15 (winter semester) |
| **Tuition** | Free (~€360.45/semester fee) |
| **Location** | Studio Campus Charlottenburg, Straße des 17. Juni 135, 10623 Berlin |
| **Link** | [Program page](https://www.newpractice.net/study) |

**Highlights**: Fully interdisciplinary research-based program exploring art, science, society, and technology. Joint degree bearing seals from both universities. Studio-based learning with annually changing themes.

## How to Apply

1. **Apply via TU Berlin portal (TuPort)** — submit documents + motivation letter
2. **Upload portfolio** via Google Form
3. **Attend interview** (mid-June – mid-August, on-site)
4. **If accepted**: Enroll at UdK Berlin and register at TU Berlin

### Application Timeline
- **Application period**: April 1 – May 15
- **Interviews**: Mid-June – mid-August
- **Start of studies**: October (winter semester)

### Portfolio Tips
- Any media format accepted
- Demonstrate individual approach, interests, and skills
- Portfolio consultation meetups held regularly before deadline
