# TU Berlin & UdK Berlin (Joint Program)

New Practice in Art and Technology is a research platform jointly operated by the Technical University of Berlin (TU Berlin) and the Berlin University of the Arts (UdK Berlin). It fosters a post-disciplinary practice addressing social and technological transformations, bringing together positions from art, science, technology, and theory. The platform is home to the joint Master's programme Design & Computation, an interdisciplinary degree that bridges artistic and technical disciplines between the two universities.
