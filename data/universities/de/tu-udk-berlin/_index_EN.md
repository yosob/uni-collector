# TU Berlin & UdK Berlin (Joint Program)

## Partner Institutions

### Technische Universität Berlin (TU Berlin)
- **Type**: Public technical university
- **Strengths**: Engineering sciences, natural sciences, computer science
- **Website**: https://www.tu-berlin.de

### Universität der Künste Berlin (UdK Berlin)
- **Type**: Public arts university
- **Strengths**: Art, design, music, performing arts
- **Size**: One of the largest arts universities in Europe
- **Website**: https://www.udk-berlin.de

## Research Platform

**New Practice in Art and Technology** is a joint research platform operated by TU Berlin and UdK Berlin, dedicated to post-disciplinary practice, addressing social and technological change, and bringing together perspectives from art, science, technology, and theory. The platform hosts the joint master's program Design & Computation.

## Joint Master's Program

### Design & Computation M.A.
- **Degree**: Master of Arts (M.A.)
- **Duration**: 4 semesters
- **Language**: German / English
- **Partnership**: Joint degree from TU Berlin + UdK Berlin
- **Certificate**: Includes seals from both universities
- **Features**: Interdisciplinary design research at the intersection of art, science, society, and technology
- **Location**: Studio Campus Charlottenburg, Straße des 17. Juni 135, 10623 Berlin
