## How to Apply

1. **Portal**: Apply online via [Folkwang Application Portal](https://www.folkwang-uni.de/home/service/bewerbung/)
2. **Timeline**:
   - **BA programs**: Portal open Jan 15 – Mar 15 → Aptitude assessment in May → Enrollment end of September
   - **MA programs (Practice strand)**: Portal open Jan 15 – Mar 15 → Interview in May
   - **MA programs (General)**: Portal open Jan 15 – May 31 → Interview June/July
3. **Materials**: Digital portfolio, motivation letter/text, declaration of independent work
4. **No application fee** for Art and Design Studies MA; others may have fees
5. **Language**: DSH 2 / C1 German required for international students
