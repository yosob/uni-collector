# Folkwang University of the Arts

Folkwang University of the Arts is a public art university located in Essen, Germany, founded in 1927, renowned for its interdisciplinary arts education. The Gestaltung (Design) division comprises 3 Bachelor's and 5 Master's programs.

> This directory only covers Gestaltung (Design) programs and does not include Music/Theater/Dance programs.
