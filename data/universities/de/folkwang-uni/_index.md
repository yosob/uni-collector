# Folkwang University of the Arts

The Folkwang Universitat der Kunste is a public university of the arts based in Essen, North Rhine-Westphalia, with additional campuses in Duisburg, Bochum, Dortmund, and at the UNESCO World Heritage Site Zollverein. It encompasses five main disciplines: music, theater, dance, design (Gestaltung), and academic studies (Wissenschaft), offering programs ranging from bachelor's to doctoral degrees. The university maintains specialized institutes for pop music, Gregorian chant, and art and design science.
