# Art and Design Studies M.A.

The cross-university single-subject Master's program Art and Design Studies (M.A.) at Folkwang University of the Arts (in cooperation with the University of Duisburg-Essen) is a consecutive offering for graduates of design and humanities/social science programs.

The program serves the academic engagement with design phenomena. In contrast to the practice-oriented programs, the Master's program primarily imparts knowledge and skills for the scholarly examination of design-oriented and artistic artifacts and positions. Complex design processes are discussed from various perspectives and using different cultural studies methods.

The program includes the possibility of preparing for a doctoral degree and completing it within a corresponding program at Folkwang University of the Arts.

**Admission**: Bachelor's degree in Industrial Design, Photography, Communication Design, Art Education, or a social science/humanities subject with a grade of 2.5 or better.
