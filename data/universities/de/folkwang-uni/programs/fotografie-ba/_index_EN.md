# Photography B.A.

The Bachelor's program in Photography at Folkwang University of the Arts is an artistic-academic program that combines practical photography with image theory and art history. The goal is the development of an independent artistic-photographic stance.

The program provides an in-depth engagement with the medium of photography in all its facets — from artistic to documentary to staged photography. Teaching takes place in an interdisciplinary environment that seeks connections to other design disciplines as well as to music, theater, and dance.

**Program Structure**: Project-based study with artistic studio projects (core subject A & B), foundation courses (design fundamentals), and accompanying academic seminars. 8 semesters standard duration, 240 ECTS credits.
