# Photography Studies and Research M.A.

The Master's program Photography Studies and Research combines photographic practice with academic research. The program is aimed at graduates who wish to engage intensively with the theory and history of photography.

In contrast to the Practice strand, the research perspective is at the forefront here. Students develop their own research projects at the intersection of photography, visual studies, and curatorial practice. The program qualifies students for doctoral studies and for careers in research, curating, and cultural mediation.
