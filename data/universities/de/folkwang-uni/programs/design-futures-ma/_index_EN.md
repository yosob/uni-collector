# Design Futures M.A.

The consecutive Master's program Design Futures (formerly Industrial Design MA) is aimed at designers who wish to engage with the social, ecological, and technological challenges of the future.

The program combines design practice with research and critical reflection. Central to the program is the question of how design can contribute to shaping future living environments — beyond speculation, social design, and sustainability. The transdisciplinary approach enables collaboration with other disciplines and fosters experimental, research-based design practices.

There are particular connections to the Master's program Intermedia Design, which expands the thematic and methodological possibilities.
