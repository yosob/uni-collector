# Intermediale Gestaltung B.A. (Intermedia Design)

The Bachelor's program Intermediale Gestaltung (formerly Communication Design) continues the tradition of Folkwang University of the Arts in understanding design as an artistic engagement with social, media, and cultural contexts.

Intermediale Gestaltung understands design disciplines as interconnected practices. The starting point of teaching is the fundamental design means of **image, text, and code**, which become carriers of complex design processes through critical reflection and mutual interpenetration. In teaching areas such as Free Graphics, Moving Image, Illustration, Typography, Visual Identities, Interface Design, and Information Design, media-related, aesthetic, and conceptual questions are explored experimentally.

Teaching fosters free, artistic-research work in which images, words, data, spaces, and interfaces serve as an open field of experimentation. The goal of the program is the development of a critical authorship that questions aesthetic conventions and social narratives.
