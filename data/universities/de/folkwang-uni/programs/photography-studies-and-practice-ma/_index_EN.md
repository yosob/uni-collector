# Photography Studies and Practice M.A.

The consecutive Master's program Photography Studies and Practice deepens the photographic-artistic competencies acquired during the Bachelor's program. The goal is the development of an independent artistic position at the intersection of photographic practice and image-theoretical reflection.

The program is aimed at graduates of photography and related design disciplines who wish to further develop their artistic practice at the Master's level. The education encompasses both the deepening of practical skills and the theoretical embedding of one's own work in current discourses of photography and visual culture.
