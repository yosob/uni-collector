# Product Design B.A. (formerly Industrial Design)

The Bachelor's program Product Design (formerly Industrial Design) at Folkwang University of the Arts understands product design as an artistic-design practice that incorporates social, ecological, and technological contexts.

The program is organized as project-based study that combines experimental work with conceptual depth. Students learn to understand design as a critical practice that not only develops functional solutions but also questions the cultural and social implications of design.

The department's workshops offer opportunities for analog and digital fabrication. The close connection to the other design disciplines and the interdisciplinary education across Folkwang are central features of the program.
