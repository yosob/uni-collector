# HTW Dresden - University of Applied Sciences

HTW Dresden (Hochschule für Technik und Wirtschaft Dresden) was founded in 1992 and is located in the Saxon capital of Dresden. It is one of the largest universities of applied sciences in Saxony. The university comprises 8 faculties covering engineering, computer science, economics, energy and environment, agriculture, architecture, geoinformation, and design.

**Fakultät Design (Faculty of Design)** is one of the university's distinctive faculties, focusing on **integrative design education** that combines product design, communication design, and interaction design. The faculty has approximately 150 students and 9 professors, offering small classes and personalized guidance. From the foundational phase, the three design dimensions — physical, media, and communication — are integrated, training designers to tackle the challenges of hybrid products and systems.

## Design Faculty Highlights

- **Integrative design philosophy**: Physical, media, and communication design are combined from the very beginning
- **Small classes**: Approx. 150 students, 9 professors, intensive mentoring
- **Practice-oriented**: Project-based learning, industry collaborations, studio practice
- **Semester Werkschau**: Public exhibition of student work at the end of each semester
- **Comprehensive workshops**: Model making, woodwork, metal, ceramics, textiles, printing, photography, video, VR lab
