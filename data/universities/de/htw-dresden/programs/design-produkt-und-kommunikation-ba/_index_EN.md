# Design: Product and Communication (B.A.)

## Program Overview

The **Bachelor Design: Produkt und Kommunikation** at HTW Dresden is an integrative design undergraduate program spanning 7 semesters (210 ECTS), taught in German only, with winter semester intake.

The program's core philosophy is **integrative design** (Integratives Design), combining **product design, communication design, and interaction design** from the very first semester rather than treating them as separate tracks. Students learn to design the overall experience of physical products, software products, and web services — so-called **hybrid products and systems** (Hybride Produkte und Anwendungen).

## Curriculum Structure

### Semesters 1-3: Foundation Studies
- Design fundamentals (2D/3D/interaction)
- Drawing and visual expression
- Color theory and application
- Typography and type design
- Materials and processes
- Design methodology

### Semesters 4-5: Specialization
- Product design projects (Produktdesign)
- Communication design projects (Kommunikationsdesign)
- Interface and interaction design (Interfacedesign)
- Design research methods
- Interdisciplinary projects

### Semester 6: Internship
- 16-week professional internship
- Can be completed at companies or design agencies in Germany or abroad

### Semester 7: Bachelor Thesis
- Bachelorarbeit (bachelor thesis)
- Graduation exhibition (Werkschau)

## Application Process

### Aptitude Test (Eignungsprüfung)
Consists of three components:
1. **Portfolio assessment** (Mappe) — weight 40%
2. **Practical exam** (Praktische Prüfung) — weight 40%
3. **Interview** (Kolloquium) — weight 20%

### Timeline
- **Application period**: May 15 – July 15
- **Aptitude test**: July
- **Admission notification**: August
- **Start of studies**: October (winter semester)
