## Institutional Highlights

### Tradition in Art and Design Education
ABK Stuttgart is one of the oldest art academies in Germany, with a history spanning over 250 years. The institution offers professional education across four major disciplines—Art, Design, Architecture, and Restoration—making it one of the most comprehensively structured art academies in Germany.

### No NC Admissions
As an art university, ABK does not impose a Numerus Clausus (NC), lottery procedures, or waiting lists. All admissions are determined directly by the academy through portfolio review and entrance examinations.

### Campus Location
The academy is situated in Stuttgart's renowned Weißenhof housing estate (a modernist architectural heritage site). The campus offers a unique environment that blends historic buildings with modern teaching facilities.
