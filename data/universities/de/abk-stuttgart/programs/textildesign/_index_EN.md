# Textile Design - Diplom

> Staatliche Akademie der Bildenden Künste Stuttgart

## Basic Information

| Attribute | Information |
|-----------|-------------|

| Degree | Diplom |

| Department | Design |

## Program Overview

The Textile Design program trains students in textile and fabric design, including fashion textiles, interior textiles, technical textiles, and other areas.

## Curriculum

- Foundations of textile design
- Fabric structure and weaving
- Printing and dyeing techniques
- Digital textile design
- Sustainable textile design
- Textile materials science

## Admission Requirements

### Academic Requirements
- General higher education entrance qualification
- Or subject-specific higher education entrance qualification
- Or aptitude test (Begabtenprüfung)

### Portfolio Requirements
- **Quantity**: Approximately 20 works
- **Type**: Free works, sketches, drafts, textile design works, etc.

## Application Process

### Application Timeline
- **Application window**: April 13 – April 30
- **Entrance exam**: June 22 – June 26

### Application Steps
1. Apply online via ABK-Campus-System
2. Submit portfolio (digital version)
3. Attend entrance examination
4. Register online after admission

- Knitting design (Strickdesign)
- Textile printing and dyeing (Textildruck und Färberei)
