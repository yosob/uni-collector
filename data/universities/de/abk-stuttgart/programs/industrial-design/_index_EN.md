## Program Overview

The Industrial Design program trains students to become product designers with independent design skills and critical thinking. The curriculum emphasizes the design process, user needs, and sustainable development.

## Curriculum

- Foundations of product design
- User research and ergonomics
- Materials and manufacturing processes
- Sustainable design
- Digital design tools
- Design project management

## Admission Requirements

### Academic Requirements
- General higher education entrance qualification (Allgemeine Hochschulreife)
- Or subject-specific higher education entrance qualification (Fachgebundene Hochschulreife)
- Or aptitude test (Begabtenprüfung)

### Internship Requirements
- **3-month internship** (Vorpraktikum)
- Proof of internship must be submitted with the application

### Portfolio Requirements
- **Quantity**: Approximately 20 works
- **Type**: Free works, sketches, drafts, photos, etc.
- **Focus**: Demonstrate the creative process and thought process, not just final results

## Application Process

### Application Timeline
- **Application window**: April 13 – April 30
- **Entrance exam**: June 22 – June 26

### Application Steps
1. Apply online via [ABK-Campus-System](https://campus.abk-stuttgart.de/)
2. Submit portfolio (digital version)
3. If pre-selected, receive a homework assignment
4. Attend on-site entrance examination
5. Register online after admission

## Entrance Examination

### Pre-selection
Based on portfolio review; successful candidates proceed to the exam phase

### Homework Assignment
Pre-selected candidates will receive a homework assignment topic

### On-site Exam
- Dates: June 22–26
- Location: ABK Stuttgart campus
