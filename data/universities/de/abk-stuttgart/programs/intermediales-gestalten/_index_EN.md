## Program Overview

Intermediales Gestalten is an intermedia design specialization offered at ABK Stuttgart as a minor (Teilstudiengang) within the Art Education teaching degree (Künstlerisches Lehramt). The program focuses on creative practice that transcends traditional media boundaries, integrating digital media, interactive design, sound art, video, net art, and other emerging fields.

## Curriculum

- Digital media art
- Interactive design
- Video and animation
- Sound art
- Net art
- Intermedia creative methods
- Media theory
- Design thinking

## Admission Requirements

### Academic Requirements
- General higher education entrance qualification (Allgemeine Hochschulreife)
- Applied as part of the Art Education teaching degree

### Portfolio Requirements
- **Type**: Intermedia works, digital media works, interactive works, etc.
- **Focus**: Demonstrate cross-media creative ability and understanding of new media
