## Program Overview

The Stage and Costume Design program cultivates students' abilities in spatial design for stage and costume design in the fields of theater, opera, dance, and film. As one of the few institutions in Germany offering this specialization, ABK Stuttgart enjoys an outstanding reputation in stage design.

Under the guidance of specialized professors, students learn the entire process from text analysis to spatial concept realization. The program maintains close collaborations with theaters and opera houses in Stuttgart and the surrounding region, providing students with practical experience opportunities.

## Curriculum

- Foundations and advanced stage design
- Costume design (Kostümbild)
- Spatial conception and model making
- Dramaturgical text analysis
- Stage technology
- Lighting design
- Art history and theater history
- Project-based practice (theater collaborations)
- Digital stage design tools

## Admission Requirements

### Academic Requirements
- General higher education entrance qualification (Allgemeine Hochschulreife)
- Or subject-specific higher education entrance qualification (Fachgebundene Hochschulreife)
- Or aptitude test (Begabtenprüfung)

### Portfolio Requirements
- **Quantity**: Approximately 20 works
- **Type**: Spatial design works, drawings, model photos, stage design sketches, etc.
- **Focus**: Demonstrate spatial imagination and theatrical understanding

## Application Process

### Application Timeline
- **Application window**: April 13 – April 30
- **Entrance exam**: June 22 – June 26

### Application Steps
1. Apply online via [ABK-Campus-System](https://campus.abk-stuttgart.de/)
2. Submit portfolio (digital version)
3. If pre-selected, attend on-site entrance examination
4. Register online after admission
