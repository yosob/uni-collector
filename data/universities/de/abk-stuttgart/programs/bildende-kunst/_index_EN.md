## Program Overview

The Fine Arts program is one of the core programs at ABK Stuttgart, cultivating students' ability to work independently in the field of free art. The program encompasses painting, sculpture, printmaking, photography, installation, new media, and other art forms.

Students can choose different art classes (Klasse), developing their personal artistic language and creative methods under the guidance of renowned artists. The academy emphasizes the independence of artistic practice and encourages cross-media and experimental approaches to creation.

## Curriculum

- Painting (Malerei)
- Sculpture (Bildhauerei)
- Printmaking and graphic arts (Graphik)
- Photography and media art (Fotografie und Medien)
- Installation art (Installation)
- Performance art (Performance)
- Art theory (Kunsttheorie)
- Art history (Kunstgeschichte)
- Class critiques (Klassenbesprechung)

## Admission Requirements

### Academic Requirements
- General higher education entrance qualification (Allgemeine Hochschulreife)
- Or subject-specific higher education entrance qualification (Fachgebundene Hochschulreife)
- Or aptitude test (Begabtenprüfung)

### Portfolio Requirements
- **Quantity**: Approximately 20 works
- **Type**: Free artistic works, painting, sculpture, installation, photography, video, etc.
- **Focus**: Demonstrate personal artistic language and creative development

## Application Process

### Application Timeline
- **Application window**: April 13 – April 30
- **Entrance exam**: June 22 – June 26

### Application Steps
1. Apply online via [ABK-Campus-System](https://campus.abk-stuttgart.de/)
2. Submit portfolio (digital version)
3. If pre-selected, attend on-site entrance examination
4. Register online after admission

## Entrance Examination

### Pre-selection
Based on portfolio review

### On-site Exam
- Includes practical art assignments
- Interview with professors (Kolloquium)
