# Communication Design - Diplom

> Staatliche Akademie der Bildenden Künste Stuttgart

## Basic Information

| Attribute | Information |
|-----------|-------------|
| Program Name | Kommunikationsdesign |
| Degree | Diplom |
| Duration | 9 semesters |
| Start Semester | Winter semester (WiSe) |
| Department | Design |
| Website | https://www.abk-stuttgart.de/studienangebot/kommunikationsdesign/ |

## Program Overview

The Communication Design program trains students in visual communication design, covering graphic design, brand design, information design, and related fields.

## Curriculum

- Foundations of visual communication
- Typography and type design
- Brand and corporate identity design
- Information design and data visualization
- Digital media design
- Design theory and methods

## Admission Requirements

### Academic Requirements
- General higher education entrance qualification
- Or subject-specific higher education entrance qualification
- Or aptitude test (Begabtenprüfung)

### Portfolio Requirements
- **Quantity**: Approximately 20 works
- **Type**: Free works, sketches, photos, graphic design works, etc.

## Application Process

### Application Timeline
- **Application window**: April 13 – April 30
- **Entrance exam**: June 22 – June 26

### Application Steps
1. Apply online via ABK-Campus-System
2. Submit portfolio (digital version)
3. Attend entrance examination
4. Register online after admission

## Entrance Examination

### Pre-selection
Based on portfolio review; successful candidates proceed to the exam phase

### Homework Assignment
Pre-selected candidates will receive a homework assignment topic

### On-site Exam
- Dates: June 22–26
- Location: ABK Stuttgart campus

## Workshops and Facilities

- Typography and type design workshop (Typografie und Schriftgestaltung)
- Printing workshop (Buchdruck)
- Reproduction and offset printing (Reproduktion und Offsetdruck)
- Photography (Fotografie)
- Audio-visual and video (Audiovision und Video)
- Computer applications (Computeranwendungen)
- Bookbinding and packaging techniques (Buchbinde- und Verpackungstechniken)
