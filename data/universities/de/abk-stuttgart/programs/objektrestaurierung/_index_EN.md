## Program Overview

The Object Conservation program trains students in the professional conservation of three-dimensional artworks and cultural heritage objects. The scope includes sculptures, artifacts, decorative arts, ethnographic objects, and various other types of objects.

## Curriculum

- Three-dimensional object conservation techniques
- Material analysis and examination
- Conservation of metal, ceramic, wood, and other materials
- Art history and stylistics
- Conservation science
- Documentation methods
