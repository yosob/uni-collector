## Program Overview

The Wall Painting Conservation program trains students in the professional conservation of wall paintings and architectural decoration. The program covers conservation and restoration of wall paintings from antiquity to modern architectural painting.

## Curriculum

- Wall painting conservation techniques
- Architectural painting conservation
- Wall painting material analysis
- Environmental monitoring and control
- Conservation materials science
- Art history and architectural history
- Documentation methods
- On-site conservation practice
