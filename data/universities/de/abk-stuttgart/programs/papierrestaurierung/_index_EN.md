## Program Overview

The Paper Conservation program trains students in the professional conservation of paper-based artworks and cultural heritage. The scope includes books, prints, watercolors, maps, archival documents, photographs, and various paper-based media.

## Curriculum

- Paper object conservation techniques
- Book conservation and binding
- Print and drawing conservation
- Photograph conservation
- Scientific analysis of paper
- Conservation materials and methods
- Art history and stylistics
- Documentation methods
