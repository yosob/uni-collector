## Program Overview

Körper, Theorie und Poetik des Performativen is an interdisciplinary master's program centered on performative art. The program explores the body as an artistic medium in theory and practice, integrating performance art, dance, body art, installation, and other forms.

## Curriculum

- Performance art practice
- Body philosophy and aesthetics
- Dance and choreography
- Performance art
- Contemporary art critical theory
- Interdisciplinary projects
- Installation and spatial art
- Sound and media art

## Admission Requirements

### Academic Requirements
- Bachelor's degree in a related art or humanities discipline
- Or equivalent artistic professional qualification

### Portfolio Requirements
- **Type**: Performance documentation, videos, conceptual texts, artworks, etc.
- **Focus**: Demonstrate understanding of performative art and personal creative direction
