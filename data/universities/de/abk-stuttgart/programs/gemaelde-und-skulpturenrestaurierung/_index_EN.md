## Program Overview

The Painting and Sculpture Conservation program trains students in professional conservation of paintings and sculptures. The curriculum covers conservation and restoration techniques for artworks from antiquity to contemporary art, combining natural science methods with art historical knowledge.

## Curriculum

- Painting conservation techniques
- Sculpture conservation techniques
- Material analysis and scientific examination
- Conservation science
- Art history and stylistics
- Conservation ethics and law
- Documentation methods
- Laboratory practice

## Admission Requirements

### Academic Requirements
- General higher education entrance qualification (Allgemeine Hochschulreife)

### Internship Requirements
- Relevant conservation internship experience

### Portfolio Requirements
- Demonstrate manual skills and artistic understanding
- Include drawings, craft works, etc.
