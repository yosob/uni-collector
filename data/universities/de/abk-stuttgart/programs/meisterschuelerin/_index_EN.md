## Program Overview

The Meisterschüler*in program is one of the highest-level qualifications in the German art education system, aimed at outstanding artists who have already completed a Diplom or equivalent degree. In ABK Stuttgart's Weißenhof Programme, students further develop their personal artistic practice under the guidance of renowned professors.

## Curriculum

- Independent artistic creation
- Professor-led master class (Meisterklasse)
- International exchange programs
- Exhibition planning and realization
- Art theory research
- Interdisciplinary collaboration

## Admission Requirements

### Academic Requirements
- Diplom or equivalent art degree
- Outstanding artistic portfolio

### Application Process
- Submit application to the relevant professor
- Submit portfolio and artistic proposal
- Interview
