## Program Overview

The Media Conservation program is one of the few programs in Germany specifically training conservators for media artworks. The curriculum covers conservation and restoration of film, video, audio, digital media, and other time-based media.

## Curriculum

- Film and video conservation
- Audio preservation and restoration
- Digital media preservation
- Time-based media art conservation
- Data migration and long-term preservation
- Media technology foundations
- Art history and media theory
- Documentation methods
