## Admission Summary

### Design Programs (Industrial Design, Communication Design, Textile Design)
- **Application**: April 13–30
- **Entrance Exam**: June 22–26
- **Process**: Online application → Portfolio → Homework assignment → On-site exam

### Architecture
- **Application**: April–May
- **Entrance Exam**: June–July
- **Requirements**: Higher education entrance qualification + pre-study internship

### Fine Art & Art Education
- **Application**: April 13–30
- **Entrance Exam**: June 22–26
- **Process**: Online application → Portfolio → On-site exam + Interview

### Restoration Programs (all)
- **Application**: January 26–February 13
- **Entrance Exam**: April 13–17
- **Requirements**: Higher education entrance qualification + relevant internship
