## Notes

- The `/design/` root URL redirects to the homepage; individual program pages are accessible directly
- Keramik- und Glasdesign and Spiel- und Lerndesign are Studienrichtungen (specializations) within the Industrial Design B.A. program, listed separately for application
- Textildesign is a Studienrichtung within Mode- und Textildesign B.A.
- The Mode- und Textildesign B.A. page redirects to the homepage; application via /bewerben/
- Interior Architecture M.A. and Furniture and Interior Design M.A. are specializations within the Innenarchitektur department
- Art faculty programs are located at Kröllwitzer Str. 2 (art campus, across the river)
- Design faculty programs are located at Neuwerk 7 (main campus)
