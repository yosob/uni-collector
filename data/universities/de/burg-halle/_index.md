# Burg Giebichenstein University of Art and Design Halle

Burg Giebichenstein Kunsthochschule Halle is a university of art and design in Halle (Saale), Saxony-Anhalt, organized into two main faculties: Art (Kunst) and Design. Its departments span sculpture, painting, time-based arts, book art, interior architecture, industrial design, product design (ceramics and glass), conceptual textile design, play and learning design, art education, and fashion. The school operates BurgLabs including BioLab, SustainLab, and XLab, and maintains the Burg Galerie as an exhibition space.
