# Burg Giebichenstein Kunsthochschule Halle

## Overview

Burg Giebichenstein Kunsthochschule Halle is one of Germany's leading art and design universities, with approximately 1,000 students across two faculties: Art (Kunst) and Design (Design). Founded in 1915, it has a rich history of combining artistic practice with design innovation. The university is known for its strong workshop culture, individual artistic development, and close supervision in small groups.

## Location

Located in Halle (Saale), Saxony-Anhalt, the university is spread across two campuses:
- **Campus Design (Neuwerk)**: Neuwerk 7, 06108 Halle (Saale) — Design faculty programs
- **Campus Art (Giebichenstein)**: Kröllwitzer Str. 2 / Unterburg Giebichenstein, 06108 Halle (Saale) — Art faculty programs (historic castle location)

## Application Process

### Bachelor / Diplom Programs

1. **Online registration**: Starting December 1 via my.burg-halle.de
2. **Document upload**: Portfolio (PDF, max. 50 MB, up to 20 works), CV, motivation letter, proof of qualification
3. **Aptitude test**: March/April (digital and on-site)
4. **Interview**: Part of the aptitude process

### Key Dates (Design Faculty)
- Application deadline: March 17, 14:00 (extended)
- Aptitude tests: March/April 2026

### Key Dates (Art Faculty)
- Application deadline: February 17, 14:00
- Aptitude tests: March/April 2026

### Master Programs
- Application period: April 1 - May 15, 2026
- Portfolio and motivation letter required

## Portfolio Guidelines

- Format: PDF, max. 50 MB
- Content: Up to 20 works demonstrating interests, enthusiasm, and design/artistic abilities
- May include: Nature studies, sketchbooks, photographs, spatial studies, free works
- No specific format, content, or technique requirements

## Special Features

- **No NC (Numerus Clausus)**: Admission based solely on aptitude test, not grades
- **Exceptional talent provision**: Possible admission without Abitur through special talent examination
- **International applicants**: VPD from uni-assist required (allow 4-6 weeks processing time)
- **Semester ticket**: MDV public transport included in semester fee (Saxony-Anhalt)
- **Strong workshop culture**: Extensive workshops for ceramics, glass, wood, metal, textiles, photography, printmaking, digital media
- **Small group teaching**: Individual supervision and mentoring
- **Dual enrollment (Lehramt)**: Art practice at BURG, second subject at MLU Halle-Wittenberg
