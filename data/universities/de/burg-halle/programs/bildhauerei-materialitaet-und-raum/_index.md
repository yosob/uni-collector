# Bildhauerei/Materialität und Raum (Sculpture / Materiality and Space) Diplom

## Program Overview

Sculpture/Materiality and Space at Burg Giebichenstein Kunsthochschule Halle is a 10-semester Diplom program within the Art Faculty. Unlike the figurative sculpture program, this specialization focuses on contemporary sculptural practice, material experimentation, installation art, and spatial concepts. It emphasizes the relationship between materiality, space, and contemporary art discourse.

## Focus Areas

- Spatial sculpture and installation
- Material experimentation and research
- Public art and site-specific work
- Conceptual sculpture
- Digital fabrication and new media
- Contemporary art discourse

## Admission Process

1. **Online registration**: Starting December 1 via my.burg-halle.de
2. **Portfolio submission**: Up to 20 works including spatial/conceptual work
3. **Aptitude test**: March/April 2026
4. **Interview**: Part of the aptitude process

## Key Facts

- Degree: Diplom (equivalent to Master)
- Duration: 10 semesters
- Faculty: Fachbereich Kunst
- Location: Kröllwitzer Str. 2 (Art campus)
- Start: Winter semester only
- Focus on material and space rather than figurative work
