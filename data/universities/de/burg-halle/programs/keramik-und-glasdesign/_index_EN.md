# Ceramic and Glass Design B.A.

## Program Overview

Ceramic and Glass Design at Burg Giebichenstein University of Art and Design Halle is an 8-semester Bachelor program focusing on material design, craft techniques, and product development. Students explore ceramic and glass as design materials through hands-on workshop practice.

## Focus Areas

- Ceramic design and porcelain
- Glass design and glassblowing
- Material experimentation
- Product development
- Craft and serial production
- Sustainable material approaches

## Admission Process

1. **Online registration**: Starting December 1 via my.burg-halle.de
2. **Portfolio submission**: PDF, max. 50 MB, up to 20 works
3. **Aptitude test**: March/April 2026
4. **Interview**: Part of the aptitude process

## Key Facts

- Degree: Bachelor of Arts (B.A.)
- Duration: 8 semesters
- Faculty: Fachbereich Design
- Location: Neuwerk 7 (Design campus)
- Start: Winter semester only
