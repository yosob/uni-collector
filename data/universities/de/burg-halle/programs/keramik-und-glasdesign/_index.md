# Keramik- und Glasdesign B.A.

## Program Overview

Ceramic and Glass Design at Burg Giebichenstein Kunsthochschule Halle is an 8-semester Bachelor program focusing on material design, craft techniques, and product development in ceramic and glass.

## Focus Areas

- Ceramic design
- Glass design
- Material design
- Product design
- Craft techniques
- Sustainable materials

## Admission Process

1. **Online registration**: Starting December 1 via my.burg-halle.de
2. **Portfolio submission**: PDF format, max. 50 MB, up to 20 works
3. **Aptitude test**: March/April 2026 (digital and on-site)
4. **Interview**: Part of the aptitude process

## Workshops

The program benefits from the excellent workshop facilities at Burg Giebichenstein for ceramic and glass work.

## Master Projects

Recent Master thesis projects include ceramic stacking systems and material research.
