# Innenarchitektur B.A.

## Program Overview

Interior Architecture at Burg Giebichenstein Kunsthochschule Halle is an 8-semester Bachelor program focusing on spatial design, interior concepts, and furniture design.

## Focus Areas

- Interior design
- Space design
- Furniture design
- Exhibition design
- Spatial concepts
- Materiality

## Admission Process

1. **Online registration**: Starting December 1 via my.burg-halle.de
2. **Portfolio submission**: PDF format, max. 50 MB, up to 20 works
3. **Aptitude test**: March/April 2026 (digital and on-site)
4. **Interview**: Part of the aptitude process

## Pre-Internship

**6 months required** in related craft trades:

Recognized trades:
- Furniture maker (Möbeltischler*in)
- Construction and furniture carpenter
- Builder (Bauhandwerker*in)
- Shop fitter (Ladenbauer*in)

**Important**: At least 3 months must be completed by study start. Remaining time can be completed during the first academic year.

## Notable Projects

- "Kiezhotel" – Overnight stays in urban vacant spaces
- "Parcours" – Garage stories for Chemnitz European Capital of Culture 2025
