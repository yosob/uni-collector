# Conceptual Textile Design M.A.

## Program Overview

Conceptual Textile Design is a 4-semester Master program at Burg Giebichenstein Kunsthochschule Halle focusing on advanced textile design practice, material research, and textile theory.

## Focus Areas

- Textile design
- Conceptual design approaches
- Material research
- Sustainable textiles
- Textile theory
- Surface design

## Admission Process

1. **Online registration**: April 1 - May 15 via my.burg-halle.de
2. **Portfolio submission**: Advanced textile design work
3. **Review and selection**

## Prerequisites

- Bachelor degree in textile design, fashion design, or related field
- Strong portfolio demonstrating conceptual textile work
- Interest in research and innovation

## Notable Projects

- "Mind the gap" – Conceptual textile design thesis project
- Research on textile interfaces
