# Furniture and Interior Design M.A.

## Program Overview

Furniture and Interior Design Master at Burg Giebichenstein Kunsthochschule Halle is a 4-semester program focusing on advanced furniture design, interior design, and product design. It is one of two Master programs offered within the Innenarchitektur department alongside Interior Architecture.

## Focus Areas

- Furniture design and prototyping
- Interior design
- Product design
- Material studies and experimentation
- Sustainable design strategies
- Digital fabrication

## Admission Process

1. **Online registration**: Via my.burg-halle.de
2. **Portfolio submission**: Advanced furniture/interior design work
3. **Motivation letter**: Required
4. **Interview**: Part of the selection process

## Prerequisites

- Bachelor degree in furniture design, interior design, product design, or related field
- Strong portfolio demonstrating design competence
- Workshop experience with materials and prototyping
