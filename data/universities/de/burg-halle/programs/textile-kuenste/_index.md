# Textile Künste (Textile Arts) Diplom

## Program Overview

Textile Arts at Burg Giebichenstein Kunsthochschule Halle is a 10-semester Diplom program within the Art Faculty. It treats textile art as a fine art discipline, encompassing weaving, fiber art, textile installation, and conceptual approaches to textile materials.

## Focus Areas

- Textile art as fine art
- Weaving and textile construction
- Fiber art and thread drawing
- Textile installation and space
- Conceptual textile work
- Material experimentation and research

## Admission Process

1. **Online registration**: Starting December 1 via my.burg-halle.de
2. **Portfolio submission**: Up to 20 works demonstrating artistic abilities
3. **Aptitude test**: March/April 2026
4. **Interview**: Part of the aptitude process

## Key Facts

- Degree: Diplom (equivalent to Master)
- Duration: 10 semesters
- Faculty: Fachbereich Kunst
- Location: Kröllwitzer Str. 2 (Art campus)
- Start: Winter semester only
