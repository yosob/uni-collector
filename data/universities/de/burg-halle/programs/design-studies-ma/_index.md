# Design Studies M.A.

## Program Overview

Design Studies Master at Burg Giebichenstein Kunsthochschule Halle is a 4-semester theoretically oriented program focusing on design theory, design research, design history, and cultural studies. It complements the practice-based design programs at the BURG.

## Focus Areas

- Design theory and methodology
- Design research
- Design history
- Curatorial practice
- Visual culture and cultural studies
- Critical design discourse

## Key Facts

- Language: German and English
- Duration: 4 semesters
- Degree: Master of Arts (M.A.)
- Start: Winter semester

## Admission Process

1. **Online registration**: Via my.burg-halle.de
2. **Portfolio/writing samples**: Design research or theoretical work
3. **Motivation letter**: Required
4. **Interview**: Part of the selection process

## Prerequisites

- Bachelor degree in design, art history, cultural studies, or related field
- Portfolio or writing samples demonstrating research and analytical capabilities
- Strong interest in design theory and discourse
