# Kunstwissenschaften (Art Studies) M.A.

## Program Overview

Art Studies at Burg Giebichenstein Kunsthochschule Halle is a 4-semester Master program within the Art Faculty. It focuses on art history, art theory, visual culture, and curatorial studies. The unique aspect of this program is its location within an art school, providing direct contact with artistic practice and contemporary discourse.

## Focus Areas

- Art history (modern and contemporary)
- Art theory and aesthetics
- Visual culture studies
- Curatorial practice and exhibition-making
- Design history
- Art criticism and writing

## Admission Process

1. **Online registration**: Via my.burg-halle.de
2. **Writing samples**: Art historical or theoretical work
3. **Motivation letter**: Required
4. **Interview**: Part of the selection process

## Key Facts

- Degree: Master of Arts (M.A.)
- Duration: 4 semesters
- Faculty: Fachbereich Kunst
- Location: Kröllwitzer Str. 2 (Art campus)
- Start: Winter semester
- Theoretical program within an art school context
- Close connection to artistic practice and studios
