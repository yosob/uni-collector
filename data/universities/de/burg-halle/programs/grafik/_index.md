# Grafik (Graphic Arts / Printmaking) Diplom

## Program Overview

Graphic Arts at Burg Giebichenstein Kunsthochschule Halle is a 10-semester Diplom program within the Art Faculty. It provides comprehensive education in printmaking, drawing, and contemporary graphic arts, with access to well-equipped workshops for various printing techniques.

## Focus Areas

- Printmaking (etching, lithography, screen printing, woodcut)
- Drawing and illustration
- Book illustration and artist books
- Mixed media graphics
- Contemporary print practices
- Artistic research

## Admission Process

1. **Online registration**: Starting December 1 via my.burg-halle.de
2. **Portfolio submission**: Up to 20 works demonstrating graphic abilities
3. **Aptitude test**: March/April 2026
4. **Interview**: Part of the aptitude process

## Key Facts

- Degree: Diplom (equivalent to Master)
- Duration: 10 semesters
- Faculty: Fachbereich Kunst
- Location: Kröllwitzer Str. 2 (Art campus)
- Start: Winter semester only
