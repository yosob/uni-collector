# Malerei (Painting) Diplom

## Program Overview

Painting at Burg Giebichenstein Kunsthochschule Halle is a 10-semester Diplom program within the Art Faculty (Fachbereich Kunst). It provides a comprehensive artistic education with focus on contemporary painting practices, supporting both figurative and abstract approaches.

## Focus Areas

- Contemporary painting
- Drawing and printmaking
- Figurative and abstract painting
- Mixed media and experimental approaches
- Artistic research and discourse
- Studio practice

## Admission Process

1. **Online registration**: Starting December 1 via my.burg-halle.de
2. **Portfolio submission**: Up to 20 works demonstrating painting abilities
3. **Aptitude test**: March/April 2026
4. **Interview**: Part of the aptitude process

## Key Facts

- Degree: Diplom (equivalent to Master)
- Duration: 10 semesters
- Faculty: Fachbereich Kunst
- Location: Kröllwitzer Str. 2 (Art campus)
- Start: Winter semester only

## Faculty

The program is part of the Fachbereich Kunst at Burg Giebichenstein, with dedicated professors and studio spaces for painting.
