# Bildhauerei/Figur (Sculpture / Figurative Art) Diplom

## Program Overview

Sculpture/Figurative Art at Burg Giebichenstein Kunsthochschule Halle is a 10-semester Diplom program within the Art Faculty. It focuses specifically on the human figure in sculpture, combining traditional figurative techniques with contemporary sculptural practice. The program has a strong emphasis on anatomical studies and figurative modeling.

## Focus Areas

- Figurative sculpture and the human form
- Modeling in clay and plastiline
- Bronze casting and lost-wax technique
- Stone and wood carving
- Anatomical studies and life drawing
- Contemporary figurative art

## Admission Process

1. **Online registration**: Starting December 1 via my.burg-halle.de
2. **Portfolio submission**: Up to 20 works, ideally including 3D/sculptural work
3. **Aptitude test**: March/April 2026
4. **Interview**: Part of the aptitude process

## Key Facts

- Degree: Diplom (equivalent to Master)
- Duration: 10 semesters
- Faculty: Fachbereich Kunst
- Location: Kröllwitzer Str. 2 (Art campus)
- Start: Winter semester only
- Specialization in figurative sculpture
