# Multimedia Design M.A.

## Program Overview

Multimedia Design Master at Burg Giebichenstein Kunsthochschule Halle is a 4-semester program building on the BA Multimedia|VR-Design program. It focuses on advanced interactive media, UX/UI design, virtual and augmented reality, and digital storytelling.

## Focus Areas

- Interactive media design
- UX/UI design
- Virtual Reality and Augmented Reality
- Web and app design
- Motion design
- Digital storytelling
- Creative coding

## Admission Process

1. **Online registration**: Via my.burg-halle.de
2. **Portfolio submission**: Advanced multimedia design work
3. **Motivation letter**: Required
4. **Interview**: Part of the selection process

## Prerequisites

- Bachelor degree in multimedia design, communication design, or related field
- Strong portfolio demonstrating multimedia and interactive design competence
- Technical proficiency in relevant software and tools
