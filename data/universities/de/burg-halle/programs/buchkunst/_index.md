# Buchkunst (Book Art) Diplom

## Program Overview

Book Art at Burg Giebichenstein Kunsthochschule Halle is a 10-semester Diplom program within the Art Faculty. It combines traditional bookbinding and publishing techniques with contemporary artist book practices, typography, and printmaking. The program is one of the few dedicated Book Art programs in Germany.

## Focus Areas

- Artist books and experimental book forms
- Bookbinding and book construction
- Typography and letterpress printing
- Printmaking (letterpress, etching, lithography)
- Paper art and papermaking
- Publishing and edition
- Digital book formats

## Admission Process

1. **Online registration**: Starting December 1 via my.burg-halle.de
2. **Portfolio submission**: Up to 20 works demonstrating book art abilities
3. **Aptitude test**: March/April 2026
4. **Interview**: Part of the aptitude process

## Key Facts

- Degree: Diplom (equivalent to Master)
- Duration: 10 semesters
- Faculty: Fachbereich Kunst
- Location: Kröllwitzer Str. 2 (Art campus)
- Start: Winter semester only
- Unique specialization in Germany
