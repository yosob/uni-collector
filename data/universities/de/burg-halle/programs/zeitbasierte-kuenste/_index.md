# Zeitbasierte Künste (Time-Based Arts) Diplom

## Program Overview

Time-Based Arts at Burg Giebichenstein Kunsthochschule Halle is a 10-semester Diplom program within the Art Faculty. It encompasses video art, performance art, sound art, installation, and new media art as contemporary artistic practices. The program emphasizes experimental and interdisciplinary approaches to art that unfolds in time.

## Focus Areas

- Video art and moving image
- Performance art and body art
- Sound art and audio experiments
- Interactive and digital installation
- New media and technology in art
- Documentary and experimental film

## Admission Process

1. **Online registration**: Starting December 1 via my.burg-halle.de
2. **Portfolio submission**: Up to 20 works or documentation of time-based work
3. **Aptitude test**: March/April 2026
4. **Interview**: Part of the aptitude process

## Key Facts

- Degree: Diplom (equivalent to Master)
- Duration: 10 semesters
- Faculty: Fachbereich Kunst
- Location: Kröllwitzer Str. 2 (Art campus)
- Start: Winter semester only
- Interdisciplinary approach combining media, performance, and technology
