# Industrial Design M.A.

## Program Overview

Industrial Design Master at Burg Giebichenstein Kunsthochschule Halle is a 4-semester program focusing on advanced product design, design research, and sustainable innovation. The program builds on the strong tradition of industrial design education at the BURG.

## Focus Areas

- Product design and development
- Sustainable design strategies
- Material innovation and research
- Design research methodologies
- System design
- User-centered design

## Admission Process

1. **Online registration**: Via my.burg-halle.de
2. **Portfolio submission**: Advanced industrial/product design work
3. **Motivation letter**: Required
4. **Interview**: Part of the selection process

## Prerequisites

- Bachelor degree in industrial design, product design, or related design field
- Strong portfolio demonstrating design competence
- Interest in design research and innovation

## Faculty

The program is part of the Fachbereich Design (Design Faculty) at Burg Giebichenstein.
