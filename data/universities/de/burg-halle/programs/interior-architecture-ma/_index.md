# Interior Architecture M.A.

## Program Overview

Interior Architecture Master at Burg Giebichenstein Kunsthochschule Halle is a 4-semester program building on the BA Innenarchitektur program. It focuses on advanced spatial design, adaptive reuse, and sustainable building strategies.

## Focus Areas

- Interior architecture and spatial design
- Adaptive reuse and building conversion
- Sustainable building and material studies
- Exhibition design
- Furniture design
- Digital planning and visualization

## Admission Process

1. **Online registration**: Via my.burg-halle.de
2. **Portfolio submission**: Advanced interior architecture/spatial design work
3. **Motivation letter**: Required
4. **Interview**: Part of the selection process

## Prerequisites

- Bachelor degree in interior architecture, interior design, architecture, or related field
- Strong portfolio demonstrating spatial design competence
- Interest in sustainable design strategies
