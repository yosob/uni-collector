# Communication Design B.A.

## Program Overview

Communication Design at Burg Giebichenstein University of Art and Design Halle is an 8-semester Bachelor program focusing on visual communication, graphic design, and brand design.

## Focus Areas

- Typography
- Photography
- Illustration
- Information Design
- Intermedia Design
- Editorial Design
- Brand Design

## Study Structure

- **Grundstudium** (Semesters 1–4): Foundations in typography, photography, illustration, information design, and intermedia design
- **Hauptstudium** (Semesters 5–8): Specialization in chosen focus areas with project-based work

## Facilities

- Offset printing workshop
- Screen printing workshop
- Photo studio
- Computer labs
- KD-Lab (with coding workshops, Riso printing)

## Admission Process

1. **Online registration**: Starting December 1 via my.burg-halle.de
2. **Portfolio submission**: PDF format, max. 50 MB, up to 20 works
3. **Aptitude test**: March/April 2026 (digital and on-site)
4. **Interview**: Part of the aptitude process

### Required Documents

- Study motivation letter
- Tabular CV
- Proof of higher education entrance qualification
- Portfolio
- International applicants: VPD from uni-assist

## Pre-Internship

3 months recommended in:
- Advertising agency
- Media agency
- Photo studio
- Publishing house
- Design office
