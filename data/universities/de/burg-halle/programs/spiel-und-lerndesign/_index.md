# Spiel- und Lerndesign B.A.

## Program Overview

Play and Learning Design at Burg Giebichenstein Kunsthochschule Halle is an 8-semester Bachelor program focusing on the design of games, educational tools, and playful learning experiences.

## Focus Areas

- Game design
- Learning design
- Educational toys
- Interaction design
- Didactic design
- Playful learning
- Experience design

## Admission Process

1. **Online registration**: Starting December 1 via my.burg-halle.de
2. **Portfolio submission**: PDF format, max. 50 MB, up to 20 works
3. **Aptitude test**: March/April 2026 (digital and on-site)
4. **Interview**: Part of the aptitude process

## Notable Projects

- "FLIPP – From Table to Bench" – Modular seating for schools
- Designs for transforming learning environments

## Philosophy

The program approaches design from the perspective of how people learn and play, creating tools and experiences that support education and development.
