# Kunst (Lehramt) — Art Education (Teaching Degree) Staatsexamen

## Program Overview

Art Education (Lehramt) at Burg Giebichenstein Kunsthochschule Halle is a Staatsexamen program for teaching art at secondary schools (Gymnasium/Gesamtschule). The art practice component is studied at the BURG, while the second teaching subject and education science are studied at Martin-Luther-Universität Halle-Wittenberg.

## Focus Areas

- Art practice and artistic skills
- Art education methodology (Kunstdidaktik)
- Art history and theory
- Teaching practice in schools
- Curriculum development
- Second teaching subject (at MLU)

## Program Structure

- **Art practice**: Studied at BURG (Fachbereich Kunst)
- **Art education/didactics**: Studied at BURG and MLU
- **Second teaching subject**: Studied at MLU Halle-Wittenberg
- **Education science**: Studied at MLU Halle-Wittenberg
- **School practicum**: Integrated into the program

## Admission Process

1. **Online registration**: Starting December 1 via my.burg-halle.de
2. **Portfolio submission**: Up to 20 works demonstrating artistic abilities
3. **Aptitude test**: March/April 2026 (at BURG)
4. **Additional application**: At MLU Halle-Wittenberg for second subject

## Key Facts

- Degree: Erstes Staatsexamen (First State Examination)
- Duration: 9-10 semesters
- Faculty: Fachbereich Kunst (art practice) + MLU Halle-Wittenberg (second subject)
- Start: Winter semester only
- Dual enrollment at BURG and MLU
