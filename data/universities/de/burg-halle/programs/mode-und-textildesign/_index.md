# Mode- und Textildesign B.A.

## Program Overview

Fashion and Textile Design at Burg Giebichenstein Kunsthochschule Halle is an 8-semester Bachelor program focusing on textile materials, fashion, and surface design.

## Focus Areas

- Fashion design
- Textile design
- Textile printing
- Weaving
- Knitting
- Surface design
- Material innovation

## Admission Process

1. **Online registration**: Starting December 1 via my.burg-halle.de
2. **Portfolio submission**: PDF format, max. 50 MB, up to 20 works
3. **Aptitude test**: March/April 2026 (digital and on-site)
4. **Interview**: Part of the aptitude process

## Pre-Internship

**3 months recommended** in areas such as:

- Textile printing
- Paper printing
- Foil printing
- Weaving
- Embroidery
- Knitting
- Design atelier
- Room textiles
- Clothing textiles
- Technical textiles
- Home textiles

**Recognized completed apprenticeships**:
- Textile designer
- Technical drafter
- Template maker
- Printer
- Weaver
- Hand weaver
- Knitter
- Embroiderer
- Tailor
- Carpenter
- Interior decorator

## Master Option

Graduates can continue with the Master program in **Conceptual Textile Design**.
