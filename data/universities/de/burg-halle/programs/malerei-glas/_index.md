# Malerei/Glas (Painting/Glass) Diplom

## Program Overview

Painting/Glass at Burg Giebichenstein Kunsthochschule Halle is a 10-semester Diplom program within the Art Faculty. It is a unique program combining painting with glass art, including traditional stained glass techniques and contemporary approaches to glass as an artistic medium.

## Focus Areas

- Painting on glass
- Stained glass design
- Architectural glass
- Contemporary glass art
- Light, color, and transparency
- Artistic research with glass

## Admission Process

1. **Online registration**: Starting December 1 via my.burg-halle.de
2. **Portfolio submission**: Up to 20 works demonstrating artistic abilities
3. **Aptitude test**: March/April 2026
4. **Interview**: Part of the aptitude process

## Key Facts

- Degree: Diplom (equivalent to Master)
- Duration: 10 semesters
- Faculty: Fachbereich Kunst
- Location: Kröllwitzer Str. 2 (Art campus)
- Start: Winter semester only
- Unique in Germany: Painting combined with glass art
