# Multimedia | VR-Design B.A.

## Program Overview

Multimedia | VR-Design at Burg Giebichenstein Kunsthochschule Halle is an 8-semester Bachelor program focusing on virtual reality, augmented reality, and immersive digital experiences.

## Focus Areas

- Virtual reality (VR)
- Augmented reality (AR)
- Interactive media
- Game design
- 3D design
- Immersive experiences
- Digital experiences

## Admission Process

1. **Online registration**: Starting December 1 via my.burg-halle.de
2. **Portfolio submission**: PDF format, max. 50 MB, up to 20 works
3. **Aptitude test**: March/April 2026 (digital and on-site)
4. **Interview**: Part of the aptitude process

## Pre-Internship

**2 months recommended** in:

- Advertising agency
- PR agency
- Corresponding company department
- Or: Completed vocational training in print or media field

**Basic knowledge required**:
- Image editing
- DTP (Desktop Publishing)
- Web design

## Technical Requirements

Applicants should demonstrate familiarity with digital tools and creative technologies.
