# Schmuck (Jewelry) Diplom

## Program Overview

Jewelry at Burg Giebichenstein Kunsthochschule Halle is a 10-semester Diplom program within the Art Faculty. It treats jewelry as a fine art form, combining traditional gold- and silversmithing techniques with contemporary experimental approaches to jewelry and body-related objects. The program is recognized internationally for its artistic approach to jewelry.

## Focus Areas

- Contemporary jewelry art
- Body-related objects and wearable art
- Metalworking (gold, silver, alternative materials)
- Experimental jewelry design
- Material research and innovation
- International jewelry discourse

## Admission Process

1. **Online registration**: Starting December 1 via my.burg-halle.de
2. **Portfolio submission**: Up to 20 works, ideally including 3D/jewelry work
3. **Aptitude test**: March/April 2026
4. **Interview**: Part of the aptitude process

## Key Facts

- Degree: Diplom (equivalent to Master)
- Duration: 10 semesters
- Faculty: Fachbereich Kunst
- Location: Kröllwitzer Str. 2 (Art campus)
- Start: Winter semester only
- Internationally renowned jewelry art program
- Fine art approach to jewelry (not craft/design oriented)
