# Interactive Media Design B.A.

## Program Overview

Interactive Media Design is a 6-semester Bachelor program focusing on the design of digital interactive experiences. Students explore UX/UI design, game design, interactive storytelling, web/app development, and VR/AR through hands-on projects and theoretical foundations.

## Focus Areas

- **UX/UI Design**: User experience and interface design for digital products
- **Game Design**: Game mechanics, narrative, and interactive experiences
- **Interactive Storytelling**: Non-linear narrative and interactive media
- **Web & App Development**: Frontend development and digital prototyping
- **VR/AR**: Virtual and augmented reality experiences
- **Creative Coding**: Programming for creative applications

## Study Structure

The program combines:
- Design fundamentals and creative coding
- User research and interaction design methods
- Technical skills in web, app, and game development
- Project-based learning with real-world challenges

## Application Process

1. **University application**: Via the standard university application portal
2. **Aptitude test (Eignungsfeststellungsverfahren)**:
   - Portfolio submission demonstrating interactive media skills
   - Practical examination
   - Application period typically opens in spring

## Annual Exhibition

Student works are showcased in the annual **LOOP** exhibition organized by the MR department.

## Department

Part of the Department Media Design and Spatial Design (MR), located in Building I on the Grifflenberg campus.
