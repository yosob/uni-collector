# Design Interaktiver Medien B.A.

## Program Overview

Design Interaktiver Medien (Interactive Media Design) is a combinatorial Bachelor program in the Media Design and Space Design department. It focuses on designing digital experiences and interactive systems.

## Focus Areas

- Interaction design
- User experience (UX)
- Digital media
- Web design
- App design
- Game design
- Virtual and augmented reality

## Department

The program is offered by the Abteilung Mediendesign und Raumgestaltung (MR), located in Building I on the Grifflenberg campus at Fuhlrottstr. 10, 42119 Wuppertal.

## Annual Exhibition

The department organizes LOOP, an annual exhibition of student works from all programs in the department.
