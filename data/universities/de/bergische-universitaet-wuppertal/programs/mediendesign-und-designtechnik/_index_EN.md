# Media Design and Design Technology B.A.

## Program Overview

Media Design and Design Technology is a 6-semester Bachelor program covering graphic design, typography, print and digital media production, and design technology. Students develop comprehensive skills in visual communication across both analog and digital media.

## Focus Areas

- **Graphic Design**: Visual identity, branding, editorial design
- **Typography**: Type design, letterpress, digital typography
- **Print Media**: Offset, digital printing, production workflows
- **Digital Design**: Web design, digital publishing, interactive media
- **Design Technology**: Production technology, prepress, materials

## Study Structure

The program combines:
- Artistic and design fundamentals
- Technical knowledge in print and digital production
- Typography and visual communication
- Project-based learning with real-world assignments

## Application Process

1. **University application**: Via the standard university application portal
2. **Aptitude test (Eignungsfeststellungsverfahren)**:
   - Portfolio submission demonstrating graphic design and media skills
   - Practical examination
   - Application period typically opens in spring

## Master Options

- **M.Ed.** (Master of Education): For teaching careers at vocational colleges

## Annual Exhibition

Student works are showcased in the annual **LOOP** exhibition organized by the MR department.

## Department

Part of the Department Media Design and Spatial Design (MR), located in Building I on the Grifflenberg campus.
