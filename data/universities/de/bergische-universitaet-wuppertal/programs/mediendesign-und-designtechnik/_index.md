# Mediendesign und Designtechnik B.A.

## Program Overview

Mediendesign und Designtechnik (Media Design and Design Technology) is a combinatorial Bachelor program covering graphic design, typography, and both print and digital media production.

## Focus Areas

- Graphic design
- Typography
- Print media
- Digital design
- Design technology
- Media production workflows

## Department

Part of the Abteilung Mediendesign und Raumgestaltung (MR), located in Building I on the Grifflenberg campus.

## Master Options

Graduates can continue with the Master of Education (M.Ed.) in Mediendesign und Designtechnik for teaching careers.

## Annual Exhibition

Works are showcased in the annual LOOP exhibition.
