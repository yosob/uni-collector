# Audiovisual Media Design B.A.

## Program Overview

Audiovisual Media Design is a 6-semester Bachelor program that focuses on the artistic and technical aspects of audiovisual media production. Students explore film, video art, animation, sound design, and visual effects through both practical and theoretical approaches.

## Focus Areas

- **Film & Video**: Narrative and experimental filmmaking, documentary
- **Animation**: 2D and 3D animation techniques
- **Sound Design**: Audio production, sound art, spatial audio
- **Visual Effects**: Compositing, motion graphics
- **Media Art**: Installation, projection, interactive media

## Study Structure

The program combines:
- Artistic and creative fundamentals
- Technical skills in media production
- Theoretical foundations in media studies
- Project-based learning with real-world assignments

## Application Process

1. **University application**: Via the standard university application portal
2. **Aptitude test (Eignungsfeststellungsverfahren)**:
   - Portfolio submission demonstrating audiovisual media skills
   - Practical examination
   - Application period typically opens in spring

## Annual Exhibition

Student works are showcased in the annual **LOOP** exhibition organized by the MR department.

## Department

Part of the Department Media Design and Spatial Design (MR), located in Building I on the Grifflenberg campus.
