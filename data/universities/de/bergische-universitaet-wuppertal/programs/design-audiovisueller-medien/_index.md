# Design Audiovisueller Medien B.A.

## Program Overview

Design Audiovisueller Medien (Audiovisual Media Design) is a combinatorial Bachelor program focusing on film, video, animation, and sound design. Students learn to create compelling visual narratives and audiovisual experiences.

## Focus Areas

- Film production
- Video art
- Animation
- Sound design
- Motion graphics
- Visual storytelling

## Department

Part of the Abteilung Mediendesign und Raumgestaltung (MR), located in Building I on the Grifflenberg campus.

## Annual Exhibition

Works are showcased in the annual LOOP exhibition.
