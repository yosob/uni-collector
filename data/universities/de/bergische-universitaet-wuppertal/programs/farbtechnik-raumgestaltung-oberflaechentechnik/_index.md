# Farbtechnik/Raumgestaltung/Oberflächentechnik B.A./B.Ed.

## Program Overview

Farbtechnik/Raumgestaltung/Oberflächentechnik (Color Technology/Space Design/Surface Technology) is a unique program combining artistic design with technical expertise in color, surfaces, and interior spaces.

## Focus Areas

- Color design and theory
- Interior space design
- Surface treatment and finishing
- Paint technology
- Material science
- Room aesthetics

## Program Options

- **B.A.**: Combinatorial Bachelor of Arts
- **B.Ed.**: Teaching degree for vocational schools

## Master Options

Graduates can continue with the Master of Education (M.Ed.) for teaching careers at vocational colleges.

## Department

Part of the Abteilung Mediendesign und Raumgestaltung (MR), located in Building I on the Grifflenberg campus.

## Annual Exhibition

Works are showcased in the annual LOOP exhibition.
