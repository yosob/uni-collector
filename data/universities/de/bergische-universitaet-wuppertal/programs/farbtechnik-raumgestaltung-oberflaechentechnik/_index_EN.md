# Color Technology/Space Design/Surface Technology B.A./B.Ed.

## Program Overview

Color Technology/Space Design/Surface Technology is a unique program combining artistic design with technical expertise in color, surfaces, and interior spaces. It is one of the few programs in Germany offering this specialized combination.

## Program Options

- **B.A.** (Bachelor of Arts): Combinatorial degree — students combine FRO with another subject
- **B.Ed.** (Bachelor of Education): Teaching degree for vocational schools (Berufskolleg)

## Focus Areas

- **Color Technology**: Color theory, color systems, paint technology
- **Space Design**: Interior space design, room aesthetics
- **Surface Technology**: Surface treatment, finishing, material science

## Study Structure

The program combines:
- Artistic and design fundamentals
- Technical knowledge in chemistry and materials
- Practical workshops and projects
- Pedagogical methods (for B.Ed. track)

## Master Options

- **M.Ed.** (Master of Education): For teaching careers at vocational colleges
- Further specialization in color and space design

## Annual Exhibition

Works are showcased in the annual **LOOP** exhibition organized by the MR department.

## Department

Part of the Department Media Design and Spatial Design (MR), located in Building I on the Grifflenberg campus.
