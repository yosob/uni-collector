# Strategic Product and Innovation Development M.A.

## Program Overview

The Master's program in Strategic Product and Innovation Development is a 4-semester interdisciplinary program that combines design, engineering, and business perspectives to prepare graduates for leadership roles in product innovation.

## Key Facts

| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Degree | Master of Arts (M.A.) |
| Duration | 4 semesters (120 ECTS) |
| Start | Winter + Summer semester |
| Language | German + English |
| Admission | Open (no numerus clausus) |
| ECTS per semester | 30 |

## Admission Requirements

- Bachelor degree with 180 ECTS in:
  - Industrial Design / Product Design
  - Mechanical Engineering
  - Business Administration
  - Or related fields
- Minimum grade: 2.0 or better
- No numerus clausus (open admission)

## Focus Areas

- Strategic product development
- Innovation management and methodology
- Design thinking and process
- Product strategy
- Sustainable product innovation
- Interdisciplinary collaboration

## Career Prospects

Graduates work in:
- Product development leadership
- Innovation management
- Design strategy consulting
- Research and development
- Entrepreneurial ventures

## Department

Part of the Department Industrial Design within the Faculty for Design and Art, University of Wuppertal.
