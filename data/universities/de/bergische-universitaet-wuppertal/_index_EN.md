# University of Wuppertal

## Overview

The University of Wuppertal (Bergische Universität Wuppertal) is a modern university founded in 1972, with approximately 25,000 students across 7 faculties. The Faculty for Design and Art (Fakultät für Design und Kunst, FK8) is one of the leading design faculties in Germany.

## Location

Located in Wuppertal, North Rhine-Westphalia, the university benefits from its central position in the Rhine-Ruhr metropolitan region. The Faculty for Design and Art is housed in Building I on the Grifflenberg campus.

## Faculty for Design and Art (Fakultät 8)

The Faculty offers a wide range of design and art programs across three main departments:

### Department ID (Industrial Design)
- Industrial Design B.A. (8 semesters)
- Strategic Product and Innovation Development M.A. (4 semesters)

### Department MR (Media Design and Spatial Design)
- Media Design and Design Technology B.A. (6 semesters)
- Audiovisual Media Design B.A. (6 semesters)
- Interactive Media Design B.A. (6 semesters)
- Color Technology/Space Design/Surface Technology B.A./B.Ed. (6 semesters)
- Public Interest Design M.A. (4 semesters)

### Department Kunst (Art)
- Fine Art B.A./B.Ed. (6 semesters)

## Special Features

- **No NC for Industrial Design**: Admission based solely on aptitude test
- **Industry partnerships**: Cooperations with companies like Bosch, Kettler, Sonos
- **International network**: Partner universities in Europe, Asia, USA
- **LOOP**: Annual exhibition of student works from MR department
- **Strong career prospects**: ~80% employment rate within 6 months for Industrial Design
- **Open admission for Strategic Product and Innovation Development**: No NC, Bachelor with grade 2.0+
