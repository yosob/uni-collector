# Bergische Universität Wuppertal

The Bergische Universitat Wuppertal is a dynamic and future-oriented university located in the heart of Europe. It comprises nine faculties spanning the humanities, social sciences, economics, mathematics and natural sciences, architecture and civil engineering, electrical engineering and information technology, mechanical engineering and safety technology, design and art, and education. The university is situated at Gaußstraße 20 in Wuppertal, North Rhine-Westphalia.
