# Bergische Universität Wuppertal

## Overview

The Bergische Universität Wuppertal (University of Wuppertal) is a modern university founded in 1972, with approximately 25,000 students across 7 faculties. The Faculty for Design and Art (Fakultät für Design und Kunst, FK8) is one of the leading design faculties in Germany.

## Location

Located in Wuppertal, North Rhine-Westphalia, the university benefits from its central position in the Rhine-Ruhr metropolitan region. The Faculty for Design and Art is housed in Building I on the Grifflenberg campus.

## Faculty for Design and Art (Fakultät 8)

The Faculty offers a wide range of design and art programs across two main departments:

### Department ID (Industrial Design)
- Industrial Design B.A.
- Strategische Produkt- und Innovationsentwicklung M.A.

### Department MR (Mediendesign und Raumgestaltung)
- Mediendesign und Designtechnik B.A.
- Design Audiovisueller Medien B.A.
- Design Interaktiver Medien B.A.
- Farbtechnik/Raumgestaltung/Oberflächentechnik B.A./B.Ed.
- Public Interest Design M.A.

## Special Features

- **No NC for Industrial Design**: Admission based solely on aptitude test
- **Industry partnerships**: Cooperations with companies like Bosch, Kettler, Sonos
- **International network**: Partner universities in Europe, Asia, USA
- **LOOP**: Annual exhibition of student works from MR department
- **Strong career prospects**: ~80% employment rate within 6 months for Industrial Design
