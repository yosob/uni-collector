# Darmstadt University of Applied Sciences — Department of Design — University Profile

## University Overview

**Darmstadt University of Applied Sciences (h_da)** is one of the largest universities of applied sciences in Germany. Its Department of Design (*Fachbereich Gestaltung*) offers well-established Diplom programs in Industrial Design and Communication Design, with Photography as a specialization within Communication Design.

- **Location:** Darmstadt, Hesse, Germany
- **Type:** Fachhochschule (University of Applied Sciences)
- **Website:** https://design.h-da.de
- **Main University:** https://www.h-da.de

### Program Details

#### Industrial Design (Diplom)

Comprehensive 8-semester program covering product design methodology, materials and manufacturing, user-centered design, and design management. The Diplom degree is equivalent to a Master's level qualification. Students develop practical skills through project-based learning and industry collaborations.

#### Communication Design (Diplom)

8-semester program covering visual communication, typography, editorial design, digital media, branding, and advertising. The program emphasizes both conceptual thinking and technical craftsmanship.

#### Photography (within Communication Design)

Photography specialization within the Communication Design Diplom program. Covers artistic and applied photography, visual storytelling, and photographic techniques.
