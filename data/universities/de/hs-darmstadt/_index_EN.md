# Darmstadt University of Applied Sciences — Department of Design

The Department of Design (Fachbereich Gestaltung) at Darmstadt University of Applied Sciences (h_da) offers two Diplom programs: **Industrial Design** and **Communication Design** (with a specialization in **Photography**). The programs are project-oriented and lead to the academic degree Diplom (FH).

## Programs

| Program | Degree | Semesters | Start |
|---|---|---|---|
| Industrial Design | Diplom | 8 | Winter semester |
| Communication Design | Diplom | 8 | Winter semester |
| Photography (specialization in CD) | Diplom | 8 | Winter semester |

## Special Features

- **Project-based Learning**: Project-oriented learning from the 1st semester
- **EUT+**: Cooperation within the European University of Technology
- **Research**: Institute for Design Research (IDF), Human Factors Lab
- **International**: Erasmus partnerships, international guest lecturers
- **Awards**: Merck Design Prize (since 2022)
- **Spring School**: Annual international project format
- **Workshops**: 8 workshops and labs (analog to digital)
- **Library**: ~14,000 titles on design, art, photography

## Application

- Online application: my.h-da.de (German applicants) / uni-assist.de (international applicants)
- Application period: April to June 1 (Winter semester)
- Aptitude test: June/July
- Requirements: Higher education entrance qualification + aptitude test (portfolio + assignment)
