# Industrial Design — Diplom

The Industrial Design program at h_da encompasses the design and development of concrete objects and product systems — from household and medical devices to furniture and vehicles. At the same time, it deals with strategic design solutions: planning, developing, and communicating interactions, processes, and services.

## Curriculum Structure

### Foundation (Semesters 1–4)
- Fundamentals of form-giving and design
- Design methodology and theory
- Technical drawing and representation
- Materials science and manufacturing
- Ergonomics
- CAD and digital tools
- **Pre-diploma** after semester 4

### Advanced (Semesters 5–8)
- Project work in the 4 teaching areas
- **Internship semester** in semester 5
- Deepening and specialization
- **Diploma thesis** (6 months)

## 4 Teaching Areas

1. **Design & Ergonomics** — Prof. Nicola Meloni, Prof. Jenny del Corte Hirschfeld
2. **Human-System Interaction** — Prof. Dr. Gesa Foken
3. **Design & Technical Design** — Prof. Tobias Katz, Prof. Tom Philipps, Prof. Mike Richter
4. **Spatial Design** — Prof. Carolin Himmel
