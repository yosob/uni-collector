# Communication Design — Diplom

The Communication Design program at h_da is dedicated to visual communication in all its forms — from typography and corporate design to poster design and editorial design, through to digital media and illustration. The program combines craft precision with conceptual depth.

## Curriculum Structure

### Foundation (Semesters 1–4)
- Fundamentals of Visual Communication
- Typography and typesetting
- Drawing and Illustration
- Design theory (colour, form, composition)
- Analogue and digital techniques
- Print workshop and screen printing
- **Pre-diploma** after semester 4

### Advanced (Semesters 5–8)
- Project work in the teaching areas
- **Internship semester** in semester 5
- Deepening and specialization
- **Diploma thesis** (6 months)

- Project-based study with small groups
- Traditional print techniques (lead typesetting, screen printing) and digital media
- Close integration with the Photography specialization
- EUT+ European University of Technology cooperation
- International exchange opportunities (Erasmus)
- Merck Design Prize
