# Photography — Diplom

> Note: Photography is formally a specialization within the Communication Design Diplom program. However, it is managed as an independent area with its own teaching and projects.

## Overview

The Photography specialization at h_da provides a comprehensive understanding of photographic visual language — from analogue darkroom work through digital post-production to artistic authorship.

## Curriculum Structure

### Foundation (Semesters 1–4)
- Fundamentals of photography (analogue and digital)
- Photographic visual language and composition
- Photo theory and history of images
- Darkroom techniques
- Studio photography (lighting technology)
- Digital post-production
- **Pre-diploma** after semester 4

### Advanced (Semesters 5–8)
- Project work: Documentary, artistic, and applied photography
- **Internship semester** in semester 5
- Development of an independent photographic position
- **Diploma thesis** (6 months)

## Facilities

- **Photo Studios**: 2 studios with professional lighting equipment
- **Post-Production Lab**: Analogue, digital, and hybrid
- **Darkroom**: Classic analogue development and enlargement

## Special Features

- Project-based study with small groups
- Strong analogue tradition complemented by digital techniques
- EUT+ European University of Technology cooperation
- International exchange opportunities (Erasmus)
