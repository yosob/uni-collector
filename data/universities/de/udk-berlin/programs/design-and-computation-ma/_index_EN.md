# Design and Computation (Master of Arts)

## Program Overview

Design and Computation Master — a joint program of UdK Berlin and TU Berlin. Combining design practice and computational knowledge. 4 semesters, 120 CP. A unique program at the intersection of design and technology.

## Key Features

- **Dual university partnership**: Joint degree from UdK Berlin + TU Berlin
- **Interdisciplinary**: Design + Computer Science
- **Cutting-edge fields**: Generative design, interactive design, parametric design

## Focus Areas

- **Generative Design**: Algorithm-driven creative design
- **Interactive Design**: Human-computer interaction and interactive systems
- **Design Informatics**: Computational methods in design
- **Parametric Design**: Parameter-driven architectural design
