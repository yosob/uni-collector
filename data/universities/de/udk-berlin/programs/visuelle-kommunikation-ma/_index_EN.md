# Visual Communication (Master of Arts)

## Program Overview

Visual Communication Master at UdK Berlin — in-depth artistic and academic engagement with visual design. 4 semesters, 120 CP.

## Application Process

1. **Online registration**: Create an account at my.udk-berlin.de
2. **Pay application fee**: EUR 30.00
3. **Submit application documents**: Portfolio, Bachelor's certificate, transcripts, etc.
4. **Pre-selection**: Based on submitted documents
5. **Aptitude test**: Interview / portfolio presentation

## Application Deadlines

| Semester | Opens | Deadline |
|----------|-------|----------|
| Winter Semester (WS) | March 1 | April 1 |

## Language Requirements

- **German**: C1 (B2 certificate accepted at the time of application)
