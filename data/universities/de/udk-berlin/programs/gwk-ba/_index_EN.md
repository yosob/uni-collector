# Society and Economy Communication / GWK (Bachelor of Arts)

## Program Overview

GWK Bachelor at UdK Berlin — an interdisciplinary program at the intersection of communication, design, and business. 6 semesters, 180 CP. A unique program combining artistic design with societal and economic communication.

## Application Process

1. **Online Registration**: Create an account at my.udk-berlin.de
2. **Pay Application Fee**: EUR 30.00
3. **Submit Work Sample**: Online submission via Bemus platform (max. 3 DIN A4 pages, 20 MB PDF)
4. **Pre-selection**: Based on work sample review
5. **Entrance Exam**: 4-hour online written exam
6. **Possible Interview**: Based on exam results

## Application Deadlines

| Semester | Start | Deadline |
|----------|-------|----------|
| Winter Semester (WS) | 01.03. | 01.04. |

## Portfolio Requirements (Work Sample)

- Maximum 3 DIN A4 pages
- Maximum 20 MB PDF
- Online submission via Bemus platform
- Should demonstrate communication and design skills

## Language Requirements

- **German**: C1 (B2 certificate accepted at time of application)
- **English**: B1 (A2 certificate accepted at time of application)
