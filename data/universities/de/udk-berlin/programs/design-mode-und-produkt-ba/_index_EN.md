# Design - Product Design & Fashion Design (Bachelor of Arts)

## Program Overview

Design Bachelor at UdK Berlin — a comprehensive design program with two specializations: Product Design and Fashion Design. The program is characterized by experimental design and interdisciplinary work. 8 semesters.

## Specializations

### Product Design
- Industrial product design
- Experimental design methods
- Material and process research
- Sustainable design

### Fashion Design
- Fashion design
- Experimental clothing design
- Material exploration
- Fashion and identity

## Application Process

1. **Online Registration**: Create an account at my.udk-berlin.de
2. **Pay Application Fee**: EUR 30.00
3. **Submit Portfolio**: Online submission via Bemus platform
4. **Pre-selection**: Based on portfolio review
5. **Admission Exam**: On-site exam
