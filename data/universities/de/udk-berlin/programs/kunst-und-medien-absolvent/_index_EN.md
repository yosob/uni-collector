# Art and Media — Graduate (Absolvent*in)

## Program Overview

Art and Media Graduate (Absolvent*in) at UdK Berlin — an artistic program focusing on audiovisual media (film, video, sound, animation, digital art). 6 semesters. Unique degree: Absolvent*in (not a conventional BA/MA).

## Highlights

- **Non-traditional degree**: Upon completion, you receive the title "Absolvent*in" (not a Bachelor or Master)
- **Summer + Winter intake**: Apply for either the summer or winter semester
- **Portfolio-oriented**: Emphasis on individual artistic development and creative work

## Admission Requirements

- At least 4 semesters of foundational studies in audiovisual media (120 CP)
- Proof of artistic aptitude

## Application Process

1. **Online registration**: Create an account at my.udk-berlin.de
2. **Pay application fee**: EUR 30.00
3. **Submit portfolio**: Upload via the Bemus platform (works + list of works)
4. **Pre-selection**: Based on portfolio review
5. **Aptitude test**: Portfolio presentation / interview

## Application Deadlines ⚠️

| Semester | Opens | Deadline |
|----------|-------|----------|
| **Winter Semester (WS)** | May 20 | June 20 |
| **Summer Semester (SS)** | Dec 20 | Jan 20 |

> ⚠️ **Note**: The winter semester application period is in May–June (not the usual February–April)! Summer semester applications are also accepted.

## Language Requirements

- **German**: B2
