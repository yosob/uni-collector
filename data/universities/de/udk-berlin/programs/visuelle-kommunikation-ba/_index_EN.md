# Visual Communication (Bachelor of Arts)

## Program Overview

Visual Communication Bachelor at UdK Berlin — comprehensive education in graphic design, typography, illustration, photography, and moving images. 8 semesters.

## Focus Areas

- **Graphic Design**: Graphic design, editorial design, poster design
- **Typography**: Type design, layout design
- **Illustration**: Illustration, visual storytelling
- **Photography**: Fine art photography, documentary photography
- **Moving Images**: Motion graphics, animation, video

## Application Process

1. **Online registration**: Create an account at my.udk-berlin.de
2. **Pay application fee**: EUR 30.00
3. **Submit portfolio**: Upload via the Bemus platform
4. **Pre-selection**: Based on portfolio review
5. **Aptitude test**: On-site examination

## Application Deadlines

| Semester | Opens | Deadline |
|----------|-------|----------|
| Winter Semester (WS) | March 1 | April 1 |

## Language Requirements

- **German**: C1 (B2 certificate accepted at the time of application)
