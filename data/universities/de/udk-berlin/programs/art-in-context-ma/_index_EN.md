# Art in Context (Master of Arts)

## Program Overview

Art in Context Master at UdK Berlin — a postgraduate program for artists with professional experience who want to develop their practice in social contexts. 4 semesters (or 6 part-time), 120 CP. Spans two faculties (Fine Arts and Design).

## Key Features

- **Part-time option**: 6-semester work-study model available
- **Interdisciplinary**: Intersection of art, design, and communication
- **Practice-oriented**: Project work closely integrated with social practice

## Admission Requirements ⚠️

- Bachelor's degree in Art/Design
- **At least 1 year of relevant work experience** (unique requirement!)
- Proof of artistic ability

## Application Process

1. **Online Registration**: Create an account at my.udk-berlin.de
2. **Pay Application Fee**: EUR 30.00
3. **Submit Work Samples**: 5-10 pages of artistic works (max. 5 files, 3 GB) via Bemus platform
4. **Pre-selection**: Based on submitted works
5. **Admission Exam**: Interview

## Application Deadlines

| Winter Semester (WS) | 15.02. | 15.03. |

> ⚠️ **Note**: The Art in Context application period (Feb-Mar) starts earlier than most other UdK programs!
