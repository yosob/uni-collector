# Society and Economy Communication / GWK (Master of Arts)

## Program Overview

GWK Master at UdK Berlin — advanced interdisciplinary studies at the intersection of strategic communication, design research, and societal design. 4 semesters, 120 CP.

## Application Process

1. **Online Registration**: Create an account at my.udk-berlin.de
2. **Pay Application Fee**: EUR 30.00
3. **Complete 4 Tasks** (submit via Bemus platform):
   - Task 1: Motivation letter
   - Task 2: Project description
   - Task 3: Course concept
   - Task 4: Creative task
4. **Pre-selection**: Based on submitted materials
5. **Entrance Exam**: Interview

## Application Deadlines ⚠️

| Semester | Start | Deadline |
|----------|-------|----------|
| Winter Semester (WS) | 01.04. | 30.04. |

> ⚠️ **Note**: The GWK MA application deadline (end of April) is later than other UdK programs (March - early April)!

## Application Task Details

- **Maximum 6 DIN A4 pages**
- **Optional video/audio**: Max. 5 minutes, 500 MB
- Online submission via Bemus platform

## Language Requirements

- **German**: C1 (B2 certificate accepted at time of application)
- **English**: B1 (A2 certificate accepted at time of application)
