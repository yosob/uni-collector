# Design - Product Design & Fashion Design (Master of Arts)

## Program Overview

Design Master at UdK Berlin — advanced engagement with product design or fashion design at an artistic-experimental level. 4 semesters.

## Specializations

### Product Design
### Fashion Design

## Application Process

1. **Online Registration**: Create an account at my.udk-berlin.de
2. **Pay Application Fee**: EUR 30.00
3. **Submit Application Documents**: Portfolio, bachelor's certificate, transcripts, etc.
4. **Pre-selection**: Based on application documents
5. **Admission Exam**: Interview / portfolio presentation
