# Art and Media — Master Student (Meisterschüler*in)

## Program Overview

Art and Media Meisterschüler*in at UdK Berlin — the highest artistic qualification level. 2 semesters. Individual artistic work under professorial supervision. Unique Meisterschüler degree.

## Highlights

- **Highest artistic qualification**: The highest-level artistic degree at UdK Berlin
- **Master mentorship**: Individual artistic creation under professorial guidance
- **Short and intensive**: Only 2 semesters
- **Summer + Winter intake**: Apply for either the summer or winter semester

## Available Supervising Professors (Professor*innen)

- **Thomas Arslan** — Film/Video
- **Alberto de Campo** — Sound/Digital Art
- **Nina Fischer** — Media Art/Photography

## Admission Requirements

- Degree in Art and Media (Absolvent*in or equivalent Master's)
- Minimum grade "sehr gut" (very good)
- Supervision commitment from a professor (Betreuungszusage)

## Application Process

1. **Contact a professor**: Obtain a supervision commitment from a professor
2. **Online registration**: Create an account at my.udk-berlin.de
3. **Pay application fee**: EUR 30.00
4. **Submit documents**: Supervision commitment + written project proposal
5. **Assessment**: Project presentation (Präsentation des Vorhabens)

## Application Deadlines

| Semester | Opens | Deadline |

| **Winter Semester (WS)** | May 20 | June 20 |
| **Summer Semester (SS)** | Dec 20 | Jan 20 |

## Language Requirements

- **German**: B2 **or** **English**: B2 (one is sufficient)
