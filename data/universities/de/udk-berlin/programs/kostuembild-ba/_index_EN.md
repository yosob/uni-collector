# Costume Design (Bachelor of Arts)

## Program Overview

Costume Design Bachelor at UdK Berlin — training in costume design for theatre, film, and performance. 8 semesters. Part of the Faculty of Performing Arts.

## Application Process

1. **Online Registration**: Create an account at my.udk-berlin.de
2. **Pay Application Fee**
3. **Submit Portfolio**
4. **Pre-selection**: Based on portfolio review
5. **Admission Exam**
