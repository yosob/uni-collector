# Architecture (Bachelor of Arts)

## Program Overview

Architecture Bachelor at UdK Berlin — an artistically oriented architecture program focusing on design, urban planning, and spatial design. 8 semesters, 240 CP.

## Application Process

1. **Online Registration**: Create an account at my.udk-berlin.de
2. **Pay Application Fee**: EUR 40.00
3. **Submit Portfolio**: Approx. 20 original works, max. DIN A 2, max. 5 kg, submit by post
4. **Pre-selection**: Based on portfolio review
5. **Admission Exam**: On-site exam (4-hour written exam + interview)

## Application Deadlines

| Semester | Start | Deadline |
|----------|-------|----------|
| Winter Semester (WS) | 05.02. | 05.03. |

## Portfolio Requirements

- Approx. 20 original works
- Theme-based
- Maximum size: DIN A 2
- Maximum weight: 5 kg
- Submit by post

## Language Requirements

- **German**: C1 (B2 certificate accepted at time of application)
