# Berlin University of the Arts (UdK)

The Universität der Künste Berlin (UdK) is one of the largest, most versatile, and most tradition-rich art universities in the world. It unites all artistic disciplines and their related sciences across four faculties — Bildende Kunst (Fine Arts), Gestaltung (Design), Musik (Music), and Darstellende Kunst (Performing Arts) — offering more than 70 degree programs for approximately 4,000 students. With full doctoral and habilitation rights, the UdK Berlin is one of the few art universities in Germany with full university status, and its history traces back to the founding of the Brandenburg-Prussian Academy of Arts in 1696.
