# University of the Arts Bremen (HfK)

The University of the Arts Bremen (Hochschule für Künste Bremen, HfK) is a public art and music university with approximately 1,000 students from around 70 countries. Founded in 1873, it offers programs in Fine Arts, Integrated Design, Digital Media, and Music, as well as a PhD program established in 2020.

The HfK is located at Speicher XI in the Überseestadt district of Bremen, with a second campus (Dechanatstraße) for the Music department. With 65 professors and over 400 public events per year, the HfK is both regionally rooted and internationally oriented.

## Art & Design Programs

### Design Programs
1. **Integrated Design B.A.** — 7 semesters, 210 ECTS, German/English
2. **Integrated Design M.A.** — 3 semesters, 90 ECTS, German/English

### Digital Media Programs (Joint with Universität Bremen)
3. **Digital Media B.A.** — 6 semesters, 180 ECTS, German/English
4. **Digital Media M.A./M.Sc.** — 4 semesters, 120 ECTS, English

### Fine Arts
5. **Fine Arts Diplom** — 10 semesters, 300 ECTS, German (equivalent to Master's degree)
