# University of the Arts Bremen

The University of the Arts Bremen (HfK) is a public art and music university with approximately 1,000 students from around 70 countries. It offers programs in Fine Arts, Integrated Design, Digital Media, and Music, as well as a PhD program established in 2020. With 65 professors and over 400 public events per year, the HfK is both regionally rooted and internationally oriented.
