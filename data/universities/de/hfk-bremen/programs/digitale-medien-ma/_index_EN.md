## Program Description

The Digital Media master's program is a joint project between HfK Bremen and Universität Bremen, taught entirely in English. Aimed at graduates of design, art, computer science, and related fields, it offers in-depth study of digital media. The curriculum emphasizes the intersection of design, theory, and new technologies, cultivating students' research and creative capabilities in the digital media field.

Students can choose to focus on Media Design at HfK or Media Informatics at Universität Bremen.

## Specializations

- **Media Design**: At HfK Bremen, focusing on design and art practice
- **Media Informatics**: At Universität Bremen, focusing on technology development
- **Interaction Design**: Human-computer interaction, interface innovation
- **Physical Computing**: Sensors, embedded systems
- **Generative Art & Design**: Algorithmic creation, AI art
- **Virtual / Augmented Reality**: Immersive experiences
- **Creative Coding**: Processing, openFrameworks, etc.
- **Sound Art**: Sound design, interactive sound
- **Media Theory**: Critical media studies

## Curriculum Structure

### Semesters 1-2
- Specialization Projects
- Theory Seminars
- Research Methods
- Interdisciplinary Collaboration

### Semesters 3-4
- Master Project
- Master's Thesis
- Master Colloquium (defense)

## Application Information

### Timeline (WS 2026/27)
- **Application Period**: February 1 — May 15
- **Application Portal**: https://application.hfk-bremen.de/

### Requirements
- Bachelor's degree (B.A. or B.Sc.) in a relevant field or equivalent
- At least 150 ECTS (if degree not yet completed)
- English proficiency: CEFR C1 / TOEFL iBT ≥95 / IELTS 7.0

### Application Materials
1. **Letter of Motivation**: One page, explaining motivation, interests, and expectations
2. **Portfolio**: 10-20 works, including artistic, design, academic, or professional pieces
3. **Curriculum Vitae (CV)**: Concise tabular CV
4. **Degree Certificate**: Bachelor's or equivalent
5. **English Certificate**: TOEFL iBT ≥95 / IELTS 7.0 / Cambridge CAE / CEFR C1
