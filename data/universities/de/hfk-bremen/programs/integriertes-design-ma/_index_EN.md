## Program Description

The Master's program in Integrated Design is a 3-semester advanced course for applicants with a degree in design or a related discipline. Building on the interdisciplinary approach of the bachelor's program, it emphasizes independent research-oriented design and academic depth.

Students can specialize in Product Design (Produkt-Design), Communication Design (Kommunikations-Design), or Design Research (Designforschung). The master's program focuses on self-directed project work, interdisciplinary collaboration, and the integration of theory and practice.

## Specializations

- **Product Design**: Industrial design, product systems, sustainable design
- **Communication Design**: Visual communication, branding, information design
- **Design Research**: Design methodology, design theory, research-based design practice

## Curriculum Structure

### Semesters 1-2
- Specialization projects
- Theory seminars
- Interdisciplinary collaboration
- Research methods

### Semester 3
- Master's thesis
- Oral examination
- Final exhibition
