## Program Description

The Digital Media bachelor's program is a joint project between HfK Bremen and Universität Bremen. Students study Media Design (Mediengestaltung) at HfK and Media Informatics (Medieninformatik) at Universität Bremen. The program sits at the intersection of design, theory, and new technologies, cultivating students' interdisciplinary creative abilities in the digital media field.

The curriculum covers interaction design, interface design, generative design, new media art, web design, video, animation, typography, photography, and more, emphasizing experimental methods and innovative practice.

## Specializations

- **Media Design**: At HfK Bremen, focusing on design practice
- **Media Informatics**: At Universität Bremen, focusing on technical implementation
- **Interaction Design**: Interface design, user experience
- **Generative Design**: Creative coding, algorithmic design
- **New Media Art**: Digital art, media installations
- **Video / Animation**: Experimental film, motion graphics
- **Sound Design**: Sound art, interactive sound
- **Physical Computing**: Hardware interaction, sensor art

## Application Information

### Timeline (WS 2026/27)
- **Application Period**: February 1 — May 15
- **Portfolio Upload**: May 19–21 (until 18:00 CEST)
- **Portfolio Review**: May 28–June 2
- **Results Notification**: from June 8
- **On-site Exam**: June 23–24
- **Final Results**: from June 30
- **Application Portal**: https://application.hfk-bremen.de/

### Application Process
1. Register and submit online application at the portal
2. Upload digital portfolio (interaction design, generative design, new media art, web design, video, animation, typography, photography, drawing, conceptual works, etc.)
3. Submit letter of motivation and CV
4. If shortlisted, attend on-site exam (assigned task + subject interview)

### Application Materials
1. **Portfolio (Digital)**: Digital works demonstrating design and artistic ability
2. **Letter of Motivation**
3. **Curriculum Vitae (CV)**
4. **Academic Certificate**: Abitur or equivalent
5. **Language Certificate**: DSH-2 / TestDaF 4x4 / telc Deutsch C1
