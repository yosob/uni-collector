## Program Description

The Integrated Design bachelor's program is the core design program at HfK Bremen, employing an interdisciplinary approach that integrates product design, communication design, and media design. Students are not limited to a single design discipline but freely explore and combine multiple design fields through a flexible curriculum.

The program emphasizes design research, experimental methods, and critical thinking, encouraging students to build connections across different design areas. The curriculum covers typography, photography, illustration, drawing, 3D design, digital media, and more.

## Specializations

- **Typography**: Type design, layout, print design
- **Photography**: Art photography, documentary photography, commercial photography
- **Illustration**: Editorial illustration, picture books, visual storytelling
- **Drawing**: Sketching, color painting, experimental drawing
- **3D Design**: Product design, spatial design, material design
- **Design Research**: Research methodology, design theory
- **Digital Media**: Interaction design, motion graphics, digital tools
- **Gender / Interdisciplinary Studies**: Gender studies, cross-disciplinary topics

## Curriculum Structure

### Foundation Studies — Semesters 1-2
- Artistic Fundamentals
- Design Fundamentals
- Workshop Introductions
- Theory and History of Design

### Main Studies — Semesters 3-6
- Project Work in Specializations
- Advanced Study in Focus Areas
- Interdisciplinary Projects
- Theory Seminars

### Final Semester — Semester 7
- Bachelor's Thesis
- Bachelor Exhibition

## Application Information

### Application Timeline
- **Application Period**: March 1 — April 15 (winter semester)
- **Application Portal**: https://campus.hfk-bremen.de/
- **Portal Opens**: February 1

### Application Materials
1. **Portfolio (Digital)**: Approx. 20-30 original works demonstrating artistic and design ability, PDF format
2. **High School Certificate**: Abitur or equivalent
3. **Curriculum Vitae (CV)**
4. **Letter of Motivation**
5. **Language Certificate**: DSH-2 / TestDaF 4x4 / telc Deutsch C1 Hochschule

### Application Process
1. Register at Campus-Net portal
2. Upload portfolio (digital submission)
3. Attend artistic aptitude assessment (Eignungsprüfung)
4. Interview (by invitation)
