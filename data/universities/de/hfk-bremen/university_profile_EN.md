## How to Apply

1. **Portals:**
   - Integrated Design / Fine Arts: https://campus.hfk-bremen.de/
   - Digital Media: https://application.hfk-bremen.de/
2. **Deadlines:**
   - Integrated Design & Fine Arts: March 1 — April 15
   - Digital Media: February 1 — May 15
3. **Process:**
   - Online registration and application
   - Upload digital portfolio
   - Aptitude assessment (on-site exam for some programs)
   - Interview (by invitation)
4. **Language:** DSH-2 / TestDaF 4x4 / telc Deutsch C1 (German programs); CEFR C1 / TOEFL iBT ≥95 / IELTS 7.0 (Digital Media M.A.)
5. **Fees:** No tuition; semester contribution ~€350-413 (incl. semester ticket)
