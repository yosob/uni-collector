# Visuelle Kommunikation / Visual Communication (Diplom)

## Program Overview

The Visual Communication program at Kunsthochschule Kassel is a 10-semester artistic-design program leading to a Diplom degree. It is one of the most diverse visual communication programs in Germany, covering a wide spectrum from traditional graphic design to cutting-edge digital media.

The program emphasizes experimental and project-based learning, encouraging students to develop their own visual language across multiple media formats.

## Focus Areas

- **Animation** — Classical and digital animation techniques, storytelling through motion
- **Film und bewegtes Bild** — Film production, cinematography, experimental film, video art
- **Intermediale Fotografie** — Intermedia photography, documentary and artistic photography
- **Games** — Game design, interactive media, game art
- **Illustration/Comic** — Illustration, comic art, visual storytelling, editorial illustration
- **Neue Medien** — New media art, interactive installations, digital culture
- **Grafikdesign** — Graphic design, typography, editorial design, visual identity
- **Sound** — Sound design, audio art, acoustic communication

## Study Structure

- **Grundstudium (Semester 1-4):** Foundations in visual design principles, media technology, and creative methodology. Exploration of different visual communication disciplines.
- **Hauptstudium (Semester 5-10):** Specialization in chosen focus areas. Independent project work, cross-media experimentation, and preparation of the Diplom thesis.

## Admission

Admission requires a general higher education entrance qualification (Abitur) and successful completion of the artistic aptitude test (Eignungsprüfung). This involves portfolio submission and, upon pre-selection, an interview. Exceptional artistic talent may qualify applicants without formal qualifications.

## Application

- **Deadline:** March 15 (for winter semester)
- **Portal:** Online application portal of Kunsthochschule Kassel
- **Requirements:** Portfolio with visual communication work, CV, proof of qualifications
