# Design (Diplom)

## Program Overview

The Design program at Kunsthochschule Kassel is a comprehensive 10-semester artistic-design program leading to a Diplom degree. It offers a uniquely interdisciplinary approach to design education, integrating multiple design disciplines under one roof.

Students develop their own design position through project-based work in workshops and studios, guided by professors across various design specializations. The program combines practical design work with theoretical reflection.

## Focus Areas

- **Industrial Design** — Industrial product design, user-centered design, design for sustainability
- **Furniture and Exhibition Design** — Furniture and exhibition design, spatial design concepts
- **Textile Product Design** — Textile product design, material innovation, surface design
- **Theory and Practice of Design** — Design theory, design methodology, creative processes
- **Sustainable Product Design and Development** — Sustainable product design and development
- **Design Management** — Design strategy, project management, design leadership

## Study Structure

- **Basic Studies (Semesters 1-4):** Foundations in design thinking, methods, and techniques. Introduction to various material and digital design tools.
- **Main Studies (Semesters 5-10):** Specialization in chosen focus areas. Project-based work, interdisciplinary collaborations, and preparation of the Diplom thesis.

## Admission

Admission requires a general higher education entrance qualification (Abitur) and successful completion of the artistic aptitude test. This involves portfolio submission and, upon pre-selection, an interview with the admissions committee. Exceptional artistic talent may qualify applicants even without formal university entrance qualifications.

## Application

- **Deadline:** March 15 (for winter semester)
- **Portal:** Online application portal of Kunsthochschule Kassel
- **Requirements:** Portfolio with original design/artistic work, CV, proof of qualifications
