# Art Education — Teaching Degree (Master of Education)

## Program Overview

The Art Education teaching degree at Kunsthochschule Kassel is a 4-semester Master of Education (M.Ed.) program offered in cooperation with the University of Kassel. It prepares students to become art teachers at various school levels in the German education system.

The program combines artistic practice with art pedagogy and educational sciences, providing students with both creative skills and teaching methodology.

## School-Type Specializations

- **Primary School (Grundschule)** — Art education for ages 6-10
- **Secondary School Level I (Haupt- und Realschule)** — Art education for lower secondary levels
- **Gymnasium** — Art education for secondary levels I and II (up to Abitur)
- **Special Education / Inclusion (Förderschule)** — Art education with focus on special needs and inclusive pedagogy

## Study Structure

The 4-semester Master program consists of:
- **Art as Subject (Fachwissenschaft Kunst)** — Deepening art theory and practice
- **Art Education Didactics (Fachdidaktik Kunst)** — Methodology and didactics of art education
- **Educational Sciences (Bildungswissenschaften)** — Educational theory and pedagogy
- **School Internships (Schulpraktika)** — Practical teaching experience in schools

## Admission

Admission requires a Bachelor's degree in a teaching-related program or equivalent qualification, with proof of subject-specific and pedagogical prerequisites for the chosen school type. German language proficiency at C1 level (DSH-2 or TestDaF TDN 4) is required.

## Application

- **Deadline:** July 15 (for winter semester; may vary by school type)
- **Portal:** Application portal of the University of Kassel
- **Note:** This program is offered in cooperation with the University of Kassel
