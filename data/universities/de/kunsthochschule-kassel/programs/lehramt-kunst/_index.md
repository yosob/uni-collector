# Lehramt Kunst / Art Education (Master of Education)

## Program Overview

The Art Education teaching degree at Kunsthochschule Kassel is a 4-semester Master of Education (M.Ed.) program offered in cooperation with the University of Kassel (Universität Kassel). It prepares students to become art teachers at various school levels in the German education system.

The program combines artistic practice with art pedagogy and educational sciences, providing students with both creative skills and teaching methodology.

## School Type Specializations

- **Grundschule (Primary School)** — Art education for ages 6-10
- **Haupt- und Realschule** — Art education for secondary school levels I
- **Gymnasium** — Art education for secondary school levels I and II (up to Abitur)
- **Förderschule / Inklusion** — Art education with focus on special needs and inclusive pedagogy

## Study Structure

The 4-semester Master program consists of:
- **Fachwissenschaft Kunst** — Art theory and practice deepening
- **Fachdidaktik Kunst** — Art education methodology and didactics
- **Bildungswissenschaften** — Educational sciences and pedagogy
- **Schulpraktika** — School internships and practical teaching experience

## Admission

Admission requires a Bachelor's degree in a teaching-related program or equivalent qualification, with proof of subject-specific and pedagogical prerequisites for the chosen school type. German language proficiency at C1 level (DSH-2 or TestDaF TDN 4) is required.

## Application

- **Deadline:** July 15 (for winter semester; may vary by school type)
- **Portal:** Application portal of the University of Kassel
- **Note:** This program is offered in cooperation with Universität Kassel
