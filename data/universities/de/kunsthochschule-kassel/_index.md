# Kunsthochschule Kassel

The Kunsthochschule Kassel is an art school in Kassel, Hesse, offering programs in fine art (Bildende Kunst), art education (Lehramt Kunst), art history (Kunstwissenschaft), design, and visual communication (Visuelle Kommunikation). The school also houses the documenta Institut and operates a graduate school for moving image (Bewegtbild) alongside postgraduate programs including Meisterschuler and doctoral studies. Students have access to extensive workshop facilities including digital labs, a fablab, photography, wood, ceramics, and metal workshops.
