## How to Apply

### Artistic Programs (Design, Visual Communication, Fine Art)
1. **Timeline**: Winter semester only, apply by March 15
2. **Materials**: Portfolio of artistic/design work, CV, proof of qualifications
3. **Process**: Online registration → Portfolio submission → Pre-selection → Aptitude test with interview
4. **Special policy**: Exceptional artistic talent may qualify without formal Abitur

### Academic Programs (Art History B.A./M.A.)
1. **Timeline**: Winter semester, apply by July 15
2. **Materials**: Proof of qualifications, CV, (for M.A.) motivation letter and B.A. certificate
3. **Process**: Apply via the online portal of Kunsthochschule Kassel or University of Kassel

### Teaching Degree (Art Education M.Ed.)
1. **Timeline**: Winter semester, apply by July 15
2. **Materials**: Teaching-related B.A. degree, proof of pedagogical prerequisites
3. **Process**: Apply via the University of Kassel application portal
