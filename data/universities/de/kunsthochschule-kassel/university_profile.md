## Overview

Die Kunsthochschule Kassel (KhK) ist eine der ältesten Kunsthochschulen Deutschlands mit einer über 240-jährigen Tradition. Als teilrechtsfähige Einrichtung der Universität Kassel bietet sie ein breites Spektrum an künstlerischen, gestalterischen und wissenschaftlichen Studiengängen.

Besonders bekannt ist die Hochschule durch ihre Verbindung zur **documenta**, der weltweit bedeutendsten Ausstellung zeitgenössischer Kunst, die alle fünf Jahre in Kassel stattfindet. Viele Lehrende und Studierende der KhK sind eng mit der documenta verbunden.

## Program Landscape

### Artistic Programs (Diplom — 10 Semesters)

1. **Bildende Kunst (Fine Art)**
   - Unique class-based system (Klassenmodell)
   - 7 artist classes led by renowned professors
   - Basisklasse foundation year before joining a class
   - Focus: Painting, sculpture, installation, media art, performance, conceptual art

2. **Design**
   - Interdisciplinary design education
   - Focus areas: Industrial design, furniture/exhibition design, textile design, sustainable design, design management
   - Project-based learning in well-equipped workshops

3. **Visuelle Kommunikation (Visual Communication)**
   - Broad spectrum of visual media
   - Focus areas: Animation, film, photography, games, illustration/comic, new media, graphic design, sound
   - Experimental and cross-media approach

### Teaching Degree (Master of Education — 4 Semesters)

4. **Lehramt Kunst (Art Education)**
   - Offered in cooperation with Universität Kassel
   - School types: Grundschule, Haupt- und Realschule, Gymnasium, Förderschule/Inklusion
   - Combines artistic practice with art pedagogy

### Academic Programs

5. **Kunstwissenschaft B.A. (Art History Bachelor — 6 Semesters)**
   - Art history from Middle Ages to contemporary
   - Direct exchange with art and design students
   - Fachgebiete: Medieval/Modern Art History, Classical Modernism/Contemporary Art, Aesthetics/Art Theory

6. **Kunstwissenschaft M.A. (Art History Master — 4 Semesters)**
   - Research-oriented program
   - Curatorial practice and museum studies
   - Preparation for doctoral studies

## Application & Admission

### Artistic Programs (Bildende Kunst, Design, Visuelle Kommunikation)
- **Deadline:** March 15 (winter semester only)
- **Process:** Portfolio submission → Pre-selection → Aptitude test with interview
- **Special:** Exceptional artistic talent may qualify without formal qualifications (Abitur)
- **Portal:** Online application portal of KhK

### Academic Programs (Kunstwissenschaft)
- **Deadline:** July 15 (winter semester)
- **Process:** Standard application with proof of qualifications
- **Language:** DSH-2 / TestDaF TDN 4 (C1)

### Teaching Degree (Lehramt)
- **Deadline:** July 15 (winter semester; may vary by school type)
- **Process:** Application through Universität Kassel portal
- **Requirements:** Bachelor's degree in related field

## Special Features

- **documenta connection:** Close ties to the world's most important contemporary art exhibition
- **Class system:** Personal mentoring by established artists in small groups
- **Interdisciplinary:** Art, design, visual communication, art history, and education under one roof
- **Small size:** ~800 students ensure intimate class sizes and personal attention
- **University of Kassel connection:** Access to university resources while maintaining artistic autonomy
- **Kassel art scene:** Vibrant local art scene with galleries, museums, and cultural events

## Profile Summary

Kunsthochschule Kassel is a compact but highly respected art academy that combines tradition with innovation. Its unique position as part of the University of Kassel while maintaining artistic autonomy, combined with the documenta legacy, makes it one of the most distinctive art schools in Germany. The interdisciplinary approach—bringing fine art, design, visual communication, art history, and art education together—creates a rich learning environment where students can develop their own artistic positions with strong theoretical grounding.
