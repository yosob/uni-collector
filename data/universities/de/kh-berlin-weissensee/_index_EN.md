# Berlin Weißensee School of Art and Design (Kunsthochschule Berlin-Weißensee)

## Overview

The Weißensee Kunsthochschule Berlin (KHB) is a public art and design university founded in 1946, located in the Weißensee district of Berlin. With approximately 800 students, it is one of Germany's leading art schools, offering programs across fine art and design disciplines.

The university is known for its interdisciplinary approach, with all students completing a one-year shared foundation program (Künstlerische Grundlagen) before specializing. Strong emphasis is placed on sustainability, social responsibility, and experimental approaches to art and design.

## Departments (Fachgebiete)

### Design Programs (BA/MA)
- **Product Design** — Industrial, sustainable, and social design (BA 8 sem / MA 4 sem)
- **Fashion Design** — Sustainable and experimental fashion (BA 8 sem / MA 4 sem)
- **Textile and Material Design** — Smart materials and textile innovation (BA 8 sem / MA 4 sem)
- **Visual Communication** — Graphic design, typography, illustration (BA 8 sem / MA 4 sem)

### Fine Art Programs (Diplom, 10 semesters)
- **Painting** — Painting (Diplom Freie Kunst)
- **Sculpture** — Sculpture (Diplom Freie Kunst)
- **Stage and Costume Design** — Stage and costume design (Diplom Freie Kunst)

### Graduate-only Programs
- **Art Therapy** — Art therapy (MA, 6 sem, tuition-based ~5,400€/sem)
- **Spatial Strategies** — Spatial strategies, public art (MA, 4 sem)

## Application
- **Application portal**: [bewerbung.kh-berlin.de](https://bewerbung.kh-berlin.de)
- **BA programs**: Application deadline ~March 15 for winter semester
- **MA programs**: Deadlines vary (December–March)
- All programs require portfolio submission and aptitude test (Eignungsprüfung)
- Programs are mostly tuition-free (semester fee ~358€ including BVG ticket)
- Exception: Art Therapy MA has tuition fees (~5,400€/semester)
