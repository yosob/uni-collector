# Weissensee School of Art and Design Berlin

Founded in 1946 as an alternative to the traditional academy and rooted in Bauhaus ideas, the Weissensee Kunsthochschule Berlin offers a unique interdisciplinary foundation year followed by diploma programs in Fine Art and bachelor/master programs in Design for over 800 students. The school covers disciplines including sculpture, painting, stage and costume design, fashion design, product design, textile and material design, and visual communication, complemented by master's programs in art therapy and spatial strategies. Its 19 workshops and research labs focus on experimental research into sustainable design strategies and media technologies.
