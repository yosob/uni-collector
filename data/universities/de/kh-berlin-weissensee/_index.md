# Weißensee School of Art and Design Berlin

Founded in 1946 as an alternative to the traditional academy and rooted in Bauhaus ideas, the weißensee kunsthochschule berlin offers a unique interdisciplinary foundation year (Künstlerische Grundlagen) followed by diploma programs in Fine Art and bachelor/master programs in Design. With approximately 834 students, the school is located in the Weißensee district of Berlin in a historical building (former chocolate factory).

## Study Structure

The school is distinctive for its **one-year interdisciplinary foundation program** (Künstlerische Grundlagen), which all design students complete before specializing. This is complemented by extensive workshop facilities and research labs.

### Design Programs (Bachelor + Master)
- Produkt-Design (BA 8 semesters / MA 4 semesters)
- Mode-Design (BA 8 semesters / MA 4 semesters)
- Textil- und Material-Design (BA 8 semesters / MA 4 semesters)
- Visuelle Kommunikation (BA 8 semesters / MA 4 semesters)

### Fine Art Programs (Diplom, 10 semesters)
- Malerei (Painting)
- Bildhauerei (Sculpture)
- Bühnen- und Kostümbild (Stage and Costume Design)

### Master-only Programs
- Kunsttherapie (MA, 6 semesters part-time, summer intake)
- Raumstrategien (MA, 4 semesters part-time)
