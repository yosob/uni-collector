# Stage and Costume Design (Fine Art)

## Overview

The Stage and Costume Design department at Weißensee Kunsthochschule Berlin offers a 10-semester Diplom program in Fine Art. Students develop skills in scenography, theatre spatial design, and costume design for theatre, opera, and film. The program benefits from close collaboration with Berlin's vibrant theatre and opera scene.

## Degree Program

### Diplom Stage and Costume Design (Fine Art, 10 semesters)
- Full-time, winter semester intake
- Diplom degree in Fine Art (equivalent to MA)
- First year: interdisciplinary foundation (Künstlerische Grundlagen)
- Focus: stage design, costume design, scenography, theatre/film spatial design
- Application deadline: ~March 15
- Collaboration with Berlin theatres and opera houses
