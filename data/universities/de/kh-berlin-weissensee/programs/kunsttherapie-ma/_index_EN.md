# Art Therapy — MA

## Overview

The Art Therapy program (MA) at Weißensee Kunsthochschule Berlin is a 6-semester continuing education Master's program that combines artistic practice with therapeutic theory and clinical practice. It covers clinical art therapy, psychosocial counseling, trauma therapy, and the role of artistic processes in therapeutic settings. The program is located at a dedicated campus location.

## Degree Program

### MA Art Therapy (6 semesters)
- Full-time, winter semester intake
- Master of Arts degree
- Combines artistic practice with therapeutic theory
- Includes clinical placements
- **Tuition fees apply: approximately 5,400€ per semester**
- Requires professional experience in social/health/pedagogical field
- Located at dedicated Kunsttherapie campus
