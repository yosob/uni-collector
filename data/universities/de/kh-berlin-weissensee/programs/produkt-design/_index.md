# Produkt-Design (产品/工业设计)

## Overview

The Product Design department at weißensee kunsthochschule berlin offers both BA and MA programs focused on sustainable design strategies, interaction design, and social design. Students begin with a one-year interdisciplinary foundation before specializing.

## Degree Programs

### BA Produkt-Design (8 semesters, 240 ECTS)
- Full-time, winter semester intake
- Includes interdisciplinary foundation year
- Focus: sustainable product design, interaction design, social design

### MA Produkt-Design (4 semesters, 120 ECTS)
- Full-time, winter semester intake
- Advanced design research and sustainable design strategies
- Application deadline: November 10
