# Product Design

## Overview

The Product Design department (BA/MA) at Weißensee Kunsthochschule Berlin focuses on sustainable, socially responsible, and experimental product design. Students benefit from extensive workshop facilities including model-making studios (wood, metal, plastics), digital fabrication labs, and the greenlab research platform.

## Degree Programs

### BA Product Design (8 semesters, 240 ECTS)
- Full-time, winter semester intake
- First year: interdisciplinary foundation (Künstlerische Grundlagen)
- Focus: industrial design, sustainable design, social design, interaction design
- Application deadline: ~March 15

### MA Product Design (4 semesters, 120 ECTS)
- Full-time, winter semester intake
- Advanced design research with individual project focus
- Application deadline: ~December 31
