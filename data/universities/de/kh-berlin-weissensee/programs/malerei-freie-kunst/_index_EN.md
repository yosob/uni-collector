# Painting – Fine Art

## Overview

The Painting department at Weißensee Kunsthochschule Berlin offers a 10-semester Diplom program in Fine Art (Freie Kunst). Students develop their own artistic position through intensive studio practice, individual tutorials, and theoretical seminars. The program emphasizes contemporary painting practices while encouraging cross-disciplinary exploration.

## Degree Program

### Diplom Painting (Fine Art, 10 semesters)
- Full-time, winter semester intake
- Diplom degree in Fine Art (equivalent to MA)
- First year: interdisciplinary foundation (Künstlerische Grundlagen)
- Focus: painting, drawing, printmaking, contemporary art
- Application deadline: ~March 15
- Access to printmaking workshops, photography studio, computer studio
