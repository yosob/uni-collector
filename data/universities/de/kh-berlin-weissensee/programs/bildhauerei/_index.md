# Bildhauerei (雕塑)

## Overview

The Sculpture department offers a 10-semester Diplom program in Fine Art (Freie Kunst). Students develop their artistic practice through work with diverse materials and techniques, benefiting from the school's bronze foundry, ceramics, metal, and mixed media workshops.

## Degree Program

### Diplom Bildhauerei (Freie Kunst, 10 semesters)
- Full-time, winter semester intake
- Diplom degree in Fine Art
- Focus: sculpture, installation, spatial art
- Application deadline: May 31
