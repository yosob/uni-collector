# Malerei (绘画)

## Overview

The Painting department offers a 10-semester Diplom program in Fine Art (Freie Kunst). Students develop their individual artistic practice through intensive studio work, critical discourse, and engagement with contemporary painting.

## Degree Program

### Diplom Malerei (Freie Kunst, 10 semesters)
- Full-time, winter semester intake
- Diplom degree in Fine Art
- Focus: contemporary painting, artistic practice, critical discourse
