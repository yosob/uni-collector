# Sculpture – Fine Art

## Overview

The Sculpture department at Weißensee Kunsthochschule Berlin offers a 10-semester Diplom program in Fine Art. Students develop skills in three-dimensional form, installation, and spatial art. The workshops for bronze casting, ceramics, and metal/woodworking provide excellent working conditions.

## Degree Program

### Diplom Sculpture (Fine Art, 10 semesters)
- Full-time, winter semester intake
- Diplom degree in Fine Art (equivalent to MA)
- First year: interdisciplinary foundation (Künstlerische Grundlagen)
- Focus: sculpture, plastic arts, installation, spatial art
- Application deadline: ~March 15
