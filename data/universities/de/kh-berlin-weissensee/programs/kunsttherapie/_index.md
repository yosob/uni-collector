# Kunsttherapie (艺术治疗)

## Overview

The Art Therapy program is a 6-semester part-time Master's program (continuing education/Weiterbildung). It is the only program at weißensee kunsthochschule berlin that charges tuition fees and admits students in the summer semester only.

## Degree Program

### MA Kunsttherapie (6 semesters, part-time)
- Part-time, summer semester intake only
- Master of Arts
- Tuition: 5.400€ per semester
- Focus: art therapy theory and clinical practice
