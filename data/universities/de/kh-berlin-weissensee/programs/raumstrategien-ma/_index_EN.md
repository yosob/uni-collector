# Spatial Strategies — MA

## Overview

The Spatial Strategies program (MA) at Weißensee Kunsthochschule Berlin is a 4-semester Master's program focusing on artistic engagement with public space, urban interventions, art in the public realm, and the social dimensions of space. The program takes an interdisciplinary approach combining art, architecture, and urban theory, with students developing site-specific projects in Berlin's dynamic urban fabric.

## Degree Program

### MA Spatial Strategies (4 semesters, 120 ECTS)
- Full-time, winter semester intake
- Master of Arts degree
- Interdisciplinary: art, architecture, urban theory
- Focus: public art, urban interventions, art in public space, social space
- Application deadline: ~March 15
- Students develop site-specific projects in Berlin
- Bilingual: German and English
