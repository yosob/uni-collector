# Mode-Design (时尚设计)

## Overview

The Fashion Design department offers BA and MA programs emphasizing sustainable fashion, experimental garment design, and innovative textile approaches. Students begin with a one-year interdisciplinary foundation before specializing.

## Degree Programs

### BA Mode-Design (8 semesters, 240 ECTS)
- Full-time, winter semester intake
- Includes interdisciplinary foundation year
- Focus: fashion design, sustainable fashion, experimental garment design

### MA Mode-Design (4 semesters, 120 ECTS)
- Full-time, winter semester intake
- Advanced fashion design research
- Application deadline: December 31
