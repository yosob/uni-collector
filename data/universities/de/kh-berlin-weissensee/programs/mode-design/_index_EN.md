# Fashion Design

## Overview

The Fashion Design department (BA/MA) at Weißensee Kunsthochschule Berlin emphasizes sustainable fashion, experimental garment design, and innovative textile approaches. Students have access to workshop facilities including sewing studios, digital embroidery, textile dyeing labs, and knitting/weaving workshops.

## Degree Programs

### BA Fashion Design (8 semesters, 240 ECTS)
- Full-time, winter semester intake
- First year: interdisciplinary foundation (Künstlerische Grundlagen)
- Focus: fashion design, sustainable fashion, experimental garment design
- Application deadline: ~March 15

### MA Fashion Design (4 semesters, 120 ECTS)
- Full-time, winter semester intake
- Advanced fashion design research with individual project
- Application deadline: ~December 31
