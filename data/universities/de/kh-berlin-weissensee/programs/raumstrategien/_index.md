# Raumstrategien (空间策略)

## Overview

The Spatial Strategies program is a 4-semester part-time Master's program focusing on public space design, urban interventions, and art in architectural and urban contexts. It intersects art, architecture, and urban planning.

## Degree Program

### MA Raumstrategien (4 semesters, part-time)
- Part-time, winter semester intake
- Master of Arts
- Focus: public space design, spatial strategies, urban interventions
- Application deadline: July 15
