# Bühnen- und Kostümbild (舞台与服装设计)

## Overview

The Stage and Costume Design department offers a 10-semester Diplom program in Fine Art (Freie Kunst). Students develop skills in scenography, theatre spatial design, and costume design for theatre, opera, and film.

## Degree Program

### Diplom Bühnen- und Kostümbild (Freie Kunst, 10 semesters)
- Full-time, winter semester intake
- Diplom degree in Fine Art
- Focus: stage design, costume design, scenography
