# Textil- und Material-Design (纺织与材料设计)

## Overview

The Textile and Material Design department offers BA and MA programs focusing on innovative textile design, smart materials, and sustainable material research. Students benefit from extensive workshop facilities including weaving, knitting, dyeing, and digital embroidery studios.

## Degree Programs

### BA Textil- und Material-Design (8 semesters, 240 ECTS)
- Full-time, winter semester intake
- Includes interdisciplinary foundation year
- Focus: textile design, smart materials, sustainable material research

### MA Textil- und Material-Design (4 semesters, 120 ECTS)
- Full-time, winter semester intake
- Advanced material research and design
