# Visuelle Kommunikation (视觉传达)

## Overview

The Visual Communication department offers BA and MA programs covering graphic design, typography, illustration, photography, and motion graphics. Students begin with a one-year interdisciplinary foundation before specializing in their chosen area.

## Degree Programs

### BA Visuelle Kommunikation (8 semesters, 240 ECTS)
- Full-time, winter semester intake
- Includes interdisciplinary foundation year
- Focus: graphic design, typography, illustration, photography, motion graphics

### MA Visuelle Kommunikation (4 semesters, 120 ECTS)
- Full-time, winter semester intake
- Advanced visual communication research and practice
