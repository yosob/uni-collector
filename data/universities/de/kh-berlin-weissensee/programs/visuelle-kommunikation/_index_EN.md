# Visual Communication

## Overview

The Visual Communication department (BA/MA) at Weißensee Kunsthochschule Berlin covers graphic design, typography, illustration, photography, motion graphics, and digital media. Students begin with a one-year interdisciplinary foundation before specializing in their chosen area.

## Degree Programs

### BA Visual Communication (8 semesters, 240 ECTS)
- Full-time, winter semester intake
- First year: interdisciplinary foundation (Künstlerische Grundlagen)
- Focus: graphic design, typography, illustration, photography, motion graphics
- Application deadline: ~March 15

### MA Visual Communication (4 semesters, 120 ECTS)
- Full-time, winter semester intake
- Advanced visual communication research and practice
- Application deadline: ~December 31
