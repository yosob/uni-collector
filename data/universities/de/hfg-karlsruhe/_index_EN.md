# Staatliche Hochschule für Gestaltung Karlsruhe

The Karlsruhe University of Arts and Design (HfG Karlsruhe) is a public art university located in Karlsruhe, Baden-Württemberg.

## Overview

HfG Karlsruhe officially opened on April 15, 1992, as a reform university. It was co-founded by Prof. Heinrich Klotz between 1989 and 1992, together with the ZKM | Center for Art and Media. This unique union of teaching, research, and exhibition institutions embodies the artistic and educational mission of combining traditional arts with media technology and electronic production processes.

## Distinctive Features

- **Unique Partnership with ZKM:** HfG and ZKM (Center for Art and Media) form the world's only combined teaching, research, and exhibition institution of its kind
- **Non-modularized Degree System:** Committed to academic freedom, the university does not offer bachelor's/master's degrees but awards Diplom and Magister degrees (fully compatible with the Bologna Process and internationally recognized)
- **Interdisciplinary Project Learning:** Cross-disciplinary project work from the first semester
- **Low Student-to-Faculty Ratio:** Small group teaching, typically no more than 10 students per professor
- **Unique Campus:** The university and ZKM share a former munitions factory (Industriebau) with unique light and space qualities

## Program Group Reform (from Winter Semester 2023/24)

Starting from the winter semester 2023/24, HfG merged its original 5 program groups into 3 administrative groups:

1. **Medienkunst** (Media Art)
2. **Produkt- und Kommunikationsdesign** (Product and Communication Design)
3. **Medienphilosophie und Kunstwissenschaft / Ausstellungsdesign und Szenografie**

**All 5 individual degree programs remain unchanged** — the reform only affects administrative structures.

## Application Process

1. **Portfolio Consultation** (bookable at the beginning of the year)
2. **Fast Open Application:** Online submission from March 1 to April 30
3. **Admission Examination** (Zulassungsverfahren): Multi-stage review
4. **Enrollment** (Immatrikulation): Complete by August 15

### Admission Requirements
- General or subject-specific university entrance qualification (Fachhochschulreife is not sufficient)
- Proof of artistic or professional aptitude through the admission examination
