## How to Apply

### All Programs
1. **Timeline**: Winter semester only, online application March 1 – April 30
2. **Materials**: Portfolio + letter of motivation (artistic programs), or academic writing sample + motivation letter (theory program)
3. **Process**: Portfolio consultation (optional) → Fast Open Application → Multi-stage admission examination → Enrollment by August 15
4. **Requirements**: General or subject-specific university entrance qualification (Fachhochschulreife is NOT sufficient)
5. **Special features**: Non-modularized degree system (Diplom/Magister, not Bachelor/Master); interdisciplinary project learning from the first semester
