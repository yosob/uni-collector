# Produktdesign (Product Design) — Diplom

## Program Overview

The Product Design program at HfG Karlsruhe addresses society's growing demand for modern design in private households, public spaces, and work environments. The discipline focuses particularly on socio-cultural change — beyond classic mass-production product design, product designers are increasingly expected to provide conceptual contributions that critically examine the diverse conditions of industrial culture.

## Educational Focus

- **Interdisciplinary Orientation:** Interdisciplinary project-based learning from the first semester
- **Project-Driven Learning:** Imparting both factual foundations and intuitive-emotional methods through concrete projects
- **New Perspectives and Forms:** Priority given to projects that showcase new perspectives in working fields and new forms of presentation and resource management
- **kkaarrlls Edition:** Since 2009, the product design department has participated in the Milan Furniture Fair and other events with its own collection edition, kkaarrlls
