# Kommunikationsdesign (Communication Design) — Diplom

## Program Overview

Design communication means giving form to reality. Communication Design students are challenged to integrate their skills into all areas of social life — whether through the design of books, posters, websites, films, exhibitions, or events, or through actions, objects, or "design raw materials" such as typefaces, illustrations, and photography.

## Educational Features

- **Project-Driven Learning:** In-depth exploration of all themes through concrete projects, working individually or in teams to develop, research, and implement
- **Low Student-to-Faculty Ratio:** Typically no more than 10 students per professor
- **Excellent Studios:** Students have access to the school's diverse, well-equipped studios at all times
- **Hands-On Craftsmanship:** Manual work holds a significant share of the curriculum
- **International Guest Professors:** Renowned designers from around the world regularly visit to conduct workshops and seminars
- **Design Entrepreneurship:** Students establish agencies or galleries during their studies — this "design entrepreneurship" is consciously cultivated
