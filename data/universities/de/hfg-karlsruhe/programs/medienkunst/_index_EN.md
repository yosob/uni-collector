# Medienkunst (Media Art) — Diplom

## Program Overview

The Media Art program offers not only an extraordinary artistic education using the latest media technologies, but also opens up possibilities for students to jointly research the future of media art. Internationally renowned artists and cultural producers provide innovative education in the expressive qualities of different media forms.

## Specializations

- **Digital Art / InfoArt:** Researches the revolutionary transformation of information culture and investigates data visualization phenomena in a broad context. Following the "Content driven technology" principle, it studies network- and 3D-based real-time generated telepresence phenomena.
- **Film:** Goes beyond classic narrative formats to research and produce new concepts and formats for documentary, experimental, and auteur cinema.
- **Photography:** Oriented towards an expanded concept of photography including installations and time-based formats; discusses and develops artistic research methods.
- **Moving Images:** Focuses on installation art film/moving image installations, researching the technical, aesthetic, and post-production conditions of moving images.
- **Sound:** The sound lab covers electronic and electroacoustic music, experimental radio plays, sound installations, and film sound.
- **Expanded 3Digital Cinema Laboratory:** An internationally leading research area for 3D, VR, and artificial intelligence / artistic intelligence.

## Distinctive Features

- **Dynamic Curriculum:** No fixed courses for each specialization — learning objectives, content, and processes are continuously redefined by professors
- **Excellent Studio Facilities:** High-end equipped studios supporting multiple media productions
- **BEYOND Festival:** International research and presentation platform organized jointly with ZKM
- **ichiigai Label:** Independent audiovisual label founded in 2003, promoting the fusion of sound, music, video, and art
- **GameLab:** Games laboratory exploring computer games as an artistic expression medium
