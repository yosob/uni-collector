# System Design / Game Design (MA)

## Overview

The Master's program in System Design / Game Design at HTW Berlin focuses on game development, playable environments, game thinking and extended realities. The program enables artistic projects, research initiatives or team-based start-ups.

## DE:HIVE — Studio Environment

Master's students work in the **DE:HIVE**, a professional and multifunctional studio environment with state-of-the-art technical infrastructure at Slabystraße in Berlin-Schöneweide.

## Notable Alumni Projects

- **Dorfromantik** (Toukana) — Minimalistic Landscaping Puzzle Strategy
- **AnotherWhere** — Level and Gameplay Generator
- **Emberstorm** — Building an Indie Studio
