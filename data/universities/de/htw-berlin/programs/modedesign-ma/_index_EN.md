# Fashion Design (MA)

## Overview

The Master's program in Fashion Design at HTW Berlin enables in-depth specialization in various areas of fashion design. The program fosters the further development of the creative identity already established during the Bachelor's studies.

## Labs and Studios

- CAD Fashion Studio
- Computer Studio Fashion, Media, Presentation
- Manual Pattern Making
- Model Studio
- Knitting Lab
- Studio_A
- Textile Surface Design
- Drawing Studio
