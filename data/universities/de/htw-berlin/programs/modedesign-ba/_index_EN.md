# Fashion Design (BA)

## Overview

The Bachelor's program in Fashion Design at HTW Berlin offers comprehensive training in all areas of fashion design — from the first sketch to the finished product. The program combines creative design with craft precision and technical know-how.

## Labs and Studios

- CAD Fashion Studio
- Computer Studio Fashion, Media, Presentation
- Manual Pattern Making
- Model Studio
- Knitting Lab
- Studio_A
- Textile Surface Design
- Drawing Studio
