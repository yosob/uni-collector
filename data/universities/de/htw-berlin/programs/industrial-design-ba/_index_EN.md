# Industrial Design (BA)

## Overview

The Bachelor's program in Industrial Design at HTW Berlin trains students in the design of everyday objects and processes. The focus is on intuitive, sustainable and inclusive design for as many people as possible. Interdisciplinarity and openness to other fields of knowledge are particularly encouraged.

## Curriculum

- **Product Design**: Design and creation of consumer goods
- **User-Centered Design**: Usage-oriented design approaches
- **Sustainability**: Ecological and social aspects of design
- **Drawing and Visualization**: Sketching, rendering, presentation
- **Materials and Manufacturing**: Materials science, production methods
- **Digital Tools**: CAD, 3D modeling, prototyping
- **Design Methods**: Research, analysis, conception, evaluation

## Application

- **Aptitude test required**: Yes (multi-stage)
  1. Registration via the online application form
  2. Submission of portfolio, assignment and introductory video (deadline: ~March 23)
  3. Selection process by commission
- **Language requirement**: German B2
