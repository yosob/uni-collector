# Applied Landscape and Field Archaeology (MA)

## Overview

The Master's program in Applied Landscape and Field Archaeology provides advanced competencies in archaeological prospection, excavation technology and landscape analysis. The focus is on connecting modern technological methods with traditional archaeological approaches.
