# Communication Design (BA)

## Overview

The Bachelor's program in Communication Design at HTW Berlin is one of the university's most renowned design programs. It provides comprehensive competencies in visual communication, typography, digital media, editorial design, branding and illustration.

## Curriculum

The program covers a broad spectrum of visual communication:
- **Typography and Type Design**: Fundamentals and experimental approaches
- **Editorial Design**: Print and digital publications
- **Digital Media**: Interface design, interactive media
- **Branding and Corporate Design**: Brand development and visual identity
- **Illustration**: Free and applied illustration
- **Photography and Visual Design**
- **Audiovisual Media**: Video, animation, motion design

## Application

- **Aptitude test required**: Yes
- **Components**:
  1. Assignment (topic published on the last Monday in February, 4 weeks to complete)
  2. Portfolio (online submission)
  3. Interview (if pre-selected)
- **No pre-internship required** (but recommended)
- **Program start**: Winter semester (October)

## Studios and Facilities

The program has professionally equipped studios for printing, photography, digital media and more.
