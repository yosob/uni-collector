# Museum Management and Communication (MA)

## Overview

The Master's program in Museum Management and Communication at HTW Berlin qualifies students for leadership positions in the museum and cultural sector. It combines management competencies with design and communication expertise.

## Curriculum (1st Semester)

- **Critical Museum Theories**: Analysis and reflection of the museum landscape
- **Fundamentals of Museum Management**: Management theory for cultural institutions
- **Fundamentals of Visual Communication in Museums**: Corporate design, orientation systems, infographics
- **Multimedia Applications in Museums**: Digital media in exhibition design
- **Ethics and Law in Museums**: Legal and ethical issues

## Practical Projects

- Conception and realization of exhibitions with Berlin museums
