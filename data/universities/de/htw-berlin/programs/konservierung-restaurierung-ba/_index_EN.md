# Conservation and Restoration/Excavation Technology (BA)

## Overview

The Bachelor's program in Conservation and Restoration/Excavation Technology combines cultural studies foundations with practical craft expertise. Students learn the preservation, restoration and documentation of art and cultural artifacts as well as the techniques of archaeological excavations.
