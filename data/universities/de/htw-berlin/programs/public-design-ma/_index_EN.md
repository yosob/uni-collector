# Public Design (MA)

## Overview

The Master's program in Public Design at HTW Berlin is a unique program focusing on design in the public sector. It combines service design, information design, interaction design and transformation design with topics such as organization, law, politics and IT.

## Project Work

Projects are carried out with external partners — from research to the evaluation of prototypes in concrete situations.

## Accreditation

Concept-accredited by the HTW Berlin university management on January 22, 2025, valid until March 31, 2033.
