## How to Apply

1. **Portal:** https://studium.hbk-bs.de
2. **Deadline:** March 15 (winter semester only)
3. **Process:**
   - Online application via meinStudium portal
   - Submit digital portfolio (for design/art programs)
   - Invitation to aptitude assessment (upon positive pre-selection)
   - On-site aptitude assessment with interview
4. **Language:** DSH-2 or equivalent for international applicants
5. **Fees:** No tuition; semester contribution ~€380 (incl. semester ticket)
