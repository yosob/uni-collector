# Media Studies (B.A. / M.A.)

## Overview
The Media Studies program at HBK Braunschweig addresses the theoretical and practical engagement with media and their cultural, social, and technological dimensions. The B.A. provides a broad foundational education, while the M.A. deepens knowledge in research and practice.

## Focus Areas
- Media Theory and Media History
- Digital Media and Net Culture
- Film and Television Studies
- Audiovisual Media Practice
- Media Art and Media Aesthetics
- Media and Society
- Interactive Media

## Curriculum Structure
### Bachelor (B.A.) — 6 semesters, 180 CP
- Fundamentals of Media Studies
- Media Practice Modules
- Electives
- Bachelor's Thesis

### Master (M.A.) — 4 semesters
- Advanced Study in Specialized Areas
- Research Projects
- Master's Thesis

## Admission Requirements
### Bachelor
- General university entrance qualification (Abitur) or subject-specific entrance qualification
- Proof of media interest and competence
- Online application via meinStudium portal

### Master
- Completed Bachelor's degree in a relevant field
- Proof of media studies knowledge
- Letter of motivation

## Application Process
1. Online application via https://studium.hbk-bs.de by March 15
2. Submission of application documents
3. Possible selection interview

## Language Requirements
- German (language of instruction)
- For international applicants: DSH-2 or equivalent
