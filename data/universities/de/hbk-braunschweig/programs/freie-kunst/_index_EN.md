# Fine Art (Diplom)

## Overview
The Diplom program in Fine Art is the heart of HBK Braunschweig. Studies take place in individual artist classes, each led by a professor. The program is divided into basic and advanced studies, enabling comprehensive artistic development across a broad spectrum of artistic media.

## Focus Areas
- Painting
- Sculpture
- New Media / Media Art
- Photography
- Video / Film
- Performance
- Installation Art
- Drawing
- Printmaking
- Sound Art

## Curriculum Structure
- **Basic Studies:** Fundamentals of artistic practice, workshop techniques, theory
- **Advanced Studies:** Deepening in one's own artist class, free artistic development
- **Diplom Thesis:** Independent artistic work with written documentation
- Guest critiques, exhibition participation, excursions

## Admission Requirements
- General university entrance qualification (Abitur) or subject-specific entrance qualification (exceptions possible)
- Portfolio with artistic work (portfolio mandatory)
- Successful aptitude assessment
- Online application via meinStudium portal
- Meisterschüler*in graduation possible after Diplom

## Application Process
1. Online application via https://studium.hbk-bs.de by March 15
2. Submission of digital portfolio
3. Invitation to aptitude assessment (upon positive pre-selection)
4. On-site aptitude assessment with artistic sample and interview

## Language Requirements
- German (language of instruction)
- For international applicants: DSH-2 or equivalent
