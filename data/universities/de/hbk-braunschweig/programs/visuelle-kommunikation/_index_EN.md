# Visual Communication (B.A.)

## Overview
The Bachelor's program in Visual Communication provides in-depth knowledge of the design practice and theory of visual communication. Students learn to conceptualize, design, and realize visual messages.

## Focus Areas
- Typography and Layout
- Graphic Design
- Illustration
- Photography
- Film and Animation
- Interface Design / Interaction Design
- Corporate Design / Branding

## Curriculum Structure
- **Total:** 180 Credit Points (CP), 6 semesters
- **Professional Development Area:** 30 CP
- **Bachelor's Thesis:** 9 weeks processing time
- Practical design projects
- Theoretical foundations of Visual Communication
- Workshop work and material techniques

## Admission Requirements
- General university entrance qualification (Abitur) or subject-specific entrance qualification
- Portfolio with design work (portfolio mandatory)
- Successful aptitude assessment
- Online application via meinStudium portal

## Application Process
1. Online application via https://studium.hbk-bs.de by March 15
2. Submission of digital portfolio
3. Invitation to aptitude assessment (upon positive pre-selection)
4. On-site aptitude assessment

## Language Requirements
- German (language of instruction)
- For international applicants: DSH-2 or equivalent
