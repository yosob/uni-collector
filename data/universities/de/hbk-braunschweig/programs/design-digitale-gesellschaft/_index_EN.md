# Design in Digital Society (B.A.)

## Overview
The Bachelor's program Design in Digital Society combines design competencies with technological knowledge and social issues. Students learn to design digital applications, interfaces, and systems that actively shape the transformation of society.

## Focus Areas
- Interaction Design / Interface Design
- User Experience (UX) Design
- Digital Media and Technologies
- Code and Creative Coding
- Human-Computer Interaction
- Digital Transformation and Society
- Prototyping and Digital Tools

## Curriculum Structure
- **Total:** 6 semesters
- Design Fundamentals
- Technological Foundations (Coding, Prototyping)
- Theory of Digital Society
- Project Work and Applied Design Research
- Bachelor's Thesis

## Admission Requirements
- General university entrance qualification (Abitur) or subject-specific entrance qualification
- Portfolio with design and/or digital work
- Successful aptitude assessment
- Online application via meinStudium portal

## Application Process
1. Online application via https://studium.hbk-bs.de by March 15
2. Submission of digital portfolio
3. Invitation to aptitude assessment (upon positive pre-selection)
4. On-site aptitude assessment

## Language Requirements
- German (language of instruction)
- For international applicants: DSH-2 or equivalent
