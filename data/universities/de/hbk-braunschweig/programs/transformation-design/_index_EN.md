# Transformation Design (M.A.)

## Overview
The Master's program in Transformation Design is aimed at graduates of design and art bachelor's programs. The program combines design research with social transformation, enabling students to address complex social, ecological, and technological challenges through design strategies.

## Focus Areas
- Design Research
- Social Innovation and Social Change
- Participatory Design / Co-Design
- Sustainability and Ecological Transformation
- Speculative Design / Critical Design
- Systems Thinking and Complex Problem-Solving

## Curriculum Structure
- **Total:** 120 Credit Points (CP), 4 semesters
- Research-based design projects
- Theoretical foundations of design research
- Master's thesis with a design-research approach
- Interdisciplinary collaborations

## Admission Requirements
- Bachelor's degree in a design or art program
- Portfolio with design and/or research-oriented work
- Letter of motivation
- Successful aptitude assessment
- Online application via meinStudium portal

## Application Process
1. Online application via https://studium.hbk-bs.de by March 15
2. Submission of digital portfolio
3. Letter of motivation and CV
4. Invitation to aptitude assessment (upon positive pre-selection)
5. On-site aptitude assessment

## Language Requirements
- German (language of instruction)
- For international applicants: DSH-2 or equivalent
