# Art History / Art Science (B.A. / M.A.)

## Overview
The Art History / Art Science program provides comprehensive knowledge of art history and visual studies, embedded in the context of an art university. The close connection between theory and artistic practice enables a unique perspective on art and visual culture from the early modern period to the present.

## Focus Areas
- Art History (all periods)
- Contemporary Art
- Visual Studies
- Architectural History
- Media and Photography History
- Gender and Postcolonial Studies in Art
- Museology and Curating

## Curriculum Structure
### Bachelor (B.A.) — 6 semesters, 180 CP
- Fundamentals of Art History
- Methods of Art History
- Period Specializations
- Internships in Museums/Galleries
- Bachelor's Thesis

### Master (M.A.) — 4 semesters, 120 CP
- Advanced Study in Specialized Topics
- Research Seminars
- Master's Thesis

## Admission Requirements
### B.A.:
- General university entrance qualification (Abitur)
- Proof of prior knowledge in art history
- Online application via meinStudium portal
### M.A.:
- Bachelor's degree in Art History or a related field
- Online application via meinStudium portal

## Application Process
1. Online application via https://studium.hbk-bs.de by March 15
2. Proof of qualifications (certificates)
3. Possible selection interview

## Language Requirements
- German (language of instruction)
- For international applicants: DSH-2 or equivalent
- Latin knowledge recommended
