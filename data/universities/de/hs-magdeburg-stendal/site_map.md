## Key Discovery Notes

1. **Website Migration:** The university has migrated from `hs-magdeburg.de` to `h2.de`. The study programme details are hosted on `studieren.h2.de` (separate subdomain).
2. **3 Design Programs Confirmed:** Industrial Design (BA), Engineering Design (MA), and Interaction Design (MA) — all in the IWID department.
3. **No other design/art programs found.** The university offers Journalism (BA/MA) and Mensch-Technik-Interaktion (BA) which are related but not core design/art programs.
4. **All programs require Eignungsprüfung** (aptitude test with portfolio, practical exam, and interview).
5. **All programs start in both WS and SS.**
