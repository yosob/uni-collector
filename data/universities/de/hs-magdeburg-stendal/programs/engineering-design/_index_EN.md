# Engineering Design (M.A.) — Magdeburg-Stendal University of Applied Sciences

## Programme Profile

The consecutive master's programme in Engineering Design is aimed at graduates of the bachelor's programme in Industrial Design and related engineering disciplines. The programme prepares students for careers in technically oriented industrial design and in the development of technical products with a design focus. Special competencies in integrated product development, bionics and system design are acquired.

## Key Areas / Content

### Compulsory Modules
- **Integrated Product Design:** Product development, CAD/CAM, mechanics and strength
- **Fundamentals of Design and Technology:** Investment goods design, product development
- **Integrated Product Development:** Product development, Computer-Aided Design and Manufacturing (CAD/CAM), mechanics and strength

### Elective Modules Design and Technology
- Design Methodology
- Sustainable Design
- Fundamentals of Fibre-Reinforced Composites
- Computer-Aided Design
- Fundamentals of Simulation Techniques
- New Interaction Technologies

### Project Work
- Close cooperation with industrial companies and research institutions (e.g. STIMULATE, Fraunhofer IFF, Audi, BMW, Volkswagen, Porsche, DLR and many more)

## Admission Requirements

- Good or very good bachelor's degree in Industrial Design or related engineering sciences (min. 7 semesters / 210 ECTS)
- With at least 180 ECTS: individual assessment possible
- Proof of sufficient English language skills (B2)
- Successful aptitude assessment (3 stages)

## Aptitude Assessment

1. **Portfolio review** (minimum grade for admission to exam: 2.7)
2. **Practical exam** (max. 6 hours)
3. **Interview** (max. 30 minutes)

Submit portfolio and motivation letter digitally (PDF, max. 2 MB) to onlinebewerbung@h2.de.

## Special Features

- Close cooperation with the **STIMULATE** research campus (image-guided minimally invasive medical technology)
- Numerous design awards: iF Design Student Award, Wonder Global Awards, UX Design Awards, Green Concept Award, VDID New Talents Award, Bundespreis Ecodesign and many more
- Part-time study possible (when employed, raising children, providing care, etc.)
- Start possible in both winter and summer semester
- Combination of engineering sciences and design
