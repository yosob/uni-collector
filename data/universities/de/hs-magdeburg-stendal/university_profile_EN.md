# Magdeburg-Stendal University of Applied Sciences

**Magdeburg-Stendal University of Applied Sciences (H2)** is a public university of applied sciences with campuses in Magdeburg and Stendal in Saxony-Anhalt. Founded in 1991, it offers around 50 degree programmes across four departments, including three design-oriented programmes in the IWID (Engineering Sciences and Industrial Design) department.

## Bachelor Programmes (1)

### Industrial Design (B.A.)
- **Duration:** 7 semesters | **Language:** German | **Start:** WS + SS
- **Tags:** Industrial Design, Product Design, Interaction Design
- **Focus:** Product design, 3D visualisation, prototyping, UX design, material science, manufacturing techniques
- **Admission:** 3-stage aptitude assessment (portfolio → home assignment → exam + interview)
- **Portfolio required:** Yes — institute tasks + home assignment
- **Special features:** 16-week internship semester in 6th semester; combination of creative design and engineering expertise; workshops with 3D printing, CNC
- **Deadline:** WS 31.05. / SS 30.11.

---

## Master Programmes (2)

### Engineering Design (M.A.)
- **Duration:** 3 semesters | **Language:** German + English | **Start:** WS + SS
- **Tags:** Industrial Design, Product Design, Sustainable Design
- **Focus:** Technical industrial design, bionics, system design, integrated product development, investment goods design, CAD/CAM
- **Admission:** Bachelor in Industrial Design or engineering (210 ECTS preferred); 3-stage aptitude assessment (portfolio review min. grade 2.7 → 6hr exam → 30min interview)
- **Portfolio required:** Yes — digital portfolio (PDF max. 2 MB) + motivation letter
- **Special features:** Cooperation with STIMULATE research campus; numerous design awards; part-time possible
- **Deadline:** WS 31.05. / SS 30.11.

### Interaction Design (M.A.)
- **Duration:** 3 semesters | **Language:** German + English | **Start:** WS + SS
- **Tags:** Interaction Design, Human-Computer Interaction, Digital Media, Digital Product Design
- **Focus:** Human-machine interaction, UX design, social design, extended reality (XR), tangible interfaces, critical making, data-driven design
- **Admission:** Bachelor in design or related fields (210 ECTS preferred); transdisciplinary — also accepts social sciences, CS, psychology, EE; aptitude assessment
- **Portfolio required:** Yes — creative/design works portfolio
- **Special features:** XR I LAB, UX Lab, Schauwerk creative lab; transdisciplinary approach; societal topics; part-time possible
- **Deadline:** WS 31.05. / SS 30.11.

## Application Process Overview

1. **Apply online** via [onlinecampus.h2.de](https://onlinecampus.h2.de)
2. **Submit portfolio** (specific requirements vary by programme)
3. **Aptitude assessment** (Eignungsprüfung) — all design programmes require this
4. **Deadlines:** Winter semester: 31 May | Summer semester: 30 November

## Language Requirements

- **German:** C1 level required for all programmes (Goethe-Zertifikat C1, DSD II, DSH-1, TestDaF Niveaustufe 3, TELC C1 Hochschule, or ÖSD C1)
- **English:** B2 required for Engineering Design M.A.

## Special Facilities

- **XR I LAB:** Virtual, Augmented and Mixed Reality laboratory
- **UX I User Experience Lab:** Usability and UX research with eye-tracking
- **Schauwerk:** Creative lab in Magdeburg city centre (Große Münzstraße 10)
- **Workshops:** 3D printing, CNC, model making
