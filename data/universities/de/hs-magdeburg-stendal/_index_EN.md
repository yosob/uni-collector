# Magdeburg-Stendal University of Applied Sciences

Magdeburg-Stendal University of Applied Sciences (H2) is a public university of applied sciences with campuses in Magdeburg and Stendal in Saxony-Anhalt. Founded in 1991, it offers around 50 degree programmes across four departments.

## Design and Art Programmes

H2 offers three design-oriented programmes in the **IWID (Engineering Sciences and Industrial Design)** department:

| Programme | Degree | Semesters | Start |
|---|---|---|---|
| [Industrial Design](programs/industrial-design/) | B.A. | 7 | WS + SS |
| [Engineering Design](programs/engineering-design/) | M.A. | 3 | WS + SS |
| [Interaction Design](programs/interaction-design/) | M.A. | 3 | WS + SS |

## IWID Department

The Department of Engineering Sciences and Industrial Design (IWID) combines engineering and design disciplines. It offers a broad range of bachelor's and master's programmes with a strong research focus and interdisciplinary, practice-oriented approach. Key research areas include the STIMULATE research campus (image-guided minimally invasive medical technology), XR technologies, and sustainable product development.

### Special Facilities

- **XR I LAB:** Laboratory for Virtual, Augmented and Mixed Reality
- **UX I User Experience Lab:** Usability and UX research laboratory
- **Schauwerk:** Creative lab in Magdeburg city centre (Große Münzstraße 10)
- **Workshops:** 3D printing, CNC, model making, etc.
