# MA Entrepreneurship & Digital Transformation — Hochschule München

## Program Overview

The Entrepreneurship and Digital Transformation Master's program (MA Entrepreneurship and Digital Transformation, also known as DeepDive Master) at Munich University of Applied Sciences is an innovative, international, and interdisciplinary full-time master's program. It is jointly offered by the Strascheg Center for Entrepreneurship (SCE) and multiple HM faculties.

The program's core consists of two semesters of real-world project work — students either develop their own digital startup project (Entrepreneurship Track) or collaborate with corporate partners on innovation projects (Co-Creation Track). The entire learning experience follows a "Learning-by-Doing" philosophy, applying methodologies directly to real projects.

## Program Features

### Two Learning Tracks

#### Entrepreneurship Track
- Develop your own digital startup project
- From idea validation to business model construction
- Covering mobility, health, food, manufacturing, and more
- Close collaboration with EIT Urban Mobility

#### Co-Creation Track
- Solve real digital challenges with corporate partners
- Guided by professors and startup coaches
- Gain hands-on corporate innovation experience

### Core Highlights
- **Two-Semester Real-World Projects**: Not theoretical — actual startup/innovation projects
- **HM × SCE**: University academic quality + startup ecosystem (mentors, workshops, events, network)
- **Responsible Entrepreneurship**: Innovation with responsibility
- **Tools-First Thinking**: Modern digital methods and tools including AI
- **Portfolio Over Pure Theory**: Tangible outcomes, prototypes, learning experiences, and pitch experience

## Application Information

### Admission Requirements
- First higher education degree (e.g., Bachelor's), at least **180 ECTS** (180 ECTS may require additional conditions)
- English **B2** level (recognized language certificate)
- **Relevant experience/knowledge** in (intra-)entrepreneurship, digital transformation, and project implementation
- Submit a **project description** and **Pitch-Reel** (in specified format)
- Pass the **selection interview**

### Application Process
1. Online application (PRIMUSS)
2. Document review
3. Selection interview invitation (typically early July, online)
4. Interview (typically mid-July, online)
5. Results notification (typically late July, via PRIMUSS)

### Application Deadline
- Winter semester: **July 15**

### Special Requirements for International Applicants
- Must apply for VPD (Vorprüfungsdokumentation) via **uni-assist**
- Apply early — processing takes approximately **8 weeks**
- Degree recognition must be confirmed (via anabin database)

## Curriculum Structure

- **Compulsory Modules**: Entrepreneurship and Digital Technologies
- **Elective Modules**: Choose from master's courses across HM's eight faculties
- **Two-Semester Project Work**: Entrepreneurship or co-creation project
- **Coaching and Mentoring**: Professors + SCE startup coaches
