# MA Angewandte Designforschung — Hochschule München

## Program Overview

The Applied Design Research Master's program (MA Angewandte Designforschung) at Munich University of Applied Sciences is a research-oriented master's program focusing on two key areas: "Social Change and Transformative Processes" (Sozialer Wandel und transformative Prozesse) and "Design Theory and Design Culture" (Designtheorie und Designkultur).

Students explore Social Design and Transformationsdesign topics through research-experimental approaches, addressing significant social, technological, or cultural transformations. The program goes beyond the development of artifacts (Artefakte), emphasizing the creation of "mentefacts" (Mentefakte) and social constructs (Soziofakte).

## Research Focus Areas

### Social Change and Transformative Processes
- Social Design
- Transformationsdesign
- Digital social transformation
- Resource conservation and sustainable development

### Design Theory and Design Culture
- Design science (Designwissenschaft)
- Design theory (Designtheorie)
- Design criticism (Designkritik)
- Studies of everyday and consumer culture phenomena

## Application Information

### Application Timeline ⭐
- **Enrollment**: **Summer semester** each year (mid-March)
- **Application Period**: **November 15 — January 15**
- ⚠️ Note: Unlike most design programs, this program starts in the summer semester

### Admission Requirements
- Design-related Bachelor's or Diplom degree with a grade of "good" (2.5) or better
- At least 210 ECTS credits, including 6 theoretical semesters + 1 practical semester (or at least 18 weeks of relevant professional experience)
- If only 180 ECTS without a practical semester, can be compensated during the first year
- International applicants: German DSH-2 or TestDaF level 3

### Application Process
1. **PRIMUSS Online Application**: Submit data and documents during the application period
2. **HMster Portfolio Upload**: Register and upload digital portfolio at nine.hm.edu
3. **Admission Interview**: Interview with the MA examination board (3 professors)

### Portfolio Requirements
| Content | Requirements |
|---------|-------------|
| Letter of Motivation | Required, entered via form |
| CV and Photo | Required (photo optional) |
| Work Samples | Max 25 works |
| File Formats | .png, .jpg, .gif, .pdf |
| File Size | Max 50 MB per file |
| Additional Notes | Description text can be added for each work |

⚠️ **Important**: Both the PRIMUSS application and HMster portfolio upload must be completed. It is recommended to upload the portfolio a few days early to avoid server congestion.

## Program Features

- **Research-Experimental Methods**: Exploring design issues through research-based experimentation
- **Interdisciplinary and Intercultural**: Open to graduates of domestic and international design schools and related disciplines
- **Design Research Practice**: Practice-oriented design research (praxisbasierte Designforschung)
- **Applied Design Theory**: Combining theory with practice (anwendungsbezogene Designtheorie)
- **Close Connection to dci Institute**: Opportunity to participate in research projects at the Designkulturen Institut
