# B.Sc. Informatik und Design — Hochschule München

## Program Overview

The Computer Science and Design Bachelor's program (B.Sc. Informatik und Design) at Munich University of Applied Sciences is a unique interdisciplinary program jointly offered by the Faculty of Computer Science and Mathematics (FK07) and the Faculty of Design (FK12), operated by MUC.DAI (Munich Center for Digital Sciences and AI).

The program closely integrates computer science with design thinking, training students to become digital product creators who understand both technical and design languages. Students gain comprehensive training in programming, interaction design, user interface design, and creative design methods.

## Teaching Philosophy: 6C Framework

The program's curriculum is built around six core competencies:

1. **Capability**: Technical problem-solving skills
2. **Computational Thinking**: Algorithmic thinking and data-driven decision-making
3. **Communication**: Effective interdisciplinary communication
4. **Collaboration**: Teamwork and project management
5. **Creativity**: Innovative thinking and design methods
6. **Critical Thinking**: Reflective practice and ethical awareness

## Core Learning Areas

- Programming and software development
- User interface design (UI Design)
- User experience design (UX Design)
- Interaction Design
- Data visualization
- Fundamentals of AI and machine learning
- Creative Coding
- Design methodology
- Project management

## Curriculum Structure

- Semesters 1–2: Foundation courses in computer science and design
- Semesters 3–5: Advanced courses, UI/UX, interaction design, programming projects
- Semester 6: Practical semester
- Semester 7: Bachelor's thesis (Bachelorarbeit)

## Teaching Features

- **Interdisciplinary Integration**: Computer Science + Design Thinking
- **MUC.DAI Operation**: Access to cutting-edge digital science and AI resources
- **Practical Projects**: Extensive real-world project development experience
- **Dual Faculty Resources**: Leveraging both FK07 and FK12 faculty and facilities
- **Industry Orientation**: Aligned with digital product development career demands
