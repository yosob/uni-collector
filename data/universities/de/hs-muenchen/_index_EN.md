# Hochschule München — Munich University of Applied Sciences

Hochschule München is the largest Bavarian university of applied sciences and one of the most diverse in Germany, with approximately 18,800 students. It offers around 100 degree programs across 14 faculties, covering engineering, business, social sciences, and design. The university is distinguished by its applied research orientation, strong industry connections, and its role as an excellence startup university.

## Faculty of Design (Fakultät für Design, FK12)

HM's Faculty of Design is one of the most traditional design education institutions in Germany, with over a century of design education history. The faculty is located in the Altes Zeughaus (Old Arsenal), a historic building in central Munich.

### Design-related Bachelor's Programs (2)
1. **BA Design** — Photography / Industrial / Communication Design (7 semesters, 210 ECTS)
2. **B.Sc. Informatik und Design** — Computer Science and Design (7 semesters, 210 ECTS)

### Design-related Master's Programs (2)
3. **MA Angewandte Designforschung** — Applied Design Research (3 semesters, 90 ECTS, summer intake)
4. **MA Entrepreneurship & Digital Transformation** — Entrepreneurship and Digital Transformation (3 semesters, 90 ECTS, English-taught)

### Associated Institutions
- **MUC.DAI** (Munich Center for Digital Sciences and AI): operates the Informatik und Design program
- **SCE** (Strascheg Center for Entrepreneurship): operates the Entrepreneurship & Digital Transformation program
- **dci** (Designkulturen Institut für Angewandte Designforschung): applied design research institute
