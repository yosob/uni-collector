## University Features

HM is the largest Bavarian university of applied sciences and one of the largest in Germany, located in the economic center of Munich. It has 14 faculties covering engineering, business, social sciences, and design.

### Teaching Features
- **Practice-Oriented**: Applied scientific methods combined with industry practice
- **Corporate Partnerships**: Close cooperation with BMW, Siemens, Google, and other major companies
- **Internationalization**: Extensive exchange programs and English-taught programs
- **Entrepreneurship Culture**: Designated "Exzellenz-Gründungshochschule" (Excellence Startup University)
- **Interdisciplinary**: Multi-faculty joint programs and courses

## Faculty of Design (Fakultät für Design, FK12)

### Faculty Overview
- **History**: Over 100 years of design education tradition
- **Location**: Altes Zeughaus (Old Arsenal) historic building in central Munich
- **Features**: Small classes, studio culture, strong practical orientation

### Bachelor's Programs

#### BA Design
- **Degree**: Bachelor of Arts (B.A.)
- **Duration**: 7 semesters / 210 ECTS
- **Language**: German
- **Start**: Winter semester only
- **Application Period**: May 2 — May 31
- **Specializations**:
  - 📷 Photography Design (Fotodesign)
  - 🏭 Industrial Design (Industriedesign)
  - 📐 Communication Design (Kommunikationsdesign)
- **Features**: Aptitude test (Eignungsprüfung), digital portfolio, 5th semester internship

#### B.Sc. Computer Science and Design (Informatik und Design)
- **Degree**: Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.)
- **Duration**: 7 semesters / 210 ECTS
- **Language**: German
- **Start**: Winter semester
- **Features**: Interdisciplinary (FK07 + FK12), operated by MUC.DAI, 6C teaching framework
- **Focus**: UI/UX design, interaction design, creative coding, front-end development

### Master's Programs

#### MA Applied Design Research (Angewandte Designforschung)
- **Degree**: Master of Arts (M.A.)
- **Duration**: 3 semesters / 90 ECTS
- **Language**: German
- **Start**: ⭐ **Summer semester** (mid-March)
- **Application Period**: November 15 — January 15
- **Focus**: Social change and transformation, design theory and design culture
- **Features**: Research-oriented, closely linked to Designkulturen Institut (dci)

#### MA Entrepreneurship & Digital Transformation
- **Degree**: Master of Arts (M.A.)
- **Duration**: 3 semesters / 90 ECTS
- **Language**: **English**
- **Start**: Winter semester
- **Operator**: Strascheg Center for Entrepreneurship (SCE)
- **Features**: 2 semesters of real-world projects, entrepreneurship or co-creation tracks
- **Tuition-free**: Standard semester fees only
