# Fine Art Education (Teaching Degree for Gymnasium) — Staatsexamen

## Program Overview

The **Lehramt an Gymnasien Kunst** (Teaching Degree for Gymnasium – Art) at Bauhaus-Universität Weimar is a **9-semester** program leading to the **First State Examination (Erstes Staatsexamen)**, qualifying graduates to teach art at Gymnasium level (secondary school).

Offered in two variants:
- **Doppelfach Kunst** (Double Subject Art) — Art as both subjects
- **Einzelfach Kunst** (Single Subject Art) — Art combined with a second subject from another faculty

## Key Features

- Combines studio-based artistic practice with art history and art didactics
- Educational science modules and school practicum integrated into curriculum
- Follows the Weimarer Modell of project-based, interdisciplinary study
- Close mentoring by professors and scientific staff in small groups
- Access to fully equipped workshops of the Faculty

## Admission

- **General higher education entrance qualification** (Abitur) or equivalent
- **Artistic aptitude test** consisting of homework assignment, portfolio, and aptitude interview
- **German language proficiency** at C1 level

## Application

- **Portal:** Bauhaus.CampusPortal
- **Three-phase process:** Registration (Feb 2–28) → Homework (early March) → Complete application (March 31)
- **Start:** Winter semester only

## Tuition

No tuition fees. Standard semester fee applies.
