# Lehramt an Gymnasien Kunst (Teaching Degree – Art)

## Program Overview

The Lehramt an Gymnasien Kunst (Teaching Degree for Gymnasium – Art) at Bauhaus-Universität Weimar is a 9-semester program offered by the Faculty of Art and Design (Fakultät Kunst und Gestaltung). It leads to the First State Examination (Erstes Staatsexamen).

The program is offered in two variants:
- **Doppelfach Kunst** (Double Subject Art) — Art as both subjects
- **Einzelfach Kunst** (Single Subject Art) — Art combined with a second subject from another faculty

## Key Features

- Combines studio-based artistic practice with art history and art didactics
- Educational science modules included
- School practicum integrated into the curriculum
- Follows the Weimarer Modell of project-based study
- Prepares for teaching art at Gymnasium level (secondary school)

## Admission

Admission requires a general higher education entrance qualification (Abitur) or equivalent, along with successful completion of an artistic aptitude test (Eignungsprüfung). The aptitude test involves a homework assignment and an interview.

German language proficiency at C1 level (DSH-2 or TestDaF TDN 4) is required.

## Application

- **Portal:** Bauhaus.CampusPortal (digital)
- **Phase 1 — Registration:** February 2 – February 28
- **Phase 2 — Homework:** Published beginning of March
- **Phase 3 — Complete application:** Due March 31 (including homework)
- **Start:** Winter semester only
