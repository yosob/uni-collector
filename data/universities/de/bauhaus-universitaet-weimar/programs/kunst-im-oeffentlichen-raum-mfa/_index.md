# Kunst im öffentlichen Raum M.F.A. (Public Art and New Artistic Strategies)

## Program Overview

The Master of Fine Arts in »Kunst im öffentlichen Raum und neue künstlerische Strategien / Public Art and New Artistic Strategies« at Bauhaus-Universität Weimar is a 4-semester program offered by the Faculty of Art and Design (Fakultät Kunst und Gestaltung).

The program focuses on artistic engagement with public space and the development of new strategies for art in social contexts. Students explore site-specific interventions, participatory art, social practice, and experimental approaches to public art.

## Key Features

- Focus on public art and spatial interventions
- International orientation with exchange opportunities
- Close collaboration with the professorship for Public Art and New Artistic Strategies
- Project-based study following the Weimarer Modell
- Access to well-equipped workshops and studios

## Admission

Admission requires a first academic degree (Bachelor or Diplom) in fine arts or a related field, along with exceptional artistic aptitude demonstrated through portfolio and aptitude test.

## Application

- **Portal:** Bauhaus.CampusPortal
- **Deadline:** March 31 (for winter semester start)
- **Start:** Winter semester only
