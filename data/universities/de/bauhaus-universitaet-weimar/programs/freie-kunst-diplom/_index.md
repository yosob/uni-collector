# Freie Kunst – Diplom (Fine Art)

## Program Overview

The Diplom program in Freie Kunst (Fine Art) at Bauhaus-Universität Weimar is a 10-semester program offered by the Faculty of Art and Design (Fakultät Kunst und Gestaltung). The Diplom degree is equivalent to a Master's degree.

The program follows the **Weimarer Modell** (Weimar Model) of project-based, interdisciplinary study. Students develop their own artistic positions through intensive studio practice and close collaboration with professors. The focus is on free artistic work in areas including painting, drawing, sculpture, installation, and performance.

## Key Features

- Individual artistic development in studio-based settings
- Interdisciplinary project work across all professorships of the Faculty
- Close mentoring by professors
- The Diplom degree is recognized as equivalent to a Master's degree
- Access to well-equipped workshops and studios

## Admission

Admission requires a general higher education entrance qualification (Abitur) or equivalent, along with exceptional artistic aptitude demonstrated through portfolio and aptitude test (Eignungsprüfung).

German language proficiency at C1 level (DSH-2 or TestDaF TDN 4) is required.

## Application

- **Portal:** Bauhaus.CampusPortal (digital)
- **Application period:** February 2 – March 31
- **Start:** Winter semester only
- **Portfolio:** Approximately 20 work samples demonstrating artistic development
