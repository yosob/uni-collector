# Product Design — MA

## Program Overview

The **Master of Arts in Product Design** at Bauhaus-Universität Weimar is a **4-semester** program offered by the Faculty of Art and Design.

The program follows the **Weimarer Modell** (Weimar Model) of project-based, interdisciplinary study. The MA program enables students to **deepen their individual design positions** and engage with advanced design research across four professorships.

Students work across Design und Management, Transformatives Design, Experimental Industrial Design, and Emerging Technologies and Design. The program emphasizes **self-directed, complex project work** and critical reflection of one's own design practice.

### Workshops & Facilities

Excellent workshops for **wood, metal, plastic, and ceramics**, plus infrastructure for **digital fabrication**.

## Admission

- **First academic degree** (Bachelor or Diplom) in design or closely related field with above-average grades
- **Artistic aptitude test**: portfolio review and interview
- **German C1 level** required

| 2. Documents | By March 31 | Upload motivation letter, CV, portfolio, degree copy, ID/passport |
| 3. Interview | After pre-selection | Personal interview with the commission |

## Career Perspectives

Senior industrial design, sustainable & circular design, strategic design consulting, innovation management, transformative design, doctoral research, creative economy, digital product design, human-centered design, exhibition design, social innovation, speculative design.
