# Product Design B.A. (Produktdesign)

## Program Overview

The Bachelor of Arts in Product Design at Bauhaus-Universität Weimar is a 6-semester program offered by the Faculty of Art and Design (Fakultät Kunst und Gestaltung).

The program follows the **Weimarer Modell** (Weimar Model), a project-based study approach that emphasizes interdisciplinary collaboration, individual artistic development, and the integration of design theory with practical studio work. Students work across multiple professorships covering industrial design, transformation design, and emerging technologies.

## Admission

Admission requires a general higher education entrance qualification (Abitur) or equivalent, along with successful completion of an artistic aptitude test (Eignungsprüfung). The aptitude test involves portfolio review and an interview with the selection commission.

German language proficiency at C1 level (DSH-2 or TestDaF TDN 4) is required.

## Application

- **Portal:** Bauhaus.CampusPortal (digital)
- **Deadline:** March 31 (for winter semester start)
- **Start:** Winter semester only
