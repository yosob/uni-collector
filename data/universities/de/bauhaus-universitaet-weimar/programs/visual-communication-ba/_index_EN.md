# Visual Communication — BA

## Program Overview

The **Bachelor of Arts in Visual Communication** at Bauhaus-Universität Weimar is an **8-semester** program offered by the Faculty of Art and Design. Rooted in the Bauhaus tradition, it combines artistic practice with design methodology.

The program follows the **Weimarer Modell** (Weimar Model) of project-based, interdisciplinary study. Students develop innovative approaches to design tasks, combining conceptual-artistic methods with theoretical-scientific-technical knowledge.

## Professorships

| Professor | Chair |
|-----------|-------|
| Prof. Markus Weisbeck | Grafikdesign (Graphic Design) |
| Jun.-Prof. Katharina Köhler | Typographie und Schriftgestaltung (Typography & Type Design) |
| Prof. Jörn Hintzer & Prof. Jakob Hüfner | Crossmediales Bewegtbild (Cross-Media Moving Image) |
| Prof. Dr. Birgit Wudtke | Fotografie (Photography) |
| Prof. Burkhart von Scheven | Bild-Text-Konzeption (Image-Text Conception) |

## Admission

- **General higher education entrance qualification** (Abitur) or equivalent
- **Artistic aptitude test**: homework, CV, and aptitude interview with work samples
- **German C1 level** required

## Application

| Phase | Timeline | Action |
|-------|----------|--------|
| 1. Registration | February – March 31 | Register via Bauhaus.CampusPortal |
| 2. Homework | Early April | Assignment published |
| 3. Upload | Early April – April 30 | Upload homework, CV, documents |
| 4. Interview | After pre-selection | Aptitude interview with work samples |

No tuition fees. Semester fee of approx. €200 includes semester ticket and student services.
