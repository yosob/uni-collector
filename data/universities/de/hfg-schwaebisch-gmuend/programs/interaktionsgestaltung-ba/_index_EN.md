# Interaction Design (BA)

Interaction designers develop digital products and services. The program at HfG Schwäbisch Gmünd prepares students for the design of apps, websites, software, and interactive products. The education is project-based with presentations and few written exams.

## Specializations (from semester 4)

1. **Interface Design** — Human-computer interfaces, software products, UI/UX
2. **Physical Computing** — Tangible interfaces, embedded systems, IoT, physical-digital interaction

## Career Fields

- **Application Design** — Concept, appearance, and behavior of software products
- **Interface Design** — Interfaces between humans and computers
- **Interactive Communication Systems** — Knowledge transfer in museums and companies
- **Invention Design** — Research-experimental study for novel products

## Program Structure

- **Foundation Studies (semesters 1–3):** Design practice, theory, technology
- **Specializations (semesters 4–6):** Interface Design or Physical Computing
- **Internship Semester:** 26 weeks mandatory internship before semester 6
- **Bachelor Thesis (semester 7)**

## Application

- **Online Portal:** https://me.hfg-gmuend.de/
- **Deadline WS:** May 15 (exclusion deadline)
- **Deadline SS:** November 15 (exclusion deadline)
- **Application Period WS:** Early April to mid-May
- **Application Period SS:** Early October to mid-November
- **Homework Assignment:** mandatory (program-specific)
- **Work Samples:** optional
- **Aptitude Test:** practical exam (full day) + oral interview (approx. 15 min)
- **Aptitude Test WS 2026/27:** June 3, 2026
- **Results:** in the personal applicant portal
- **Semester Start:** expected first week of October (WS) or mid-March (SS)
