# Digital Product Design and Development (B.A.)

> ⚠️ **Currently no applications are possible — the program is being phased out.**
>
> Successor program: [Digital Product Design and Development (B.Eng.) — Hochschule Aalen](https://www.hs-aalen.de/de/courses/66)

## Overview

Digital Product Design and Development combines design with technology. The program focuses on connected, digital and intelligent products — smart homes, fitness trackers, self-driving cars. Students network products via the internet and develop solutions with robotics, artificial intelligence and user experience.

## Focal Points

1. **Spatial Technologies** — Fixed/installed technologies: Smart Home, fine dust detectors, automated systems
2. **Portable Media** — Mobile digital technologies: wearable sensors, drones, data transmission
3. **Physical Computing** — Hardware development: voice assistants, sensors, Arduino, Raspberry Pi
4. **User-Centered Design** — Creative and research methods for user-centered innovation

## Curriculum

Students spend most of their time in the **Prototyping Lab**, **Media Lab** and **Workshops**. Tools include 3D printers, sensors, actuators, microcontrollers (Arduino, Raspberry Pi) and laser cutters. Solutions are created with VR glasses, bone conduction headphones, voice assistants and new technologies not yet available on the market.

Solid foundations in design and creative methods form the basis, complemented by technical prototyping skills.
