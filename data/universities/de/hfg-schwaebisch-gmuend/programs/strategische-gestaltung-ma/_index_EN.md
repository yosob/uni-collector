# Strategic Design: Design, Management and Research (MA)

Effective design must be strategic — problem-oriented, concept-driven, and goal-directed. The Master's program **Strategic Design** at HfG Schwäbisch Gmünd combines design, management, and research in an interdisciplinary, applied program.

## Program Profile

**Three Pillars:**
1. **Design** — Interdisciplinary design projects, design research, cultural studies
2. **Management** — Strategic and operational management, process and project management
3. **Research** — Scientific methods, design research, publication strategies

**Characteristics:**
- **Interdisciplinary:** No separation between product, communication, and interaction design — instead a holistic, interdisciplinary approach drawing from economics, sociology, rhetoric, and futures studies
- **Cooperative:** Teamwork, collaborations with institutions in science, technology, business, education, and culture; thesis often in cooperation with companies
- **User-centered:** Strong focus on user needs and user research

## Program Structure

- **Semester 1:** Strategic design project, strategic management (strategy development/formulation), scientific methods, cultural studies/design research
- **Semester 2:** Design project (focus on planning/organization, communication strategies), operational management, design research, scientific writing and publication strategies
- **Semester 3:** Master thesis (design project + scientific-theoretical work), typically in cooperation with external project partners

> **Note:** A study abroad semester is not included in the curriculum. Erasmus funding is therefore not available.

## Learning Objectives

- Holistic, interdisciplinary working as a designer
- Team leadership, communication, and mediation skills
- Multi-perspective approach to problem-solving
- Socially and ecologically responsible role of designers
- Ability to work on research tasks in design
- Use of evaluation methods for process optimization

## Application

**Application Periods:**
- **Winter Semester** (start: first week of October): Early April to **May 15** (exclusion deadline)
- **Summer Semester** (start: mid-March): Early October to **November 15** (exclusion deadline)

**Application Portal:** [me.hfg-gmuend.de](https://me.hfg-gmuend.de/)

**Required Documents:**
1. Tabular CV
2. Certified copy of university entrance qualification
3. Certified copy of qualifying university degree
4. Exmatriculation/study progress certificate/Transcript of Records
5. Passport photo
6. Proof of professional training, experience, or internship
7. Project sketch (approx. 2–3 pages, max. 500 words: objectives, methods, content/formal realization, literature/sources)
8. Work samples as PDF (max. 10 pages, max. 20 MB)
9. Optional: motivation letter (approx. 1 DIN-A4 page)

**Selection Interview:** Second part of the selection process; invitation at least 7 days in advance.

**International Applicants** additionally: TestDaF 4/DSH 2, recognition via Studienkolleg Konstanz, possibly APS certificate (China).

## Career Prospects

- Leadership positions in the design economy (R&D, communication, consulting)
- Self-employed designer / entrepreneur
- Design offices, agencies, publishers
- Research institutions, cultural and educational establishments
- Design research and university teaching
- **PhD possible** (with good/very good grade average)

## Supervision and Facilities

Professors and lecturers from communication, product, and interaction design as well as cultural and economic sciences provide personal supervision. High-profile professionals from industry and research as visiting lecturers. Design and scientific expertise alongside practice-oriented support from external project partners.
