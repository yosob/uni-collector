# Product Design (BA)

Product designers create objects and conceptualize processes. From headphones to coffee machines, from e-bikes to local trains — product designers are involved everywhere. Product design is demanding and diverse. All objects, including devices and machines, are becoming "smarter," communicating with us and each other. They are embedded in systems and will be capable of much more in the future.

## Specializations (from semester 4)

1. **Product Design** — Innovative products and product systems. Simple solutions for specific problems, optimal functions for products, devices, tools, or machines. Making usage and operation clear and pleasant.
2. **Process Design** — From the first step to a concrete goal: logistics, safety, maintenance, and every action in a workflow. Designing the entire service or process.

## Program Structure

- **Foundation Studies (semesters 1–3):** Form, material, design practice, technology, 3D model building. Group project work from the first semester.
- **Specializations (semesters 4–6):** Project work with prototyping, few written exams.
- **Bachelor Thesis (semester 7):** Independent bachelor project.

## Philosophy

HfG focuses on what is helpful and useful — no mere "beautification" or disposable products. Simply what is good, for everyone, in uncompromising quality.

## Application

- Online application via portal (WS: early April to May 15, SS: early October to November 15)
- Homework assignment mandatory
- 12–15 work samples mandatory (flat works, films, models, animations)
- Aptitude test (practical + oral): WS 2026/27 on June 3, 2026
