# Schwäbisch Gmünd University of Design — University Profile

## University Overview

**Schwäbisch Gmünd University of Design** (Hochschule für Gestaltung Schwäbisch Gmünd) is the only state-funded university in Baden-Württemberg exclusively dedicated to design. Located in the historic city of Schwäbisch Gmünd, it offers a focused, interdisciplinary design education with small class sizes and close faculty-student interaction.

- **Founded:** —
- **Location:** Schwäbisch Gmünd, Baden-Württemberg, Germany
- **Type:** Fachhochschule (University of Applied Sciences)
- **Website:** https://www.hfg-gmuend.de

## Application Process Summary

### Bachelor Programs

1. **Online Application:** Register at https://me.hfg-gmuend.de/
2. **Application Period:**
   - Winter Semester: Early April to May 15
   - Summer Semester: Early October to November 15
3. **Submit Documents:** University entrance qualification, CV, proof of study orientation, passport photo
4. **Prepare Portfolio/Homework:** Program-specific design homework (mandatory); work samples (varies by program)
5. **Aptitude Test:** Full-day practical exam (design, representational, technical-constructive tasks) + 15-min oral interview
6. **Results:** Published in the personal applicant portal
7. **Semester Start:** Early October (WS) or mid-March (SS)

### Master Program (Strategic Design)

1. **Online Application** via portal during application periods
2. **Submit Documents:** CV, certified degree copies, transcript, project sketch (2–3 pages), work samples (PDF, max 10 pages), optional motivation letter
3. **Selection Interview:** Invited after document review (at least 7 days notice)
4. **Requirements:** Completed Bachelor's degree (210 ECTS; 180 ECTS possible with bridging semester)
